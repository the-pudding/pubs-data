Old Blue left this review about The Ship

This struck me as a friendly, old fashioned local on my Sunday evening visit. From the four hand pumps the Tongham TEA wasn’t available and I paid £3.55 for a decent pint of Pride in preference to Courage Best or Abbot. Not the most interesting selection as has been said before, but I thought it was an interesting pub, liked its atmosphere, and wouId happily come back again if I was in the area.
