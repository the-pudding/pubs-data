Alex Conway left this review about The Last Drop Inn

York brewery pub with plain paneling and basic decor. Six real ales on the bar and I opted for a pint of Terrier for £3.15, not a bad price in this area of York and was very nicely kept.
Not the most entertaining atmosphere and a little uncomfortable with the hard wooden chairs as well as a cold draught from people walking in and out of the smoking area.

A nice little pub to stop off at with average beer but York brewery is not always to everyone taste. not the sort of place to stay all night but you could do a lot worse.
