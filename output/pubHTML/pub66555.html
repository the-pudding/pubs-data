Old Boots left this review about Morefield Motel

A friendly bar in a motel hidden among bungalows on the north side of Ullapool. There is a single L shaped dining room cum bar with a conservatory at the front of the building, a more formal restaurant occupies another part of the building. The bar section has comfortable bench seating and tables while the dining area is conventionally laid out with tables and chairs. There are three handpumps usually selling Scottish microbreweries ales plus Thatchers Cider, Becks, Guinness, and the usual scots kegs, Belhaven Best, Tennants and Youngers Best . More than 50 malts line the shelves above the bar. Only Red Cullin available during my quiet winter visit, a bit too cold but probably tasting a bit better than the “Feck Ale Left – tastes as fresh as the Scottish air, 0.0% ABV” advertised on another pump. A good collection of pump clips from Scottish micros behind the bar including Brew Dog. There's also a beer festival every October.
