Graham Coombs left this review about Ship Inn

Riverside pub in small village, although river frontage given over to car park. Much trade from nearby caravan parks. Building appears old but is fully modernised inside, so not a great deal of character. Bar to left, with right hand bar being more aimed at diners, but still drinking room. Large area of tables outside.Food was pretty good; beer from Black Sheep and others was tolerable but that sampled not in best condition. Not bad overall, but could be better.

On 2nd May 2015 - rating: 6
