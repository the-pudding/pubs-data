lyppy lyppy left this review about The Cornishman Inn

We ordered food - wish we hadn't. The burger and chips were undercooked and when we took it back the barman (Giles?) started eating off the plate whilst arguing with us saying it wasn't raw. We said we didn't want the food especially as he had cut into the burger and ate some chips. He finally went off to the kitchen, came back saying they would cook another burger but it wasnt raw. Less than 5 minutes later another (?) burger arrived, equally raw, and the chips were full of oil. We understand that there can be problems with food but it was the rudeness of the barman that was out of order! We will never go back there again!
