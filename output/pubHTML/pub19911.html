Peter Kelly left this review about The Commercial

This pub is under new management and i must say they are a lovely married couple. Since the 1/6/2014 they have and continue to make massive alterations to improve both customer enjoyment and safety. They have live bands once a month and entertainment Friday, Saturday and Sunday, it is truly becoming the venue for up and coming bands and a place for a great night out. Although it is still a little rough around the edges i am positive this couple will make it enjoyable to visit with their friendly personalities.

ROB Camra left this review about The Commercial

As you enter the bar is on the right, there's a DJ rig and a mirror ball in the room to the left and there's another room to the rear. Tracksuit bottoms, trainers and football shirts seem to be the attire of choice here. Or it may not be choice, some looked like these were the only clothes they owned. There's no cask ale of course so I had a half of fizzy stuff and watched Castleton's low flyers prepare for their Friday night binge. I was in here at about 8 p.m. on a Friday, I wouldn't have liked to be here at 11, it would have been scary. It's a dump and it suits the clientele perfectly.
