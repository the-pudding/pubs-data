ROB Camra left this review about The Dial

Having been to some of Burton's finest beer pubs we decided to call in here to get the other half a good glass of red wine. To my surprise there were 2 handpumps on the bar with the boring Doom Bar on one, but the absolutely excellent Long Man APA on the other. The bar itself is a long rectangle with lots of comfy chairs semi separated into different areas. The clientele were mainly Burton's "Nail Technicians" and other TOWIE wannabee airheads, but there were a smattering of more mature people as well. It's not going to be a regular haunt, but I'd call in again to see what's on the handpumps next time round.

On 14th October 2013 - rating: 6
