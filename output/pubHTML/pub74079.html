Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Angel Hotel

Fine old coaching inn, but nearly didn't go in - as I was guessing that only a poor keg selection would be available - until I saw a Cask Marque sign near the door. Bar much as you would expect from a substantial hotel, with comfortable furniture and quiet background music. A few patio tables out front. Three handpumps, with Pedigree, Banks's Bitter and Grainstore Rutland Bitter (£3.00) on offer. Overall, somewhat better than I expected.

On 3rd October 2012 - rating: 6
