Komakino . left this review about Scotts Bar

A cocktail / sports bar located in the shadow of Bolton Wanderers' stadium, but named after a city in Montana(?). There's an island bar, exposed brick throughout, no real ale, be-aproned waiters, overloud music and, if you get here after sundown, those rotating-light ceiling balls (whatever happened to a good old-fashioned glitterball?). One or two mildly interesting bottles in the fridge (Blue Moon, Einstock), but with a pint of Birra Moretti coming in at £4.75, you're likely paying for the location. Give it a miss...
