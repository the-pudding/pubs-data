Alan Winfield left this review about The Hare & Hounds Inn

The Hare and Hounds is a really nice looking village pub.
Once inside here the pub had a cosy feel to it with beams on the ceiling and cottagy type furnishings,this is a Samuel Smiths tied house and the one real ale they brew was on here the Old brewery bitter was a realy nice drink.
This pub was doing a lot of food trade but drinkers were made just as welcome and i quite enjoyed having a drink in here.

Chris Mulliss left this review about The Hare & Hounds Inn

We visited based on Sandra's recommendation and the lasagne was very well recommended (and comes in large and small portions)and the welcome considering it is under temporary management until some time in January. Warm welcome, even warmer fire and excellent Sam Smith's Ale delivered by horse dray. It even has Sam Smith's Organic Cider and their ethical orange juice. We could also see what Sandra said about the fish and chips. We will certainly visit again when in the area.
