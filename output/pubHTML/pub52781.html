Nicholas Furr left this review about Ye Olde Kings Head

This establishment has been open under new management since August 2008. We first visited in September of that year and returned in March after work on the Macclesfield canal allowed us back on our boat. The welcome was as good as anyone will get anywhere, and the Steak and Ale pies will satisfy the most critical. Well worth a visit wherever you are in the country. If you are on a boat you must stop at the Gurnett aqueduct

On 8th March 2009 - no rating submitted
