Alan Winfield left this review about The Horns Inn

The Horns is a decent looking pub that is tucked away just off the main road through Ashbourne.
On our first visit here the pub was a free house and there were three real ales on the bar with Pedigree being a very nice drink the other beers were Marstons Owd Roger and Bass.
On our second visit the pub seemed pretty much unalterd with a few different rooms on different levels,we had a drink in a smart and comfortable room,there were two real ales on the bar and we had a drink of Smiles best which was a very nice drink the other beer was Pedigree.
This was a decent pub to have a few drinks in.
