Alan Winfield left this review about The Mardyke

The Mardyke is a large looking pub that is on the Hotwell Road.
Once inside the bar faces in an oblong shaped room which runs the width of the pub,the floor is bare boarded.
There is lots of wood panneling,there are wood bench seats and wood pew seats.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Wickwar BOB,which was a decent drink and dirt cheap,the other real ale was Doom Bar.
The pub was very busy on my Saturday morning visit.
I quite liked this pub.

Pub visited 15/7/2017
