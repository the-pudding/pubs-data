Will Larter left this review about The Bulls Head

An old village local built in the limestone of this part of the Peak District. It's quite comfortable inside, with a long bar serving three real ales. Peak Ales Bakewell Best Bitter was the only local offering, the others being Wylam Angel from the north-east and the almost ubiquitous Wells Bombardier. I was on ginger beer as I was driving, so can't comment on the beers. As it was a sunny day we took our drinks to the bench/tables in the garden to one side.
