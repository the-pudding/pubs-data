Komakino . left this review about Fusion Bar (The Grosvenor Hotel)

A small, soulless bar part of of a hotel with no real ale (although Hobgoblin Gold was spotted in bottled form in the fridge). As such, a Champions League game showing on a wall-mounted TV was a welcome distraction over a pint of Peroni.
