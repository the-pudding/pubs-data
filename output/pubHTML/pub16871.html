E TA left this review about The Bull

The earliest record of a pub on this site is from 1544, since when The Bull (also known as Ye Old Bull) has been a hotel and an inn. It was previously much larger than its current form. It used to be the prefered pub for farmers on market days and became a grand hotel in the early 20th Century. Now it's a two room high street pub, which does lunches. There was a friendly welcome from the staff , although they didn't seem to know what they were doing. Spitfire was the only on offer, but it was badly kept and tasted very gassy. Worth dropping in for a quick bite, but I wouldn't recommend it for its beer.

On 30th May 2011 - rating: 4
