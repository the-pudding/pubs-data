Graham Coombs left this review about Black Bull

Modernised pub in quiet village, hidden away off main road. The interior is very clean, bright and modern, with a slight preference towards eating but still fair enough for drinkers. Three Yorkshire beers on offer - York Terrier sampled and in good nick. Quite pleasant but not much character.

On 15th October 2016 - rating: 7
