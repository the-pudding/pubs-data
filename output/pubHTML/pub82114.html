Will Larter left this review about Red Lion

A stone-built village pub with sleeping lions above the door and one by the fireplace. It was very quiet at the time of my visit, which was after lunchtime on a Tuesday, so not surprising. There were three hand pumps with two beers available at the time of my visit (see photo). My half of the locally brewed Firebrick Blaydon Brick was in good nick. This is a pleasant pub which is well worth a visit if you're staying in the area - it's not far from Chesters on the Roman wall.

Date of visit: 2nd May 2017
