Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about White Horse Hotel

Traditional two-bar pub, simply furnished and plainly decorated. The mock-Tudor façade is usually the most notable feature, but this was largely hidden by scaffolding when I visited. Surprisingly, perhaps, it does do food (on some days, at least). Boiling Well from Ludlow and Cloud Nine from Six Bells (£3.00) available from a pair of handpumps on each counter.
