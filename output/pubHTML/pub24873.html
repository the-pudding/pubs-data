Tris C left this review about Ground Floor Bar

Quite a long pub with a side bar. The choice of drinks is rather limited and there's no real ale; there's dining upstairs and possibly another bar.

The interior is slightly shabby chic with a mix of furniture ranging from bar stools, conventional tables and chairs as well as low banquettes.

I found this place slightly more appealing than all the other Portobello Road pubs, but that's not saying much.
