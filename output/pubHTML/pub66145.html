Rex Rattus left this review about Finchley Tavern

There's no real ale and no sign of food when I visited on Wednesday afternoon. My half of Guinness was actually quite good - at £1.80 for a half.

This is a very basic shop conversion bar. There's a bit of fixed bench seating combined with standard tables and chairs at the front, and a couple of tall tables with tall stools down at the back. It's carpeted, but with a red and black tiled splash area around the counter, and has a few photos on the walls mainly of old local scenes. There are four TV screens, which means in a small place like this you can see one wherever you sit. That's either a good or bad thing, depending on your point of view (no pun!).

It's a characterless shop conversion, but it's clearly popular with some of the local population, as they were doing a reasonable trade. But I think I would prefer to spend my time in the Bohemia, solely because the beer is better - or perhaps more accurately more to my taste.
