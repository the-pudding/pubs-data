Colin Walker left this review about Robins Bar

Robin’s Bar is situated in the middle of a housing scheme and it shows by its exterior and by the take-away food joined on. But don’t let this exterior put one off. Robin himself runs the bar when I visited and the interior is altogether a far better bet. The pool table takes up a good bit off the floor. But considering the exterior the place would probably only visited by the locals. So after having a few pints of McEwans the take-away is right next-door.
