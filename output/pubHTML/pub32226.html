Gerald Scrivens left this review about The Inn On The Green

Disappointing modern estate pub offering Banks beers, Bombardier, Shropshire Gold and a guest ale (Stonehouse Bitter not 'on'). Food is also offered but not evident on the day of my visit (Saturday lunch) - a younger customer brought in chips from the local chippie. Worth a visit for the beers but do not expect too much

On 1st May 2009 - rating: 6
