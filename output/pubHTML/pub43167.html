Alan Winfield left this review about The Bessemer

The Bessemer is a modern pub set under an office block.
Once inside there was one large room with the bar facing,there was a small raised area to the right,the seating was mainly normal tables and chairs.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Sheffield Crucible Best which went down well,the other beers were Abbeydale Moonshine and Bombardier.
The pub was very busy on our early Saturday evening visit.
I would be quite happy to return to this pub at a quieter time during the day.

Pub visited 19/4/2014

ROB Camra left this review about The Bessemer

Large Smith & Jones pub that looks like a slightly upmarket JDW. Large main room with a small raised area to the right. A further room to the left has 2 pool tables. There's also an upstairs area which was closed during my visit. The toilets are down 2 flights of stair, also like a JDW. There's a table in the main room with a Christmas Tree and adverts up for Christmas. It's September FFS. Four handpumps on the bar with 3 in use serving Abbeydale Moonshine, Courage Best & Bombardier. The Moonshine was in decent condition. It's a reasonable enough pub, especially if you like JDWalikes but it's going to lose a point for the Christmas Tree.

On 26th September 2013 - rating: 5
