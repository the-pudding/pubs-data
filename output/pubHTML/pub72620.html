Alan Winfield left this review about Harrington Arms

The Harrington Arms is a nice looking pub in a nice location near the river trent and close to the church,this pub used to be a Kimberley tied house.
This pub is now a GK owned pub and once inside here the pub has got an olde worlde feel to it with real beams on the ceiling,there are a few different rooms and drinking areas one of these rooms is a restaurant and the food here is very nice.
There are four real ales on the bar with GK IPA, Abbot and St Edmunds and a GK guest beer i had a drink of St Edmunds which was a decent drink.
There is a large garden at the back of the pub which the pub has a beer festival in each August they have a large marquee and around 40 beers.
This is a really decent pub that is well worth a visit.

On 28th January 2011 - rating: 8
