Alan Winfield left this review about Hollin Bush

The Hollin Bush is a decent looking pub that is a short walk from mansfield road.
Once inside there was a square shaped room to the front,the room was carpeted and had comfy bench seating,there was a TV showing football,there was a smaller room to the rear which looked very narrow.
There were two unused pumps on the bar,i had a drink of keg Stones bitter which was decent enough,the other keg beers were John Smiths Magnet and John Smiths smooth crap.
The pub was very busy on my early Saturday evening visit,with a good lively atmosphere.

Pub visited 28/9/2013
