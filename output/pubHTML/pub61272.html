ROB Camra left this review about The Coach House

Opened up one roomed pub, which is very food orientated. The area to the rear is almost a restaurant. All the suppliers of the meat & vegetables etc are listed on the menu which is a nice touch. There's a beer patio out the front which gets very full in summer. There are 6 handpumps, but only 4 beers on, 3 from Thwaites and one from Spitting Feathers. The Spitting Feathers was a good pint. I wouldn't mind calling in again, but it would only be for one.

On 15th June 2013 - rating: 6
