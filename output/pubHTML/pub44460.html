Oggwyn Great left this review about Lamb Inn

Large pub on the Newport to Shrewsbury road , very food led , you can tell as soon as you enter and come face to face with a massive wine rack , the bar area is to the left with a couple of seating areas facing the bar , further around is another area with a pool table , to the right of the entry through large glass doors is the large resturant , the decor is clean and tidy , lots of wood and all the seating throughout is in dark brown leather , very busy on my Sunday lunchtime visit , but service was very good , the food was good and very good value .
Usual keg taps and three handpulls with Directors , Golden Hen and Batemans Hooker which was in decent nick .
Overall not a bad pub but more for food than drink
