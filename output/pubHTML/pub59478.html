Will Larter left this review about The New Harp Inn

I passed this way by accident, having taken a diversion from my planned cycle route to take in the Cottage of Content; finding that pub not open on a Monday afternoon, I was then desperate for a beer: any beer. Fortunately I found this place. Only one real ale on (out of four hand pumps, but with two pump clips turned around, so they probably have more on at the weekend), but a very good one it was, being Wye Valley Bitter. This is fairly close in style to Hobsons Best, and in my opinion a much more palatable beer than Wye Valley Butty Bach or HPA. There were a few people in, probably locals, and a family of tourists also popped in, so there is some value in country pubs opening on a Monday after all. The pub itself, not a terribly attractive building, seems to have had a bit of an internal spruce up, looking fairly modern and bright. There's a campsite attached, so I imagine they do quite well at weekends.

Date of visit: 31st July 2017
