Quinno _ left this review about The New Leathern Bottle

An old place off the main road and is fairly small. L-shaped and a traditional interior within. The ‘word of the day’ made me smile. Dart board spotted. It’s also home to a Land Rover club. Large grassed front garden with kids play area. Two ales, Truman's Runner (good) and Itchin Valley Hampshire Rose; IV Pure Gold clip turned. Old Rosie also on. Friendly service at the bar. A decent little boozer I’d happily do again.

On 21st July 2015 - rating: 7
