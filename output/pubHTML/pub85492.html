Ian Mapp left this review about Mash Guru

A real surprise and a gem of a place.

We'd sampled a few pubs in town but have stumbled on this back alley on the way to the main street, we vowed to come back to check out the live music.

Lovely cellar bar and the music didn't disappoint. Top band on, doing the usual sort of Saturday night covers.

The real revelation was the beer quality - Marble Arch Bitter in bang on form. Best pint of the night.

Give it a try!

On 26th March 2019 - rating: 10
