Real Ale Ray left this review about The Ring O Bells

Large mock Tudor Greene King pub built around the beginning of the 18oo's. You step down into the pub with the bar positioned on the right hand side with a selection of four Greene King ales, and no guest ale to be seen. The pub is one long narrow space, which is mostly geared up for dining. I thought the pub was uninspiring and the décor was tired and knocked about.
