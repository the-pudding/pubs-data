Catherine Roffe left this review about White Horse Inn

A lovely village pub: great food using fresh local ingredients; I'm sure all the beer is excellent, but my favourite is the Cotleigh Tawny (unless Buzzard is on!) and they do accommodation. Bacon and egg for breakfast and a log fire - my idea of heaven!

On 19th September 2007 - rating: 10
