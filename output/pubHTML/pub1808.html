Quinno _ left this review about Wellington Arms

L-shaped Brakspear foody pub done up in a fairly modern style with no real intrinsic internal character. Three Marstons list ales; Brakspear Bitter, Hobgoblin and a tainted Ringwood Boondoggle. Accommodation offered. The large outside front decking is a fair place to sup in the summer months.

On 1st September 2015 - rating: 5
