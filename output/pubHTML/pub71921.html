Keith Venn left this review about Bull Ring

We regularly visit this pub for Sunday lunch, but today will be the last time. My mother-in-law booked a table in the restaurant for 1pm today, three days earlier, and was told that it would be busy as there was a Christening party on, but it would be alright. When we arrived, they denied all knowledge of it and we were not able to eat. When we had been before, if you like roast dinners there was a good choice, and the price was very reasonable. Alas, this will be our last visit.

On 10th July 2011 - rating: 1
