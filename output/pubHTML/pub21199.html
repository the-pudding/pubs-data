Alan Winfield left this review about The Horseshoe

The Horseshoe is a decent looking white painted pub that is situated next to the crossroads.
Once inside there was a single room that ran the width of the pub,the left side was for drinkers,this area was carpeted and had comfy bench seating and normal tables and chairs,the right side was set up for diners with tables laid out.
The pub used to be a Mansfield house so all beers come from the Marstons list,i had a drink of Banks's Sunbeam which was a very nice drink,the other beers were Ringwood Forty Niner and Brakspear Oxford Gold.
There were not that many other customers in on our afternoon visit but there was some background music playing.
The pub is in the processs of being repainted so had no signs outside when i took a photo.
This was a fairly pleasant pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 27/7/2013
