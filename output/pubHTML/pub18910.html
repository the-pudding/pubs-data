Jon B left this review about Royal Station

an atmospheric Victorian hotel that is being restored very nicely to its former glory as the heart of Carnforth. I stumbled into the modernised 'Junction Bar' which I shared with an elderly man who didn't even touch his Guinness once while I was here. only two of the six handpumps were on, there may have been more in the bigger busier bar. Titanic beers on both (including Plum Porter) so I didn't mind. great place to visit now.

Will Larter left this review about Last Drop Inn (Royal Station Hotel)

The public bar, signed Last Drop Inn, was not open at the time of our visit, and drinkers were redirected to the hotel entrance and the bar on the left which backs onto the Last Drop. It was full to bursting on a Saturday afternoon, with groups of lads stood around drinking lager and ignoring the sports on tv. We could just discern one lonely hand pump on the bar by standing on tiptoes to peer over the scrum, but thought better of it. (Just as well we did, as we almost missed getting served at the Snug on the station, as it was just on two o'clock when we turned up there.)

Date of attempted visit: 27th November 2015
