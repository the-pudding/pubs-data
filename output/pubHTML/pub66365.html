Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Hop House

Family-focussed Fayre & Square outlet in the Knights Park entertainment complex, with a Wacky Corner as well so take your ear plugs if of a sensitive disposition and visiting at a weekend lunchtime... Otherwise, it's the usual semi-modern furniture and fittingly beige decor in the various seating / dining areas, plus a few wooden picnic benches out front for the better weather. Does do real ale, albeit just Greene King IPA from one of a pair of handpumps when I popped in.
