Old Boots left this review about Tallulahs

Reached via an alley off the market place this is a long, thin, upstairs bar with the counter at one end, divided by a chimney breast. There's just the one hand pump, serving a well kept Pennine on my visit. The other end of the room has the stage with large pictures of Marilyn Monroe and Audrey Hepburn on the wall behind, it does not however appear to be a gay bar, this is Bedale for goodness sake.
