peter ashworth left this review about Arcade Beers

Smallish bar with wooden floorboards,4 bar stools and a few sets of tables and chairs composed of a mixture of styles.The bar is to the right and at first could not detect any beer pumps,however there was 6 at the rear of the bar but I think only 3 were functioning.Not sure if they sell pints as the only measures I saw advertised were third pints, halves and two thirds.Tried a half of the Blackjack stout which was in good form and might have tried a couple more but time was a bit limited.
There are a few cabinets with cans and bottles in and one had an offer of any 6 for £15.
