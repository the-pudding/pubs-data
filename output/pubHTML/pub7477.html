Keith LEVER left this review about Kings Arms Inn

I called in at this cosy pub the other day, a Mavis Nightingale was in attendance and made my friend and I most welcome, as we sat in the lounge a brass band was rehearsing in an upstairs room. The beer was reasonably priced and well recomended, my friend asked for a coffee and the lady in charge quickly went into her private abode and came out with the piping hot drink. WE WILL DEFINITELY BE VISITING THIS PLEASANT PUBLIC HOUSE AGAIN IN THE VERY NEAR FUTURE.

On 26th March 2008 - rating: 10
