Quinno _ left this review about The Cock Inn

Grim-looking two bar local which smelt of damp and has clearly seen better days. Surprised to note this is a Wadworth pub. Plenty of pumps displaying the 6X clip but none of them dispensed it. Decided to scarper. This place needs a severe boot up the rear, it’s squalid. Did note a lone upright piano, oddly.
