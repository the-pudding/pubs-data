Michael Wright left this review about West Meon

Was formerly the "West Meon Hut"

I went in about 3pm on Friday, unfortunately the 3 draught bitters were not ready, told they ran out over the Bank Holiday and new delivery had not settled enough to serve, at least it was honest

Had a pint of a forgettable keg bitter instead, the other options were all standard lagers / ciders

Located on junction of A272 & A32, always seems busy, large car park + play area for kids

They have a large food menu, given the pub location I doubt it is a "local" pub, but does concentrate on standard pub style food

Only 2 stars because of beer shortage, maybe worth 3 but not from my visit

On 2nd June 2008 - rating: 6
