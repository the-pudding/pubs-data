Stan Frost left this review about Kings Head Hotel

The King's Head Ravenstonedale is undergoing complete refurbishment in 2010-2011 and is due to reopen in July 2011 under new management. The new and vibrant management team, not associated with the last tenant, is determined to restore the reputation of this traditional Cumbrian pub as a welcoming hostelry in which good food, real ales and excellent accommodation can be enjoyed.
