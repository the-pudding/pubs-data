Rex Rattus left this review about The Butchers Arms

This one had five ales on - the St Austell pair of Tribute and Trelawny; Gale's Seafarers, Fuller's ESB; and Courage Best.

This used to be a 'spoons, and it looks it. It has the look and character of a Stonegate pub, but it's not one of theirs. It's a shop conversion by the look of it, and it stretches back a long way, making it a long and stringy sort of pub. Right at the back is an area with three dartboards. Bucket armchairs were included among the furnishings.

I have to say that although the ESB was in good shape, I found this a characterless pub although there's nothing wrong with it per se. It's certainly not worth going out of your way to visit, but OK for a swift one if you're wandering down Barnet High Street for any reason.
