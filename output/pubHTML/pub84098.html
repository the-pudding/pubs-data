Real Ale Ray left this review about Albion Tap

A small bar that was a former bottle shop. The interior now has a long bar down the far left hand side and had a stainless steel counter, four handpumps on the bar and eight keg taps along the back wall. The seating arrangements consisted of high tables and stools along the window side wall and a low table and chairs positioned along the far wall. A few painted bees around the ceiling added some novelty. We went for the locale, which was Donkeystone Brewery Cotton Clouds, which was in excellent condition. It slipped down nicely with a hot meat and potato pie from Buckley's the bakers, just up the road on the opposite side. One of the best pies in the country at £1.60 each.
