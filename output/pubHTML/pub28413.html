Philip Gaskin left this review about Hotel Wroxham

Back in Wroxham for our annual holiday I visited the Hotel Wroxham a few times. I was pleased to see that Doom Bar was available, (albeit at £3.20,) but it did not taste like I remember Doom Bar and I did not enjoy it. When I returned later it was off. Woodforde’s Wherry, Adnams Broadside and Fox Brewery Bitter were available. I chose the Broadside which is nice but a bit heavy for a session. I returned with my wife later in the evening and switched to Carlsberg. WiFi is now available, but I could not get it to work and the barman told me it was not reliable. After that I just visited the Kings Head. It is still a nice place to be as you overlook the river, and I hope the beer selection has changed when we return next year.

Philip Gaskin left this review about Hotel Wroxham

We visit Wroxham each year, and I use the Hotel wroxham almost every day we are here. The entrance area has been repainted on the outside, and the toilets have been refurbished. Four real ales were available, Courage Best, (at £2.75,) Charles Wells Eagle IPA, Adnams Broadside and Wolf Brewery Golden Jackal. At the end of the week, the Courage Best was replaced by Adnams Bitter. It is still nice to sit next to the window or outside, and watch the boats. A useful place to have opposite to where we stay, and I shall be back next year.

On 2nd July 2012 - rating: 8
