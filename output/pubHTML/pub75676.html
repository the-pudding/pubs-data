Soup Dragon left this review about The Stag Inn

A detached Marston's cottage-styled pub with patio area. The pub is in cream render, certainly at least Victorian and has windows flanking the central entrance. The interior has a couple of rooms. The lounge is a narrow room in cream and wood, with sea pictures, a piano and a small fireplace. The bar is in two sections - at the top, in green, there is a pool and dart area that also has a TV (not on) and jukebox (not on) - at the bottom, in cream, an area with bench seats and another TV (not on). They do food, but we didn't eat. The service was friendly and a couple of people were on the front patio (there is also a side one). Beer; usual tap stuff with Pedigree and a decent Jenning's Bitter on handpull. A nice little place and worth a visit for one.
