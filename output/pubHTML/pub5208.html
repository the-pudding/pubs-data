Gill Smith left this review about The Turks Head

Called into this pub because it had been recommended for value meals, and enjoyed both the meal and the Morland Old Speckled Hen. The pub itself was popular and busy with locals at lunchtime. The bar served a large single room which had various levels and types of seating. Close to the market place.
