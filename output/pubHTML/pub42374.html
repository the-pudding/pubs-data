Ian Mapp left this review about The Tally Ho Inn

Two good beer guide pubs within half a mile of each other - in the depths of the countryside. Can only assume Worcester Camra have had a day trip out.

Stunning views from this pub over Clee Hills and Teme Valley. Remote, so relying of food trade but four decent real ales on - two from Wye Valley, Ludlow Gold and Old Hoppy Hen.

Half a Ludlow Gold for me, decent condition.

Nice pub - would come again. Visit blogged at http://bit.ly/2oB167g
