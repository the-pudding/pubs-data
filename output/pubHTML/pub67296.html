Al Bundy left this review about Hand Hotel

Good place to have a drink if you're going to sit in the beer garden during the summer. The inside is a bland main room hotel bar with a couple of deep chairs situated by the open fire. Usually only one real ale on and this is usually from Plassey Brewery. In the summer cross the narrow road outside and sit in the pretty beer garden that leads down to River Dee.

Alan Hurdle left this review about Hand Hotel

A large old style hotel overlooking the River Dee. The large bar/lounge area has traditional entertainment from the huge multi-keyboard piano/organ and singing from the likes of the Llangollen choir. Food available in the bar or outside in the terraced Dee gardens. Real Ale available from the local Plassey Brewery. Accommodation in 58 en-suite bedrooms.

On 13th October 2011 - rating: 7
