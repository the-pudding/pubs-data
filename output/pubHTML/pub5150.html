Gill Smith left this review about Barley Sheaf

Called in here while looking for something to eat early Saturday evening, and the outside signs said Sunny Spice at the Barley. We went in for a curry, and found there was a bar there with one real ale on, Wooden Hand Cornish Mutiny which we had with our meal. The front area was set as a normal pub with the main room set for dining, as was the conservatory area where we had our meal. When we finished I went to the bar and asked if it was a restaurant with a bar, or a bar with a restaurant, and the young lady behind the bar confirmed it was a bar with a restaurant. No problems with food or ale at all.

On 22nd June 2014 - rating: 6
