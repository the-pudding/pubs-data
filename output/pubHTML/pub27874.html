Thuck Phat left this review about The Horse & Barge

Approached from the adjacent canal, through the the garden, there's an air of an unloved caravan park in winter with the garden unkempt and strewn with summer detritus and a view of an empty, glass walled restaurant at the back of the pub. We weren't sure whether or not the place was open. The front isn't much better and you enter into a wide, open bar area with a pool table on the right and assorted soft furnishings.
The pub was all but empty on our visit but there were two other customers who demonstrated belching and swearing with considerable prowess which livened up the lunchtime.
We didn't eat and I'm not even sure if food was being offered.
Beer: 1 handpump with the dreaded Bombardier meant a couple of pints of Guiness which was pretty good.
I'd only re-visit if very thirsty and left with absolutely no choice.

On 17th March 2011 - rating: 1
