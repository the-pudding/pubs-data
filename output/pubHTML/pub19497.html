Al Bundy left this review about The Station

A large Fayre Square gastropub place. Situated at the end of the Morecambe prom area, this is a fairly standard pub. That said, it is quite a grand place inside and sort of L-shaped. The fixtures are nice and the bar an impressive sight. There are various areas to sit, including an outside pavement area at the front. The were 2 handpumps on the bar but only Greene King IPA on on my visit. As its a foodie place expect to find oversized menus on the tables.
