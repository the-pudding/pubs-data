Alan Winfield left this review about The Station Inn

The Station is a decent sized pub which has its main entrance to the right side.
Once inside there is a small pool room to the front left,the main bar room is through another door to the rear,this room is fairly large and L shaped,the room is carpeted and has comfortable bench seating and chairs.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Leeds Pale which was ok but slightly past its best,the other beer was Tetley Bitter.
There was some quiet background music playing,the TV was not on during my Saturday afternoon visit.
I thought this was a very pleasant and comfortable pub to have a drink in.
