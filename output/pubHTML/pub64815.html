Al Bundy left this review about The Railway

A decent enough Holts pub. Open plan with little snugs to the right as you enter and a tiny area to the left. The rear of the pub is a small lounge like area. 7 handpumps on the bar with Holts beers on most and the others for guest ales and cider.

Alan Winfield left this review about Railway Inn

The Railway is a smallish pub that is opposite the Metropolitan and in west didsburys small shopping area.
Once inside i went in the room at the front which was fairly smart and comfortable,i think there was another room towards the rear of the pub.
This pub is a Joseph Holts tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Holts bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Holts mild.
This was more of a locals pub and i was quite happy having a drink in here.
