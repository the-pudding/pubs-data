Graham Coombs left this review about The George & Dragon Inn

Fairly large pub in centre of village. Quite nice main bar, but at back by car park, with the front room laid out more for dining, but a bit spartan. A couple of fairly boring beers on handpump. Unpretentious fair value food. Generally child-friendly, including climbing frame in garden.
