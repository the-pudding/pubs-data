Soup Dragon left this review about The Shoulder Of Mutton

A large detached yellowy rendered pub, restaurant and B&B, with black paintwork and patio

The interior had two rooms a restaurant room, in a salmony colour, with cream wood panel and a bar, in cream and a toffee colour with laminate flooring. We didn't eat, but the menu was around £9. The bar had a TV - with the footy on - they were nice enough to put it on especially - and there was a dart board. The background music was general and at a decent level. The service was very friendly and the clientele all eating, but there were definatel people in there! Interest was supplied by those arty kind of pictures.

Beer; tap stuff with an OK Pedigree as the single handpull.

A pleasant enough place - especially for those that like the modern look. It lacks that extra real ale for someone like me, but if that is not an issue for you, it seems fine.
