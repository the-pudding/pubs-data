Alan Winfield left this review about Solbar/Bodega

This bar is in the centre of Bowness and it is opposite the church.
There were a few tables and chairs at the front but these were taken so we went in the left room which was called Bodega this had an island bar and tall tables and chairs there was also a view of the lake from this room,there was a corridor at the rear of this room which took you to the right hand room this was called Solbar,this room was a lot bussier and the bar was on the right side of this room the toilets were also in this room.
There was one handpump in each bar and both of these had Theakston best on this drink was alright but nothing to shout about.
This bar concentrates on foreign beers and i thought it was decent of them to put a token real ale on.
The bar had a nice relaxed atmosphere to it and we were happy enough having a drink in here.

Pub visited 21/8/2011
