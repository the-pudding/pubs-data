elfed jones left this review about The Crown Inn

This is an unspoilt local treasure! Lots of good beers in a superbly refurbished local inn. This is a tavern with a true community spirit. Plenty of room to talk and mix, or to sit quietly and enjoy the ambience. But this still has a local feel you can walk in and feel part of it after your first drink. The welcome is warm and you feel that you are valued whether passsing through or part of the local scene! To compliment the excellent drink range there is now an excellent range of food.
Good parking is available but arrive early to ensure you get a space to enjoy this excellent venue!

On 15th May 2008 - rating: 10
