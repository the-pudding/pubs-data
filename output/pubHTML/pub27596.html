Komakino . left this review about The Carpenters

A large GK Meet & Eat establishment with the usual garish decor and plethora of meal deals. Horseshoe-shaped internally, there's an area to the right commandeered by three pool tables and the clientele are as you would expect in these parts. Just the one ale - Abbot - so gave it a swerve.
