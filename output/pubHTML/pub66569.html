Alex Conway left this review about Stonegate Yard

located on little stonegate, this bar goes a bit under the radar as walking past numerous times i have never really noticed it. I went on a reluctantly went on Wednesday evening but had nothing to worry about as this turns out to be a very nice place. I was expecting some crap cocktail type place but the main emphasis is on exotic lagers and craft beers with two real ales.

I had a pint of banks Sunbeam and this was very well kept and quite reasonably priced at £3.20 espeially in this part of town. The other beer was Boon Boggle by Ringwood at the same price.

Although not really my kind of setting i rather liked this place in comparison to all the other overpriced fancy pants bars York has as it has a much more chilled out and relaxed atmosphere without being to pretentious. I would gladly return here for a drink if forced around this street.

Alan Winfield left this review about Stonegate Yard

Stonegate Yard is a pub that has eluded me so it was time to see what it is like.
I entered through a nice looking courtyard,once inside there is a long narrow room with the bar facing,the room is split into three areas,there is a small square shaped area to the left which is down two steps,this area had very comfy bench seating with tables and chairs,the bar area had normal tables and chairs.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of a beer called My Generation which was quite a nice drink,the other beer was Milestone american Pale Ale.
Decent background music was playing.
I quite liked this pub,it has a nice laid back atmosphere.
Well worth a visit.

Real Ale Ray left this review about Stonegate Yard

We popped in here on a Monday afternoon to see the guest ale was gone, so our choice was their regular Black Sheep, which was in good shape. The layout consisted of a completely covered courtyard area on entry with the bar area to the left, which were both very dark. There were two small rooms from the bar which were tastefully wallpapered and bright. The restaurant area was upstairs, the staff were friendly and chatty.
