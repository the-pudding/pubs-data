Large multi-roomed pub that has recently been restored with some of the restorations still going on.the emphasis is on food but they do have two rotating real ales. Large front and rear gardens.

Bill Mitchell left this review about Clifden Arms

Great selection of beers, olde-worlde atmosphere, good food (if a little expensive) and very large garden. Out of the way place, peaceful. Don't go when the Waterperry Art festival is on - there's no other pub for miles.
