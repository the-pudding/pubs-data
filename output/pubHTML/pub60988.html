Graham Coombs left this review about Three Shires Inn

Splendid stone-built country pub at foot of hairy pass over mountains - handy for restorative brandy on way down. One large bar room, with restaurant area to front, perhaps a bit too neatly rebuilt for much character but friendly welcome. Excellent selection of local ales, including best kept pint of the day. Food sounded above average, which with the large range of malt whisky made the 10 bedrooms sound appealing. Recommended.
