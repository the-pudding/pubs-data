Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Anvil

Solid Black Country Ales pub, quite low in stature so obviously of quite some age (a view supported when you find the exposed ceiling beams within). There is also a more recent rear extension, but all parts have been refurbished in the same semi-traditional style (with lots of local history photographs on the walls). A patio beer garden (with fake grass) and a smokers shelter can be found at the back. With a total of ten handpumps, I found four of the brewery's own beers - including Chain Ale (£3.30) - four guests and a pair of ciders.
