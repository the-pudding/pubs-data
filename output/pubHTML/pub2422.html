Komakino . left this review about Moore Place Hotel

A hotel with a small bar to the left of its reception which appeared to have to have no truck with non-patron drinking (not that I was asked whether I was resident or not). A nice-looking building from the outside, rather soulless inside. No real ale but bottled versions of Fuller's London Pride and GK / Morland Old Speckled Hen available from the fridge - I didn't bother.
