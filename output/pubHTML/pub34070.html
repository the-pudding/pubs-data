Alan Winfield left this review about The Green Dragon

The Green Dragon is a decent sized stand alone pub that is situated quite close to the Travellers and Manor Barn pubs.
Once inside there was a decent sized square shaped room to the right,the room looked quite smart,with comfy bench seating to the front and rear of the room,it was also carpeted,there were two tall tables and stools in the middle of the room plus one leather settee.
There was a smaller seperate bar to the left which had a pool table in it.
There were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of keg Stones bitter which was drinkable but nothing special,the other keg bitter was John Smiths smooth crap.
The pub was fairly busy on my late Saturday afternoon visit.
This was a decent enough pub that was let down by the lack any real ales on the bar.

Pub visited 22/3/2014
