Paul Barraclough left this review about Cue Gardens

We recently held our wedding reception at Cue Gardens and we believe that we couldn't have picked a better venue. Lynn and the staff were fantastic, the atmosphere was brilliant and the food unbelievable (Danny and his team - Thanks Guys), Aaron, the DJ played absolutely everything and anything he was asked for.
As for the Pub itself, it should be re-named "TARDIS", it looks tiny on first sight but walk through the door and the expanse is beyond belief.
All the beers are well kept and flavoursome, prices very reasonable and it's always service with a smile, All in all 10 out of 10
