Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about De Parys Hotel

From a distance, this hotel building looks little different from the long lines of substantial houses on each side of the tree-lined avenue. Closer up, you see a more recent extension and a particularly dull pub sign (with no mention of Charles Wells, the brewery owners). The entrance lobby leads to a plainly-furnished lounge with a small bar at the back (plus a restaurant which I did not investigate). Two handpumps offering Eagle IPA and Youngs Gold (£3.00), but as the interior was rather uninspiring I went out the back doors to the sizeable part-patio/part-grassed beer garden which proved to be quite a pleasant spot in the sun (with the background sound of a cricket match on the adjacent school playing fields).
