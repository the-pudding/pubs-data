Oggwyn Great left this review about The Talbot

Busy , lively pub half way up Dawleys main street , this was my first visit here for around twenty years and nothings really changed , the bar is to the left of the entrance there is a pool table to the rear and steps on the right lead down to another room with a projecter TV screen , there are a couple more TVs in the area by the window , very busy on my visit , service was good but its very much a locals pub not exactly a slauhtered lamb but not far off .
No real ale on just the usual fizzy kegs
Not really my type of place , if you took the TVs out you could be in 1974 , but they must be doing something right for it to be so busy

On 8th April 2012 - rating: 3
