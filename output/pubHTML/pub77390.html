Alan Winfield left this review about Grounded

Grounded is a decent sized bar on the Gloucester road.
Once inside there is a single L shaped room with the bar facing to the right,the room is bare boarded,the seating a mix of wood bench seats and Settees plus normal tables and chairs in the middle of the room.
There were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of keg Bath Ales Jem which was a decent enough keg bitter.
Background music was playing and food was being served.
The bar was not that busy on my Saturday afternoon visit.
I have been in better bars and lots far more worse than this.

Pub visited 2/4/2016
