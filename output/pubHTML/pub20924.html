Adam Homer left this review about The White Hart

Banks's pub with, usually two, guest beers which are well kept.
Afternoon and evening meals available.
Pool, darts and skittles table. Sky tv usually shows a football or a rugby game on Saturday afternoons.
Large carpark and open garden.
Friendly landlord.
