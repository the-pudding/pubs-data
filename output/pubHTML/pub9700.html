Alan Winfield left this review about Bull Hotel

The Bull Hotel is a largish residential hotel that has got a smallish lounge bar that is open to the public.
This pub is in the middle of Bridport and next to the market.
Once inside this pub the room was quite small and well furnished there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Otter bitter and this was a decent drink the other beer was Bass
