The pub is completely different now, the decor is rustic and cosy, slightly more expensive than the Randolph which means that there's no gadgies!!! Completely different menu.. Contemporary Scottish Fayre is the selling point, excellent cuisine!!! Live Music on Fridays, occasionally Saturdays too, no Sky TV so no meaningless footie games to spoil yer night...3 real ales...excellent variety of malts....it's all good!!!! Slanj!!!!!
