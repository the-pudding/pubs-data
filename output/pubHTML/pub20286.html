Reviews of Simms Road Inn (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about Simms Road Inn

a fairly dull and dowdy country pub that feels somewhat tired inside and out. The long planned refurb can’t come soon enough. Inside it’s open plan with a traditional pubby side and a modern restaurant like extension. The real ales were the ubiquitous Wainwrights and some dross from Ringwood. Staff and clientele were like zombies when I was in, ie. completely lifeless.
