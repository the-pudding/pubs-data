ROB Camra left this review about Waggon Inn

Multi roomed pub with quite a bit of age to it. It's always busy in here, with a decent crowd. On my Friday evening visit, this was the 2nd last pub of a very long day, so my memory is a little vague. There were a couple, maybe more, handpumps on the bar and whilst I'm struggling to remember what I had, I do remember it was a good pint. I've always quite liked it in here and I'll call back to give it a more detailed review in the near futur.
