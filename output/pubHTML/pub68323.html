Gill Smith left this review about The Chase Inn

Called in while visiting another in the area, and found it to be a large pub with dining and drinking areas. Comfy sofa area by a wood burning fire. 3 cask ales available, 2 from Wye Valley which we chose and enjoyed and the other was Otter Bitter. There is another room at the back where there is pool, TV and darts.

On 8th April 2018 - rating: 7
