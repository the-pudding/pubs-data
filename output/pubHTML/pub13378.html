Fran Weeks left this review about Cleveland Bay

Great family friendly pub (dog friendly too!). Nice outdoor area with under cover seating...always good! The garden also has a kids play area.

Kids can have half portion of the food on the menu and the menu also has healthy options...good news for the waistline.

Good Wadworth ales of course, they also have a nice selection of hot drinks too so good for the drivers and lunchtimes.

All in all I'd recommend it - nice beer selection for dads, good food offering and fun play area for kids. Lovely summer drinking spot if you're in the area!

It's under new management too, so old stories about the old landlord should be ignored.

On 25th June 2009 - rating: 8
