Jon B left this review about Horse & Farrier

disappointing - a really old quaint looking pub and it lives up to that inside. But sadly it’s a restaurant. Nowhere to sit inside with a drink on a Sunday afternoon. I was not very welcome after revealing the shocking news that I wasn’t ordering food. My pint of Hophead consumed in a garden shed at the back was nice.
