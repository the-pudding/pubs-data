ROB Camra left this review about Loopy Shrew

Called in twice during our two days in Shrewsbury. Couldn't get served either time due to incompetent staff. The fact that they only had one boring beer on the handpulls didn't encourage us to stick it out either.

Old Boots left this review about Loopy Shrew

Trendy bar place nicely furnished with sofas, proper sized tables and chairs and some stools at benches. There’s a more restauranty bit at the back. Three pulls on the counter but just a mediocre Stonehouse beer on offer, prosaic kegs, usual spirits etc.
