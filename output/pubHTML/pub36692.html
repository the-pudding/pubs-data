Michael Hutchinson left this review about The Half Moon Inn

I recently visited the North East for the first time in 16 years. Accompanied by my nephew and son in law we went to the Half Moon for a couple of drinks two weeks ago prior to going to Prague the next day. I was impressed with the ambience and decor of the pub. The John Smith's was terrific value for money and a good tasting pint. The log fire was a welcome surprise and the management were first class. I look forward to a return visit soon, if only for a game of darts and a pint.

Peter Smith left this review about The Half Moon Inn

My wife and I stopped by the Half Moon Inn in Ryton on our way to Hadrian’s Wall and were very impressed with the welcome we received and the excellent choice of real ales.
The staff really knew their stuff and we spent a very pleasurable hour or so in front of a blazing log fire with the daily papers. Best of all, though, was that the pub’s dog friendly policy meant our Labrador was welcome too!
