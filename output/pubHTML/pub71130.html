Oggwyn Great left this review about Royal Oak

Large hotel with a public bar , it has obviously been re-furbished recently but still feels some what late 70s in decor , the bar is split in two with seperate drinking and dining areas .
Usual kegs and one real ale , an OK London Pride , service was strange to say the least , i was served by a friendly chatty bloke in chefs whites when the proper barman appeared he was very standoffish and unfriendly .
