Graham Coombs left this review about New Inn

A large old town pub very much catering for locals rather than tourists. It has a slightly run down feel, although some redecoration was under way. There is however something comforting about being greeted by the pub dog bringing his ball to be thrown! To the right of the central bar is a lounge area, with a dartboard, while to the left is a pool table area, also with a dartboard. Sports TVs proliferate and there is a handy rack of betting slips for the local bookies. Despite an old grill sign, it does not appear that food is currently served. Draught beers however included Bombardier, Caledonian Edinburgh Castle and Jennings Cumberland.
