Clive Thompson left this review about Star Inn

Previously called The Star Inn but now The George Inn, believed to have changed it's name sometime around 2008. It is now the only true pub in Ardersier and relies primarily on wet sales. There are two bars, one with a pool table, TV and dart board, which attracts the younger crowd; the second is more akin to a saloon bar and on both my visits was lively with local conversation. No cask beer.
