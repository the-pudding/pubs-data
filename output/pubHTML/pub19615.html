Al Bundy left this review about Army & Navy Hotel

A rough and rough pub. It doesn't look inviting and though its not as bad as I was expecting its still only a one-time-and-never-again visit and this was midweek afternon. A single room split into 2 with the small bar to your left. Appropriately Bobby Goldsboro's 'Honey' was on the jukie at the time. I been in worse, but not many. No real ale.

Just out of town centre, you could walk it in a few minutes, on what used to be a area with pubs everywhere, now only 2 open (at time of writing) small with 2 rooms, often pub is closed down, sometimes has some disco on a Friday & Saturday, closes early 11pm, another pub where fights can break out, it is in the Red Light district. poor quality ale is on the cheap side, I have found that management and staff don't give a dam, often too drunk to notice customers are waiting.
