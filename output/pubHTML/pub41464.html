Jonathan Wilde left this review about The Fox & Hounds

This appeared to be the busier of the two pubs in the village at the time of my visit. However this is the food pub of the village so comes complete with a mix of locals and potential visitors for food although I didn't get that feel on a Friday night. Had more hand pumps than the other pub in the village, again an L shaped building and bar with a room facing the main street next to the bar seeming to be all laid up for food. A room to the left on entry that juts out as seen in the pictures also seemed to look the same leaving a limited seating area in the remainder of the bar for drinkers, although it does have a fair few bar stools available.

Both pubs in this village struggle with parking and the majority of the village is double yellows so good luck parking if travelling here on a sunny day or when a theme night... is on.

On 24th May 2013 - rating: 6
