Alan Winfield left this review about The Mews Hotel

The Mews Hotel is a short walk from the town centre.
This pub and hotel looks quite nice from the outside,once inside it is just as nice.
There is a single L shapes room with the entrance being on the right side of the pub,the bar faces,there a bench seat close to the bar,the main area is down a few steps,the bar is also in this area,the seating in the lower area is bench seating in the bay window and normal tables and chairs,the left side of the lower area had tables laid out with table cloths for diners.
The ceiling is beamed and the room carpeted throughout.
There was one hand pump on the bar which had Taylor Landlord on,this was quite a nice drink.
The pub does fresh home cooked food,i noticed it being brought in when i first arrived here.
There is a decent sized garden to the right of the pub with picnic benches.
This is a very pleasant and comfortable pub,the sort of place to take your wife or girlfriend to.
