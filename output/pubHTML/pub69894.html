Alan Winfield left this review about Cross Keys Inn

The Cross Keys is a short walk from the main shopping area of Cottingham
This pub looked fairly smart inside and it had been opened out into one room that had different drinking areas there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Tetley bitter and this was ok the other beer was Deuchars IPA.
The pub was quite busy with people watching football on tv.
