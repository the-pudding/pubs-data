Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Ship Inn

Busy community pub, set back a bit from the Falsgrave Road to allow for several wooden benches as well as a bit of car parking. Opened out to form one irregularly-shaped room, with the focus seeming to be as much on the central pool table as the corner bar area. Refurbished with light furniture and decor. Three handpumps on the counter, offering Black Sheep, Doom bar and Theakston Black Bull (£3.20).
