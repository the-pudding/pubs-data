Graham Coombs left this review about The Devil's Stone Inn

Splendid and aged stone-built village inn. The bar is in the centre, with a games area off to one side (darts and pool) and a dining room to the other. Several good local ales`and cider on offer; food not sampled but seemed popular. Nice garden area at rear. Just what you want in a village.

On 27th July 2012 - rating: 9
