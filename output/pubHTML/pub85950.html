Al Bundy left this review about H1780 Tap & Still

Now called Love Lane Brewery Bar And Kitchen.

18/12/17:
Set within a brewery warehouse this is from a newly reborn Higsons brand. Thankfully the beer isn't as awful as its predecessor. The place consists of a single large room with some seating up some steps to a mezzanine level. The interior is industrial with exposed piping and ventilation ducts above. To the back of the building the brewing kit is displayed behind glass. There are 2 serveries with a small bar as you enter, unused on this visit. The main servery has just 2 handpumps that dispense Higsons Pale and Higsons Amber. There are 14 wall mounted taps behind the bar for crafty beers.
