Real Ale Ray left this review about Ship Inn

We had a similar problem to the review below. Our problem was that we called in for a drink near the end of the lunchtime service, when people were paying their bills. We were left standing at the bar for ages, without any acknowledgement from the staff. I got my pint of Humpty Dumpty Swallowtail, which was well overpriced. The two other beers were from Adnams. The problem here is that the young staff are inadequately trained or don't give a toss.

Philip Gaskin left this review about Ship Inn

This pub looks quite large from the outside, but there is only one bar, which is small, and a small conservatory area behind that. All of the few tables in the bar were taken by pensioners eating something with chips. As I got to the bar a customer had just started ordering food for a table, and as the barman did not even bother to acknowledge me, we left. We will not be returning.
