Quinno _ left this review about The Perch and Pike

Attractive 17th century brick and flint village local. A Brakspear pub so a couple of their ales on in decent nick. Three rooms inside, not much of note and it felt a bit sparse in the atmosphere stakes. A fair beer stop-off but not necessarily one to go the extra mile for.
