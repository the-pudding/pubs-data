Alan Winfield left this review about The Priory Arms

The Priory Arms is a back street pub that is in a fairly quiet area of Salford.
Once inside there was a decent bar and a smarter lounge i had a drink in the bar which was fairly busy with locals,this pub used to be a Boddingtons tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Boddingtons bitter and this was a decent enough drink the other beer was Boddingtons mild.
This was a decent enough pub to have a few drinks in.
