Alan Winfield left this review about Revolution

Revolution is housed in an old plain looking building just off high street.
Once inside the main room is long and oblong shaped,the bar runs down the left side,there are tall tables and chairs in the middle and a slightly raised area which runs down the right side,the seating here is bench seating and mid sized chairs.
There is a separate room to the left which is smaller and was full of women who were all talking and shouting very loud,so i did not have a look in it.
There are no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Shipyard Pale Ale which was not too bad a drink,there are the other usual keg options on the bar which i failed to note.
This is by far the best of the Revolutions i have been in.

Pub visited 1/8/2015
