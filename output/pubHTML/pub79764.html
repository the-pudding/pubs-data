Ian Mapp left this review about The Coach House Inn

Beer pub of the week, after walking around the NY moors. Everywhere else seemed to be Black Sheep dreariness but here were three camra labeled ales of uniqueness. I tried an unrefined Rising Tide and moved onto a Whitby Bay Porter. Beers were not really my preferred type but in perfect condition and I can appreciate that.

The food was superb.
