Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Look Mum No Hands!

Bike shop, café and bar - a very trendy combination... Anyway, lots of plain modern wood tables crammed inside but extra seating available on the rustic wooden benches out in the side yard plus a few small tables out on the pavement. Now has six keg taps offering Moor Union'Hop (£3.50, 'happy hour' price), Kernel Table Beer, London Fields Hackney Hopster, BBF Independence, Staropramen and a cider.
