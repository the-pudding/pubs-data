Nick Davies left this review about The Oak & Black Dog

This one appears to have evolved from maybe three houses much knocked around and extended, resulting in a long room extending the length of the building, pool table and vertical drinking to the right, seating and eating to the left. There's a nice terrace, and smokers hut, then a kiddies play area out the back. Nice pint of Otter. It's fine for what it is, but not worth going out of your wa for.

On 15th July 2017 - rating: 6
