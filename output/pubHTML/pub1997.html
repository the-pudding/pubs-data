Quinno _ left this review about The Three Frogs

No longer a Hungry Horse, just a general Greene King outlet after the closure and re-branding of the Plough up the road as a Loch Fyne. The inside has been scrubbed up and seems a lot better for it. Two ales on - OSH and Ruddles County - £2.80 p/p; condition fair. Decent outside garden with brilliantly spongy astroturf. Tea/coffee available.

Never going to a 'must-visit' but has certainly improved a bit.

24 March 2007
Looks nice from the outside, but in reality this is just another low-grade Greed King Hungry Horse outlet. Only two beers on (IPA and a Belhaven, 6 Nations) which were OK, no great shakes. Inside was pretty dark and dingy, clientèle mainly scummy mummies and their offspring when I went in, along with a couple of lonely old men. Could actually be quite a nice place with a bit of effort but in its current incarnation, avoid.

On 25th September 2009 - rating: 5
