Old Boots left this review about Square & Compass Inn

An elegant, open plan, dining pub with multiple spaces laid out for eating although the main bar at the front of this large pub is a pleasant area for drinking. This space is furnished with tables and leather armchairs while there are two more formal dining areas, a more informal area to the side, although it has a chandelier, and a further dining area with a counter at the back. Six hand-pumps dispense well kept ales from local small breweries and there are well stocked fridges with a selection of interesting European and American bottled beers. The young enthusiastic staff provide a high quality of service and the food is pretty good.
