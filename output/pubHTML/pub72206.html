Will Larter left this review about New Dungeon Ghyll Hotel

The Walkers Bar has three hand pumps, of which two were in service during my recent visit. One was Thwaites Wainwright and the other was badged as Hardy Tup, with no brewer's name on it. It had the same strength as, and tasted like, Thwaites Original. The single room in which the bar stands is very basic, with chunky pine tables and outsize high backed wooden chairs, and a floor made of some sort of plastic aggregate, giving the ambience of an institutional dining room. Outside there are similarly regimented bench tables on a slate terrace, which is pleasant enough when the weather allows. This place is geared towards eating, and I can't imagine wanting to hang around after dining for another pint or two. Just a few paces away is the Sticklebarn, which at least makes some sort of effort towards creating a pub atmosphere.
