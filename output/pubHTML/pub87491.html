paulof horsham left this review about Steamworks

Steamworks is a classy little cafe/bar at Seaford Station. Along with coffee and an excellent cake selection, you'll find 5 cask and 5 keg beers (including a cider and a couple of pilseners), all local from the likes of Langham, Bedlam & Holler. Well worth missing a train for, should you find yourself in this town.
