Quinno _ left this review about The Three Horseshoes

A very upmarket Fullers place that in truth is much more restaurant than pub now. It's a really pretty interior, multi-roomed and tastefully decorated with a lovely back garden too. Child friendly and board games noted. To be fair, the friendly landlady welcomed us as drinkers and apologised for the reserved tables and directed us to one that was OK. Surprised to see four Fullers beers; ESB, Pride, Seafarers and the seasonal Jack Frost (one I usually enjoy). However this example was well past its best and was, euphemistically, taking on sherry notes. Given how restuaranty the place was maybe I shouldn’t have been surprised.

On 25th February 2016 - rating: 6
