Al Bundy left this review about Swan Hotel

Large posh-like place. Very nice inside with about half a dozen comfortable rooms to enjoy a drink. Up to 5 real ales available from the 5 handpumps on the bar which is to your left on entering, there is another bar in the room behind with 3 handpumps. Friendly staff. Posh Cheshire accent optional.
