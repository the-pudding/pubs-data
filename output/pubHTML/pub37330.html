Real Ale Ray left this review about The Bell Inn

This is a quaint looking little pub, set back off the main road. This is a two roomed pub, with each room accessible from the main front door. The bar runs along the back wall and serves both sides. Each room is painted and is fairly basic, in fact there was nothing of interest and that included the beer choice. There were no handpumps on this visit, there was a M+B keg on one side of the bar serving Brew XI.
