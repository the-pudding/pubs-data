Alan Winfield left this review about The Bell Inn

The Bell Inn is a decent looking stone built pub that is fairly close to the village church.
Once inside there were two rooms at the front of the pub with the bar being in a small room to the left,this room had a couple of tables and chairs and very narrow bench seats which looked very uncomfortable there was another small room to the right which had a couple of chesterfields and some small tables and chairs this room looked quite nice,there was a larger room to the rear which had no lights on.
The pub had a few customers in but it was far too quiet for my liking.
The pub is a Hook Norton tied house and there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Hookey bitter and this was a decent drink the other beers were Hookey mild and Gold.

Pub visited 19/5/2012
