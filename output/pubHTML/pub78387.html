Steve C left this review about The Blue Man

Blue Man is a North African themed bar and restaurant with a large tapas menu. This isn’t the biggest place, but there are two floors. At street level is a mix and match of seating and a bar on the rear wall where San Miguel, Guinness and Somersby cider are the draught options. Downstairs are the toilets and some low tables with stools. The North African theme runs through the décor and music selection, but it isn’t over the top.
All of the mains are priced under £10 and the tapas all costs £4 a dish. I found the service to be very friendly and there was a decent crowd in on the recent Saturday afternoon I visited.

To be honest this isn’t my sort of place, but it does sit well in the eclectic town of Brighton.

On 13th August 2013 - rating: 4
