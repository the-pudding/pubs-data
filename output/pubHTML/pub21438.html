Alan Winfield left this review about Marquis Of Granby Hotel

The Marquis of Granby is a smallish stone built pub that is on Wellingores narrow high street.
Once inside the pub had two rooms,there was a dining room to the left which looked quite smart and was also fairly busy,the bar was to the right this room was L shaped and fairly small,the room was carpeted and had comfortable bench seating with small stools,there was a pool table and dart board to the right,this room was also very busy with a good mix of customers.
The pub is a Greene King tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink og GK IPA which was as good as it gets the other beer was GK Libertine.
This was a nice lively pub that was busy and had a decent atmosphere.

Pub visited 23/6/2012
