Quinno _ left this review about The Black Dog

Fairly decent Greene King outlet, modernised but with a bit of quasi-rustic character. L-shaped interior with a fairly homely feel -pub carpet, red wine and magnolia shades. A nice garden area is off left. Three ales on; GK IPA, a GK cricket badged beer and Robinson Dizzy Blonde (decent), though the very high prices put me off - just under 4 quid for a half and a small glass of lemonade bubbles for Desmond. Ultimately, so-so. 5.5

On 11th September 2016 - rating: 5
