David Hodkinson left this review about The Plough

A fine Cotswold stone country pub situated down the end of a tiny single track road close to the River Thames in the hamlet of Kelmscott, a couple of miles from Lechlade.Usually 3 real ales on including one from the nearby Halfpenny brewery, great food, and nice gardens for good weather drinking, a bonus too, its open all day. Well worth a detour!

On 15th July 2011 - rating: 8
