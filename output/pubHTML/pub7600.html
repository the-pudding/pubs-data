Alan Winfield left this review about The Red Lion

The Red Lion is a pretty plain looking pub that is handily situated very close to a bus stop which i got off at.
Once inside there was a decent sized room which looked like a bar to the front,there may have been other rooms but i did'nt look,there was a pool table and darts board in this room,it was comfortable enough for me.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Pedigree which was a decent drink,the other beer was Bombardier.
This was the livliest of the three Linton pubs i visited,with mainly younger drinkers.

Pub visited 18/2/2006
