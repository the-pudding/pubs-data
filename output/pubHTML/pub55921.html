ROB Camra left this review about Yew Tree Inn

This is now an unclad, bare bricked pub. It used to be painted white and had a pullman railway carriage attached to the side which was used as a restaurant. These days it's made up of 4 fairly small rooms, all nicely decorated, with some interesting photos on the walls. It looks very modern though and has lost most of it's character as it's quite nondescript. There's a nice looking fireplace in the left hand room though. Sadly it's a Sam Smiths keg only outlet. I had a half of the Dark Mild which was still only 67p. I thought it may have been a little more expensive in here. Very quiet on my Friday evening visit, it'll be a worry unless it gets a lot busier later.
