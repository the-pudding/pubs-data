ROB Camra left this review about The Balmoral

A 3 roomed pub, all Reasonably decorated. The left hand room was full of scrotes on my Friday, early evening visit. The right hand room was the best of the three with some reasonable people in it. 2 handpumps, but no real ale although Old Rosie real cider was available. There's a dartboard in the room to the rear. This is advertised as "The New Look Balmoral". I shudder to think what the old one was like. I really didn't like this one at all or most of the clientele.
