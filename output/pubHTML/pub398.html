Jonathan Wilde left this review about The Grapes

This pub is scheduled to close due to a rent dispute with the part occupier of the Indian Restaurant on Boxing Day 2013. However all isn't to be lost as it's to reopen a few doors down on opposite side of the street in what was a former stationary store which the present owners previous had open a wine/tapas bar by New Years Eve 2013.

Time for a revisit to do it before it goes me thinks and then see what happens with the new venture across the road.

Matt Dyer left this review about The Grapes

This is a fantastic local pub having recently re-opened and serving super real ales/ciders and grolsh/carling lagers. it is not serving food, but with a curry house next door why bother! the perfect combination. It does serve the traditional snacks such as pork scratchings/pickled eggs and rolls/crisps and every sort of spirit you could ever imagine. This pub is a must try for a good atmosphere and a good pub to meet up in before hitting all the other pubs in the street, only if you can pull yourself away!
