Real Ale Ray left this review about The Glass Onion

The Glass Onion seems to be staying put as previous attempts at putting a bar here all seem to fail, so the onion must be doing something right. The interior is small with the bar facing you over on the right. There is two handpumps, with a regular JW Lees bitter on and that's about it. The pub does have a good atmosphere and doesn't attract any trouble makers.
