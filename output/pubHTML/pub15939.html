E TA left this review about The Lydden Bell

The Lydden Bell is a family owned and run country pub convenient for both the A2 and the Lydden circuit in what is usually a quiet Kentish village. It does good food, reasonably priced, in very pleasant surroundings. Children and famillies are encouraged but are not obtrusive. There are four pumps on the bar btu only one ale on when I went in - disappointingly it was Speckled Hen; I was hoping for a local beer. The Hen was drinkable but not the best I've ever had. I'd be happy to drop in for a pint and a meal when passing or visiting the racing, but I'm not sure I'd make a whole evening of it.

On 18th October 2010 - rating: 6
