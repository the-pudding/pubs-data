Reviews of The Harvest Moon (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

E TA left this review about The Harvest Moon

A modern village pub built in the style of a U-shaped 1980s 2-story chalet. It shares a large car park with the village hall. There is a bar in the centre, half of which is laid to table, the other half providing comfy seating. There is live sports screened on a large TV one side and a projector and screen the other. The two wings contain a restaurant and a carvery. Ales on were Wadworth’s Dirty Rucker, Tribute and Doombar. The beer was in good condition. Worth dropping in for a beer if passing, and it’s just a short walk from the canal and cycle paths.

On 26th February 2019 - rating: 6
