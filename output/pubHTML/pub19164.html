Alan Winfield left this review about The Swan

The Swan is a small stone built pub that is almost opposite the Park Inn.
The pub still had Matthew Brown signs outside,once inside the pub was decent enough with a fairly comfortable bar but there were no real ales on here and the only bitter was John Smiths rubbish which was a very poor drink.
This was very much a locals pub.
