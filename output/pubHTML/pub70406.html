Kez Challacombe left this review about Rising Sun

Once this was one of the most popular pubs in Crewe, since the roads were changed it lost its favour, but this one survived where as the 'Bell Vue' and 'The Vine' did not. Well worth a visit, especially if you are going to the retail park opposite. Does have real ale. Boddingtons is at least now brewed once again back in Manchester by Hydes.

Alan Winfield left this review about Rising Sun

The Rising Sun is a good walk from the town centre and this pub looks pretty ordinary from the outside,it used to be a Boddingtons tied house and sold a decent pint.
On this visit the pub was fairy comfortable inside but there were no real ales on the bar with the only bitter being Boddingtons cream rubbish so i had a Guinness which was quite nice.
