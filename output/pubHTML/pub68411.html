Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Eaton Farm

Large, family-orientated pub with play area and lots of outside benches for the better weather. Anonymous, semi-traditional furniture and bland corporate decor. Good value food (albeit from a standard Marston's menu) and real ale, with Pedigree, Boon Doggle (£3.00) and Hobgoblin available from the two banks of three handpumps on this visit.

Alan Winfield left this review about Eaton Farm

The Eaton Farm was built by Banks's brewery about 20 years ago.
This is a largish single storey pub,once inside the large area to the left is for people dining only where there is a carvery but the pub also does other meals.
The area to the left is just as smart but you can just have a drink here and dont have to have a meal.
Now this is a Marstons house the beers are from that stable with Pedigree and beers from jennings on the bar the Pedigree i had was a decent drink.
This is a decent pub to go for a meal but it is not really a drinkers pub.
