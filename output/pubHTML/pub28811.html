Phantom Pigeon left this review about The Bowling Green

Great little pub. We have been here many times. Not the easiest to find, but well worth looking for.
Woodforde's Nelsons Revenge on a hand pump, very nice indeed, in every respect.
The food is good, too. Try the ham. It is proper ham off the bone, not the reconstituted elastoplast you get from the supermarkets.
