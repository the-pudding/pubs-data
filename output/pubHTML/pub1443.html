Quinno _ left this review about The Barley Mow

A decent locals boozer with a staple shaped interior that obviously used to be two separate bars. In fact you can still tell that the left side was the lounge and the right a public bar! Carpeted throughout. Decent grassed rear garden. Busy place and quite a friendly atmosphere, though reports elsewhere indicate we may have got lucky. Live music advertised as well as Tuesday quiz night. Darts and sports TV available. Four ales; Pride and Doom Bar, plus two from Rebellion - IPA and Smuggler, the latter of which was in impressive shape. Very friendly barmaid. Based on a single visit, I thought this was a decent village pub.

On 20th June 2015 - rating: 7
