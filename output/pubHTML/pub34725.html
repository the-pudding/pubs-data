Graham Coombs left this review about The Ingate

Thriving neighbourhood pub on edge of town with strong local following. Two well furnished bars, with the larger right hand side being the games area, with 3 dartboards, table football and pool, as well as TV screens. There is also an outdoor area in the back of the rather confined car park. Draught beers include a couple of local beers alongside nationals and seemed in very good order.

On 5th July 2018 - rating: 8
