Danny O'Revey left this review about The Bell Inn

Roadside eatery.

You walk into a small bar area, some bench seating & sofas. The bar is half boards, half flag floor, there are curtains and wall paper in parts, its all kind of like someones lounge at home. There is a dart board in the corner, though I expect it gets little use.

The rear is a long thin rectangle, light and airy with plenty of windows, all set out for dining.

Its nice enough, sort of place you would come for food rather than a drink, though there are two real ales Banks bitter and Black Sheep, cold but ok.
