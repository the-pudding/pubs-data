Al Bundy left this review about The Derby Arms

An open plan pub with several raised areas inside to sit in. Lots of banquette seating and the bar is situated in the centre of the pub. Its traditional inside and is a pleasant enough place. 4 handpumps on the bar but sadly only Doom Bore on so it was Guinness.

Martin Nicks left this review about The Derby Arms

The Derby Arms is a quiet, traditional pub set in the quaint suburb of Woolton in Liverpool. It has a wide selection of real ales and other beverages. It is children friendly, has a warm and inviting atmosphere and the staff are always very welcoming. It has a function room that is available to hire and is easily accessible by bus or car and is only a five minute walk from the centre of Woolton.
