Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Philip Carter left this review about Cellar N1

Small bar near to Camino Bar. Opens in the evening from 5.00pm. No real ale. Through door to a large restaurant. Though they are connected, they appear to operate independantly. Specialises in cocktails. Update: Has a notice on the door stating 'Closed until further notice' though looking through the window, the lager pumps lights were still on. Perhaps they have gone on an extended holiday. Update, has now reopened as of July 10th.
