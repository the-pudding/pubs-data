Jon B left this review about The Red Robin

A nightmare. this is a chain fast food restaurant purpose built in the 90s to serve the new football stadium and retail park. Open plan inside, Greene King have rebranded it from the old Brewers Fayre to just 'pub and carvery' but the Wacky Warehouse is still attached. If you value your sanity then this is a must to avoid, unless you are taking your hyperactive kids to lunch.
