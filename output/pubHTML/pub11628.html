Philip Gaskin left this review about The Fox Inn

The Fox has a small restaurant area at the front, and a larger bar area at the back, leading through to an area with pool tables in it. The decor is from the 1970s, but it is a pleasant place to sit and seems to have a regular clientele. Greene King IPA was available at £2.90, with Ruddles County and Thwaites Bomber. Visit it if you want a pub rather than a restaurant.

On 19th May 2012 - rating: 7
