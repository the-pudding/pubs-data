Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Blue Bell

Reasonable-looking pub, with various seating areas forming an overall 'L' shape within the partly opened-out interior. Several older features remain, including some wood panelling and a bit of tiling beneath some of the windows. Food offers. Old Speckled Hen and Robinson's Trooper available from two of three handpumps, but for once I opted for the keg Tetley's Dark Mild (just £2.00).
