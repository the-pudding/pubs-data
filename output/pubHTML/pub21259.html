Alan Winfield left this review about The Blue Dog Inn

This is a decent stone built pub that is not that big there is a small garden at the front of the pub with a few tabels and chairs.There were 4 handpumps on the bar and i was surprised to see a Full Mash Beer on here this was Guzzlers Gold it was a very nice drink the other beer were from Cottage i car'nt remember what it was there was also the usual Greene King IPA this is'nt a bad pub to have a few in.
