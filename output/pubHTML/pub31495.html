JULIAN SMITH left this review about The Plough

This is a good solid village pub with proper pub food - great choice - at reasonable prices. Good garden area, lots of tables, good play enclosure for children. Good beer choices and friendly Landlord and Landlady. We had a very pleasant evening meal in the garden.

Quinno _ left this review about Plough Inn

Sells a couple of the beers from the Oxfordshire Ales brewery, alongside GK IPA. Looks like a grand old building from the outside, the interior isn't anything particularly special but is situated in some lovely countryside. Pretty spacious too, with comfy chairs. TV and pool available, alongside food in a separate dining room.
