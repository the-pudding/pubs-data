I L left this review about The Black Boy Hotel

This pub has a very nice mock Tudor frontage overlooking the centre of town. Inside, the main drinking area occupies the front of the pub with further seating down the side but the rear area is reserved for diners. Being a Greene King pub the ales are from their range. On our visit we had a niceint of Hardy and Hansons Rocking Rudolph.
