Old Boots left this review about Brass & Granite

An open plan room with raised areas either side of the entrance, there is a sofa area to the right just before you reach the long counter. Three handpulls here with a less than scintillating choice of Belhaven IPA, .....err that's it although an Old Speckled Hen clip was reversed, and stump 3 was blank. The beer was ok in spite of a slight whiff of fish & chips about it, but of decent quality for Kilmarnock, hence I guess its GBG entry. The decor is eclectic with lots of weird photos and signs, there's a few big screens and a pool table at the far end. It was a quiet night so the single barman was easily able to cope with the rush.

On 16th January 2014 - no rating submitted
