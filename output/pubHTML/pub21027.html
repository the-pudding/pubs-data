Alan Winfield left this review about The Stag & Hounds

We visited a pub called GRANTS in Burrough on the Hill i think this used to be the Stag and Hounds.Looking at the pub from the outside with a daft name like Grants i was'nt expecting much but was surprised to find that Parish Brewery brew their beer here there was almost the full range of their beers on the bar plus a beer from Grainstore i had Parish Bitter which was very good there was also Baz's Bonce Blower on the bar but at 12% i did'nt bother to try this one.This is a decent pub with very good beer but has a daft name but well worth going to.

On 27th May 2010 - rating: 8
