Real Ale Ray left this review about Cook & Barker Inn

This four star AA listed Inn is most definitely worth a visit. Both staff and locals alike are very friendly. This large Inn offers excellent food which is sourced from their own farm. I've eaten here several times and am always more than satisfied. I tend to go for the sirloin steak, hand cut chips and veg with a peppercorn sauce. They serve excellent house wine and the hand pulled ale is well kept. Will return.

On 16th March 2011 - rating: 9
