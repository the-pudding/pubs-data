peter ashworth left this review about The Knavesmire

As the previous reviewers mention this is a large pub and very handy for the racecourse. There were 4 cask beers on show Anglers Retreat from the Wold Top Brewery,Leeds Pale Ale,Hardy and Hanson's Olde Trip and Yankee from The Rooster Brewing Co. There is also a function room to the right of the bar plus a friendly dog.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Knavesmire

Well-run and deceptively large pub hidden away in residential streets. There is a large bar with games, TV screens etc, a rectangular middle bar with more of a saloon feel, and a slightly strange area to the side useful for eating. A very good range of local brews in good condition, including some unusual ones - Cask Marque listed. Only a brief visit but definitely intend to come back.
