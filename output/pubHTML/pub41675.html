E TA left this review about The Duck Inn

The Duck is a friendly, working village pub which has a thriving local clientele and a good atmosphere. it's basic, unpretentious and fun. Food is good value for money, and the Hopback beers are always well kept. My last Summer Lightning and GFB were both thoroughly enjoyable and I will certainly return for more. It's a way off any otehr pubs so difficult to fit into a crawl, but it's worth fininf if you're got the time, especially at weekends and lunchtimes.

On 3rd May 2010 - rating: 6
