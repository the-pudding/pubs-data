Alan Winfield left this review about General Burgoyne Inn

The General Burgoyne is a lovely old village pub,there is a small seating area at the front of the pub which was very nice on a warm day.
Once inside the pub was quite small with a small bar area,the room had a country pub feel to it.
When we went in this pub it was a Hartleys tied house and there were two real ales on the bar we had a drink of Hartleys XB and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Hartleys mild.
This was a realy nice pub to have a few pints in.
