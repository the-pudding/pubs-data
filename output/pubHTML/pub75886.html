Gill Smith left this review about Pelsall Social Club

We were using the lounge as a group booking and found this to be a clean tidy friendly place. Large lounge area with bar, and also a large bar area with a screen for sport. Smaller room with comfy seating. 2 Banks beers, and a guest beer which was a very enjoyable York Brewery Naughty Noelle. Was told that it is a sort of club/pub by someone living locally. Does have a CIU sign outside.
