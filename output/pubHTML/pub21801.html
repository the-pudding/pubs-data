Reviews of The Waters Edge (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Mike Hewitt left this review about Golden Ball

We spent the weekend before last, visiting family and went in this cosy little community pub on the Saturday night. It has low ceilings and a traditional atmosphere, The staff and the locals were very friendly. There was only one real ale on offer, but the Old Honesty bitter from Bluebell was excellent. The food being served looked good although we didn't partake. Well worth a visit.
