Alan Winfield left this review about The Nevill Arms

The Nevill Arms is a fairly large pub that is in a nice location facing a stream that runs through the picturesque village of Medbourne.
Once inside the bar was facing and this room was fairly long and narrow running across the front of the pub,there was also a small seperate area to the right which had a large TV on the wall,there is a large posh looking dining room to the rear but you are allowed to dine throughout the pub.
There were three real ales on here and i had a drink of Robinsons Build a Rocket Boys and this was quite a nice drink the other beers were Brains SA and London Pride.
The pub was quite busy and had a decent atmosphere,there was also a outside seating area with picnic benches which overlooked the stream.

Pub visited 10/3/2012
