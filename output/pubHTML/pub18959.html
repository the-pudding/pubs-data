David Bennett left this review about Euxton Mills Hotel

Have visited this Pub and restaurant many times with my wife when up North. The first time met the Landlord and his wife very unassuming couple just sitting in Restaurant like anyone else. The second and subsequent times as on the first visit welcomed by staff always cheerful and have time for their customers. In my case having to have my food specially prepared due to a medical condition, the staff, chef and all have been excellent. The food is first class and I mean this better than most alleged top class Restaurants we see on the TV. I would highly recommend this restaurant / pub to all in the area. None will be disappointed. Food always fantastic, beers top class too. Highly recommend a visit as couple or even family the welcome will be the tops. David & Anne Bennett (The Traumadocs)
