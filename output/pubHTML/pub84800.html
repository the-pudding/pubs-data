Komakino . left this review about Café Rouge

Stella Artois and Leffe Blonde available on draught with a smattering of bottles in the fridge including both Page 24 offerings (Blonde and Blanche) as well as a cracking Vedett IPA in the usual Gallic bistro-type surrounds. Service with a smile as always with this chain which could teach proper pubs a thing or two.
