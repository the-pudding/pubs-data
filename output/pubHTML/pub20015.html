Reviews of The White Lion (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about The White Lion

Many years ago I used to play darts here a couple of times a season as they were in the same league as my team. Nothing much seems to have changed since then. It's an olde worlde style lounge with lots of horse brasses etc. The landlord and the only two other people in were very friendly. The "beer garden" is 3 tables in the car park in reality. Sadly this is a keg only Sam Smiths pub so I had a pint of mild at something silly like £1.60. It's a pleasant little pub and we'd happily call in again if walking down from Nutters restaurant again as we were on this visit.

On 4th October 2018 - rating: 7
