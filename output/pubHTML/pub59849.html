Catherine Goff left this review about The George & Falcon

Fantastic countryside pub, amazing food all locally sourced where possible and all freshly cooked to order.
Alfresco dining dining available in all weathers on the decked area as well as the large riverside beer garden.
Formal dining to light lunches catered for seven days a week 12pm-3pm and again from 5pm-9pm and they now do Sunday roasts all day on a Sunday, although booking is advised as very popular!
It is a large pub that can be sectioned off for private functions such as birthdays, wakes, christenings etc.
Five AA 4* rated rooms - all stunningly decorated and offered on a B&B basis.
The new owners promise a very warm welcome and excellent service!
