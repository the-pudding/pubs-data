Alan Hurdle left this review about Green Inn

A fine old pub set back off the B4396 just outside Llangedwyn village in the Tanat valley.
The bar area has an open range fire and there is a larger lounge/restaurant area. The pub has a large garden area and large car park at the front. Changing Real Ales and good quality meals are served. At busy times the landlord, serving at the bar, running up and down stairs and out to the garden with meals is reminiscent of Basil Fawlty (but doing a much better job).
