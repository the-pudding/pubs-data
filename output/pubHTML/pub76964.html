moby duck left this review about The Exchequer

Another food led country pub down a lane close to the Basingstoke canal,a small bar with three hand-pumps dispensing mainly localish beers,surrounded by lots of seating aimed at diners and also an extended conservatory style area for more dining,a large garden area to the rear. Nothing here that really tempts me to return.
