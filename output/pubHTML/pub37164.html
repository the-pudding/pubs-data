Thuck Phat left this review about The Bell Inn

A friendly local boozer, the exterior is 1930's and distinctly art deco, inside has seen a significant refurb. although some time ago. The pub is split into two main rooms, large bar area with sky sports and a pool table tucked into a corner and an, also large and comfortable, lounge area. In addition there is a seperate and dedicated restaurant area as well as a large, attractive and well kept garden. Three real ales are on: Deuchars IPA, Brew XI and a well kept and very drinkable pint of Wadworth's 6X. Not an imaginative selection but the Bell is friendly and comfortable and does have at least one quaffable beer on.

On 6th June 2010 - rating: 7
