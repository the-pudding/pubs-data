Alan Winfield left this review about Longs Bar

Longs is an old pub with a daft name in the old town area,Once inside the pub lives up to its name as it is long and fairly narrow and as you walk through it goes down a few steps.
This is an Arkells tied house but there are no real ales on here the only bitter was keg Arkells 3B and this was a very poor drink compared to the real thing.
This pub caters for the younger crowd in the evening and it was very quiet on my dinnertime visit.
I did'nt like this pub that much.
