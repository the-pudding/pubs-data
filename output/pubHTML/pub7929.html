some guy left this review about The Malt House

Possibly the worst dining experience of my life. Despite the place being nearly empty and being seated next to the waiter station, we waited 20 minutes to have our order taken, then only after we tracked down a member of staff. When the food arrived, I had what appeared to be last week's salad and my companion had chicken so rubbery it could bounce. On complaining, a manager brusquely told us that the salad was fresh but 'goes like that when you put it under the heat lamps" (!) and that "that's what happens when you fry chicken- if we give you a replacement it will be the same". The service only escaped being non-existant in that it was rude and the food was inedible- 0 stars.

Therese Jennings left this review about The Malt House

If I could give this pub nothing out of 10 I would! From the non-existent service when I first tried to order a drink at the bar to the appalling rude manager who dealt with a member of our party very poorly, not to mention the extremely mediocre food, I would NEVER bother going to this or any other Harvester pub again! If you want a good, cheap meal consider takeaway or Pizza Hut first!
