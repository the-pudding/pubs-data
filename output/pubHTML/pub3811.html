davena arundale left this review about The Causeway Hotel

My friends and I visited over the bank holiday weekend prior to us going to Creamfields,we just so happened to be stopping at a B&B just up the road,well what an afternoon!The regulars were lovely and friendly and the landlady Jo was hilarious,we had a great day! So much so that one of my friends and me decided to leave Creamfields early and go back to the pub. What a night we had! If ever you are passing I would recommend you call in for a drink!
