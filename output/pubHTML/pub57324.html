Gill Smith left this review about The Church End Brewery Tap

I hadn't been here for a number of years, but called in with one of the regulars there and was pleasantly surprised with the good quality of the ale there. No food as such but a good selection of bar snacks available. There were around 8 of their ales on the 2 bars, but some others available from the back as well. Large car park and a lot of tables on the grassy area outside.

Danny O'Revey left this review about The Church End Brewery Tap

Basically a modern barn-like visitors cenre bar attached to the Chuch End Brewery, down a hidden access in Ridge Lane. Light and airy it does a job but is nothing spectacular. The 4 real ales available on tap were, as you might expect from the brewery tap, in very good nick.

There is a garden at the rear adjoining the car park with lots of benches.
