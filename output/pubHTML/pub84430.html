Alan Hurdle left this review about The Broad Oak

Opened 02/09/2016. Adjacent to Morrison's supermarket. Two Real Ales.
An open plan single room modern bar with bare floorboards and a tiled wall around a mirrored area behind the bar. The room is broken up into three separate areas.
The place has a similar atmosphere to some micro pubs I have visited recently but it is a little too large to quality as one.
Entrance is via a fenced path on a small mound overlooking Morrison's car park.
The pub is on the site of the former Gillespies/Derwen Deg pub, and it looks like the old building was retained before Morrison's was built on to it!
