ROB Camra left this review about The Church Inn

This pub looks to have been refurbished recently and Robbies have done a decent job on it. The room to the right as you enter has a pool table & dartboard. The main room has some TVs which were showing sport and a quiz machine. There's also a good sized beer garden at the rear. 4 handpumps on the bar with 2 clipped. Unusually for a Robbies pub they didn't have the ubiquitous Unicorn on the bar. Instead there was a choice of Dizzy Blonde or Trooper. The Dizzy was a decent pint as well. It's a very neat looking pub and probably the best on this stretch of Oldham Road.

On 19th November 2014 - rating: 7
