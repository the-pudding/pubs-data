David Ross left this review about The Clansman Bar

This irregularly shaped and quirkily decorated little bar has a real fire, two TV screens, a pool table and a pulpit like platform for a DJ or Karaoke performers. The walls are almost entirely covered with advertising posters for beer, cars etc. The clientele was mainly youthful with an equal mix of males and females. A pint of Caledonia Best cost me £3 and Tennent's, Heverlee and Stella Artois lagers, Guinness and Magner's and Strongbow ciders were also on draught. There is also an impressive range of exotically named cocktails. Don't be misled by the rather ordinary and traditional looking frontage, this is definitely one of the livelier pubs in Dunoon.
