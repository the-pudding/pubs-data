Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Drum & Monkey

Attractive old main building with several more recent extensions surrounded by tidy landscaping. The multi-room interior has been partially opened-out and refurbished in the usual light Chef & Brewer style with a mix of furniture. Very much food-led, as you would expect, with about 2/3rds of the tables laid-up for dining. Substantial beer garden to one side. Four real ales on handpump: Abbot, Landlord, the local Silhill Blonde Star and Pennine Howzat IPA (£3.35, less 10% Camra discount).

On 10th September 2017 - rating: 7
