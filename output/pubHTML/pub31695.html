Tim Bostel left this review about The Crazy Bear Hotel

If you are looking for something quirky with excellent food then this is the place. The rooms are incredible and certainly worth paying that little extra for the main rooms - they are incredible! The reception is a double decker bus in the car park.
I have used the venue 3 time salready to have client dinners in the log cabin and each time they have been received exceptionally well. Location and food aside what finally tips it over the edge are the staff who really are absolutley fantastic.

I really couldn't recommend this place highly enough - Great for that "something different"

On 4th October 2010 - rating: 10
