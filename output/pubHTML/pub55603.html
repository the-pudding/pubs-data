Danny O'Revey left this review about Marston Green Tavern

This pub sits opposite the main parade of shops and is easy walking distance from the railway station.

Its larger than it looks with a large seating area at the rear. It has an open plan bar & lounge with comfortable seating and has the feel of a larger roadside chain pubs.

It does need a lick of paint inside but it feels friendly and comfortable.

Draft beer is Marstons Pedigree and Brew 11, kept ok.
