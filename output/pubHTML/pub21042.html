Quinno _ left this review about Boot & Shoe

A traditional local tucked away off the main road and in the heart of the old village, overlooked by the church spire (which made for a lovely minute or so well the bells tolled in the late evening). Sort of L shaped inside, with a well-worn feel and little in the way of alteration over the last 40 years. Two pumps on the go with the Doom Bar and Cheshire Westwood, the latter of which was a good pint in good nick. Friendly barman who was good for chat. Perhaps not a destination pub in Rutland for sure, but an enjoyable local that’s worth stopping by for.

On 8th August 2018 - rating: 7
