ROB Camra left this review about The Queens

An unusual, good looking, 3 storey pub. Several different areas and pretty busy on our Thursday afternoon visit. We popped in intending to have a quick one before catching a bus back into the city. The problem was that there was only 1 woman serving and at least 5 people waiting in front of us, so we decided to give it a miss. 2 real ales available Marston's EPA and Jennings Cockerhoop.
