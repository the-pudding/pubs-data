Aqualung . left this review about The Spa Lane Vaults (JD Wetherspoon)

This is a fairly typical open plan Spoons as described below consisting of a long room with two sets of five hand pumps. On my Saturday afternoon visit one set had the JDW Trio Of Doom, a cider and Kelham Islan Easy Rider while the other had Locale (ish) beers including Stancil India Ruby, Maxim Swedish Blonde, Lincoln Green Quarterstaff and a Pheasantry beer. I went for the Quarterstaff as a drink with a meal so didn't catch the price but the standard guest ale price here is £2.35. Although this is more expensive than the plusher Portland Hotel this one had a better beer choice so I make that quits between the two.

ROB Camra left this review about The Spa Lane Vaults (JD Wetherspoon)

A good looking building from the outside, being brick built and also half timbered in black & white. Once inside it's the usual JDW format, with lots of high tables & chairs near the window and normal dining tables and chairs further back. A good selection of guest ales on the bar and my pint was in very good nick. It's a very pleasant JDW during the day, who knows what it's like at night though.
