Phantom Pigeon left this review about Thelbridge Cross Inn

More of a restaurant than a pub, and lacks atmosphere.
However, the food is very good and the Exmoor Ale was excellent, for a West Country beer. (I am not a fan - This is a personal opinion) Anyway, wouldn't be averse to calling in again.
