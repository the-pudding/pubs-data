Alan Hurdle left this review about Bryntirion Inn

17th Century coaching inn close to the river in the upper Dee Valley. Serving Real Ales from the Purple Moose and guest beers. Open all day. Lunch and evening meals, cream teas and coffee available. Accommodation.
