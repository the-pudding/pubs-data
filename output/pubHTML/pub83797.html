Real Ale Ray left this review about The Hopwater Cellar

An interesting and pleasant visit, with an extensive range of 500 bottles to be drank within or takeaway. One of our PG crew picked up a couple of very interesting bottles to take home on this visit. There was also a keg tap dispensing Firebrand West Coast IPA, which was decent enough. I think the owner needs to invest in a new laptop as the one on the shelf, looked a bit dated to me.

Al Bundy left this review about The Hopwater Cellar

A rather disappointing place due to the draught beer selection. The single handpulls served Dark Star Hophead, though there are two taps for crafty nonsense and a load of bottled beers. The place itself is OK and is split into 2 rooms. Sadly due to the draught beer selection it just didn't do it for me.

Soup Dragon left this review about The Hopwater Cellar

With its unremarkable brick exterior and small signage this place stands out about as much as Donald Trump at a clown convention, however, I was with people who knew where they were going so find it we did. An irregular shaped room inside, with plenty of brick and green, it has a large bottle display/selling area and a couple of bar areas - one with tables (on entry) and an area with bench seating. Beer ephemera adorns the walls. The service was good and a few people were in at our visit. There was a single pull with a decent enough Firebrand West Coast IPA. I don't really buy bottles and other places have a better choice of ale but, saying that, I enjoyed my visit and would happily go back.
