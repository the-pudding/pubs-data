Quinno _ left this review about Mariners Arms

A traditional, two room back street local. A lounge is to the left with blue carpet and fabric perimeter bench seating. Dart board spotted in the more basic public bar. Very quiet but the few locals in were making up for it. Three ales on; Ringwood Bitter, Jennings Bitter and Banks Sunbeam (fair shape). It's decent enough here but no stand-out, though it appears to be a favourite of the local CAMRA branch.

On 1st April 2018 - rating: 6

Alan Winfield left this review about Mariners Arms

The Mariners is in the back streets and away from the town centre.
This pub had Burtonwood signs outside but there were none of their beers on the bar,the pub seemed quite comfortable inside and was fairly busy with a good mix of customers.
There were three real ales on here i had a drink of Jennings bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beers were Pedigree and Banks's mild.
I thought this was a decent pub and one of the better pubs in Driffield.

On 3rd April 2011 - rating: 7
