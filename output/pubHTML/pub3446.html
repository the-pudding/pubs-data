phil morris left this review about Horse Shoe Inn

The Horseshoe Inn is an old Coaching Inn dated 1774 and sits in this idyllic part of the countryside. Totally unspoilt with farm buildings still standing from all those years ago when it used to be farmed, with eleven acres of farmland still attached to the pub allowing us to take marquees of all sizes.
The pub has an open log fire creating a cosy atmosphere for a relaxed and comfortable time, somewhere you can enjoy a chat without the interference of a TV.
We offer quality home cooked food with a menu that ranges from a simple sandwich to a mixed grill.
The garden is a suntrap during the summer months offering plenty of outdoor seating over looking the children's play area with a tractor, slide, climbing frames and swings for the children to enjoy.
We have an excellent smoking area to the rear of the pub which is fully covered and heated for those colder times.
Charles and Mary welcome all to their warm friendly pub
