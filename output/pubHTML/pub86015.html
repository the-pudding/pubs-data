Will Larter left this review about The Dorothy Pax

Occupying one of the numerous arches at Victoria Quays, this recent addition to Sheffield's real ale scene was rocking when I arrived this afternoon. There are a few steps down to the main bar area, which has a small stage in the corner, where a couple of guys on guitar and bass were entertaining a decent sized crowd with a set of country/rockabilly. In the corner is a small bar counter with three hand pumps for ale, one for cider or perry, plus a number of keg fonts. It was too crowded to write a proper description, but the beer was ok, if a tad cold and just a bit too frothy. I'm sure I'll be back on another occasion, preferably one when my ears are not subject to the assault they received this afternoon.

On 31st December 2017 - rating: 6
