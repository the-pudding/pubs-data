Old Boots left this review about The Plash Inn

A super village pub comprising a single main room with a side room alongside round a corner. There are three handpumps with Wye Valley and Three Tuns beers plus a guest. There’s a piano and a lottery syndicate and the notice board reflects the central part played in the community by the pub. Use of the dart board requires furniture removals. The natives are friendly and welcoming with a good standard of banter. Simple décor with little jugs hanging from the ceiling, and the restrained use of horse brasses over the counter otherwise the usual local photos. Local CAMRA’s Pub of the Year on numerous occasions and a GBG regular. Beer festivals are also run here.
