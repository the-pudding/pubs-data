Gill Smith left this review about Tywarnhayle Inn

Called in briefly for a bite to eat, and found they had 3 real ales on from local breweries. Sharps Doom Bar, St Austell Tribute and Skinners High Tyde. The main bar is on 2 levels, and there is a set restaurant area. There is a small car park, but a large pay and display nearby. The outside drinking area was massive and very popular.

Philip Carter left this review about Tywarnhayle Inn

Large popular pub, especially at lunch time. Sells real ale. Very friendly staff. Serves lunch & evening meals. Has a kids area. One woman, at the next table, her voice sounded like Vera Duckworth from Coronation Street.
