Aqualung . left this review about The Five Quarter (JD Wetherspoon)

This is a smaller Spoons set on the edge of a modern shopping centre. Peterlee was a coal mining town and the pub name comes from a local seam. It's a rather dull modern building and inside is a rectangular room with the bar at the back.
There are ten hand pumps which had two available soon, two festival ales doubled plus three others and Mordue Appollo 40. I went for the Hilden Pale Oat Ale which was in good nick.
I prefer these smaller provincial Spoons to the more manic city centre ones and this seemed to be a well run one.
