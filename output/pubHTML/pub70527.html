Graham Coombs left this review about The Mortal Man

Slightly peculiar small hotel tucked away at end of hillside village. One bar, with adjacent dining room. usual selection of local beers and food available. Perhaps it was the time of day, but it didn't feel quite right. Possibly needs further investigation.
