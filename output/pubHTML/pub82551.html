Colin Walker left this review about Craigmarloch Lodge

This pub comes under the market name of Sizzling Pubs. They sell draught lager, bottled beer, stout and cider, plus a huge selection of wines, spirits and soft drinks. Some spirits such as Absolut Vodka, Captain Morgan and Gordon's gin coming with a free mixer. The place sells food and it seems to be a bit better than in my opinion a Wetherspoons. The food is available Mon to Sat: 12.00 - 21.00 & Sun: 12.30 - 21.00. BT Sports & Sky Sports is available. Also free Wi-FI. Check out http://www.sizzlingpubs.co.uk/thecraigmarlochlodgecumbernauld/
