E TA left this review about The Aviator

A rambling modern Shepherd and Neame pub on a busy road junction, plenty of parking but a bit off-putting for walkers and cyclists. Inside there are gaming machines, pleasant staff and little by way of atmosphere despite the assortment of aviation-related clutter adorning the walls. It has a large garden where you can sit and watch the lorries go by, and a children's playground. Staff were a bit offhand but still efficient enough. There were three ales on. I had a pint of Earlybird which was reasonable but not brilliant. OK to drop in if passing.

On 2nd September 2013 - rating: 6

Steve C left this review about The Aviator

The Aviator is a family friendly Shepherd Neame establishment somewhat in the middle of nowhere, hence a large car park and leaning towards dry sales. Amongst the usual SN draught is Fosters and one of the four hand pumps was unused leaving Spitfire, Master Brew and Late Red as the ale options.

I found the two barmaids to be very friendly and I would have no problem popping back in for a pint if passing, but the location dictates that this isn't very likely.

On 10th February 2013 - rating: 6
