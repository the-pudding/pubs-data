Reviews of The Maidens Head (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

E TA left this review about The Maidens Head

A 15th Century pub with a car park and paved garden at the side and rear, though the views aren’t great. Inside, the single large bar is L-shaped and has an open fire at the apex. There is a dartboard at one end next to a comfy seating area. They host regular social events, and they have a good local clientele. The staff are quite welcoming, including the pub cat. Food looks home made, and they have a range of pizzas to supplement the standard pub grub mean, though the presence of a large Deliveroo sticker on the window made us wonder how ‘home made’ some of the dishes are. Only one ale on, Pride, which was drinkable. Worth dropping in for a pint as it’s close to the railway station.
