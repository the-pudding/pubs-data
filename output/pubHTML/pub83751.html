Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Drake & Morgan

Smart modern restaurant and bar of just the type you would expect if it was housed beneath Google's current offices (which it is), with the main space split about 2/3rds in favour of those dining. Also has another bar downstairs and a popular outside seating area along the frontage. Usually has a real ale on handpump, but the Doom Bar proved illusory on this occasion, so resorted to a hideously expensive Meantime Anytime IPA (£3.10 for a half).

On 21st July 2018 - rating: 5
