Colin Walker left this review about The Ochil View

The Ochil View was very busy when I visited mostly local inhabitants. A large bar area and a well spread out gantry showing its wide range of drinks for young and old. There are plenty of table and chairs in the bar area and looks more like a lounge. There is a large separate function suite to the left, which can accommodate weddings. There is parking available at the pub and some limited street parking. There is a sign hanging up showing that the place is available for lease. Here's hoping the lease is taken up soon.
