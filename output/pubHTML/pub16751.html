Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Bullfinch

A food-led McMullen pub, much larger than it first appears, with the original building facing the road extended to side and rear. Various seating and dining areas around a two-part bar counter. Refurbished with semi-modern furniture and light decor. Substantial patio / grass beer garden. Two banks of four handpumps, offering Cask, County, IPA and the seasonal Tommy's Tipple (£3.75). Not a bad spot, overall.
