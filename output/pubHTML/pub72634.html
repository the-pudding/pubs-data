Reviews of Alfred (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Old Boots left this review about Alfred

Single room bar owned by North, a bit of a cafe ambience and like sister establishment in Chapel Allerton a selection of lampshades decorate the ceiling. Other quirky decor points, a group of signs with edifying slogans such as "Onanistic" or "Death before Intimacy". Three pulls, one of their own and two guests plus selection of kegs and bottles. Excellent service and good if cold beer.

david hammill left this review about Alfred

We have been visiting this venue since it opened. The beers and lagers are of a high standard. On a recent visit I tried a pint of Kirkstall Pale Ale which was very good. Also tried a pint of Prorotype No 1 -a beer brewed by the Norht Bar in Leeds. I think this ale is excellent. The staff are always friendly and helpfull and even provide table service!
