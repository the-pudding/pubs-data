Philip Gaskin left this review about Crown & Crooked Billet

I visited this pub once before over forty years ago. There is an entrance next to the car park which we decided not to use as one of the customers was standing outside it smoking and spitting, which should have given us a indication of what to expect. Inside, it does not seem to have been decorated since the last time I visited. As we walked through the pool table area into the main bar we felt uneasy and decided not to stay. It appears to be one of those pubs that is alright if you are known. I did notice Marstons EPA and London Pride were available on hand pump plus another one. It is not one we plan to visit again.

On 12th May 2012 - rating: 4
