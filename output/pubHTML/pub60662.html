Brew Guru left this review about The Tradesmans Arms

Scenic location on the edge of Dartmoor with stunning views from the beer garden and sunroom extension (set out for dining). A single roomed pub that is divided into 3 areas with a central bar. Food was excellent with good sized portions - not normally a veggie but recommend the veg lasagne. Beers were Sharps, Dartmoor IPA and Otter Bright, the latter was in good form. A bit on the quiet side but it was a Monday - well worth a visit.

On 5th May 2011 - rating: 8
