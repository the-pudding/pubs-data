Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Ryan's

Looks and feels much more like a bar than a pub, with mixed (mostly modern) furniture and decor in the irregularly-shaped main room. Also has a restaurant upstairs (not visited). Thankfully, there is a handpump on the counter which offers the local Whitewater Belfast Ale (£3.60).
