Real Ale Ray left this review about The Queen Victoria

This pub is an absolute gem and is well worth a visit. The food and choice of ales were very appealing. Five ales on handpump on our visit, the Tyne Bank Single Blonde and Brew Star Sinistar were two very good ales. Also they serve proper home cooked food. I had the steak and ale pie and Mrs Ale had an excellent roast pork dinner. The food and beer was that good we returned the next day. Will defo return.

On 13th August 2012 - rating: 10
