Aqualung . left this review about The Ivory Peg (JD Wetherspoon)

This is a Spoons set in a depressing modern building. Inside it's one big room with a slightly raised area at the front and all the usual Spoons furnishings. There were two banks of five pumps which were put to reasonably good use. Adnam's Gunhill and London Pride were available soon, there was a cider, Ruddles, GK George and Flagon, Growler Suffolk County, Bath Gem, Shalford Levelly Gold and Nelson Trafalgar.
I went for the Levelly Gold (£2.20) which was in good nick.

Gill Smith left this review about The Ivory Peg (JD Wetherspoon)

This Wetherspoons was the 5th of the 6 pubs we chose to visit from the 2011 Good Beer Guide on 23.12.10. It was formerly a department store and has the usual Wetherspoons ales and guests. We chose Nethergate Essex Border and Hydes Yule Be Back. We did go back on 29.12.10 for breakfast. Service was prompt and staff friendly.
