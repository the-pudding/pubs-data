Michael Wright left this review about The Red Rover

This is a Greene King pub, mainly food orientated, you know the score, two meals for a tenner type of place

We visited on a Sunday lunchtime in June, mainly for food, pub lunch idea

There is a tarmacadam car park, not huge for size of the pub, but I guess most could find a spot somewhere with a bit of thought

The pub is open plan, but lots of sub compartments, loads of room, bit dark wood country pub corporate decor, nothing wrong with that, just so you know. Some areas require step down, so not all may find it easy for wheelchairs, walking sticks or Zimmer frames

Nothing much I can say about the beer, it was GK stuff, it was OK

Food, we had fish & chips, again OK, not Rick Stein of course, but then I was on the A36, in a GK pub and, for the price, was not expecting anything other than what I got, which was acceptable, hot & edible

Staff & service was polite & quick

To sum up, not a destination pub, but if on A36 Salisbury / Southampton road and need a stop for whatever reason, this will do you nicely

On 28th July 2013 - rating: 5
