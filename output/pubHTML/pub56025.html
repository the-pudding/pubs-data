Paul Ogden left this review about The 6 Acres

The pub is both beautiful inside and out. Get there early as despite it's massive size, it gets full quickly - very popular because of the pleasant atmosphere and wonderful food and range of drinks. The food is both off menu and carvery. If you do have the carvery, bring an empty stomach. My favourite beer their is Gold Sheep.

On 24th November 2014 - rating: 8
