Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Journeys End

Substantial Sizzling pub with an opened-out 'L'-shaped layout of no residual interest. Disappointingly, neither of the Doom Bar-clipped handpumps were in use, so had to resort to the John Smith's (£2.70) instead. However, on the plus side, it was still serving food in the mid-evening which was my main interest at that point...
