E TA left this review about The Greyhound

Not much has changed since Alan's review from 2001 - still Palmers, and still doing food. I found it crowded, and while the staff were pleasant enough, some of the locals seemed a bit hostile, looking down their collective noses at us. The pub is very dog-friendly, and there was actually a recumbent greyhound asleep behind the bar. Food was pub standards, well-cooked and good value. Ales were Copper, Gold, 200 and IPA, while First Press cider was also on handpump. Worth dropping in for lunch.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Greyhound

The Greyhound is a Palmers tied house in the middle of Beaminster.
Once inside here the bar area has a stone floor and fairly comfy seating and further back there is a dining area.
There were four palmers real ales on the bar i had a drink of Palmers best bitter there was also Palmers 200, Bridport bitter and Dorset Gold,the best bitter was very drinkable.
