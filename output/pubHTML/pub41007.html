Real Ale Ray left this review about Black Bull Inn

A friendly and comfortable pub with a welcoming open fire situated in a small snug area on the right of the entrance. There were four handpumps in operation on our Saturday lunchtime visit, which were TT Landlord, TT Bolt Maker, Tetley and Leeds Pale. The TT Boltmaker was a good choice.

Alan Winfield left this review about Black Bull Inn

The Black Bull is a nice looking white painted pub that is situated on Otleys market place,the pubs front had market stalls in front of it at the time of my Saturday visit.
Once inside there is a single oblongish shaped room with the bar facing,there is some comfy bench seating at each end of the room which is carpeted plus a few tall tables and stools in the middle area and some normal tables and chairs,this area had a stone floor.
There were four real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Taylor Boltmaker which was a very nice drink,the other beers were Taylor Landlord,Tetley Bitter and Leeds Pale.
The pub was really busy with both drinkers and diners on my Saturday dinner visit.
Background music was playing and i heard Fairy Tale of New York,i thought this was a bit early on the 8th of November.
I quite liked this pub.

Pub visited 8/11/2014
