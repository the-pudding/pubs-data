Alan Winfield left this review about The Corner House

This pub looked like the worse one to visit if in St Neots so it had to be done.
Once inside it did not let us down.
There is a single L shaped room which has a part carpeted and tiled floor,the bar is the the left,the seating is normal tables and chairs and settees.
There were no real ales on the bar which was no surprise,i had drink of Charles Wells Eagle Smooth which was a pretty bland drink.
There was some crap background music playing and the other customers were young drinkers who were twatting about.
Probably a good job i reviewed this pub has not many other PUG members would,i still enjoy doing crap pubs like this just to see what they are like.

Pub visited 4/6/2016
