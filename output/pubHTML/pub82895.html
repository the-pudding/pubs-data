ROB Camra left this review about Upstairs at the Grill

This is a restaurant with a bar upstairs which is used as a waiting area for people dining in the downstairs restaurant. It's quite dark, but nicely decorated. It specialises in cocktails and of course pre dinner drinks. I don't remember there even being any draught beers at all. We've dined here on a couple of occasions and it's not as good as it thinks it is. I didn't bother adding this one as it's not really a pub at all. But as someone else added it has I may as well review it to give others a clue as to what to expect.
