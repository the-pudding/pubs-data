David Ross left this review about Ye Olde Anchor Inn

An attractive old pub, both outside and in, with a seafaring themed interior featuring ships' wheels and lamps, lifebelts, model ships and nautical photographs. It was extremely busy on the Friday night I called in, with live folk music on offer. A pint of Guinness cost me £3.40 and Drygate Gladeye IPA, Caledonia Best, Magner's cider and Tennent's, Heverlee and Menebrea lagers were also available on draught. This was probably my favourite pub in town.
