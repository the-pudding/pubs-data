ROB Camra left this review about The Northern Quarter Restuarant And Bar

This is just a restaurant really. There is no bar area, it's all set up for dining. They do have tables outside on sunny days for drinkers though. It is however a very good restaurant these days. The chef has changed relatively recently and it now serves much better quality food. We visited 3 weeks ago and were impressed. Update - 09/04/10 We visited again last night, it's still very good.
