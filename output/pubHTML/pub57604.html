Will Larter left this review about The Eels Foot Inn

Typical Adnams pub near to the RSPB reserve at Minsmere; photos taken by their photographer are on display in the back room. Bitter, Broadside and Old Ale were available on my recent visits. Meals are available until 9 most evenings, but Thursdays only until 8 because of the very popular folk music session.
