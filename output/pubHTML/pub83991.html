Reviews of The Sunbeam (Average Rating: 3½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Soup Dragon left this review about Sunbeam

A new Greene King Hungry Horse outlet developed as a part of the new bus station. It is in a shared building with glass frontage. Open plan inside with a bit of Sunbeam car ephemera, silent TVs, a dartboard and naff else. Service was poor, the clientele mixed although few in. Beer was the usual 3 GK pulls: IPA, Abbot and a warm Spekky Hen. I struggle to think of anything positive to say about this place, other than the leaving of it was a delight.

Danny O'Revey left this review about Sunbeam

Modern new build Greene King Hungary Horse outlet at the bottom of a new office building, right next to the bus station.

Lots of tall glass windows provide good natural light, there is an over 18s section for those having food before catching the bus home. Poor, slow service and short measures, makes Wetherspoons look efficient & this is an all round poor chain offering.
