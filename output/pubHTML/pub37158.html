Thuck Phat left this review about The Arnold Arms

An uninspiring choice of Greene King IPA and Abbot as regulars so I tried the guest which was a special from Castle Rock. I can't remember the name but it was so appalling I didn't manage to get through half of it before giving up. If you're looking for a session on real ale I'd give it a swerve.
