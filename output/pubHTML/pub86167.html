ROB Camra left this review about A'Vucciria

Named after a famous market in Sicily this is a smart bar and restaurant. There's an upstairs area for diners and some booths downstairs for dining as well. There's loads of barstools and a table for drinkers though. On sunny days there are several tables outside on the pavement as well. No real ale of course and Moretti was £5 per pint, which is a bit steep. It is very pleasant though.

On 21st September 2018 - rating: 6
