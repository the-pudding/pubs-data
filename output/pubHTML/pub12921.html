ROB Camra left this review about The Bowling Green Inn

A tatty mess on the outside with part of the sign missing and in serious need of a paint job. It's a little better inside with a pool table to the left and a dartboard to the right. There are lots of vinyl records on the wall in one area. There's also a large beer garden to the rear. 3 handpumps on the bar, but only Lees Bitter actually available. Lees Jester was badged, but turned around. Several feral kids chasing around didn't help the atmosphere. Apparently they do accomodation. I don't know what the charge is for a night, but I'd expect them to pay me. The Lees Bitter was in OK nick and was £1.15 for a half which is cheap enough. Needs a lot of work to bring this place up to standard.

On 15th June 2015 - rating: 4
