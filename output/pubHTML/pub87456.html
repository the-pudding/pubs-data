peter ashworth left this review about The Cider Press

Situated up a track past the Cricketers Arms the Cider Press is a sort of tea room,bar and shop.
Called in around 10 45am on a Wednesday and already quite a few customers were indulging in the cider although most were drinking tea. There was 7 draught ciders on the go all reasonably priced with the most expensive a 6% Spiced one at £3.90. There was also bottled ciders plus a few bottled beers one from the Nook Brewhouse and one from The Yorkshire Heart Brewery.
