MARIE MARCH left this review about The Royal Hill

This is a fantastic place for a special meal.The food is all homecooked,nice big portions and the puddings are yummy!My favourite meal is the vegetable lasagne followed by sticky toffee pudding.The prices are very good and my partner and I rate this pub as the best in Shropshire.Also the staff are very helpful and friendly.If you want a night out that you will remember for all the right reasons,this is the place for you.

On 19th August 2009 - rating: 10
