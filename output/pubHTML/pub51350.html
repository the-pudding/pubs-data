Will Larter left this review about Lochcarron Hotel

A nice old hotel with entrance through the dining room which is in a sort of glazed verandah to give diners the benefit of the view across the loch. The bar is behind this, next to the hotel reception desk. The hotel appears to be run by an English family, who are very relaxed and informal. A handful of locals were occupying the bar stools at the time of my visit, and I imagine this would be a scene that is repeated most days, as they seemed very at home in the bar parlour-type area. There is some exposed stone and a brick fireplace in this part of the hotel, with a basic but comfortable feel to it.

On the bar are three hand pumps and numerous keg fonts. Strathcarron Red Cow and Golden Cow, plus an unexpected Bombardier were the beers on offer. Red Cow became a staple for me when in these parts, a very well made beer and in very good condition.

Date of visit: 12th September 2017
