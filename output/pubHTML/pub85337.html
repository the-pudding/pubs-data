Danny O'Revey left this review about Burning Soul Brewery

One of the new generation of tap houses, a basic bar within an industrial unit, slightly off the beaten track and currently not a pleasant walk at night. Functional and provides a selection of their keg beers (they do brew cask as well). Pool table.

Limited opening hours.
