Ian Mapp left this review about Harewood End Inn

Former coaching inn, tracing its history back to the 1600s.

In the Good Beer Guide 2019 but why, I'm not 100% sure why. Tribute and a Swan Gold were the only two ales on and a little similar. The Swan was in good condition.

Mainly geared for dining.

Friendly bar man.

Existing the car park is a life and death experience, regardless of which way you turn. Its on a bad bend and cars come flying down the A49. I bet there have been some thumps.

Graham Coombs left this review about Harewood End Inn

Tastefull-modernised roadside inn with general well-run and looked-after feel. Divided into several different areas, with lots of bare wood and decorated with old signs. Beers included St Austell and Purity and were very drinkable (Cask Marque listed). Food not sampled but looked good. Recommended.

On 27th November 2014 - rating: 8
