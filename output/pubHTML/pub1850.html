Quinno _ left this review about The Olive Tree

Sounds pretentious but is in actuality a basic L-shaped locals boozer, with the wheels of steel in full swing on our late Saturday swing-by. Rebellion IPA on clip but actually dispensed via their small keg machine. Pool and darts available. Oddly found some fancy dress face masks on a table, not sure if they were for punter use but I had a go. Friendly if utterly inebriated punters, I’ll leave the place to them in future I think.
