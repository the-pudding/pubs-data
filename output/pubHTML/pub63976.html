Gill Smith left this review about Safe Harbour

After climbing back up the hill from the harbour area to catch the bus, it was great to sit outside this pub with a beer. Through the door you enter into what appears to be a small games room with a pool table and dart board, and go up a step into the bar area. St Austell Proper Job, Trelawney and Tribute available.

Blackthorn _ left this review about Safe Harbour

Decent pub tucked away at the top of a hill. Not one you're going to stumble across walking along Fowey's main street, but worth a walk up the hill by the church. Two separate bars, one kitted out with a pool table and TV screens, the other being a lounge bar with a beamed ceiling and a decent choice of food. The upstairs dining room apparently has good views of the estuary.

Three St. Austell beers on tap, plus Blackthorn cider.

On 5th April 2010 - rating: 7
