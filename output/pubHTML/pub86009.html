Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about German Kraft @ Mercato Metropolitano

Brewery and bar located within the Mercato Metropolitan market / food court / events space. The tap room / beer hall has a row of wooden benches but if the weather permits most of the customers can be found outside in the elongated and part-covered patio beer garden (with its own bar). Four Reinheitsgebot beers available when I visited, including a decent Edel Weiss wheat-beer (£5.50). I thought this was quite good for something a bit different (and certainly much better than the faux-Bavarian bars I have visited elsewhere in Britain).
