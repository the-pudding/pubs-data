Real Ale Ray left this review about The White Lion

This well run pub is defo worth a visit. Friendly and chatty staff and serves fab ales and ciders. There were eight ales on offer on our visit. Five from the Milton Brewery Cambs and three from local micros. I went for Milton Sparta, Dionysus and Nike, also went for the White Horse Brewery Village Idiot. All the beers were superb, you can get very comfortable in this pub.

Alan Winfield left this review about The White Lion

The White Lion is a pretty ordinary looking pub that is situated on a quiet street just off the inner ring road.
Once inside this pub is a lot better with three rooms,i went in a smallish bar which was to the left,this room had a stone floor and a mixture of seating types,there is a larger room to the right which runs to the back of the pub there is a Bar Billiards table in this room and there is a lounge to the rear of the pub,all rooms had a stone floor and seemed basic but in a nice way.
There were around six pumps on the bar all with two clips on,there were four Milton beers on i had a drink of Milton Dionysus which was a very nice well kept drink, the other beers i noted were Milton Nero and Pegasus and a beer from Brewsters,there was also six real Ciders which were on the second pump clips.
This pub seemed very friendly and is the sort of pub i could stay in for a good session,if you are in this area of Norwich make sure you give this pub a go, you wont be dissapointed.
