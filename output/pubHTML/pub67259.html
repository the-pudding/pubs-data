Graham Coombs left this review about The Inn on the Shore

Well-hidden pub in hamlet strung along the cliffs. Watch out for the turning into the large car park, where you will find the pub on the edge of the cliff overlooking the beach below. It has one large 'village' bar on the land side, with a lounge bar turning into a restaurant with superb sea views on the other (book well ahead for the tables by the window!). Also an outdoor patio area. Usual beers - Tribute, Doombar etc.

On 5th July 2014 - rating: 7
