Komakino . left this review about Coach & Horses

Opposite the Jolly Postie, this is a down-at-heel boozer, packed with blokes of all ages watching a Sunday PL match on my fly-by-night visit. An L-shaped room with bar occupying the crook of the L and a beam dividing the two angles there's a large inglenook fireplace to the left, behind which hides a pool table and dartboard. A thin area extends to a wider area out back which I couldn't access due to the throng. Three stumps - all reversed (Sharp's Doom Bar x2 and Atlantic, so I wasn't missing much). A half of Grolsch was £2.10. Probably visited at the wrong time, I still get the impression it's one you're only going to visit for the "tick".

On 29th April 2018 - rating: 2
