Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Browns Bar & Brasserie

Modern bar / restaurant with the counter along one side and a large mezzanine dining area (with another small bar) at the back. Anonymous furniture and decor, as you would expect from the chain. Surprisingly prompt service on a busy Thursday evening. Lots of shiny keg fonts, but there are two handpumps dispensing Doom Bar and Pride (£3.60).
