Colin Walker left this review about The Maltings

The Maltings is a Free House which sits 6km outside of Dunfermline in the village of Cairneyhill. The hotel sells a wide range of beers and a very good selection of malt whisky, too many to mention. There is the usual sky sports & ESPN. The place holds poker every Wednesday night and starts at 8pm although you need to register at 7.45pm and a poker tournament on the last Sunday of each month with a 1.30pm Start. There is parking available at the hotel. www.themaltingshotel.co.uk/
