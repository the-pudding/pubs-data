peter ashworth left this review about Leeds Postal Service

Situated between Toast and the Lazy Lounge the Leeds Postal service has 2 rooms and although no cask beers has around 8 ales and lagers on tap plus around 35 different bottles which can be consumed on the premises or taken out.To the left of the bar is a partitioned room called the Postmasters Office which has a few leather seats a dart board and a TV. The WC is a unisex toilet with a massive mirror plus a cistern with an old fashioned pull chain flush. Quite liked this one and found the staff very helpful and friendly.
