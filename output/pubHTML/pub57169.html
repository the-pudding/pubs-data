Alan Winfield left this review about The Crown Inn

The Crown is on an old roman road called ermine street and the pub is next to a busy roundabout.
The pub is painted in a cream colour and one part of the pub is set at a different angle to the rest of the pub.
Once inside the pub was quite smart and had a cosy feel to it with cottage type seating and decor there was also a large restaurant in a seperate room.
This pub is an Arkells tied house and there were three real ales on here i had a drink of Arkells 3B and this was a decent drink the other beers were Arkells 2B and a Seasonal beer,i thought this was a decent enough pub to have a few drinks in.
