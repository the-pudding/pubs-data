Colin Walker left this review about Cardross Inn

The formerly named Cardross Inn is now called The Coach House Inn, which sits, in the very pleasant village of Cardross, which lays roughly half way between Dumbarton and Helensburgh, on the banks of the River Clyde. The Inn sits across the road from Cardross golf club and less than 300 metres from Cardross railway station, which has a half hourly service and is also on a main bus route from Glasgow. The Inn has two hand pumps, which serves Caledonian Deuchars IPA and a range of guest ales. There is a separate area for dining, a pool table and regular jazz and other functions are organized and there is a sheltered beer garden and free WI FI. The Inn is open 12 to midnight and 12 to 1am on a Fri & Sat. Parking is available at the Inn.
