ROB Camra left this review about The Fat Cat

Smart wine bar style place with several small seating areas plus a raised area. Lots of TV screene dotted about the place. It's also got a very large outside patio with lots of different types of seating. It's a real suntrap and that's the reason we were here. There are 2 handpumps on the bar which were serving Castle Rock Harvest Pale & Bombardier. The Castle Rock was a very good pint. It's Cask Marque accredited as well. It's much better than I expected and the beer was good so we stayed for a second drink. We called late on a Saturday afternoon, I expect it changes quite a bit later on.

On 2nd August 2013 - rating: 7
