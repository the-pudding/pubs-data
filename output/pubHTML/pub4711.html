Reviews of The Fort Inn (Average Rating: 7½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about The Fort Inn

as you approach this place it looks like an attractive and reassuringly 'proper' looking pub. once inside it is clear that this is sadly nothing much like a pub now and more of a huge family dining barn complete with carvery area. with that said, if you avoid busy times it can be a comfortable place to enjoy a drink, especially in the outdoor area overlooking Newquays beaches. St Austells ales available and in good nick as this is their 'flagship' north Cornwall pub.

lyppy lyppy left this review about The Fort Inn

This is the perfect pub if you have children. There is an outdoor play area overlooking the harbour and beach and an indoor play area for up to 8 year olds inside. The food is very good value for money - large portions, and the childrens menu is not just chips with everything. I recommend the ploughmans, especially if you have a large appetite!

Philip Carter left this review about The Fort Inn

Large pub, sells real ale. The garden has great views over the old harbour. The garden also has seagull netting, but some must get through as there is a notice on the wall stating: 'SEAGULLS ARE WATCHING, DO NOT LEAVE FOOD UNATTENDED.'
