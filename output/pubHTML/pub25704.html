Al Bundy left this review about Angel Hotel

A fairly basic pub but I assume its been refurbed since the last review. An open plan place with a central bar and a pool table to the right of this. A pale green artexed wall interior and white artexed ceiling. 2 Handpumps on the bar but only one with a clip on which was sadly reversed.

Alan Winfield left this review about Angel Hotel

The Angel is a nice looking white painted pub that is just along the road from the Cottage.
Once inside there was one largish room that was carpeted and had comfortable seating,i dont think there were any other rooms.
The pub was a Bass tied house when i went in it and there two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Bass and this was a decent drink the other beer was Worthington bitter which was on handpump.
This was a fairly decent pub and i was happy enough having a drink in here.
