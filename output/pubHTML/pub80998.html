Will Larter left this review about Chequers Micropub

We were less impressed than on our previous visit: the beers were too off-the-wall (Turkish Delight, anyone?) and the staff and some regulars were involved in a semi-drunken gaggle around the bar which made us feel unwelcome. We didn't even get a "good night and thank you" when we returned our glasses to the bar. Must try harder. (If, like Aqualung below, I'd been here when the Treboom Baron Saturday was on, I might have not cared so much about being ignored.)

Aqualung . left this review about Chequers Micropub

Having been going since 2013 this is now one of the older micropubs. It consists of a two tiered room with the bar on the higher part and a black and white laminate floor throughout. The furniture is low tables and chairs rather than the tall stuff favoured by many of these places.
There are five hand pumps which had Atom Half Life & Camomile Blond, Treboom Baron Saturday, Brass Castle Dreamtime and some cricket themed dross from Greene King. I went for the Half Life (£3.50) which was in good nick but looked like a Yorkshire short measure until I realised it was a lined glass.
This is another good micropub but it seemed odd having a Greene King themed beer on.

On 2nd August 2016 - rating: 9
