Real Ale Ray left this review about The Crown At Westleton

The main bar area is on the right-hand side, which consisted of scrubbed tables and assorted chairs and original brick fireplace. I thought the bar was cheap looking. Painted T&G front and a veneered bar top with a veneer strip ironed along the front. There was a smaller lounge area on the left and another seating area behind the central bar area. Then to our surprise, you walk out to a large dining area and onto a enclosed terrace garden area. The Crown also has 34 bedrooms. On the bar there were three ales, two from Adnams and Growler Umbel, also there was a cider called Burnard's Oakey Dokey. There is a BBQ each Sunday throughout the summer.

On 1st July 2013 - rating: 7

Will Larter left this review about The Crown At Westleton

Very smart country hotel with pleasant public bar and huge fireplace. Three real ales were available on my recent visit, all local: Adnams Bitter (Southwold), Green Jack Canary Pale Ale (Lowestoft) and Brandon Rusty Bucket (Brandon). Easily the most expensive beer I had on my mini-tour of Suffolk.
