Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Crow's Nest

Fairly basic bar above the Wetherspoons in The Atrium cinema complex, most likely aimed at the late evening market as it certainly didn't open on a Friday at 11:00 (as stated on What?ub) or 14:00 (as suggested on its own Facebook page). It was open (if almost empty) at 16:00 for anybody not confused about its opening hours... Apart from noting that it has a couple of pool tables and a food servery that presumably can be used for special events, there is little else to say. No real ale of course, so made do with a quick Maltsmith's IPA (£2.20, half) before swiftly moving on.
