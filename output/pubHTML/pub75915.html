Graham Coombs left this review about The National

Reasonably-large suburban pub on eastern ring road. Part of Sizzling Pubs chain, so fairly food-biased, but plenty of room for drinkers too. Furnished in typical 'comfortable dining room' style. Beers included EPA and Batemans XXXB on draught, although latter was getting a bit old so quality needs watching. I would certainly be happy to visit again, especially if looking for food.

On 14th January 2012 - rating: 8
