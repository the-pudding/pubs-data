ROB Camra left this review about Fress

This is mainly a small restaurant cum café, but there are a few seats around the bar and they were happy for me to sit at one of the tables at the bar end. Tiled walls and pictures of old bikes on the walls, a large mirror is on the opposite wall to the bike pictures making it look much bigger than it is. It's very nicely done throughout. Three craft taps n the bar and a half of Brooklyn Lager was a rip off £2.90. It's nice, but not that nice.

On 13th November 2018 - rating: 5
