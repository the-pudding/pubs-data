Alan Winfield left this review about The Crown & Glove

The Crown and Glove used to be a Wards tied house,this pub is in an elevated position in the stannington area of Sheffield.
This pub looks quite nice from the outside i went in the room on the right this was fairly smart and seemed pretty much unaltered there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Kelham Island Easy Rider this was a really nice drink the other beers were Pedigree and Stones bitter.
This is a nice comfy pub to have a few drinks in.
