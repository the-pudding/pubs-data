Alan Winfield left this review about The Crown Inn

The Crown is an old pub with several small rooms.there is no entrance at the front of this pub you have to go in the back way.
There were two real ales that i could see and i had a drink of Castle Rock Harvest Pale which was very nice the other beer was Belvoir Bitter.
We thought this was a decent pub and would have happily stayed for a few more drinks.
