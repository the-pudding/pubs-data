Old Boots left this review about Corporation Brewery Taps

More than a little difficult to get to due to Doncaster's obsession with pedestrian safety on their awful road system, however once you navigate the barriers it's a nice enough 1930s estate pub. A Sam Smiths pub but the OBB has been replaced with the keg version which of course means its not the usual cold vinegar and with low carbonation it was quite a pleasant pint.

Will Larter left this review about Corporation Brewery Taps

This is a Sam Smiths house, which means there's only one real ale, but I like Old Brewery Bitter, and it's only £1.80 a pint, so that's not a problem for me. The exterior looks more like a public convenience than a pub, but inside it's OK, with a drinking area around the bar and the rest of the main room focused on the pool table. We found the atmosphere friendly, but it was rather too warm, with radiators belting out the heat unnecessarily. A little off the beaten track, but worth a visit if you like Sam Smiths.

On 9th May 2012 - rating: 6
