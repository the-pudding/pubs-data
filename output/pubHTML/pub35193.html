Pat Spillane left this review about The Chequers

My Partner and I own this Pub - we reopened Summer 2019 .. We brew our own Craft Beer in our onsite Microbrewery
We usually have 8 fresh beers on tap and 3 craft ciders .. No Lager!
Supurb range of Hoppy IPA's, Wheat Beers, Stouts all made on site
We have a Fish and Chip Van stops out front on a Friday night between 6 - 8 pm .. customers are welcome / encouraged to bring their Fish & Chips into the Pub
Kids welcome with families that are eating (up till 8:30 ish)
The Brewery is called Munson's Brewery - named after a family who lived here, ran the pub and brewed beer in the 1700's ( before Greene King acquisition )
www.munsons.co.uk

Steve of N21 left this review about The Chequers

The Chequers is a 400 year old public house in Gazeley, near Newmarket and was purchased from the Greedy King Empire in late 2011 and made a Free House. It has been tastefully renovated internally with most of the original stone walls retained, especially for the snug bar room.
It functions more as a restaurant and social club for the local residents and has restricted opening hours of 6.00pm - 11.00pm on Thursday’s to Sunday’s. The old GK pumps now dispense regional ales although there does seem to be a focus on Adnams. And for my visit alongside Woodford Wherry and Adnams Ghost Ship, Adnams Mosaic Pale Ale was available, which was a new one on me.
A very pleasant stop and I hope the present owners continue to keep this one going for the benefit of the locals
