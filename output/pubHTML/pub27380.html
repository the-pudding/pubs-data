Graham Coombs left this review about The Little Black Dog

Cosy little pub which is very much a locals' local, although visitors seem to be greeted warmly. There are two rooms knocked through into one, with nice old woodwork and aged furniture, even with a piano. There is an outdoor area at the rear. Robinsons Trooper was the guest beer at time of visit, and in good condition, along with more usual brews. No longer any sign of food however.
