Graham Coombs left this review about TwoTwentyTwo (The Landmark)

Ultra posh restaurant and bar which is part of the 5 star Landmark Hotel but also has its own entrance down some steps on Great Central Street. Super elegant and impressive interior in a L-shaped room, with the bar and some high tables for drinkers at one end. No real ales but a few upmarket keg fonts including Meantime, and priced as you might expect. Very civilised but make sure you are not paying.
