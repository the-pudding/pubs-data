Reviews of Norden Inn (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about Norden Inn

A small bar rather than a micropub converted from Wildbloods butchers which has moved over the road. One small main room with a tiny snug to the rear. All very nicely decorated. Rob, the owner says he is intending to put a cask ale on when trade allows. Currently sells 4 craft kegs which were all Se7en Brothers on our visit, but apparently aren't all the time. Se7en Brothers Session was a very reasonable £3.60 for a craft keg and Ms CAMRA really enjoyed the red wine recommended by Rob. We'll be calling in here next time we're passing.

On 8th August 2019 - rating: 7
