Al Bundy left this review about The Millstone

Oh dear. Now that the local Legh Arms seems to have gone this place has also let itself go. What was a reasonably good pub is now a totally bland place. The giveaway is the 'Tapas Bar' that greets you before you enter and the limited opening hours. If you must go then its a 2 roomed place with the bar to your right on entering and a room to your left. The bar has 2 handpumps on but only Wainwright on. A bit of a pointless place to be honest.

24/10/15
Much better than the Legh Arms nearby. Real ale is available and the interior is quite pleasant.
