Old Boots left this review about The White Hart

A single storey building white washed inside and out with a stone floor. There are two rooms, with darts in the higher more modern styled and decorated room and a screen and machine in lower more original room There is a mix of modern & traditional wooden dining furniture and a nice big fireplace in the lower room which is home to the counter. This has three handpumps with Doombar, Bass and Double Dragon, which was terrifically cold but otherwise OK, plus the usual keg stuff. It also has two things not often seen in pubs these days, a price list and a big bell for calling time. Probably a very busy pub in tourist season. The toilets are across a covered yard cum smoking area at the rear where there is also a patio where you can just see the river.
