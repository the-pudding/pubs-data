Oggwyn Great left this review about The Baron at Bucknell

Friendly country Inn in a sleepy village on Shropshires border with Wales , converted from a farmhouse in 1986 it is multi roomed with a large wood beamed resturant complete with ancient cider press , a conservatory , lounge and bar with pool table .
The food has a very good repution , usual keg taps and three handpulls with a couple of Wye Valley beers and a decent Ludlow Gold , service and locals were pretty friendly .
The pub also has B&B facilities and its own campsite .
Worth a visit if in the area but beware it only opens in the evenings during the week
