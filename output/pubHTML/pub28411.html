Dave Day left this review about The Bridge

They keep some good beers, including Wherry, Adnams and Abbott but a bit pricey at over £3.20 a pint. The B&B is excellent value at £25 per night which includes full English breakfast and free fishing on all 5 lakes plus the river Wensum. Rooms adequate at the price. Shared toilet and bathroom on landing. Could be a bit cleaner. The restaurant prices are very good value with a good selection of grub.

On 9th October 2011 - rating: 7
