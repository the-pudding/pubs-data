Will Larter left this review about The Harp Inn

A roadside pub near a busy junction, which may struggle to attract much attention from passing traffic, though at the speed I go when cycling there was no danger of me missing it. On the three hand pumps there were two beers at the time of my visit, both from Wye Valley brewery: Butty Bach and a house beer called Glasbury Undaunted, with a picture of a tug-of-war team. (Glasbury is pronounced Glaze-brie.) This may well have been their standard bitter rebadged or just tweaked slightly; it was in good condition but nothing special. With some pretty awful 1980s pop music on the sound system I didn't stay for long and wouldn't expect to stop here again; nothing wrong with the place (apart from the music) but nothing to make it stand out either.

Date of visit: 2nd August 2017
