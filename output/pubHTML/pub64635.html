Cole Solem left this review about Mr Fox

Mr Fox is a new cocktail pub in Surrey Street. It has a wide variety of cocktails and craft beers. It now offers food such as rotisserie chicken, burgers, vegan burgers, etc.

It has been massively improved and hopes to put the venue's history in the past to help improve Croydon and bring more people into Surrey Street. It has two shuffleboard tables and is dog friendly!

Great look, great vibe, great food & drink.

Pub SignMan left this review about SLVR

This place only opens from 22:00 onwards on Friday and Saturday nights, so is a rather dubious entry on this website. The place has been through various guises over recent years, having been an Irish themed bar called Jim Thompsons before switching to the current club style venue through incarnations as Vintage and Rehab. It doesn't look very welcoming on approach - all of the windows are blacked out and there is a general scruffiness to its external appearance. Inside, it's all shiny surfaces and low level lighting with pounding music and a cocktail menu - nothing like a pub really. They had Tuborg larger on draught the last time I was here plus various bottled options. Prices weren't as horrific as I'd anticipated. The venue is split level, with the ground floor space offering a lounge to the right, bar in the centre and a dancefloor replete with large mirrors to the left and rear. Stairs lead down to a cosier second bar with a big pillar in the middle of the dancefloor and a servery along the back wall.

All in all, not much to recommend here unless you're looking for a new late night venue following the demise of the equally depressing Tiger Tiger. It's not really a pub and the low rating reflects this.

On 21st July 2016 - rating: 1
