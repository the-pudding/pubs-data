Alan Hurdle left this review about Tyn-y-Capel

This historic traditional local was closed overnight by the landlord in February 2011 and put up for sale with change of use.
The local people want to buy it and run it as a community pub. the landlord was resisting this, but after a 16 month campaign, he has agreed to sell it at an inflated price.
If more funding can be found, this pub could soon be open and selling beer again!
