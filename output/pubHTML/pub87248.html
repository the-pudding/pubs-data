Quinno _ left this review about Ketton Sports & Community Centre

A big new building which serves as the social base for a myriad of sports clubs. Interior is straightforward large room for socialising and events. 4x dart boards and a nice view of the green. 3 ales (maybe a little over eager?) - Sharps (a bit pump tired), Hancocks HB and London Pride. Good chat with the staff, who went over various beers they could order and we threw in our suggestions based on our pub knowledge.
