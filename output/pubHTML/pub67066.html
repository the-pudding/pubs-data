E TA left this review about The Stag

A single room local on what was once the main road through, now a quiet road in what is becoming a dormitory town. It's basic, no frills, friendly and cosy, no food on offer, wooden floor. The landlord was friendly enough, and the atmosphere was calm, almost dead. The only ale on was Doombar, which wasn't in the best condition. I'm not sure it's worth chasing this one down.
