Moya Hodges left this review about The White Swan

The White Swan welcomes all customers old and new. We are a traditional drinking pub with pool and darts. We have six tv screens all showing sky and espn. Entertainment includes a fully computerised juke box with just about any song you can think of we have music on every Saturday night including live bands. Jam sessions every Monday and Quiz night every Thursday.
The White Swan is also a Bed and Breakfast with five double rooms and two twin rooms all available at a very reasonable cost please feel free to ring for availability and cost.
On a nice day you can relax in our large beer garden and even bring along the kids untill 6pm.
