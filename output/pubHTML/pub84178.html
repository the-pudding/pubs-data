Quinno _ left this review about Highland Getaway Inn

Funny little L-shaped interior with quarter-length varnish wood panelling, red wallpaper and purple lit upper. No cask ale but the two local River Leven ales, Caledonian 3 Hop and Fraioch were all available in bottles. Continue up the stairs to the main selling point, an upper level restaurant area which also has darts, TV football and a magnificent outside balcony overlooking the river and sunset. If you’re in town on a sunny afternoon/early evening, this is a good place to head for a bottle.
