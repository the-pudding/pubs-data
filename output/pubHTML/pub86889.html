Delboy 20 left this review about The Woolaston Inn

The Woolaston has been refurbished and is now home to the Gurkha restaurant as well as the pub. I called in for a takeaway and enjoyed a pint of Bass in the bar area and beer garden. There was also the usual array of fizzy stuff if that is your thing! The food is Nepalese cuisine and was excellent. The service was also extremely good and if I lived nearby I would use it again. Good luck to them!
