E TA left this review about Ye Olde Greene Manne

A Vintage Inns roadside pub in a pleasant semi-rural location just outside town. You enter the main bar through the car park and find a flagstone floor, sober music and a friendly atmosphere. Exposed brick and timber attempt to create a rustic feel but somehow don't quite hit the mark. There is a restaurant area to the rear, and there is limited outside seating which is made less pleasant by the busy main road. They run regular evening events, usually with themed food. The staff are friendly and effective, but not efficient. The seating is comfortable, and the overall experience pleasant. There were 3 ales on – I had Hobgoblin, which was in excellent condition. I would be happy to return for a meal or a few beers anytime and would include on a crawl.

On 8th April 2014 - rating: 7
