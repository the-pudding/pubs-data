Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Soho Kitchen & Bar

Modern bar / restaurant with a variety of seating around a central servery and open-plan kitchen. Also has a small patio tables along the pavement frontage. Features one elongated keg fount on the counter, with a total of 12 taps dispensing Amstel, Heineken, Kronenbourg, Belhaven Stout and two from Camden (Pale Ale and Gentleman's Wit); all priced at £5.00 (or £3.00 for a half).
