ROB Camra left this review about The Artisan

This is a small music oriented bar on 2 floors. It was very quiet at 6 on a Friday, but then again they only opened at 5. I was told it was going to be very busy later on. The stage is downstairs and there's a few tables and stools, but it's obviously based on vertcal drinking. Upstairs has a few sets of tables and chairs. A friendly barmaid and regulars add to the attractions as do the 2 handpumps which were serving beers from Bespoke brewery. I tried both of them and they were in good nick. They get their beers through Brightside, so I would expect their beers to be on the bar at times as well. I'll happily call in here again.
