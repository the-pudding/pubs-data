S W left this review about The Wheatsheaf

As of end January 2013 a new manager in place. Local ales now on bar (Titanic, Joules, Slaters, Lymestone). The food is heading back to good old counrty rustic style with new menus promised including a cheaper bar menu, local produce and family dining options. Great carvery on Sundays. Play area for the kids. Fantasic letting rooms (I had a peak!!. Topped off with good service..and a smile !!! Definitely recommend
