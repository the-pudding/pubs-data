Steve C left this review about Prince Of Wales Inn

I visited the Prince of Wales on a recent Saturday evening when there was a dart competition in progress on a board located to the left of the entrance. A pool table is located at the rear where access can be gained to a large picnic table lined beer garden. A plasma screen was showing Sky Sports.
I found the service to be very friendly from behind the bar that supports a standard draught selection alongside San Miguel. If I lived locally I would use this pub.

On 9th September 2016 - rating: 6
