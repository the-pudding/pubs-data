Graham Coombs left this review about The White Horse

Very smart, well-maintained pub in the centre of the village. A clean, bright interior features a comfortable drinking area to the front, with a dartboard tucked down the side of the bar, with a larger well-tabled room more aimed at diners stretching down the other side to a conservatory by the car park. Garden at rear. Draught ales included TT Boltmaker and something numeric from Isaac Poad and condition was good (Cask Marque). All in all a nice pub.

On 26th August 2018 - rating: 8
