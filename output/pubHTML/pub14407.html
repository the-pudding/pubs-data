Bucking Fastard left this review about Crystal Palace

Two very seperate rooms in this small pub.Front bar with pool table and TV ,definately the public bar while the rear lounge is now set up with tables for dining,although you can just drink in here if you wish.Food is always available and there is now chinese and other asian dishes available both within and to take out,in a joint venture.English pub food(the landlady's description) is available until 3pm and on Sundays only a carvery operates until 4pm.Three handpumps with GK IPA,Old Speckled Hen and Spitfire as a guest ,at £3.20 a little pricey for the ambiance on offer.Functional but not the best of the three canalside pubs in Berko.

Steve of N21 left this review about Crystal Palace

Two bar locals pub with a public and saloon bar set up. Bit of an old style decor but comfortable and friendly enough.
Public bar is dominated by a pool table and TV, and lounge is more set to tables
Its a GK house with the usual OSH and IPA, but also had a guest ale on at the time of my visit.
But the top thing about this pub is the location next to the canal. And a few outside benches provide a great place for sitting outside and watching the wildlife float by.

Clive Thompson left this review about Crystal Palace

An attractive two bar pub with a canal side location and a few tables out on the tow path. The bars have comfortable seating and the pub is well presented both inside and out. Pool table in public bar, games and books are available and the lounge bar is laid out with tables for the English or Chinese food which is available all day. Greene King IPA, Old Speckled Hen and one other. Friendly and efficient service.
