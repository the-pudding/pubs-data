Alan Winfield left this review about The Bull

The Bull is a large pub that is on the busy london road A6 to the south of Bedford.
This pub looks quite grand from the outside with a balcony and also a bull above the front door.
Once inside there was a large room that had a dining area and a normal drinking area,this room was quite smart and also busy on my dinnertime visit.
This pub is a Greene King tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Greene King IPA and this was a decent drink the other beer was GK Abbot.
This is a decent enough pub if you want something to eat and drink and you live in this part of Bedford.
