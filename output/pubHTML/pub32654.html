Graham Coombs left this review about The Wilbraham Arms

Large purpose-built pub in its own grounds, mainly centred on the food trade but pleasant enough for drinkers. Beers from Robinsons - Unicorn, Trooper and Wizard on handpump at time of visit, plus a Weston's Cider. Beers in good condition (Cask Marque listing)and food was also very good.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Wilbraham Arms

The Wilbraham Arms is well away from the middle of Alsager and it is a newish pub built to serve the estate that it is on.
This pub is a Robinsons tied house and once inside the pub is open plan with different drinking areas the pub is very smart and comfortable.
There were three Robinsons beers on the bar i had a drink of Unicorn bitter and this was a very nice drink.
I thought this was a very nice pub to have a few drinks in
