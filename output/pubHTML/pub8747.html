Quinno _ left this review about The Harbour

An idyllic-looking thatched stone building. A Hall & Woodhouse place and fairly blandly made-over inside with a lot of plain wood. Bland muzak and awful framed motivational phrases in the gents, urgh. Three ales on; a house beer, First Call and a fairly terrible Tanglefoot that tasted like it had been fished out of the River Axe which runs through the rear of the garden. On this form it’s best left to the middle classes and their dinners.
