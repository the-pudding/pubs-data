Graham Coombs left this review about The George Inn

Slightly disappointing town centre pub which seems to be much more geared up to eating than traditional pub activities. It has a fairly bland, modern interior, although there is a garden at rear. Draughts ales on offer were Doom Bar and 49er, neither of which were in the best condition.

BRUMMIE STEVE left this review about The George Inn

While awaiting for a bus to Shanklin where I was staying I popped in The George for a quick pint of bitter as it is right next to the bus station, the pub was quite busy how'ever I was charged £4.00 for a pint of average bitter, it was my first pint on the Island and by far the most expensive.

john mcgraw left this review about The George Inn

A comfortable town centre pub selling a fair range of real ales with the "Yates" blonde being in good condition. secluded rear drinking area. This seems to be a fair pub for real ale as there are no pubs in Newport that are in the 2009 Good Beer guide which is a shame being this is the capital of the Isle of Wight
