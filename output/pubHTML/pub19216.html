ROB Camra left this review about The Horse & Jockey

This one looks like it's on its last legs. Up for sale and badly ripped seating in the "lounge" area of the pub. There's a dartboard in the front area, but it's difficult to see how it could be played as there's a table football in front of it. Flashing Xmas decorations up everywhere in the middle of November and it still looks sad. Clientele and prices both at the lower end of the spectrum. No real ale of course so I had a half of fizz. I fully expect this pub to be closed next time I go past.

On 17th November 2013 - rating: 3
