Alan Winfield left this review about The Ship Inn

The Ship Inn is a very nice looking stone built pub that is in the middle of Mevagissey.
Once inside there is a single room that runs to the rear,the bar is on the mid left side,there are lots of different areas to sit in off the main room,the ceilings are quite low and there were lots of fairy lights.
The pub is a St Austell tied house,there were four St Austell beers on the bar,we had a drink of St Austell Proper Job,which went down a treat,the other beers were HSD,Cornish Best and Tribute.
Food was being served but did not take over the pub as you could sit anywhere.
We thought this was a very nice pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 15/8/2017
