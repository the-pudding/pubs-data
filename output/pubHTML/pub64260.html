Will Larter left this review about Jug & Glass

I've often passed this pub on the A515 on my way to or from Ashbourne and thought it looked a little run down, but it seems to have been spruced up recently - in fact it looks quite smart. Inside it's quite small, with low ceilings. The seating was mostly low black leather sofas and upholstered low red stools.

At the bar there was only one beer available, though there are two hand pumps. This was Rambler amber ale from the Wincle Beer Company. I can't review the beer as was driving so I had a ginger ale.
