Quinno _ left this review about The Bruce Tavern

A basic locals boozer with a nice (if fairly battered) J shaped servery. Quite tight in the front area with a larger rear section featuring a dart board. Windowsill bench seating. Robust locals. No ale, so a Heverlee lager sufficed.

Colin Walker left this review about The Bruce Tavern

A typical traditional style pub just 30 yards up from the High Street. Considering its location the place was not overcrowded even on a Saturday afternoon. At least this makes it easier to get served even when the local football is on.
