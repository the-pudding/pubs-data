Old Boots left this review about Fox & Hounds

An Ember Inn, the closest pub to Horsforth railway station, a bit of a pubs-R-us venue in typical modern dining pub style probably ok for a swift one while waiting for a train. Hopelessly understaffed on a Saturday evening when I was waiting for a train so I can't comment on the beer quality as railway timetables don't wait on the convenience of Ember Inns, meaning I didn't actually get served in the 10 minutes I spent in the place. 8 pulls in a bunch of 5 and 3 on the two faces of the counter – always a problem for inept bar staff I grant you, national and local beers in evidence plus the usual kegs.
