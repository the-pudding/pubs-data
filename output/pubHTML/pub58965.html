Steve C left this review about The Phoenix

It took a while to get served in here on Tuesday afternoon as the lone barmaid was collecting plates from the tables. The food is cheap and I got a Wetherspoons feel from the place, that is apart from the Sky Sports and plasma screens throughout and lack of real ale. Breakfast is available from 10:00 daily and there are extended hours until 01:00 at the weekends.

The Phoenix advertises itself as a party pub and is run by the Barracuda group, one to avoid if you've ever paid income tax.
