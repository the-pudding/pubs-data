Alan Winfield left this review about Yew Tree Inn

The Yew Tree is a nice looking stone built pub that is on the norhtern edge of Otley.
Once inside the pub still has two separate rooms,i had a drink in the lounge to the right which is L shaped and carpeted,the seating is comfy bench type and normal tables and chairs,the bar to the left is smaller and oblong shaped,there is a pool table in this room,it was too busy to have drink in this room.
There were three real ales on the bar and one real Cider,i had a drink of Titanic Night to Remember which was a very nice drink,the other beers were Tetley Bitter and Doom Bar.
The pub does food and there were plenty of people having meals on my Saturday afternoon visit.
I quite liked this pub,it was comfortable and warm and busy with locals.

Pub visited 8/11/2014
