Komakino . left this review about The Generals Arms

Not anymore it ain't. OK, so it's had a makeover, there's now piped music, TV screens showing live sports (on mute) and dartboard was even spotted, but there's the same bar counter, similar drinks offers ("double up on selected spirits for £1.35) and same Spoons-type clientele. A half of Vale Gravitas was £1.95 and was joined by a yawn-worthy band of supplicants including Courage, Abbot, IPA and CW Bombardier Glorious English.
