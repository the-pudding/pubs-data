Oggwyn Great left this review about The Chester Fields

Large pub/restaurant , as young Mr Bundy says in the previous review more a restaurant with a bar , its all very nice in here with lots of wood and leather seating , there is a Mezzanine floor , several outside drinking areas which would be great in summer , not so great in the snow of my visit .
Friendly staff , the Italian(?) barman a real character , several locale real ales on from Weetwood and Cheshire Brew Bros , not tried as i was driving .

Very nice pub/restaurant. A bit pricey but it does real ale and has a cracking beer garden for summer drinking. That said it isnt very pubby. The modern interior is what it is - a restaurant with bar facilities.
