Alan Winfield left this review about The Queen Hotel

The Queen is a ugly looking pub that is close to bradford interchange.
Once inside here the pub had one room with different drinking areas and there were no real ale son the bar with the only bitter being John Smiths smooth crap so i had a Guinness.
The pub was smart and comfortable but was let down because no real ales were available.

ROB Camra left this review about The Queen Hotel

Now reopen and still rough as a robbers dog. They have a sign out side which says "Try One Of Our Cask Ales". They didn't have any last night as both clips were turned around. Horseshoe shaped room with a pool table on a raised area to the right. Full of transients which is normal for a pub next to a station. It's also full of low-lifes which is also normal. As there was no ale I decided to just have a seat whilst waiting for my train. After about 5 minutes I decided I'd rather stand on the freezing cold platform than sit in here, so I left. It's dire.

Jonathan Crewdson left this review about The Queen Hotel

Only visited once recently after several years and was far from impressed. Very rough place. Not much in the way of real ales. Staff were indifferent to my presence. Interior was worn and in desperate need of TLC. Sooo many better pubs in Bradford to visit so won't be going back unless it changes radically.
