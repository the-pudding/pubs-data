ROB Camra left this review about Roach Hotel

A bit of a strange pub is this one. Both of the areas to the left as you enter were in darkness at 6:30 on a Friday. This left a few puinters congregated around the bar. There's a pool table in one of the dark rooms. One handpump on the bar serving Lees Bitter. I had a quick half and then went to catch my bus. Another one better left to the locals I think.
