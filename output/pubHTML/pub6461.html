M Bryce left this review about Churchills Wine Bar

What a disaster! Avoid at all costs! My hubbie and I stopped in for a drink and a meal on Sunday 26th June 2011! We should have noticed that no-one else was eating! We ordered our meals and sat with our drink at the table outside the front door. The tables were grubby, covered with pre-dried sticky drinks! Our food arrived. I ordered the chicken and chips. THIS WAS THE SMALLEST PORTION OF CHICKEN I HAVE EVER SEEN IN MY LIFE. IT WAS MORE 'A CHICKEN DRUMSTICK'. The chicken was so dry, I fed it to my dog. The chips were reheated and the salad, although fresh, was merely a bit of lettuce stuck on the plate with a couple of small strips of pepper. By this time, some loud drinker was having a go at singing on the BLARING LOUD KARAOKE, we couldn't hear each other talk for the noise.
IT IS SO SAD IN THIS DAY, WHEN PUBS ARE CLOSING DOWN DAILY BECAUSE OF LACK OF TRADE, HERE IN WINDERMERE, WITH A THRIVING TOURIST TRADE, YOU ARE OFFERED SUB STANDARD MEALS LIKE THIS. I did complain to the young guy behind the bar and his reply was 'what was wrong with it'!!! EVERYTHING.............. Do not waste your money by going to this pub for food!
