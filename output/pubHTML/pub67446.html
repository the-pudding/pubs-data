Will Larter left this review about The Crown

The external appearance of this pub promises a traditional town centre pub with a bit of character, but someone has put paid to that by stripping out anything they could get their hands on and making it instead into a modern drinking barn. Of course, it is necessary for someone to pay for this improvement, and my half of Arkells Hoperation IPA was £2 (compared with £1.50 for a similar strength beer at the Wheatsheaf just down the road). The beer, "Hoperation" indeed, was quite at home here, being also a product of modern attitudes to traditional styles. I drank it as quickly as I could - fortunately I was rather thirsty after a long cycle ride in warm weather - and left.

Date of visit: 10th May 2018
