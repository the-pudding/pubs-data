Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Wykebeck Arms

Fair-sized pub, although only the slightly split-level rectangular main bar along the frontage seems to be in regular use. Pool table at one end along with a (very welcome, on a windy winter day) real fire. No real ale, unfortunately, but the greeting was friendly and the John Smiths only £2.40.
