ROB Camra left this review about The Flute

A massive bare floorboarded sports bar with a couple of raised areas. TV screens all over the place and a pool table in the middle. Quite a few girls in the bar which is unusual for a sports bar. 5 handpumps on the bar, but only 2 beers available, although 2 more had clips turned around. All the beers were from the extended Marston's stable. My pint of Marston's New World Pale was in decent nick, but Ms CAMRA's red wine was poor. If anything it's a bit better than I was expecting, but I'd probably still give it a miss next time I'm around here.

On 20th February 2016 - rating: 6
