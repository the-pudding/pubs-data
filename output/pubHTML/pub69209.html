Malden man left this review about The Anderton

A massive single room, slightly raised to the rear, rather reminiscent of a Wetherspoons in layout albeit with more subdued lighting and a considerably worse beer choice. Smith and Jones branded, cheap food and drinks deals, the pub probably appeals to a younger crowd in the evenings. Mixed seating including a few leather banquettes around the perimeter and in booths.
Three handpumps, two unclipped, one with Bombardier on. TVs on mute, banal music playing. If you like characterless barns then fair enough but even then you'd do much better to cross the road to The Penny Black where you at least get a good ale choice plus 'spoons prices. Dismal.
