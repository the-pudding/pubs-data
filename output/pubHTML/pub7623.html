Graham Coombs left this review about The Castle Inn

Town centre pub with fairly basic interior, popular with locals. At time of visit had three ales from St Austell on (the sampled HSD in good form) although judging from the pump clips on display it has others also, plus a couple of ciders.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Axminster Castle

When i went in this pub it was just called the Castle Inn so since then it has lost the Inn and gained the Axminster.
This is a smallish pub that was nothing special inside but this may have altered since my visit,there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Courage Directors which was ok but nothing special the other beer was GK Abbot.
