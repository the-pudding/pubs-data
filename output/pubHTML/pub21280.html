Alan Winfield left this review about Hare & Hounds

The Hare and Hounds is a strange looking pub has it has no proper frontage,the pub is entered from the car park and through a nice looking courtyard.
Once inside there was an L shaped room that had a few tables for drinkers and most laid out for diners,i also think there was a seperate dining room but i did'nt see this,there was also what looked like a mezzanine towards the front of the pub,the main room was carpeted and had a beamed ceiling which had been painted over in cream like the rest of the pub.
I think this pub is a Marstons house has there were three of their beers on the bar,i had a drink of Marstons EPA which was a decent enough drink the other beers were Pedigree and Banks's Lions Roar.
The pub was very busy with a mixed age range.
This was not really the sort of pub i like,the main bar area seemed more like a waiting area before going through to the hidden room for a meal, having said that as drinkers we were made just as welcome here.
