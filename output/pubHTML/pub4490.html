Reviews of The Lemon Arms (Average Rating: 8½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about The Lemon Arms

A smart pub, with a nice large bar and a couple of separate areas leading off. Nice traditional furnishings and well looked-after; you can pick up the feel of a well-kept pub immediately. Beers from St Austell in excellent form (Cask Marque accreditation) and the locally-sourced food is excellent, including fresh seafood. Outside there is a patio area. Darts and pool may not be available when busy with food. An excellent village pub.
