Will Larter left this review about The Star

A very old building in local stone which stands at a busy crossroads. Once inside there are two rooms, with the bar in between. Some people were watching the early kick off in the football in the room to the left. I went to investigate the hand pumps, which are behind a pillar in the room to the right. A fairly mundane selection (see photo) but I thought I'd have a quick half to wash away the taste of the bad beer I'd had at Coopers up the road. I waited, and waited. I thought whoever was running the bar must have gone to the cellar for something. I waited, and waited...after four or five minutes I just gave up. No rating submitted, as no drink was taken.
