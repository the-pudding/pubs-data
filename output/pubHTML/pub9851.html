Old Blue left this review about The Dolphin Inn

A seemingly very foodie Hall & Woodhouse pub. Large car park leads to main entrance at the side of the pub, tables on the right set for dining and to the left the pub area. I didn't stay as I'd missed the weekday 2pm finish on serving lunch and I had to find something to eat. Apparently they do serve food all day on Sundays though.
