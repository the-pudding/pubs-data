Al Bundy left this review about The Greyhound Inn

Out of the way pub in a small village. Multi-roomed with approximately 6 areas to sit. The servery is as you enter and has 2 sides. There are 8 handpumps on the bar to the right. 5 local ales from Eastgate were on today. The inside is fairly traditional, if basic. There is a large beer garden at the rear. To the right of the pub is another building but it looks like its just a storage area. A decent pub but out of the way.

On 8th August 2017 - rating: 6
