Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Sleepers Bar (Great Northern Hotel)

Very much an upmarket hotel bar, albeit set within a historic building directly opposite the main station entrance. Several small seating areas around the servery, all with modern leather chairs and sofas and rather bland decoration. On the plus side, there are three handpumps on the counter, and these were dispensing Rutland Osprey (£4.00) and the seasonal Calcutta from Grainstore plus Stony Ford's Sheepmarket Supernova Straw.

Real Ale Ray left this review about Great Northern Hotel

Whatpub has an interesting write up about this Hotel, so that should save me a bit of keyboarding. They also had three Grainstore beers on handpump, we went for the Ten Fifty and the Steelback IPA and I agree with Will, that their beers were pricey. The bar is situated on the left from the reception area and the majority of the room was over cluttered with tables and chairs. A large screen on the back wall providing a catch up on Sky TV. There was also a small patio area out front for those sun worshippers. The beer condition was excellent and agree this is a handy pit stop before the train.
