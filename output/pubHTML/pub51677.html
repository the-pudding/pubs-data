Martin McGreskin left this review about Coppies Bar

Coppies Bar has been under new Management for just under a year.

A popular bar with a high standard of local Darts and Pool players and a friendly welcome from one of the bar staff is never in doubt.

There are regular functions and events heldin the premises and there is a nice lounge for a quiet evening as well.

The Jukebox caters for young and old and there are always a few locals on hand to tell you a story or two as well.

All in all a good local, well run.

On 31st December 2005 - rating: 10
