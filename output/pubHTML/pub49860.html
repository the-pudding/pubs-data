peter ashworth left this review about The Kirkhouse

This Shettleston pub and old coaching house is situated on the main road and only a short walk to the railway station.On entry the first thing that catches your eye is the impressive oval wooded bar,complete with the internal gantry with either side of this drinking and dining areas with the right side having a wooded floor and the left a smart flagged one.Called in around 1pm on a weekday just before my train was due and found the pub quite busy although most of these were diners,but did not see any cask pumps on show but found the pub very modern and clean and would certainly call in again.
