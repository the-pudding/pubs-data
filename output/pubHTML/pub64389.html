Clive Thompson left this review about Lancer

A bit weird, barn of a place, so much room, so few punters. Very well presented place and good service. Half a dozen tables outside at the back. Quite foodie but they have three ales, Bombardier , Courage and Morland Original. The latter is on good form and a reasonable £2.30 a pint. Can't pick up a WiFi signal. Signage suggest a Smith and Jones pub.

On 26th May 2013 - rating: 5
