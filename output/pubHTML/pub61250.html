Gill Smith left this review about Ship Inn

Caught a 551 bus from Truro to Portloe to start a walk in the area, and enjoyed a Tribute from St Austell brewery before I started the walk. Tidy locals pub with decent beer. It closed at 2.30 for the afternoon so I couldn't have a beer when I finished my walk.
