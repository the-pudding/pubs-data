Oggwyn Great left this review about Stumble Inn

Friendly country pub well off the beaten track , it has a two room layout with the cosy bar to the left of the entrance and a large dining area(shut on my visit) to the right .
There is a large wooden propeller mounted on one of the beams along with a variety of amusing signs and a large collection of pump clips .
Usual fizzy kegs and two handpulls with Cottage Western Glory and a nice Robinsons Dizzy Blonde .
Service was good from the chatty landlord and plenty of banter flying about with the locals who were a bunch of characters .
Worth a visit if in the area .
