Colin Walker left this review about Sportsters

Sportsters is situated within the former Falkirk picture hall. The place has a very open plan interior when entering and a large bar greets you on the back wall. There is over 50 Screens including 8 projector screens showing Sky Sports and BT Sports. The food menu has such deals as Hot Rock steaks where you can get two Hot Rock Steaks and 2 drinks from £30. There is daily deals running from Monday - Friday along with the new "Man VS Pig" where if you beat the challenge you eat free. There is also a private function room available plus there is a pool hall located upstairs which has 5 American pool tables and 6 British pool tables. Also free Internet access & Playstation games consoles.

On 15th April 2016 - rating: 10
