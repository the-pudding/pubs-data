Quinno _ left this review about The Old Buttercross

Nothing old about this place! It is a large new-build Marstons eatery situated just off the A606 Oakham bypass. L-shaped inside with some broken-up areas. Four ales on, Hobgoblin, Pedigree, Wainwright and Oakham JHB, the latter of which was faintly turning and Desmond reported a very poor quality lemonade too. We retreated to the outside garden and we either helped or hindered the stunted summer grass by pouring the Oakham onto it. Boring, poor bar skills and just a general waste of time.

On 2nd August 2018 - rating: 3
