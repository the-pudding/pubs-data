Komakino . left this review about Weavers

An estate-style pub a five minute walk from the town centre near to Aylesbury's prison. A rough L-shape internally, this houses a pool table in a barren, sunken area towards the rear of the pub as well as several TV screens showing a Premier League match on a Sunday afternoon. Just the one ale on handpump - Theakston's Hogshead - with a further pump unclipped. The decor is a little on the spartan and tired side and unless you're looking to complete the Aylesbury Town tick-wise, you can give this a swerve.

On 30th April 2017 - rating: 3
