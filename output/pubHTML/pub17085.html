Al Bundy left this review about The Station

A fine traditional style pub that's as tatty as f*** but isn't as bad as you'd think. Only 3 of the 6 handpumps were on but at least the fire in the room to your left as you enter was roaring away. I liked this pub and it does seem to like its "rock music". No Sonic Youth or Half Man Half Biscuit to be heard or seen but not to worry. Lots of pictures of steam trains. Naturally.

ROB Camra left this review about The Station

I last visited this pub 22 years ago. I don't think it's been decorated since, even the carpet looks the same, although it's so worn in places it's difficult to tell. There's a pool table in the room to the right as you enter and a small lounge with a great fireplace to the left which has a real fire in Winter. The bar is straight in front of you as you enter and there's also a beer garden. There's lots of railway memorabilia and the pub is very dog friendly. This is very much a rock pub, with live bands and the clientele sport long hair, studs, earrings and tattoos, all very friendly though. 6 handpumps with 3 beers available. The Triple fff Moondance was in good nick. As a pub it's a bit of a shambles really, but I still like it.

On 27th June 2014 - rating: 7
