Al Bundy left this review about Farmers Arms

A decent enough pub on the edge of the town centre. Basically a 3 roomed pub with a central servery. A pool table is to one side and two snug-like areas to the other. There was one real ale on on this visit. The untidy exterior belies a reasonably tidy pub. A steep walk back up to the town centre though.
