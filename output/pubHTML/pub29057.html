Alan Winfield left this review about The Victoria Hotel

The Victoria is in the middle of Hornsea and faces a busy road junction.
Once inside here i sat at the front of the pub where i could look out of the window this area of the pub was quite pubby and fairly comfortable there was a dining area at the back of the pub.
There were Four real ales on the bar i had a drink of Shepherd Neame Early Bird and this was a very nice drink the other beers were Tetley bitter, Black Sheep bitter and Bombadier.
I thought this was a decent pub to have a few drinks in.

Brian thompson left this review about The Victoria Hotel

On the beautiful East Yorkshire Coast, The Victoria Bars and Restaraunt is an ideal location for that special occasion, a family get together or just to kick back and relax. You've earned it!

Whether you just want to drop in for a beer and a chat, arrange that long overdue meal for two or even a full Sunday carvery for the family, our locally sourced fresh produce is prepared to order and served to your satisfaction.

With heritage back to the 1840's, then named the Prince Of Wales, The Victoria is a long standing icon in Hornsea and has been providing locals and holiday makers alike with top quality beers, wines and food. From a freshly made sandwich to a full banquet, we will cater for your individual requirements, leaving you to relax and enjoy!
