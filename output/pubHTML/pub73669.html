ROB Camra left this review about The Alchemist

Smart lager bar in the centre of Spinningfields. Obviously aimed at the suits from the surrounding tower blocks of offices. It's very high and airy with some interesting lighting and a great looking bar back with a large clock dominating. None of the lager pumps have names on them so I'm not sure what the full range is, but I ended up with a Becks, which was freezing cold and tasteless. It was £3.60 which is quite reasonable for this part of Manchester. Not a bar I'll be visiting on a regular basis I suspect.

On 16th December 2011 - rating: 4
