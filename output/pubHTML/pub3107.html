Gill Smith left this review about Shuckburgh Arms

Enjoyed our visit yesterday to this popular village local that features in the 2019 Good Beer Guide. The pub is run by the local community with shareholders and a committee and we enjoyed our Grainstore Red Kite beer here. There is a bar and restaurant , then a covered patio area at the rear and a large garden and the village cricket pitch.
