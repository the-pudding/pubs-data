Reviews of The Hedley Verity (Lloyds No 1) (Average Rating: 6½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about The Hedley Verity (Lloyds No 1)

this is unashamedly a big, brash Lloyds bar and it’s absolutely hideous inside including ghastly purple lighting. in parts it rather reminds me of the Lime Kiln in Liverpool and I never liked that one either. the group of freshers students sat behind me were also irritating beyond belief. Staff were good and i enjoyed my porter so hey, you may like it!

ROB Camra left this review about The Hedley Verity (Lloyds No 1)

I liked this JDW even though it's supposed to be a Lloyds it wasn't obvious to me. Loads of small areas to sit in which made it feel quite pub like even though it's one big room really. We didn't visit the upstairs area either as we didn't even realise it was there. A good selection of beers on the bar and whilst my notes fail me on what I had they do say it was a well kept pint. Possibly my favourite JDW in central Leeds.

Al Bundy left this review about The Hedley Verity (Lloyds No 1)

I didn't mind this Wethers. A decent enough place which was an open plan pub with an upstairs bit that I never visited. The ground floor bar had a reasonably decent choice of beers from the 10 handpumps. For those in the know I didn't tread on the lines in the pavement or see the more cultural aspects.
