Blackthorn _ left this review about The Golden Lion

This has obviously undergone a refurbishment and is considerably improved since my last visit, although this was a few years ago.

Pleasant L shaped pub, with a fire and some comfy sofa's. There's a seperate room with a pool table.
Beer was Otter, Butcombe and Doom Bar, plus a good choice of ciders - Thatcher's Gold, Ashton Press, Blackthorn and Thatcher's Pear.
