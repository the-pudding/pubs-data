Reviews of The Foresters (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Jonathan Wilde left this review about Foresters Arms

After previously visiting this pub some decade ago or longer it was time to revisit. The pub from the exterior has a deceptive look as it appeared to look closed. So after peering through the door and noticing full bins it was deemed as open. We eventually found somewhere that didn't have double yellow lines and ventured in. Fairly larger L shape seating and bar area with garden at rear. Theme of the pub is Rugby with lots of signed and framed shirts all around the pub on the walls. Only appeared to be a couple of ales on tap Trelawney being one and the lager was limited. Nice pub and will revisit again once it comes that time again.

On 13th October 2012 - rating: 6
