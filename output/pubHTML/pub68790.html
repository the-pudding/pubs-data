Steve C left this review about The Six Bells

This isn’t the friendliest pub that I have ever been into and I found the woman behind the bar to be slightly threatening when I had to pull her away from a conversation to pour my Guinness. Abbot Ale was the only cask beer, but there was a selection of standard draught. I counted five plasma screens dotted around the pub which is a lot as this isn’t a huge place. Some modern chart music was playing which was at a good volume and I also spotted a dartboard, but I won’t be returning in a hurry to play on it.

On 3rd October 2009 - rating: 3
