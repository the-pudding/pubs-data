Will Larter left this review about The Crown

The beers were all Theakstons when I was here: Old Peculier, Best Bitter, XB and Lightfoot, spread between two banks of hand pumps, four in the front room and three around the corner. It was fairly lively at the time of my late Sunday afternoon visit, which I imagine must be a busy day for all the pubs here. I went for the OP, although my 10 mile cycle ride from Ribblehead hardly justified my selection in terms of replenishment of energy expended. By way of punishment for my audacity, this was not by any means the best half of this fine beer that I've sampled this year. Not bad enough to put me off coming back next year, mind.
