Alan Winfield left this review about Redgate

The Redgate looks like an early 60s built estate pub,the pub is a decent walk from the middle of Mansfield.
Once inside the pub had two rooms with a smart comfortable lounge and a decent bar,i had a drink in the bar which was quite busy on my mid Saturday afternoon visit.
When i went in this pub it was a Kimberley tied house and there were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of keg Kimberley bitter which was a pretty poor drink.
This is a locals pub,i only did this pub because it was one of the few pubs in Mansfield that i had'nt been in.

Pub visited 26/3/2005
