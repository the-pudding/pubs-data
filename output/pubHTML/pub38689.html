Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Wychbury Inn

Now branding itself as a 'village pub and Thai restaurant', the emphasis is very much on the latter aspect but there is still some general seating in half of the 'L'-shaped bar. The remaining tables, and those in the adjoining dining room, are fully laid up for those eating. Fairly anonymous semi-modern furniture and decor, but a friendly welcome. Thankfully, there was also a decent pint of Butty Bach (£3.55) available from one of two handpumps.
