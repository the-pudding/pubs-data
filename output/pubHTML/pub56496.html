David Ross left this review about The Old Silent Inn

I came upon this place when walking back to Haworth from Top Withens farmhouse (the alleged inspiration for the house in "Wuthering Heights") and was attracted by the unusual name. The interior was actually quite modern and it was full of families with children having meals. Consequently, it wasn't exactly silent either, but a good pub nevertheless.
