Alan Winfield left this review about Saddle Inn

The Saddle Inn is a nice looking pub that is set in the back streets.
Once inside the main room is oblong shaped with the bar on the left side,the room is carpeted and has comfy bench seating with small stools,there was a TV to the front left showing the Grand Prix preview,there is a separate room to the rear right which is oblong shaped,there is a pool table in it and leather settees for seating.
Sadly in such a nice pub there were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Worthington Cream crap which it was,but probably slightly better that the other option of John Smiths Extra Cold crap.
This is a very pleasant pub to have a drink in,just a shame it lacked one real ale.
