Alan Winfield left this review about The Rising Sun

The Rising Sun is at the top end of beverley road in north hull.
This looks like a 30s style pub from the outside and there are a few picnic benches at the front to watch the traffic go by,once inside there is one L shaped room and there was a small raised seating area at the front of the pub the main drinking area was carpeted and had seating bays with comfortable bench type seating.
There were two real ales on here i had a drink of Jennings cumberland and this was quite a nice drink the other beer was Hobgoblin.
I thought this was quite a nice pub to have a few drinks in.

On 3rd May 2011 - rating: 7
