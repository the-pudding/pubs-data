Oggwyn Great left this review about Yockleton Arms

Formerly the Elephant and Castle this is now primarly a Resturant , there is a small Bar area and patio for people just wanting a drink .
Only open in the evenings , no real Ale during my visit just fizzy keg .
The food has a good reputation but if your just after a pint i would not bother .
