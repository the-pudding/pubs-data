Old Boots left this review about All Bar One

Looking like a long thin shop conversion into a chain bar, it's professionally done with a good choice of wood and tile floor finishes, wood, tile and mirrored wall treatments and a range of seating solutions to suit many tastes. A bewildering array of Edison light bulbs will excite even the most avid of lightophiles. There is a wide range of keg beers available, a few not from the national collection although they are served at a highly chilled temperature and are well carbonated too. It seems quite girl friendly so perhaps a place where cougars gather to drink chilled white wine as on my visit. There's a family friendly dining space adjacent to an open to view kitchen space at the rear and a long climb to the toilets.
