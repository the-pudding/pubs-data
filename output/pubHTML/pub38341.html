Soup Dragon left this review about Bush Inn

A nice looking white rendered detached pub, with black paintwork.

The interior had two rooms, bar and lounge in which there was a TV and a roaring fire. The decor was white and fake black beams, with wood panel and red ceiling. There was a plethora of plates and other ephemera giving the traditional feel. The fireplace was tiled and there were a number of prints and pictures. The service was fine, the clientele mature and friendly, with pub games going on. The music was at a decent level and mixed.

Beer; tap stuff, with an excellent Ansell's MILD. On handpull there were the untried Spitfire and Timothy Taylor Landlord.

Another great pub, with a different MILD to be enjoyed and Timothy Taylor for back-up. I will return here.
