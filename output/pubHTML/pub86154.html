Delboy 20 left this review about Boston Tea Party

A small selection of craft cans and bottles (Camden pale ale, Brooklyn lager etc) are available but this is really a coffee shop! That said I was happy enough to pop in and enjoy a beer. Quite pricey at £3.75 for a small can. The outdoor seating is a suntrap and the place was quite busy.
