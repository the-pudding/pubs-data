Reviews of Castle Hotel (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Rob Hunter left this review about Castle Hotel

A proper traditional feeling pub without feeling shabby. Not been for a few years so paid a visit last night. 10 hand pulls at the bar, half of those offering Robinsons beers, one was off, and the others were guests.

The small bar area gets busy quickly but we managed to get a table in the room behind the bar. This has an old piano and a rather nice fireplace (though partially hidden by the table in front of it).

It's a shame about the graffiti in the gents, in my opinion. I seem to remember it being like this when I last visited too.

I'm not mad keen on Robinsons beers so the guests make this pub worth a visit in my opinion. My Beer Brothers Session IPA was fine.

Ian Mapp left this review about Castle Hotel

Very handsome grade II listed pub that wears its 200 years well. Shabby chic, I'd call it.

Great jukebox, quality Sunday papers and tiled front bar area. Nice locals. Robinsons beers in decent condition.

Worth seeking out for some old school drinking.
