Mike Cooper left this review about The Welcome Inn

This pub has recently reopened and renamed back to the Freemasons. Wonderful refurbishment and a wonderful warm welcome on arrival. Now serving cask ale again with ales coming from wells, robin sons and even Hogarths own brew from its sister pub in Bolton. Trading now as a traditional pub which specialises in live sports. No discos in the establishment as seen all the young trouble move away which has left a more mature clientale.
