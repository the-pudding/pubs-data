Steve C left this review about The Roebuck

The Roebuck is a large sprawling Old English Inns (Greene King) hotel, restaurant and bar. The hotel reception is at the entrance and access to the restaurant is gained through a door on the left. Straight through reception is the Beagle Bar where a limited standard draught selection is available alongside Greene King IPA and Old Speckled Hen. Food is also available in the bar area and the mains come in around £9 or £10 which is very good value as my wife and I really enjoyed our meals. To top it off the Guinness was good and the service very friendly and not in a corporate ‘I have to be polite’ way. The bar is laid out very comfortably and there are a few snug areas along with a pool table that is in a small area at the rear.

There is a car park and large garden and I must say that the building looked very pretty when I visited a couple of weeks ago as it was covered in snow and icicles were hanging above the windows. I half expected to walk in and find Santa having a pint!

This is a hotel so there is not much atmosphere to speak off, but it is ideal for a quick bite and a pint if passing.

On 20th December 2010 - rating: 7
