Alan Winfield left this review about The Tiger

The Tiger is in the middle of Cottingham,when i went in this pub it was very busy with people of all age groups having a drink.
There was one largish room that i noticed with the bar facing as you enter and there was only one real ale on here this was Bass and this was a decent drink.
