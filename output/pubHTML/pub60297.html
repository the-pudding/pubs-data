ROB Camra left this review about The Dram Shop

Newly refurbished and now selling real ale & cider and giving a CAMRA discount.

18/05/2016
This is a really good looking pub on the inside. A great ceiling coloured in green & brown. There's a circular island bar, some interesting statues, a glass porthole window, dark wooden panelling and a good looking fireplace. There's a pool table, lukebox and a quiz machine. Sadly it's all a bit shabby and smelled a bit musty. There's no real ale here so I just had a half of fizz and had a good look around. Shame to see it at such a low ebb.

Richard Ellarby left this review about The Dram Shop

an amazing pub with some great people and staff. the beer prices are reasonable and the surroundings are amazing! Take a look at the one of a kind marvellous celling and the tastefully done decoration. The weekends see great music from an even better DJ, Friday night is Karaoke and Saturday is a random anything goes style music night, great fun for people of all ages. In short the Dram Shop is a must visit pub for everyone!
