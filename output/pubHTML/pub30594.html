Will Larter left this review about The Black Swan

The entrance to this pub is by a series of ramps and steps at the side, leading into a bar area near the back of the pub. We had a quick look at the hand pumps, which were offering Sharps Doom bar, Westons cider and a blonde whose name I neglected to note. The atmosphere was a bit Friday-evening and we didn't much fancy either of the beers just to tick the pub, so gave it a miss. I wouldn't want to put other drinkers off, it just wasn't for us at this time.

Date of visit: 3rd June 2016
