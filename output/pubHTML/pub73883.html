Alan Winfield left this review about Coach & Horses

The Coach and Horses is a decent looking pub in a nice village setting.
The bar was facing as you entered and this area was carpeted and there were two large leather chairs near the bar like the one Ronnie Corbett sat on when telling a story.
There was another seating area round the corner from the entrance.
This is a Donnington tied house and there were two of their beers on the bar i had a drink of Donnington SBA and this was a very nice drink.
This was a locals pub and seemed more of a drinkers pub and we were quite happy having a drink in here.

On 12th May 2011 - rating: 7
