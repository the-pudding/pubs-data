Oggwyn Great left this review about Red Lion Inn

This is a dining pub , no seperate area for drinkers , there is a lovelly old fireplace as the centrepiece , all tables laid out for dining even those in the beer garden . 5 handpulls but only 2 beers GK IPA and Butty Bach which was fine . Would visit for food but not for drinks
