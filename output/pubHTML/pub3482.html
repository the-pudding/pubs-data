Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Cheshire Cheese

Large Greene King roadhouse-style pub, having reverted from its previous Indian restaurant incarnation. Currently looking a bit lost on the southern outskirts of Crewe, but in a potentially prime location with all the building work going on in the area. Semi-modern furniture and light decor in the various seating areas around the irregularly-shaped bar, with value food offer menus on the tables. Pool table and dartboard. Front terrace and beer garden to the rear. Two banks of three handpumps, but just St Edmunds (£3.00, and with a hand-written clip) available, with Landlord having just gone off apparently. Under temporary management.
