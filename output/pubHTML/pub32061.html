Soup Dragon left this review about The Bull Hotel

A terraced hotel, restaurant and bar with a nice courtyard patio area. The Marston's pub part has a Georgian styled facade,which is predominantly in white, with sympathetic windows. The interior has one long room that curves around the serving bar. at one end there is a dining area (top) at the bottom there is a sofa area that has a nice Victorian grate fireplace and china cabinets. The decor is cream, white and red, with a part wood floor. A silent TV was showing something and the jukebox was knocking out mixed music at a decent level. There are a few old photos on the walls, as well as mirrors and there is a forlorn looking piano opposite the bogs. The clientele seemed local and the service was friendly. Beer; usual stuff with Pedigree and a decent enough Cockerhoop. A happy enough stop-off for one.
