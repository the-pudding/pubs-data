Old Boots left this review about The Victoria

Very handily placed just outside Cattal railway station, but sadly with no afternoon opening through the week. A little anchronistic in its decor with red and cream predominating, that's in the carpets and the upholstery but all done in the best possible taste. There are two linked drinking areas on the station side of the building with a common counter, one side in brick, the other in wood. It only has two handpumps, one serving the house beer Balky's Best Bitter brewed by nearby Rudgate and the second was also a special from Rudgate although I believe other guests make an appearance. To each side are dining areas and there's a garden at the rear. Surpisingly busy given its slightly isolated location it is very popular for food.
