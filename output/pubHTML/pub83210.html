Colin Walker left this review about Newton Arms

The Newton Arms has had many names in the past such as - Coach & Horses --- The Newton Inn --- MacKay’s --- The Black Bull. The pub has been opened since its official opening in July 2014. The pub has had a massive facelift on the exterior and interior since it was called Coach & Horses. The pub now has a large beer garden, BT sports and WI-FI. The place has a restaurant feel with its floor area lade out with lots of tables, but there is still seating at the bar. There is parking available across the road and at the pub.
