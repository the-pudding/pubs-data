Will Larter left this review about The Swan on the Square

A smartly refurbished building, slightly unnecessarily renamed with directions, given that there's only one Swan around here, unless you include Huntington. Bare boards and plain walls with a light green painted bar suggests that the emphasis here is on food, but at the time of my Wednesday evening visit there was no one eating. A choice of three beers on the bar (see photo) and a log fire made for a pleasant half hour stay, spoiled slightly by background music aimed at a much younger age group than all those present.

Date of visit: 19th April 2017
