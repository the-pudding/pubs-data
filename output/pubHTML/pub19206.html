ROB Camra left this review about Grants Arms

This is one of those large lager barns that Heywood seems so fond of. There's a pool table to the right at the front of the pub. A DJ stand straight in front of you and a large seating area just beyond it. There's also a large outside patio. No real ale of course and the barman looked at me with complete disdain as I ordered a half of Carling/Fosters or some such garbage. The clientele on my Friday evening visit were mainly blokes in filthy boots & jeans wearing high visibility jackets and a couple of the large tables were taken up with what also looked like post work Friday pint type parties. It's a chavvy mess and I don't think I'll ever visit again.

On 5th July 2011 - rating: 2
