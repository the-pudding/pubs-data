Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Brown Cow

Substantial Ember Inn, featuring a partially opened-out layout with multiple seating / dining areas around the central servery. Usual, semi-modern furniture and inoffensive light decor. Although there are a total of 13 handpumps on different parts of the counter, the choice boiled down to the 'house' Pale Ale, Black Sheep, Bombardier, Leeds Pale (£3.05) and Old Rosie cider.

On 16th February 2019 - rating: 6

custodian 42 left this review about The Brown Cow

Many years back this used to be a multi roomed place with the pool room to the left. This now appears to be a function room and the rest is one large room round a central bar. Ten hand pumps were on offer with a lot from the Leeds brewery. A decent selection of ales means I will definitely revisit this good pub.

On 12th May 2017 - rating: 7
