Reviews of The Butchers Arms (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Nick Davies left this review about The Butchers Arms

Excellent community local in the middle of the village, next to the social club and chippy. The building is nothing special, now knocked through into three-sided arrangement, lounge at one end round to a public bar area and pool table the other, and outdoor seating too. Friendly service and doing a good trade on a weekday afternoon. Long, keenly-priced menu suggests much use of the deep-freeze, but no doubt perfectly good for what it is. Bombardier and Tribute in reasonable shape.

I'd be pleased to have this pub as my local.
