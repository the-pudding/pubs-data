Alan Winfield left this review about Hollybrook Tavern

The Hollybrook Tavern is on the south western edge of Derby and was built to serve the new heatherton estate.
The pub looks quite nice outside with a small garden area at the front and once inside the pub was smart and comfortable,the bar was facing and there was a dining area to the right but this area still looked quite pubby.the drinking area to the left was quite a large area with plenty of seats in different styles.
There were eight pumps on the bar but three of these were repeated so there were five real ales on,i had a drink of Triple F Altons Pale Ale and this was a very nice refreshing drink,the other beers were Cairngorm Trade Winds, Taylor Landlord, GK IPA and Pedigree.
There were a few other drinkers in here on my mid afternoon visit and the dining area was empty,i thought the atmosphere was quite nice and relaxed and i was quite happy having a very nice well kept beer that was also at a decent price.
