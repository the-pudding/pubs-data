Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Swan

Seafront pub and hotel set behind a patio terrace. Three main public rooms along the side of the building: the bar in the centre, a dining room with views of the beach at the front and a lounge (with a dartboard) at the back. Refurbished with semi-modern furniture throughout. Four real ales on handpump: London Pride, two from Gadds - No 3 and No 5 - and Northdown Muggish Porter (£3.90).
