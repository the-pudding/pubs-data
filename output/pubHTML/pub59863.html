Michael Wright left this review about The Bishop On The Bridge

Lunchtime visit, a showery day early September. Like Steve I reckon more could be made of this pub, but as it was filling up when we left,loads of reserved tables, tourist coming in a steady stream. I guess thinking is if it aint broke why fix it?

Had London Pride, which is not my normal Fuller's tipple but no HSB on my visit. The LP was in good condition, so I had another

Main mission was a bite to eat, and we were nicely surprised, the food, or at least what we had, was good, and from what I observed everyone else was enjoying it also, with huge portions being served, so perhaps beer is not the prime reason for this pub? But a Fuller's pub is never going to beat some of the other pubs in Winchester on beer,so go for food & tourists

And it seems to work for them

PS, staff polite and helpful, upstairs loo, but has disabled loo on ground floor
