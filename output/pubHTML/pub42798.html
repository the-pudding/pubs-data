Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Midland Hotel

Recently revamped, both outside and in, and much more modern than I was expecting. The new version probably won't appeal to all, but there was a good mid-week crowd in dining and drinking when I stopped by. The interior may now look a little gastro-pub, but the atmosphere remains a friendly local (and with one of the older regulars talking me through the various local history B&W photographs this proved an interesting visit). Two real ales on handpump - Doom Bar and (thankfully) Leeds Pale (£3.90) - plus a mini-cider festival from a few boxes perched on the counter.
