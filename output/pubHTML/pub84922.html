Reviews of Bar 56 (Average Rating: 3 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about Bar 56

Small, two level fizz bar, surprisingly being run by an Asian couple. Lots of decorative wood around the place including fake barrels. It looks like they've spent quite a bit of money on this place, but have managed to achieve bland. A half of Heineken as a rip off £2. I'll be missing this one out on my next visit to Saltaire.
