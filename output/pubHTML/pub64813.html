Colin Walker left this review about Elliots

Elliots is a former hotel recently converted into a modern bar/restaurant and is situated on the Main Street a few hundred yards south of Prestwick Cross. Depending on your choice, pints start from £2.95p and bottled beers from £2.00p. Food is available all day, with breakfast served from 8.30am - 12, lunch from 12 - 2.30pm and evening meals served from 5pm until 10pm (10.30pm on Fridays and Saturdays) - light bites are served between these times. Quiz night is Tuesday and karaoke nights Thursdays and Sundays. Parking is available at the rear of the building. www.elliots-prestwick.co.uk/
