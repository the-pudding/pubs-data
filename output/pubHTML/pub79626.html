Aqualung . left this review about Hat & Feathers (JD Wetherspoon)

The Hat And Feathers is a conversion of a horrible 1960s built shop with a hideous turquoise signage. Inside it's a roughly square split level room with the bar on the left. On my Thursday morning visit it was doing a good trade of breakfasts. Their traditional breakfast is cheaper here than normal (£2.99 rather than £3.30) and the one I ordered was fine.
The bar has ten hand pumps which with two unused and doubling up boiled down to Ruddles, Doom Bore and three festival ales. I went for the Shepherd Neame Continental IPA (£1.95 festival price) which was a bit warm but OK apart from that.
I thought this one was fairly average.

On 3rd April 2017 - rating: 6
