Oggwyn Great left this review about The Gardeners Arms

Recently re-opened after what appears to be a few years closure , the exterior looks a bit shabby and the garden overgrown but a couple of workmans vans and banging from upstairs and the kitchen area seems like some serious work is going on .
The pub itself has three roooms and is larger than it looks , a traditional bar , a games room and a dining rooom to the rear , three handpulls with Butty Bach , Doombar and an OK Buttcombe Bitter .
It should be pretty nice in here when the work is finished .
