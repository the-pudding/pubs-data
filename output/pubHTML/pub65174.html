Aqualung . left this review about The Picture House (JD Wetherspoon)

This one is clearly a large former cinema but between that and JDW taking it over it was also a bingo hall and a night club.
There's not much inside to show it was a cinema as it's really just a long room that tapers at the rear and has typical fixtures and fittings. I wasn't particularly happy about seeing an A board by the bar advertising BT Sports and Sky Sports which should have no place in any JDW. Happily at the time nothing was showing.
There are ten hand pumps which had the JDW Trio Of Doom doubled, Phoenix Moonshadow, Sharp's Atlantic, Stonehenge Danish Dynamite and Woodforde's Electric IPA. I went for the Danish Dynamite which was in good nick.
Although this was a reasonable Spoons the TV Sport would put me off returning plus the fact that this particular Sutton isn't the easiest place to get to.

On 24th July 2017 - rating: 6
