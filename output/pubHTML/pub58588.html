Alex Conway left this review about The Black Bull Hotel

Aside from the manager of the pub leaving her own 10 star review and completely defeating the object of pubs galore I have left a lower rating than it actually is to balance this out and fairly represent this pub!! (would have got a 7)

This is a fairly standard chain sizzler pub and as Alan mentioned is more family dining orientated. Two real ales were available with Greene King IPA and another I cant remember at 5%. Neither took my fancy so I opted for a Guinness and this was ok but nothing special but good value at a touch over £3.

This is an ok place to have a drink but is not really suited to drinking, I would return for food but not with my friends for a beer.
