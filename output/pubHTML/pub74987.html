Komakino . left this review about The Wheatsheaf

This is a food-oriented establishment located on the main drag through this toothsome Gloucestershire market town. A small bar faces you upon entry from the front, and on my visit proferred a choice of Hook Norton Old Hooky, Butcombe Bitter, Sharps Cornish Coaster and Bath Gem (the latter a reasonable £1.75 for a half). With dining areas set off to the left and right of the bar area, scores of waiting staff rushing here and there, and the bar area itself filled with locals enjoying a post-work Friday evening drink, I had to call upon years of vertical drinking expertise to see off my half in quick time and leave them all to it.

On 3rd April 2016 - rating: 5
