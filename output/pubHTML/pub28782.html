Steve Johnson left this review about The Golden Dog

The Golden Dog is a brilliant pub, serving a variety of real ales & premium lagers.
( The coffee is also lovely, if that's your thing. :) )
The landlords & bar staff are very friendly & welcoming.
Under new management, "The Dog's" large beer garden has gained the addition of new garden furniture, a new childrens activity centre, childs swing seat & new outdoor games, making it much more family friendly.
The bar has also been given a make-over with new furniture in order to bring it up to the high decorative standard of the restaurant.
The food served in the restaurant & bar is of an excellent standard using high quality, locally sourced ingredients which are freshly cooked to order on site. As it is the only restaurant in Shipdham, I'd recommend booking your table in advance to avoid disappointment, as it's very popular place.
