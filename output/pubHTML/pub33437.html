Old Blue left this review about Kings Head

One of Mitchell & Butler’s Sizzling Pubco establishments, a large pub predictably knocked through into one room, with modern decor and a mix of high and low tables and seating . Our recent afternoon visit found it populated largely by families with small children, giving it a sort of Wetherspoons-like feel except cleaner and less economical. However, in the evening it had a very different feel, with a fairly young crowd and a quiz night with a good buzz to it. Disappointing for ale choice, with two banks of three hand pumps only hosting a single Bombardier clip between them, but friendly and efficient service. I wouldn’t mind coming here again for quiz night (Tuesdays and Thursdays) if in the area, but would otherwise go across the car park to the Tilted Barrel.
