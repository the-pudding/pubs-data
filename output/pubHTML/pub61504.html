Real Ale Ray left this review about Sir Henry Royce

We thought that this was a Spoons as it was so similar in style and layout, obviously we were mistaken. This is a large one roomed pub with a long bar situated over on the left hand side. The entrance area had some booths on the right and the walls in this section were wallpapered. Five ales on handpump and one cider, so we went for the Grainstore Brewery Triple B. This pub was ok for a fleeting visit and my beer was reasonably priced.

On 16th July 2016 - rating: 6
