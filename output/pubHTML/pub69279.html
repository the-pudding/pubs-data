Aqualung . left this review about The William Owen (JD Wetherspoon)

William Own was a former mayor of Haverfordwest. The pub named after him is a conversion from a shop and latterly a hotel. The front part of this place consists of several areas with the bar on the left then at the rear there is what looks like a modern extension which is a single open plan area with low tables and chairs that leads to a decent sized patio.
The bar has two sets of five hand pumps which had GK IPA & Abbot doubled, Doom Bore, Glamorgan Cwrw Gorslas, Butcombe Brunel Atlantic IPA, Quantock Plastered Pheasant, Tring Side Pocket For A Toad and Lancaster Black. I went for the Brunel IPA (£2.19) which was in good nick.
This one is GBG 2017 listed and seemed a decent enough Spoons.

David Lands left this review about The William Owen (JD Wetherspoon)

The William Owen offers the usual Wetherspoon's offerings in the centre of town. There are two smaller rooms in addition to the main bar area and a rear courtyard. On my recent visit the pub was a little short of guest beer options to go alongside the usual Greene King beers. Visit if you are in the area and like 'spoons pubs.
