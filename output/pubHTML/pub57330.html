Komakino . left this review about Bell & Barge

A Harvester serving a Premier Inn and doing a good advert for Sharp's, with Atlantic, Doom Bar and Doom Bar Amber the ale options. Restaurant to the right and a cosy bar area to the left with some secluded spots. Nothing to set the world on fire, but food-wise, does what it says on the tin.
