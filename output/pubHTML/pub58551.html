mark collins left this review about The New Inn

The new inn at appletreewick is a fantastic old english public house in the middle of the beautiful yorkshire dales.It boasts so many different continental beers you will lose count at 50-60 i think plus its food menu is fantastic and the views from the pub itself are breathtaking.It is also a b&b and everyone is welcome i.e-dogs-walkers-mountain bikers everyone from all walks of life are welcome at this quaint old english pub.It is well worth the trip out to bask with a fantastic ale and enjoy the company any the views around.
