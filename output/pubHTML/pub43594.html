Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Malt Shovel

Nice old stone pub in picturesque village, with very friendly service. Front bar area with long room to one side and a smaller snug to the other, but all laid up for eating. Beers were Tetleys and Copper Dragon and in good condition (Cask Marque listed). Food seemed to be largely home made and of good quality, although perhaps not exceptional. Large car park area and some outdoor tables to rear.
