Aqualung . left this review about Admiral Napier

This building looks more modern than some of the other Canton pubs. I went in the small public bar at the front which had a few TVs and wood panelled walls. There were a couple of locals in on my early Friday afternoon visit. I didn't get to see the other bar.
There were three hand pumps which had Brains Dark, Bitter and Rev James Gold. I went for the Dark (£2.60) which I had to send back as it was vinegar. It was replaced with the bitter which was in decent nick.
This is very much a locals pub and I can't imagine returning.

The Admiral Napier is an ordinary looking town pub that is situated in cantons main shopping area.
Once inside there were two seperate rooms,i had a drink in the bar,which had a pool table in it,there was a seperate lounge which looked more comfortable.
This pub is a Brains tied house,there was one real ale on the bar,which was Brains bitter,this was a decent drink.
I quite like doing these sort of pubs,this one did'nt dissapoint me.
