Oggwyn Great left this review about The Saddlers

Difficult to find estate pub handy for Shrewsbury Towns new ground if you dont mind a walk across a golf course , Couple of ales on from the nationals which are usually Ok . Friendly enough but be warned a few of Shrewsburys Hooligans tend to hang out here on match days away from the polices prying eyes , apart from that its not to bad
