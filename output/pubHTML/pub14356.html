Komakino . left this review about The George IV

A GK eatery about a half-mile out of Baldock's centre with plenty of Farrow & Ball-esque flourishes. They offer a Sunday Carvery for £8.95 which gets bums on seats, and ale-wise I was limited to GK IPA, OSH and a GK brew called 'Barmy Army' which was an OK, inoffensive light ale.
