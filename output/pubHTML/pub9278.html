Reviews of White Lion Inn (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

E TA left this review about White Lion Inn

A wonderful 17th Century village pub that has been recently refurbished. The pub sits in the middle of the village making it a natural focus for village life. There is a small beer garden hidden away at the back, and a couple of metal tables at the front. There is also a covered smoking area at the side. The single square bar is comfortable, carpeted and homely. There is an open fire one side, and a small TV the other near the dartboard. The staff were very friendly and the atmosphere was convivial. Three ales and a cider on draft – I had a pint of Copper Ale, which was in excellent condition. Food is freshly cooked and good value. Definitely worth a revisit.

On 13th December 2018 - rating: 8
