Quinno _ left this review about The Balfour

A large and recently refurbished place a fair distance from the town centre. Enter to the locals area with pool, darts and TV football. To the left is a knocked-through partition to a larger area with banquette seating and a dining area further beyond that. The ales are in this left hand side, so don’t be put off when you first come in! Six pumps with five on; Sharps Doom Bar and Atlantic, St Austell Tribute, GK Abbot and Dartmoor Jail Ale which was decent but needed more conditioning as it lacked depth and was quite carbonated. Quite lively on a Saturday lunchtime with a couple of oldsters in as well. Solid local. 6.5
