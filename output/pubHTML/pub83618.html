Aqualung . left this review about Slater's Craft Beer Bar

This is a long narrow modern bar where the minimalistic decor is predominately white. There seemed to be a limited range of spirits on a shelf behind the bar and a couple of cider boxes but apart from that all I could see was the ten hand pumps and the ten keg pumps at the back. I didn't venture upstairs if it was even open on a Tuesday afternoon.
One beer was out of action but the other nine were Slater's Premium, Top Totty & Queen Bee, Green Jack Gone Fishing, Black Paw Gala Ale, Milton Forum, Dow Bridge Bonum Mild, Loch Lomond Brave Hop IPA and Maxim Hapi. I went for the Loch Lomond IPA (£3.60) which was in first class nick. Back in March I had updated the CAMRA Discount tab below but did I remember this? Of course not! £3.60 sounds expensive for the area but this was a 6% ABV beer so would have been pretty good value with the discount. Serving cider from a box on the counter is a poor means of dispense and given that one hand pump was unused I can't help thinking it would be better served that way and kept in the cold room / cellar.
Despite being a modern bar I liked this place and would return especially as the Lych Gate is just around the corner.

On 23rd May 2016 - rating: 8
