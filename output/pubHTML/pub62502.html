Colin Walker left this review about The Side Door Inn

The pub has a name change from Side Door Inn to East End Inn. Probably named after the Dunfermline football ground. The place has a large seated bar area and a large seated lounge area. No real ale. Belhaven Best, Tennent’s and Blackthorn selling at £2.80 a pint. BT Sport for viewing. There is a function room available for free hire. The pub participates in the livepubpokerleague.com, east Scotland league.
