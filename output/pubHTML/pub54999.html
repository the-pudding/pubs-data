Olga Mathews left this review about The Running Pump

A traditional country pub and restaurant. Lovely old building, welcoming atmosphere. The Robinsons Unicorn beer was very good. The food is delicious, all freshly cooked to order, and the bar menu would put many restaurants to shame. There is a separate restaurant menu. We enjoyed an exceptionally good Friday lunch & will definitely be returning.
Parking to side. Level access.
