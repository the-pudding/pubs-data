custodian 42 left this review about Crown Tavern

Marstons pub with a large room. Bar to ear as you enter. Surprising selection of ales - Snecklifter, Old Empire, Youngs Special and of course, Pedigree. Worth the walk out from town.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Crown Tavern

Busy Marstons community pub, partly opened out to form 'J'-shaped bar with various seating areas. Also has a few wooden tables out in the garden. Food offers, pool and darts. Four handpumps on the counter, with Burton Bitter, Pedigree, Old Empire and Czech mate (£2.74).
