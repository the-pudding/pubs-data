BobOs . left this review about The Beachcomber

OK, so it's actually a bar in the Warren Farm Holiday & Touring Centre, but if you're on the beach for the day it's a handy place to pop into. Nothing special but it saves an otherwise long trek to the nearest pubs at Uphill. On the whole below average - certainly not the stupid 'perfect pub' 10/10 of the previous reviewer's sole review.

Richard Arwkrite left this review about The Beachcomber

Chalet style pub at the front of a caravan park. There is a carpark opposite and everything is on the level so its very good for wheelchair users.
The beach is hidden by the sand dunes.
The interior has a bar/cafateria type severy and huge club room. Beer and lager in kegs. Very busy during the holiday season with families and can be quite noisey.Then again they are on holiday.
