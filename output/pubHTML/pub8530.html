David Lands left this review about Dartmoor Union Inn

The Dartmoor Union has been extensively modernised recently (I haven't been there for more than two decades!). It is now polished floorboards and a mixture of seating, with a dinaing area to the rear. The lacvk of soft furnishing makes it quite noisy (from conversation) when busy. Two handpumped beers, both from St Austell. The food is of a modern type, presented on breadboards, but reasonably priced. The fish & chips were excellent.

On 6th July 2014 - rating: 6
