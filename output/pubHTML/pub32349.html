Alan Winfield left this review about The Crown & Cushion

The Crown and Cushion is set back from Biddulphs high street.
Once inside the pub has two seperate rooms,the front room is L shaped,part carpeted and with a stone floor,there is comfy bench seating around the edge of the room, there is a pool table to the front right,there was a TV on showing the Aston Villa v Newcastle Utd game,there is a seperate room to the rear which is bare boarded and had bench seating.
There were no real ales on the bar,the keg option was the horrible Boddingtons cream crap which tasted horrible.
This is a decent enough pub that is let down badly by the lack of one real ale on the bar.

Pub visited 23/8/2014
