Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Inn on the Beach

On a good day (which it was when I visited), this food-led pub occupies the prime position suggested by its name. However, being at the west end of the Hayling Island seafront, it probably not overwhelmed during the summer months but was still doing good business very early in the season. The main bar and dining areas are on the first floor but another bar can be found at beach level, at there is a split-level terrace in front of both right by the sea. Three real ales on handpump: a 'house' Best Bitter plus Frigate and Invincible from Irving.
