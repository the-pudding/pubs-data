Reopened after the floods. Wasn't as badly hit as many places. Cash only at the moment though.

More of a cafe than a bar although they do have one pump dispensing a draught lager. On Friday it was full of Hebden's "Yummy Mummies" drinking strange smelling cups of tea. Not a place for someone who likes pubs, but it does seem popular.
