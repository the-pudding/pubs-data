Al Bundy left this review about The Culcheth Arms

A large modern pub. There are a few areas to sit with a function room available. Its a pleasant enough place but doesn't really have any character to it. There is a pool table to the right rear. 5 handpumps on the bar but sadly they are a fairly unadventurous set of ales with Dizzy Blonde, Theakstons Old Peculiar, Bombardier and an end of barrel Xmas beer by Hardy Hanson.
