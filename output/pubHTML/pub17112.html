ROB Camra left this review about Hare & Hounds

A old looking pub on the outside, but totally modern inside. It's one main room with a smaller alcove with a pool table that obviously used to be a separate room. There's a big screen, but this was rolled up on my Friday afternoon visit. There's also a karaoke rig in one corner. Two handpumps on the bar, but only one beer available, Reedley Hallows Beer O'clock which was in good nick. It's a pleasant enough pub, but any character it may have had has been removed during the modernisation.

On 25th September 2018 - rating: 6
