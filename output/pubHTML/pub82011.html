Reviews of The Tavern on the Bay (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Ian Mapp left this review about The Tavern on the Bay

Superb location on the coast path, with extensive views and outside decking to make the most of those views. Inside, a chainy, Wetherspoons kind of feel. Mainly setup for dining and the menu did look superb, but we only stopped for a drink. Two real ales on and I was allowed a sample before choosing on California by Conwy Brewery.

Really can't beat the location!

On 10th September 2018 - rating: 8
