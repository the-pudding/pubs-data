Real Ale Ray left this review about The New Inn

A typical local's pub serving keg only. The barmaid was friendly and pleasant and told us they didn't have much call for cask ales. The pub had three areas, a small room on the left of entrance and the main bar on the right with seating along the front windows. There was also a small pool room at the rear. The pub was well kept and had a fabulous collection of various cobs on the bar counter, piled up on a plate, priced £2 each. The locals on our Saturday lunchtime visit were harmless enough. Nice to see Spoons hadn't ferried them away, as there ain't one in Pershore yet.
