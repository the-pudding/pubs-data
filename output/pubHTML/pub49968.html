Alan Kuhlwilm left this review about The Antonine Arms

Great wee pub. Great beer. Great food. A lot of time and effort has been spent on this pub over the year, making it one of the best, friendliest village pubs I have ever frequented. Nothing is to much of a problem for Andy and Kathy (your hosts). Ask for something and they will do their best to get it for you, whether it be beer or food. Andy and Kathy are wonderful, friendly hosts who are working hard (along with the rest of their team) to turn The Antonine Arms into a wonderful pub/eating place.
