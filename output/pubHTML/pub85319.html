Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Breeze

New-looking bar / restaurant in the Island Harbour marina on the east bank of the Medina between Newport and Cowes. Looks very inconspicuous from a distance as it is a single-storey structure; in fact, you see the umbrellas on the front decking terrace first and then a gazebo and a plethora of beer garden benches. The main bar area was full of diners on a sunny Saturday evening, but the stools by the counter were in use by those just having a drink, as were groups of people outside. Doom Bar available on handpump.
