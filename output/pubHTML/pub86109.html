Reviews of The Bitter End (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about The Bitter End

apparently this used to be an off-license by the same name - but it's now a microbar. It promised a lot too, with the gravity dispense real ales and box cider on the bar. I was a little disappointed though. I was hoping for some interesting local brews but the real ales were all standards that I have tasted before - London Pride, Jaipur etc. - rather odd for a place like this and expensive at £5 a pint. Craft keg also available. to be honest the cider is probably the most interesting option here if the beer choice is always like this. The bar was quiet and entirely old/male so the discussions about 'microbars replacing the old pubs' felt a bit off the mark. The plain pub next door was packed with a mixed clientele, but make of that what you will.

On 31st January 2019 - rating: 5
