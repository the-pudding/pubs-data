Graham Coombs left this review about The Two Rivers

Modern Marstons Inn in typical house style aimed squarely at food trade on hill at edge of town. Interior pleasant without being exceptional; at least it manages to avoid the recycled brick and distressed timber so often found in these. Beers from the Marstons stable - Pedigree and a Hobgoblin at time of visit and condition seemed fair. Food of usual roast and grill type was adequate but left room for improvement. Outside is a secure garden area and accommodation is in separate block adjacent. OK if you want a family dining pub with good parking.

On 15th August 2016 - rating: 6
