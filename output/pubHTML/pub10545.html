Tris C left this review about The Railway Bell

This pub is situated almost at the mouth of the railway station's entrance and receives a glowing review on WhatPub?.
Given the location, the pub holds no surprises but being Brighton, is friendlier than most railway transients' pubs. It's carpeted, has games machines and is big on TV sports with multiple screens dotted about the place, generating much noise.
Ales: Harvey's Sussex Best at £2.20 a half which is a bit steep though at least it was in good condition.
This place will appeal to the sports' enthusiast but has retained little for the pub traditionalist and given the location, there are plenty of local alternatives.
