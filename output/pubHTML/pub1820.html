Steve of N21 left this review about The Crown Inn

Country pub tucked away in the back lanes of Farnham Royal just north of Slough.
Fairly spacious inside, having two areas either side of the central serving area which have a traditional decor and open fires. Then there is a function room beyond the right hand area which houses the monthly music nights held in the pub.
It advertises itself as a pub and restaurant, so obviously focuses on food as well as beer. But as we were here for our firms Christmas drink up I cannot comment on the food side.
But there are three real ale hand pumps on the bar which were dispensing seasonal Xmas brews from smaller breweries. I can’t remember what these were now except that when they ran out the barrel was changed efficiently and one of the ones that went on was Batemans Rosey Nosey.
Service was good and the beer was well kept.
It also benefits from a large garden out the back so can imagine it’s a very pleasant place for a pint in summer. Wouldn’t hesitate to visit again when back in the area.
