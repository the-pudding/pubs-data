Nick Davies left this review about The Three Horseshoes

Pleasant enough stop on the road between Southam and Coventry. It's a three hundred year old building much altered over the years with several distinct drinking and dining areas and a pleasant garden with views over the surrounding countryside. A friendly welcome and worth calling in if you're passing this way. Standard beer range, Adnams, Bombardier, Pedigree when we called.

On 2nd April 2012 - rating: 6
