Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Steve C left this review about The Fulwood

I had a few pints and a bit of lunch in here on Friday after spotting it out of the corner of my eye down a walkway. It's a strange place that feels like it should be on a lay-by off a main Spanish road. The food was pretty standard with my burger being a little iffy, but the misses loved her toasted ham and tomato sarnie.

There's not much of a drinks selection as the only pints available are Carling, Grolsch and Guinness. When the nearby offices started to empty out I realised why there wasn't a large draught selection as all and sundry were drinking white wine. This place was formerly called Grayz Inn Wine Bar so perhaps the clientele hasn't changed from then.

I got the feeling that this place livens up in the evening and turns into a disco type venue as there is not much seating and plenty of standing room.

I prefer a more traditional pub, but I suppose this place is ok for a change.

On 30th July 2008 - rating: 5
