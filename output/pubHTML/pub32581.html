Soup Dragon left this review about The Swan Inn

A 'V' shaped corner pub in white render, with black paintwork.

The interior has two rooms; bar and lounge. The lounge is in white, with lots of old photos and brass ephemera and has a large central chimny feature and wood bar. The service was very friendly, especially as twenty odd people suddenly descended on the pub. There was no other clientele until we arrived - which to be fair, was only 12pm.

Beer; tap stuff, with 7 real ale handpulls; all the beers were well kept and i had or tasted all of them. Keltek 6 Nations, Bass (exception), Moles Brewery's Molecatcher, Oakham Inferno, Cotleigh's Charity, Abbeydale Deception and Brecon Brewery's Sin Bin, which was a little weak. The Molecathcer was a decent drop, as was the Oakham.

A great place to start our ale coach trip, a must for any visit, especially with the Plough around the corner
