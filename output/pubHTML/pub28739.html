Real Ale Ray left this review about York Tavern

This late Victorian back street corner pub is well worth a visit. Staff are friendly, customers are mixed and seemed like a decent bunch. Four ales on handpump, we went for the Woodfordes Sundew which was a right quaffable ale. Will call again

Alan Winfield left this review about York Tavern

The York Tavern is a back street corner pub.
I really liked this pub it had bare boards and there was a longish bar with five real ales on it i had a drink of Woodfordes Wherry and this was very nice the other beers were Sharpes Doom Bar,Adnams Broadside,York tavern Mandolin and a beer called Golden Bitter.
I thought this was a really nice pub and i would happily spend an afternoon in here.
