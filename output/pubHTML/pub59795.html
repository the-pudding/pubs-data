Daniel Adshead left this review about The Old Beams Inn

A beautifully restored Thatched Pub offering excellent traditional home cooked food at affordable prices. Outside there is a fantasic raised decking area to the rear with beautiful gardens surrounding. I was very impressed with the friendly service by all the staff and Management. A Fantastic atmoshere...perfect for all the family. Well worth a visit!!
