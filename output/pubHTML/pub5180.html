Graham Coombs left this review about The Victory

Roadside pub set up on the hills between Porthtowan and St Agnes with a holiday area sort of feel to it. Very much a lived-in, slightly tatty, friendly atmosphere to everything from the car park onwards. Inside there is a smallish bar counter set in a clutch of connected areas, including a conservatory, all liberally sprinkled with nautical and seaside knick-knacks. Draught ales are Betty Stogs, Tribute and Lushingtons with guests. Worth stopping off at.

Gill Smith left this review about The Victory

Called in this 2011 Good Beer Guide pub, and made it as the kitchen was closing, but they were really good and let us have a meal even though the chef had tidied up in the kitchen. It was a good food menu with plenty to choose from and very tasty too. Chose the Skinners Cornish Knocker to drink with the meal and it was good. The pub is situated on the coastal 547 bus route that runs between St Ives and Newquay, just up the hill from Porthtowan.

On 24th July 2011 - rating: 7
