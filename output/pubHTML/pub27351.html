Tom Fairburn left this review about The Ash Tree

I popped in the ASH TREE the other day to watch a football game, on their large "fuzzy" TV screen! The place was quite busy for mid-week, there must have been 50 people in the pub (mainly men, watching the footy!) - BUT, there was only ONE very young person serving behind the bar (...doing a very good job, in the circumstances...but you need MORE than one person serving when it's that busy)! Management to note. Actually, the manager was there...on the other side of the bar drinking+chatting with locals. Didn't seem to notice any particular problem. I waited about 7 minutes for my first pint, okay I was being entertained by the fuzzy football game, but another time or place and I would have walked out. When it came to a second pint...I couldn't wait any longer than 5 minutes...! I went to another pub, not far away (Kings Head) and got served instantly. There's a lesson there somewhere. But I don't expect the manager of the ASH TREE will spot it. Unless they've heard of "Pubsgalore.co.uk" :o) I'm down-rating to 3/10
