phil coates left this review about The Jolly Bowman

Nice friendly pub with reasonable food and drinks prices, nice beer garden at rear, but its probably going to be next summer before its warm enough to use now!, worth a call in for a pint and a bite.
(Note. Pub is near big college and gets quite busy at lunch during term time with hoards of would be hairdressers and artists talking rubbish and sharing half a cola and bowl of cheesy chips between 15 of them!!)
