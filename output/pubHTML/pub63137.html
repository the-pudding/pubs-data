Alan Winfield left this review about Wellington Inn

The Wellington Inn is a decent looking pub that is set sideways onto the road,the pub is situated on Eastwoods main shopping street.
Once inside there are a few different rooms and i have found this to be a nice pub to have a drink in,the pub used to be a Kimberley tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Kimberley bitter and this was a decent drink the other beer was Kimberley mild.
This pub is more than likely a GK house now.
