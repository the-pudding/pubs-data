Rob Hunter left this review about Library Bar (Glyde House)

Visited on a weekday afternoon earlier in the month. Quiet at the time of my visit but the chap serving, who I assume to be the manager, was friendly and welcoming. Of the four pumps, three were on and the fourth was due to be Two Hoots. The chap behind the bar recommended the Bad Kitty, which was a very pleasant porter, priced at £1.90 for a half. The other two were pales if I remember rightly. A bit of a hidden gem within the Gumption Business Centre and well worth a visit. I'd probably score a 7.5 but as half points are not an option I'll be generous and give it an 8. I think opening times are restricted to that of the business centre, so it's going to need a daytime visit to try this place out. In front of the bar is a small seating area with a few tables, there is a snug room off this room which is larger and has sofas etc, but the door was closed so not sure if that was open at the time of my visit.

John Yeadon left this review about Glyde House

Glyde House is a cafe, bar (The Library Bar) and function room for parties, receptions and events. The building also offers office space and office services as the Gumption Centre. I went inside to check out the bar and saw the rooms were well populated and business thriving.

See: The Library Bar http://www.glydehouse.co.uk/?page_id=10844

Glyde House is a grade II listed building: http://www.britishlistedbuildings.co.uk/en-336750-glyde-house-
