Al Bundy left this review about The Augustus John

As long as you can tolerate students this is a decent pub. 5 ever changing handpumps plus a couple more for cider. The place is just one room and up until a couple of years ago there was a pool table but this has been removed. Can sometimes be busy at the end of lectures but is usually quite quiet. Its not usually open at weekends. A welcome place for an after work drink at least.

Old Boots left this review about The Augustus John

Situated at the edge of the Liverpool University campus this insipid modern building has the atmosphere of a students union bar although it is not just frequented by students. Comprising a long corridor like room with a conservatory at one end housing a pool table, games machines and some screens for the sports channels. Decorated in red and cream, seating is low booths of bench seats down the window side of the room and there is an alcove area opposite. The bar is in one corner by the conservatory, as is a food servery. Five handpulls, although when I visited the Abbott had just gone and the remaining choice was Tetley Cask or Spitting Feathers. Tasting notes for the cask beers are helpfully posted on a notice board next to the bar. The beer was well kept and in good condition but neither brewery is on my must have list. The bar is a regular in the Good Beer Guide and appears there again this year (2010).
