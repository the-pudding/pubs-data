Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Plough

Basic boozer located just a bit of a walk out from the station and town centre. Fairly limited furniture and decor restricted to some football memorabilia. A couple of benches can also be found out back. On the plus side, I got a friendly welcome despite arriving some 15 before what turned out to be their normal opening time, and the exterior signage made me laugh. Butcombe Bitter and Doom Bar (£3.20) available from two of three handpumps.

On 27th December 2015 - rating: 6
