moby duck left this review about The Jolly Farmer

A decent country pub, a Fullers pub with only there beers on, four pumps with Pride doubled up,H.S.B. and Olivers Island, the conditioning of which was excellent.Multi roomed/partitioned inside with a mixture of stone tiled and wood flooring,food plays a major part of this pubs set up as you would expect of a country pub nowadays and there is a purpose built barbecue kitchen/servery with a wood tiled roof in the middle of the extensive beer garden. All in all not a bad place.

On 11th July 2015 - rating: 7

I just love this lovely country pub. The food is not cheap, but by god it is good. Lovely atmosphere, perfect in both summer (great grounds for outdoor drinking and eating) and winter (lovely and cosy with an open fire to warm you). Highly recommended!
