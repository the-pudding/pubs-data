Roy Collins left this review about The Holyhead

Pleasant enough modern interior, the usual Ember Inns thing. Say they serve, 4 real ales but often only 2 or more available. However they do not take the empty pump clips off and the staff say they are being changed, but this never happens .Quality of the beer is variable but never great.Silent big screen TV showing 24 hour news end of the room - just what you need !

On 30th June 2011 - rating: 4
