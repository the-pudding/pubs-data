Bucking Fastard left this review about The Babington Arms (JD Wetherspoon)

A very standard looking 'spoons,oblong shop conversion with little of note inside apart from a very good selection of real ale from array of handpumps with only minimal incursion of the dreaded 'spoons regular ales.There is a TV screen listing the options (Birmingham Wellingtonesque) and my Titan Gingerbread Brown was well kept and interesting.As this was my penultimate crawl stop,I failed to note the full guest list,but I just remember being quite impressed.Worth popping in to see whats on offer.

On 17th November 2015 - rating: 7
