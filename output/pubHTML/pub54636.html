Thuck Phat left this review about The Royal Oak

This has very much the feeling of a modern bar and eating establishment than a traditional boozer. Despite it's large airy feel and the early evening time of visit it had a gentle buzz and good atmosphere. Beer, as you might expect from a TT house, was excellent and the full range is offered. A great place for a top quality pint of Landlord.

UPDATE - April 2011

Little changed except the time of visit. Busy on a weekday lunchtime with drinkers and diners which seemed to make the staff immensely grumpy.
Landlord still in decent condition.
