ROB Camra left this review about The White Hart

This is a big pub with a very large main room. There's also a smaller room to the rear with a quiz machine and a pool table. There's a stage in the main area for live music. It was getting to the end of a long day, so my notes are a bit sparse. Sadly there's no real ale, so I had a quick half of fizz and moved on.
