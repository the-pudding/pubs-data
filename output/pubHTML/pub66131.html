Helen Iwanczuk left this review about Angel Inn

The Angel Inn is a community pub in every sense of the word. When the pub was in danger of closing, a group of locals clubbed together to buy the pub, that in itself is a testement to the essence of The Angel, and to community spirit in general The furnishings are simple, but the interior is adorned with musical instruments and posters of music events from a bygone era. The staff are friendly and helpful, which is also a bonus. The Angel also has the distinction of being situated a stones throw away from Grosmont Castle, a very picturesque ruin. Pubs like these, are in danger of being lost forever without the support of the general public.
