Al Bundy left this review about The Rake & Pikel

A nice enough roadside pub sort of in the middle of nowhere. A two roomed pub with a bar in each room. The bar to the right is the lounge and is L-shaped with an outside covered area and a nice beer garden to the side. 3 handpulls on the bar but sadly only Doom Bore on. A dart board is in the room to the left. 3 handpulls also on this bar but unused. A nice, if dull, pub.
