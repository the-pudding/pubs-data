Komakino . left this review about Thirteen High Street

A bar aimed at the under-30s which had six keg taps with a couple of crafts from Portland, Peroni, Appleshed Cider and a couple of lagers from Dortmunder (offers on "fishbowls" and "4 shots for £10" give you an idea of the demographic). It's not the worst space in the world, and being the only customer on a Sunday afternoon gave me a chance to look at it at my own leisure, but having seen a video or two on their Facebook page, give it a miss on a Friday or Saturday night.

On 28th May 2017 - rating: 2
