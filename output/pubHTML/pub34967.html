Graham Hopcroft left this review about The Case Is Altered

Graham & Mags welcome you to our Village pub.We have now opened the pub all day (from mid-day) A good pub grub type menu and real ales in beautiful surroundings. There is now the added bonus of a pool table!!!
Its child friendly and has a large enclosed garden. There are various walks that start from the pub so why not pop in for a drink and something to eat.
