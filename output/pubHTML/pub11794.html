Quinno _ left this review about The White Hart

Home of the Hart of Stebbing microbrewery, this is a C15th whitewashed timbered inn. Plenty of character, within, horse brasses, numerous framed and mounted cigarette card collections. A ‘GR’ era postbox frontage is mounted on one of the ceiling supports. Quality of the homebrew was a bit hit-and-miss. The rest of my notes have disappeared (if I ever actually wrote them, I rather overdid it at the previous stop).
