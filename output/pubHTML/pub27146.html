Andrew Bryers left this review about Colliers Arms

Run by husband & wife Les & Sue, the Colliers is a local favourite. Good beer, with always at least 3 guest beers to choose from. A warm welcome is always on offer & the food is brill. It gets very busy at the weekend so get there early if you want to eat, or, if you prefer, settle back & have a beer & wait for a table, the wait will be worth it. The food is all good home fayre, the large cod is a real treat. The menu is extensive & there is always a good range of specials to tempt you. A hidden gem, in a small village, deserving of it's good local reputation.

On 26th April 2009 - rating: 10
