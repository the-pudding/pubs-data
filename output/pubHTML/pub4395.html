Gill Smith left this review about Masons Arms

Dating from before Napoleonic times, this pub claims to be the oldest licensed premises in town. The lively and welcoming slate-flagged public bar provides pub games - darts and dominoes are played - whilst regular entertainment includes a quiz night on Tuesdays, and a folk night Fridays when the local folk club meet. The recently-extended lounge is quieter and preferred by locals seeking a more relaxed pint, whilst a function room is also available. There are always one or two interesting guest ales served in this friendly pub, with the emphasis on local breweries. The pub is near the Camel Trail. We enjoyed the Tintagel and Cottage beers and the friendliness of the landlord in here.

On 20th July 2014 - rating: 9
