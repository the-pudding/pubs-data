Mark Warburton left this review about The Red House

Great Village Pub and Restaurant Rugby was on the TV in the Bar and I was made to feel welcome. Its a Dog friendly Pub too. When I visited there was a choice of 4 real ales of UBU, Hop to IT, Abbot Ale and Strongarm also the usual carling/strongbow/somersby with a great selection of wines and Spirits. Would love to live a bit nearer as really is a proper local.Big car park at the back.Really worth a visit as others have told me great food too but I was only there for a few pints waiting for a party to begin at the local village Hall

On 20th March 2016 - rating: 9
