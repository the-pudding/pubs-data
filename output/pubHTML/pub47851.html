Colin Walker left this review about Smiths Bar & Restaurant

There are always plenty of food and drinks offers available. For food, every time you spend £10 on food you will be entitled to a scan. When you reach 10 scans you will be rewarded with a £15 voucher to spend on food any other time you visit So If you spend £35 on food on 1 visit you will be rewarded with 3 scans. Early Evening Menu, two courses for £9.99 (starter & main course or main course & dessert) Mon-Fri 4pm onwards. For drinks, Pint Carling £2.79, Bottle of Kronenbourg £1.49, Glens Vodka & dash £1.49 25ml &Jack Daniels & Dash £2.59 to name just a few. Plus lost of special events such as Halloween & Christmas party nights. Check http://www.smiths-bar.co.uk/
