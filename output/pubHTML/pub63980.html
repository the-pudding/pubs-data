Robert Gibson left this review about Ship Inn

Village pub in area of outstanding natural beauty. Mawgan born and bred landlord Lynton Williams, with partner Marta, seek to put the pub right back where it should be - at the heart of the village. A warm welcome for all. Wholesome, home-cooked food. Good local beers (in CAMRA good pub guide 2009). Garden. Dogs welcome.
