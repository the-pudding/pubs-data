Gill Smith left this review about The Thirsty Scholar

Called in on 24th May as they were holding a beer festival along with Number 20 down the street. On entering the bar area is on the right, and there is seating on different levels. Going down stairs they had the stillage area and a an outside seating area down more stairs from there. There was a good selection of ales and we enjoyed Padstow Pride and Skinners River Cottage.

On 1st June 2014 - rating: 7
