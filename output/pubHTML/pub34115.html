Alan Winfield left this review about The Queens Hotel

The Queens is a stand alone bare brick built pub that is situated in a residential area.
Once inside there was a small room to the right,this room was carpeted and had bench seating and normal tables and chairs,the room to the left was similar to the right hand room,there was a larger room to the rear which was bare boarded,there was a pool table and darts board plus a DJ rig to the rear left.
There were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of John Smiths Extra Smooth crap,which it was,the other keg bitter was John Smiths Extra Cold crap.
This is a pretty basic pub of the type that are dissapearing fast.

Pub visited 7/9/2013
