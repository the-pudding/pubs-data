paulof horsham left this review about The Old Bike Store

2 cask, but 8 keg micropub. Money has clearly been spent on the tiled back bar & floor, the room stretching a fair way back, but all this space was attracting just a couple of us on a Saturday lunchtime. The other punter was a curious local, so maybe there's hope for larger crowds in the future. As it was, the Langham ale was discounted to shift when I was in.

There's no shortage of micropubs in Worthing and its suburbs, but this one's worth a look-in if you're passing by.

On 2nd February 2019 - rating: 6
