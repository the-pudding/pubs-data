Steve C left this review about The Pipeline

This place is a good sized rock themed bar with a couple of pool tables, three pinball machines and a couple of projector screens that were showing rock concert videos. The music was very loud, but there was a very friendly atmosphere and this was one of the busier establishments on my weekday evening visit. The barman was on form and he apologised for giving me a short looking half Guinness in an oversized European lager glass. There are three hand pumps with one of them being unused and the other two drawing Spitfire and Landlord.

This is a comfortable theme bar and the Guinness was as good as the welcome. If I was a big fan of rock music I would have given a higher rating.
