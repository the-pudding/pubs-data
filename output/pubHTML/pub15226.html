Graham Coombs left this review about Kings House

The old Victorian building belies the interior, which is a thoroughly-modernised café bar. The front room is more of a café, with a cosier lounge area behind, and then opening out into a sun room and terrace with superb views across the sea front and bay below. Beers included Gales Seafarers and London Pride.
