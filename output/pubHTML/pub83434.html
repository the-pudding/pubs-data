philip george left this review about Lyme Regis Brewery Tap

The Brewery Tap can be found at The Town Mill in Lyme Regis, a short walk from the main street and the seafront There is a microbrewery, a bar and an off licence. The pub 'room' is the beautiful medieval courtyard of The Town Mill. As you can see from the photo, it is uncovered, so it is a far-weather only venue. Lyme Regis Brewery brew a range of six regular beers and a range of seasonal specials. There are always 3-5 cask ales available on handpump. I enjoyed the beers - all good quality and well-kept. The flight of three x third-pints is a good way of starting out. Highly recommended.

On 24th July 2016 - rating: 9
