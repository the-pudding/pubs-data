Soup Dragon left this review about The Kingswinford

This is a modern, pretty featureless Beefeater restuaruant, which is a part of a complex with a Premier Inn. In two-tome brick (with the added excitement of an upended blue brick string course) there is a conservatory area, a patio and a beer garden area. Generally a long room for dining, but there are a couple of tables where you could feel that you didn't have to eat. Green and brown seemed to feature on the walls around where i stood and a few landscape pictures were on the walls, as well as some old photos. The TV had the news on and the background music was mixed and at a decent level. The service was fine and the people i could see were all eating. Beer; usual tap stuff with St Austell Tribute and a flatish Ringwood Boondoggle on handpull. Not really a place you go just for a drink.
