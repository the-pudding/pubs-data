Steve C left this review about Farm Tavern

The Farm Tavern is a little Greene King tied house that I visited last Tuesday evening when there were a handful of locals in attendance. There is a small bar area to the left and a longer seating area to the right. The bar supports three hand pumps that were drawing Olde Trip, St Edmunds and Hardy and Hanson’s bitter. There is also a limited draught selection and I didn’t see any food advertised other than the Sunday roasts that are available from midday until 19:00. The opening times are 16:00 until late during the week and noon until late at the weekends.

I found the woman on duty to be very friendly, as were the regulars. The actual pub isn’t really all that to be honest, but I would return for the atmosphere.

On 7th March 2012 - rating: 7
