Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The George & Dragon

The better of the two open pubs in Headcorn, housed in a substatial mock-Tudor building. Interesting old (but very faded) pub sign. Nicely decorated bar, with all sorts of bits and pieces about the place. Mostly set out for dining, with a reasonable looking menu featuring several local products. Two real ales on handpump, usually offering something from Shepherd Neame plus a beer from further afield (e.g. Batemans All Seasons at £3.50).
