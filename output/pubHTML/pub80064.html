Komakino . left this review about Prego

An Italian restaurant which has a standalone bar with Peroni the only beer on draught (and £5.10 a pint for the privilege). There is a "Wine, Beer & Spirits" menu which suggests they have Purity Mad Goose and Longhorn IPA available in bottles, but I missed that after ordering a Birra Moretti for £3.95 (the bottled ales are the same price). The welcome was warm, and, as mentioned, there's no truck with liquid-only diets, but quite 'why' you'd want to do just that escapes me...

On 28th May 2017 - rating: 2
