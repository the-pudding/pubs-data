Komakino . left this review about The Red Lion

Now reopened as a tea room at the end of 2017 albeit with the facade and external signage remaining the same as before and limited opening hours (9-4 Tues-Sat, 10-3 Sun, closed Mondays). This has two Rebellion beers served via keg taps - IPA and Lager - the IPA coming in at £1.75 for a half.
