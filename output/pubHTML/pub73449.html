Soup Dragon left this review about The Water Hole

A terraced bar and nightclub with a patio area at the front, under a canopy supported by columns. The building looks 60s with its curved white upper front, with scalloping effect. The lower facade has large windows flanking a central entrance.

The interior has an open plan room that is in a coffee and yellow decor, with some white. There is a two level ceiling, high at the front of the room, lower at the back where there is a pool table. There are mirrors and a few pictures of people on the walls. There are strange flower petal fans and sofas and high stools to sit on. There are several big TVs and the pumps have little TVs in them, showing some music channel videos - the level was OK though. The was fine and there were a few people in. They do food, but i didn't eat so can't comment on that.

Beer; usual tap stuff with Hobgoblin and and OK Bateman's XB on handpull

A bar for those that like that type of thing, but it does food and has real ale, as well as pool. I didn't expect much considering my personal tastes, but left happier than i expected.
