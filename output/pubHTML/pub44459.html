Old Boots left this review about Kings Head

Two room pub with pool room at back, there's a small public to left which requires a sharp swerve to the right to find a small counter that is home to four pumps with a mix of Marstons, and I think as guests Robinsons & Shepherd Neame. I had the Shepherd Neame Whitstable Pale Ale rather than the Spitfire and it was very good. The games room is behind here with pool, darts and a couple of machines, there are a few screens dotted about as well. Decent if unexceptional decor with wheel back chairs, sewing machine tables and with rough plastered walls painted white, carpet or modern tiles throughout. There's a nice smoking shelter in the rear garden. It appears to have been two different buildings at one time and there's no easy access between the bars so I didn't explore the right hand side.

Oggwyn Great left this review about Kings Head

Right at the end of the main street , a multi roomed pub with beer garden , the first room you enter has a bar to the right and a corridor leading to another smaller bar and the beer garden , to the left is a slightly plusher area with a TV showing sport , there is a pool room towards the back of the pub which is smaller thab it looks from the outside , the staff and customers seem friendly enough , this is the best selection of beer in town ,Marstons Bitter , Pedigree , Wye Valley HPA , Bankss Bitter , Sharps Doombar , i had HPA which was OK , its a bit run down and tatty but as a local it would be fine
