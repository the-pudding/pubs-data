Graham Coombs left this review about The Church House Inn

Fairly boring road frontage belies a splendid unmodernised stonebuilt inn. Two bars - the public has stone floors and mismatched wooden tables and seems popular with locals and walkers, with a more comfortable lounge bar adjacent. Good range of local ales as usual for area. Nice outdoor area to rear. Recommended.
