peter ashworth left this review about Potting Shed / Firepit

The Firepit is situated directly below the Potting Shed and is one large room with lots of high tables and chairs,large TV screens and numerous beer taps. The place can be entered either from the steps down from the Potting Shed or one of two entrances on the street below. Word of warning if you enter from the street on the right hand smaller entrance be careful of the step half way down it cost me 4 hours in Royal Blackburn hospital yesterday.

peter ashworth left this review about Potting Shed / Firepit

After a 5 month delay the Potting Shed has finally opened last week and is certainly a large place that towers over the Wetherspoons next door.
On the ground floor is the large bar plus a miscellany of gardening utensils in true Botanist and Potting Shed style,and on the upper floor is a bar with a rooftop garden. Downstairs in the basement is The Firepit which is a smokehouse and Sports bar type of place.
Called in around 2 30pm om a Wednesday and was quite impressed with the conversion although i did not go in the Firepit or the upper floor. There was 3 isolated cask beer pumps all about 6 ft apart and i think only 2 of these were in operation.
