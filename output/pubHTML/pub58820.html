Al Bundy left this review about Hickory's Smokehouse

Where did it go all wrong? Formerly The Moby Dick now a Hickory Smokehouse place. Overrun with kids and unless you're eating in here don't expect to sit down for long before a member of staff tells you to move because the table is needed. Has 2 real ales if you don't mind being shifted every 10 minutes. It seems to be popular but if you just want a drink I honestly just wouldnt bother. The Moby Dick wasnt great but this... Oh dear. Its crap.

On 17th May 2015 - rating: 1
