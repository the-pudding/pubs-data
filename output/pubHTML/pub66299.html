Will Larter left this review about The Hostel

An interesting building constructed in 1903, according to the inscription above the door. The lower part is stone, with an added half-timber upper storey, loosely based on Tudor town houses. I was unable to inspect the inside, unfortunately, as a note attached to the door explained that they had closed early that day "due to unforeseen circumstances". A return visit is being planned.
