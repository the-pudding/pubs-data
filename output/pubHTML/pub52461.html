Aqualung . left this review about Battesford Court (JD Wetherspoon)

This is a long narrow Spoons with a low ceiling, the bar is on the left and there are some smaller areas to sit on the right. There is a paved patio at the rear and a much smaller one on the right. It wasn't that busy on my Saturday late afternoon visit.
The bar has two banks of five hand pumps. one had the two GK beers, Doom Bore, Broadside (available soon) and the International Adnam's Feral Hop Hog (available soon). The other five had Titanic Desert Hero, Mauldon's Blackadder, May Bee & Blackberry Porter with one pump having a turned Mauldon's clip. I went for the Desert Hero (£1.99) which I thought was rather unimpressive for a Titanic beer but seemed to be in good condition.
This is a reasonable sort of Spoons, a lot quieter than some.
