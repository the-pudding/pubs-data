Al Bundy left this review about Royal Oak

A large looking pub but just one room that's L-shaped. The seating is now dark brown, possibly black. There was no real ale and no draught of any kind due to "storms" on this visit. Its not the best place but isn't one of the worst I've ever been in.

ROB Camra left this review about Royal Oak

A good looking pub from the outside, but once inside it's just one large room with a pool table in one area. There's green seating all around the walls and there are also loads of TV screens. There's a small beer garden to the rear and a few seats outside at the front. No real ale and a half of Fosters was an expensive £1.60. Another nondescript pub.
