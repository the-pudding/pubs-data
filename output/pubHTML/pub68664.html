Al Bundy left this review about Tudor Rose

A large roadhouse Brewers Fayre pub. Two rooms with the larger used for more restauranty activities. The bar area has a few offshoots to sit. A couple of real ales available but nothing of interest really. A typical Brewers Fayre to be honest. In the middle of nowhere so a car is your best bet if you want to get there.
