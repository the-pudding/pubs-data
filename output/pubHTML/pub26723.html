Al Bundy left this review about The Lutine Bell

A basic locals boozer that was popular today with the horsey horsey brigade. A single room with a large lounge-like area to the left of the bar. Decorated with Liverpool FC memorabilia including a mural to the right of the bar of Bill Shankly and Bob Paisley. 5 European Cups decorate the wall behind the bar. No real ale. Despite the Liverpool FC stuff its still a pub I've very rarely been in. And that doesn't look like changing any time soon.
