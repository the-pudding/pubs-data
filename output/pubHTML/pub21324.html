Quinno _ left this review about The White Swan

A basic village local. Two rooms knocked through a bit in the main bar (padded bench seating, wood burning stove, pool, darts and TV snooker) and it felt a bit higgledy-piggledy and quite messy, like someone was halfway through moving house. Not having some of the lights on also made it feel a bit gloomy on an already grey afternoon. Discovered later was a room round the side wasn't obvious and was smarter than the main bar. Four pumps overall, two in either bar (we didn’t know of the existence of the second set of pumps until after the event). The Hook Norton was off leaving Whole Hop, Hop on the Mow which was a bit so-so. As we sat down we noted the blackboard claiming Adnams Bitter and Doom Bar elsewhere. GBG listed and praised elsewhere, I struggled to get much out of this one, unfortunately – maybe I hit it on a bad day? 5.5

Alan Winfield left this review about The White Swan

The White Swan is a smallish corner pub in the middle of Barrowby.
Once inside there was a small room to the left which was carpeted and had comfortable bench seating and there was a longish bar to the right this had a pool table at the far end of it.
There were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Adnams bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beers were Adnams Broadside and London Pride.
This was a nice comfortable pub to have a drink in.
