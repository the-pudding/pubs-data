Old fashioned ornamentally rounded corner pub on the junction of West Derby Road and Belmont Road.Only frequented the room to the rear which was a large curved one with high ceilings that were heavily stained with nicotine,plenty of wood and a slightly raised area on the right hand side.Could not see any real ale on show although the pint i had was ok.
