Ian Mapp left this review about The Red Lion

A Classic Cotswold Pub. Beautiful exterior and interior. Geared for dining but there are some high chairs at the bar. Three beers on - I had a quality pint of Cotswold Way for £3.30 - which I thought was very reasonable.

Dog Friendly.

Would love to stay and eat here - a lovely village in some prime walking country.

On 30th December 2014 - rating: 9
