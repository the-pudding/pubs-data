Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Wotton Hatch

Roadside inn on A25, now described as "Country pub and eating house" which pretty much sums it up. Still retains a cosy pubby area by the bar, but largely given over to eating. Décor in typical modern style, with rustic touches - not unpleasant. The garden is equally aimed at diners. The food quality was very good however, and no complaints about the ales (Cask Marque listed).

On 8th October 2014 - rating: 7
