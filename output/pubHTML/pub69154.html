Oggwyn Great left this review about Ring O Bells

A former two up two down cottage on the outskirts of Dawley , set back from the road it is easy to miss . The pub has been extended over the years macking it much bigger than it looks , you enter down a narrow drive , a small smoking shelter to the right , the front door is very low so you need to duck , the interior was once two small rooms but the wall has been removed and replaced by the central bar with drinking areas all around it , the left is the more bar like with a dartboard and lots of trophies , there is a large TV to the back of the bar area , an extension to the rear has a pool table .
Only keg beer available , the usual fizz and Banks Bitter , service was OK as were the locals , which is rare in Dawley .
Its one of Dawleys better pubs but still not great

On 4th September 2012 - rating: 4
