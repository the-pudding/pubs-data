Colin Walker left this review about McGinlay's Bar

A pub situated above a Chinese Restaurant. The access to the bar with parking is round at the rear. The pub was very busy when I visited, as it was a football night. There are plenty of televisions on the go and a pool table down at the bottom end from the bar. A good choice of whisky, some of which I had only seen on the Internet and not for sale in pubs. The place is like a large open plan attic, but it was not claustrophobic even although it was very busy. This pub was original at 17-21 West High Street, Kirkintilloch.
