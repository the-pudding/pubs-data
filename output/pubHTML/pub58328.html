Alan Winfield left this review about Apollo

The Apollo is a large roadside pub near the top end of holderness road.I went in on a saturday dinner and it was very busy with people eating food and lads watching football on tv screens this is a Marstons house but there was only one real ale on which was Hobgoblin it was a fairly decent drink, i might have enjoyed the visit more if it was less busy.
