Komakino . left this review about Spindle & Thread

UPDATE
As suspected, the usual light pine and cosy trappings with mixtures of booth seating, tables and chairs and faux-suede bench seats. The carvery bit is to the left as you walk in with the bar dead ahead in the form of a chamfered L-shape. Four from the stable: EPA, Pedigree, Boon Doggle and Hobgoblin. All had sparklers on, and my EPA, a bargain at £3.35 was in good nick nonetheless. Well-populated on a weeknight visit with a soft-rock muzak background and few too many staff, some a little on the fawning side. Absolutely no truck with solo drinking as there shouldn't be, but if you're not a fan of the brand, or indeed this type of pub, then it ain't gonna be one for you.

25th October 2017
A NuMarston's build in the 'Hughenden Quarter' and which hides behind a large Morrison's superstore site. This is due to open on Monday 30th October and is going to be heavy on the Carvery side of things. I'm fully expecting beers from the stable, so will confirm in due course.

On 7th November 2017 - rating: 6
