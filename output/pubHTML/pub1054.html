Komakino . left this review about The White Hart Hotel

As per Will, I was a little shocked to enter a premises where the first thing I saw on the bar was cake stands(?!). However, two of the three ale line-up (binning off London Pride) were by 'Ampthill Brewhouse', a new brewery to me, and halves of the Session Ale and Honey Trap were nice and surprising alternatives in the heart of Charlie Wells country.

Will Larter left this review about The White Hart Hotel

A smart hotel with an even smarter bar, set up like a café with cakes and coffee machine, but with a surprising set of three hand pumps too. Even more of a surprise to find Jennings Bitter and Thwaites Lancaster Bomber here in the heart of Bedfordshire, and served without a sparkler too! Not so much of a surprise to be charged £2 for a half of the Jennings. Although the beer was ok, I certainly wouldn't go out of my way again to drink expensive beer in what amounts to a café.

Date of visit: 24th November 2015
