Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Harvester The Foxhunt

New-build Harvester at the south-western extremity of the Birmingham conurbation, with a not unattractive faux converted-barn façade. Of course, it's three-quarters chain restaurant and 25% bar, and all anonymous in appearance inside. However, there is also a beer garden at the back and a couple of patio tables on either side of the entrance. It does do real ale too, albeit just Doom Bar (£3.15) from one of three handpumps.

On 3rd August 2018 - rating: 6
