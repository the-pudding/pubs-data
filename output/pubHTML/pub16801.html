Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Rose & Crown

Large, food-led Vintage Inns pub located on the far side of the M25 from Dunton Green. Various seating and dining areas. Substantial patio / grass beer garden to the side. Three handpumps on the bar counter: Pride, Doom bar and Brakspear Bitter (£3.25).
