Will Larter left this review about The Unicorn

Recently reopened under the Amber Inns banner, this is now a large shopping centre pub with a long bar down the right hand side and booth seating down the left, with each area having a TV screen showing horse racing when I was there. There were four hand pumps on the bar, two of which had beers from Tetleys (Golden and a seasonal beer), the other two being reserved for beers from local Welbeck Abbey brewery. Their Henrietta, a pale session beer, was available at £2.20 a pint, with Red Feathers "coming soon" at the same price, though I don't know if this was permanent or promotional. The Henrietta was ok but nothing special. It's good to see this sort of pub, which could easily have been selling Doom Bar or Bombardier, supporting a local brewery instead. Having said which, I have to also say it's not really my kind of pub, so I won't be making frequent visits in future.
