Peter Jillings left this review about The Mill Inn

A Classic Cotswold pub. An interesting layout of small rooms give this pub a cosy feel. Definitely worth a visit, but being a Samual Smith's pub, no real ale. At the time of writing, they have started doing chicken and chips, but haven't tested this out, as they had run out of chicken on my last visit!
P.S. Sammy Smith's is gorgeous.

On 12th May 2013 - rating: 7
