Alan Winfield left this review about Fiddlers Elbow

The Fiddlers Elbow is situated in the middle of Wellingborough.
Once inside there was a decent sized oblong shaped room,the bar was to the left halfway down the room,there is a small raised area to the front right which had half circular bench seating in bays,the front left had similar seating,the middle area opposite the bar was devoid of seating,there was a pool table to the rear right,the room is bare boarded,the background music was from a local radio station.
No real ales on here,i had a drink of keg Worthington bitter which was a pretty poor drink.
There were a few other customers in on my Saturday dinner visit.
This is not a pub to go out of your way to do like i did.

Pub visited 21/6/2014
