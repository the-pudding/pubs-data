Quinno _ left this review about The Fox & Pheasant

TARDIS place, bloody huge once you get beyond the main bar. Modern interior, done up relatively well with the stand-out being a nice tiled bar back with inset fridge. Large garden with wooded overlook. Toilets pure comedy, the hand dryer coming on whenever (and wherever) you stood by the sink. Football on the TV. Three ales on; Bombardier, a dire and returned Marstons cricket-theme beer and a pretty poor Doom Bar as replacement. Some better keg choices. All fur coat and no knickers, this one.

On 14th September 2015 - rating: 4
