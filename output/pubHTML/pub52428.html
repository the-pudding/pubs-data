Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Queens Head

Pub and restaurant (with the latter in a side room off to the left side of the bar and very definitely set aside for dining, with frilly napkins, etc.). In the centre, there is a selection of semi-modern furniture opposite the counter, and another plainer opened-out room to the right which might conceivably be used for live music. Two of three handpumps in use, offering Greene King IPA and TT Landlord (£3.90).

Philip Gaskin left this review about The Queens Head Hotel

This is a long pub split into three areas. On the left is the dining area, with the bar area in the middle and a small drinking area to the right with two tables in it. To the rear, which leads to the Council car park, there is a smoking area. Greene King IPA on handpump at £2.75. It was busy, but there are not many places to sit and have drink, and it may better later in the day when it is quieter.

On 14th July 2011 - rating: 6
