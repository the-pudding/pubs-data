Reviews of Depot (Average Rating: 9 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

lyppy lyppy left this review about Cook Island

A very welcoming pub and ideal for kids as there is an indoor play area. Food is very expensive but worth it - the sizes are massive and the food was delicious. The carvery is expensive for 10.95 but you get 4 large slices of meat and as much potatoes, yorkshires and veg as you want - also a large selection of these. Drinks prices are good. The only reason I didnt give a 10 is because it can get very crowded and we sat for around 45 minutes with empty plates.
