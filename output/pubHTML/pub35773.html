Old Blue left this review about Dog & Pheasant

Had lunch today at this pleasant country pub opposite a cricket green, not that far from the A3. The inside is traditional and slightly bigger than it looks from the outside, with a three room main area, and I think a function room upstairs. Outside, there are tables both in front and to the side, and a children's play area to the rear. Had a decent ale from one of the four hand pumps. Pretty good choice and delicious food on the menu, though quite expensive. This pub is very 'gastro', but not exclusively so, and I'd happily drop in again for a pint if I was passing.
