Quinno _ left this review about The Bush Inn

Upmarket yet traditional multi-room Wadworth outlet with a rather alluring waterway (a tributary of the Itchen) outside and the Kennet nearby. Dark green interior with a number of discrete drinking areas, stuffed fish, roaring wood fire, candles on tables, jugs hanging from the bar and a general left-field vibe. The attractive garden is overlooked by mature woodland. Child friendly. Four pumps, three Waddies on; 6X, IPA, Dirty Rucker. I tried the latter which was a bit warm but in fair shape. Old Rosie cider also noted. The ginger kid behind the bar lacked social skills. Overall I thought it was surprisingly enjoyable but I imagine it’d divide opinion. 7.5

On 3rd April 2016 - rating: 7
