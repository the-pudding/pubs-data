Will Larter left this review about Crown Inn

It was quiet here at the time of my mid-afternoon refreshment stop on my way by bike from Hereford to Kington via most of the villages in between. The notice on entering "no food, no cards until further notice" may have put off some prospective customers, but wanting only a beer and being possessed of coin, I made my way to the bar. A decent selection of beer on the three hand pumps (see photo - staff and customer both amused at my hobby) and my half of Goffs Lancer saw me on my way.

Date of visit: 19th April 2017
