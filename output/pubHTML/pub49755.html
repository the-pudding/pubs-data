ALASTAIR BARTER left this review about The Drookit Dug

Here's some information I got on the same website as the Commercial Inn. It was known as The Glen Tavern in the late 19th & early 20th century until 13.07.04 when Mrs Twaddle, the manageress died. There was new management between November 1921 until December 1974 when it was known as Carlin's. Currently known as The Drookit Dug.
