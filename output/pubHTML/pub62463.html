Touring Scotland left this review about The Queens Head

This pub is about a five minute walk from the old blacksmiths shop at Gretna Green.
It only usually has one real ale on but its always in good nick.
Usually closed during the daytime and open at night except for weekends, dont think they serve food.
Its the only real ale pub for miles and has been in the good beer guide a few times.
