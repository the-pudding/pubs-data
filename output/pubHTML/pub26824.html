Al Bundy left this review about The Raven

An open plan pub with a nice, clean interior. The bar is to the rear as you enter. Banquette seating along the walls. Lots of pictures of Everton FC leg ends along the wall to the right. No real ale, unless you count the Greene King IPA that wasn't on handpump.
