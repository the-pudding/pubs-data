Mike Tait left this review about The Bridge Inn

No kidding, book early and you will not be dissapointed. Been for a quiet meal with the family and a very noisy christmas party with work, and in both cases the staff have been excellent and the food quite amazing. Highly recommended place for a romantic meal or a blummin' good banquet. (Top tip : Best tell when making a reservation that you want a quiet spot ... it can get very busy)
