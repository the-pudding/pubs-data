E TA left this review about Salisbury Rugby Club

A single story clubhouse adjacent to the rugby pitches and in sight of Old Sarum. There is plenty of parking and lots of space outside. There are three bars, all sparsely decorated and with basic furniture and various rugby memorabilia. WiFi is available and there are a couple of gaming machines, but the main entertainment is the sports matches being shown on the large TVs and projector screen. The club is open to all, and it has become something of a favourite with Salisbury CAMRA. One of the Club's sponsors is Hopback Brewery, so their beers are on draft - GFB, Crop Circle and Winter Lightning plus Thatcher's Gold cider are on handpump, while San Miguel, megakeg lager and Guinness are available on gas pumps. The ales were in excellent condition. The staff are very friendly and welcoming, the Club hosts regular beer festivals and of course it gets packed on match days. Worth coming for the events or a quick pint when passing, but it doesn't open Mondays or weekday lunchtimes.
