Quinno _ left this review about The Roebuck Inn

Utterly uninspiring Fullers outlet. Two ales, Pride and Brithop (which in fairness was good). TV showing - bizarrely - German football, not the FA Cup semi which probably explained the lack of clientèle. Fairly unremarkable interior. Lack of notes on my part probably sums it all up, really.
