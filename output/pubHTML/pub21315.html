Alan Winfield left this review about Tollemache Arms

The Tollemache Arms is a large stone built pub in a pleasant village.This pub has been shut for quite a while but has recently reopened it smelt new inside the decor seems a bit spartan with bare pine flooring and tabels dotted around the room but at least its open as a pub again,there were 2 pumps on the bar the Grainstore Ten Fifty was a decent drink the other handpump had Phipps Red Star i thought this Northamptonshire Brewery shut down years ago there was no indication on the pump clip who brews this beer i would be interested to find out who brews this beer!.
