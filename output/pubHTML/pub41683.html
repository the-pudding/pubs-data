Phantom Pigeon left this review about Fox & Hounds

We pulled into the Little Chuff car park on the A303, and we saw the sign directing drivers down the little country road.
This is a real out of the way pub, which welcomes dogs. They sell packets of designer dog biscuits at the bar.
All that aside, I had an excellent pint of Sheep Dip, and my wife enjoyed her spritzer, with our equally excellent ploughman’s. The staff were friendly and efficient, and the atmosphere was quite relaxed.
I do not see any reason for not making the detour, and calling in again.

On 25th May 2012 - rating: 7

Quinno _ left this review about Fox & Hounds

A part-thatched, whitewashed pub situated high on the hillside with extensive panoramic views overlooking the valley. Ales were Weymouth Durdle Door and Tribute. Cheddar Valley cider also. Quite foody but still welcoming to drinkers even if interior space is limited. Real fire and conservatory to be had. Spent most of my time outside gawping at the view so can’t be much more florid in my description. Worth diverting for, for the view if nothing else.

On 3rd July 2010 - rating: 7
