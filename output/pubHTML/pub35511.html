Old Blue left this review about Windmill Inn

Dropped into this unashamed gastropub when passing yesterday. Apparently there are stunning countryside views from the terrace at the rear. It was very quiet, with a dimly lit interior, and the London Pride clip was reversed on the one ale pump. The woman I took to be the landlady explained that the barrel had only arrived that morning and needed to settle. This may be a good place to come and soak up the view in the summer, but it didn’t tick any of the boxes for what we wanted on a cold December afternoon, so we didn’t stay.
