Will Larter left this review about Bruce Arms

This is a nice old-fashioned brick built corner local. There are three rooms: a tiny and basic tap room on the left, with two larger rooms to the rear. The room on the right has just a corner of the unusually shaped bar as a servery, while the larger room to the left has two hand pumps. The third hand pump is in the tap room, and this was serving Lancaster Blonde, the others being Courage Directors and Robinsons Unicorn. I had the Blonde, which was in good condition. It was very quiet on my late afternoon midweek visit, but I got the impression of a friendly local pub which is worth the short walk from the town centre.

On 20th February 2013 - rating: 7

The Bruce Arms is a back street pub that is away from the centre of town.
