Real Ale Ray left this review about Keel Row

An attractive looking road side pub, with a traditional interior. The pub consists of the main front lounge, bar facing you as you enter. There was a open fire at one end, with a Victorian cast iron fireplace. The lounge was carpeted and the undressed stone wall, made a nice feature.
There was a dining area leading off the main lounge, on the left hand side, which looked very classy. Nothing exciting to report on the bar unfortunately, Black Sheep and Bombardier.

On 22nd August 2014 - rating: 6
