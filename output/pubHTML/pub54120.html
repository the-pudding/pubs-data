Aqualung . left this review about The Marble Beer House

This is another wooden floored shop conversion but has a slightly more pubby feel than the others around here and although still expensive not quite as outrageously as others.
The bar has seven hand pumps of which one was unused and the rest had Marble Ginger, Lagonda IPA, Pint and Bitter, Buxton SPA and Ilkley Siberia. I went for the Lagonda IPA which was outstanding but pricey at £3.75.
This wasn't too bad a place but if they are charging prices like this in the Marble Arch a pub I've been to a couple of times before, I won't be going back there again and I can't see me returning here either.

On 24th June 2015 - rating: 5
