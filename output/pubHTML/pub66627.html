E TA left this review about Four Crosses

A rather drab wedge-shaped Victorian pub which has seen better days. There are a pool table, dartboard, TV screens showing football and a grumpy barmaid, but there is no real ale. Leave it to the locals.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Four Crosses

Refurbished corner pub with a 'V'-shaped bar inside quite a finely decorated façade. Looks from a distance like it might be a new-style alehouse, with the modern Little Pub Company frosted glass, but just a basic John Smith's (£1.45, half) local within. Main features are the pool table, dartboard and a few TVs. Disappointing...
