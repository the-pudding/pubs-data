Alan Winfield left this review about George & Dragon

The George and Dragon is a decent looking stone built pub that is situated next to a busy crossroads.
Once inside there was an L shaped room with the bar facing,the room was carpeted and had very comfy bench seating and normal tables and chairs,there was a pool table to the right and lots of normal tables and chairs to the left.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Oakham Citra which was a very nice drink,the other beers were Wentworth bitter and Batemans XXXB.
There is an outside terrace to the rear of the pub which overlooks open countryside.
The pub had a nice feel to it and i had a good chat with the youngish barmaid who was very helpfull in telling me about pubs in Clay Cross.
I liked this pub that much i came back for another drink of Oakham Citra before catching my bus.
