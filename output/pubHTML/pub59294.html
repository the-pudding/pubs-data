Peter Rydings left this review about Castle Garden Hotel

Called in on the 28th of April first time for many years and was pleasantly surprised.Asked the young girl behind the bar if they accept camera card she says yes but dont use it on a Monday you get 80p off its real ale day every Monday.Like all Ember Inns its clean and the decor to a high standard yes its very foody but unlike a lot of food pubs its still got a drinkers feel about it.Will call often now moved back in the area the only bad side its the only pub in the Area of Carlton
