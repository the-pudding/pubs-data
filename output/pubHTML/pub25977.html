Reviews of The Hare & Hounds (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about The Hare & Hounds

Neat little Thwaites pub with multiple rooms. There's a small bar area to the right and a seating area to the left. There's a larger dining room to the rear of the bar with all tables reserved on my Friday, late afternoon visit. There's yet another seating area to the rear left. There's a large, suntrap of a beer garden to the side, very nicely done with paved tiers and plants all around the outside. For some reason a tiny and tinny radio was playing on top of the jukebox which was crap. Apart from that though and the fried food smell I really liked this pub. There are two handpump which were serving Original & Wainwright. The Wainwright was in good nick and was decent value at £3.

On 29th January 2019 - rating: 7
