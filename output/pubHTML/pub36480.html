Julie McCue left this review about Hobson Hotel

Visited the hobson hotel Sunday 12th June for Sunday lunch. We found the service poor as we had to go to the bar to order, after waiting for quite a while. The starter was average, at best, but again we waited some time before the plates were collected. The main consisted of very watery frozen vegetables, unedible. The meat, however, was nice. Also, we found ourselves squashed onto a very small table for two, in the 'family area' when we obviously had no children with us.
