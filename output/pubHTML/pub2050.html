Komakino . left this review about Dairy Maid

A GK Hungry Horse outlet with the usual garish colour schemes inside, and, being an estate pub, more than a few colourful locals. An island bar serves the main bar area which meant an age getting served by the single member of staff. The usual meal deals, Sky Sports on numerous screens (including mini screens in booths in the dining area) and drinks offers - GK IPA, Abbot Ale and Purity Mad Goose were the ales on offer on this visit, although I didn't partake: the IPA available for £2.49 "every day".

Steve of N21 left this review about Dairy Maid

This estate pub has now been done up and converted by Greedy King into a Hungry Horse. Pool room has been converted into the dining area and the smaller bar area is now an 'adult bar area' e.g. no kids allowed. Three handpumps are on the bar with the usual GK offerings of IPA, Abbott and OSH.
