Komakino . left this review about Crawley Inn

Just dreadful. Doors open, scruffy people milling out front, but lights out inside posed the quandary as to whether it was indeed open for business or not. It was, but the interior was a tip reminiscent of some of the sites seen on the former BBC 1 reality series 'A Life of Grime'. I eschewed the one real ale on offer (Doom Bar) and opted for half a cider: as you can imagine, it was necked in record time and a swift and relieved exit made.

On 26th August 2016 - rating: 1
