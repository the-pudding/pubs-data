amanda watkins left this review about Kerry Lamb

Nice setting, clean and staff very helpful and friendly. Sunday lunches need a little more thought, main meal always enjoyable, meat cooked well, and fresh veg. Starters and desserts need to be seasonal, a Parma ham and melon would be nice in the summer months, prawn cocktail needs to be a little more adventurous, get rid of dated shells, to 80,s. But a pleasent place to eat.

On 5th June 2011 - rating: 7
