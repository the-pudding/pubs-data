Quinno _ left this review about The Slug & Lettuce

Newly-opened Slug in the redeveloped Castle heritage site. L-shaped in layout, with the usual upmarket bar touches including a mirrored bar back. A couple of ales on, my Oxfordshire Pride of Oxford was terrible and may well have been on since opening the week prior. Otherwise, the usual keg and spirit offerings. Change was given back on a silver tray. Urgh. One to avoid.

On 10th June 2014 - rating: 4
