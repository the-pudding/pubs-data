elizabeth mcgraw left this review about The Tally Ho

Lovely cosy country pub and a very welcoming landlord/landlady. 4 ales on tap one of which was straight from the barrel (Larkins) The food served looked excellent ( unfortunately we did not have time to taste on this occasion but wiil be returning soon)It is garden fresh veg and the fish looked perfect. They are going to have a beer festival at the end of July beginning of August. For more Info on this call nearer the time on 01959 533602. Well worth a visit, bus R5/R10 from Orpington stops outside the door
