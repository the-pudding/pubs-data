Komakino . left this review about Cooper's

A nice Olde Worlde building still under the auspices of Charlie Wells now converted into a "Grill House" and, looking at the pub signpost, still retaining the original name (The Beehive) although none of the branding reflects this. It's a bit of a warren inside, but has kept the low, exposed beams and all the exposed walls and panelling have been painted over in white. All tables had numbers and food menus, although there is a small portion of bar serving Young's Special and Young's London Gold and solo imbibing is still allowed. A vinegary Special came in at £2.25 for a half and was swiftly left.
