Ian Mapp left this review about Bulls Head Hotel

In a week of around 40 peak district pubs - this was the best of the week.

Great food/Service is a separate dining room.

Front bar full of life - darts team preparing for an away match. Every local with an interest in football watching the game.

Full of joy and banter.

Beer wise - a couple from Marstons - not the most exciting - but a great atmosphere.

Andy Ven left this review about Bulls Head Hotel

I knew nothing about this place before I visited but it has an appealing exterior and a cosy homely feel inside. There is a bar room with dart board and TV (dogs welcome), a pool room, a lounge to the rear leading through to a dining room. The gaffer was friendly and chatty.

On handpull there was Marston’s Burton Bitter, Peak Ales Bakewell Best Bitter and Jennings Cumberland ... oh and they sell pork scratching. Overnight accommodation is also available. It got my vote for my favourite pub in Youlgrave. If I returned to this area I would consider staying over here.

On 11th October 2010 - rating: 7
