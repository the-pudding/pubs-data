Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Birdcage

This Greene King pub doesn't initially catch the eye, but this is quite an appealing Art Deco building of you stand back and take a look. Although the bar is smaller than one might expect, there is also further seating down a few steps in the implausibly named 'Ballroom'. With two of three handpumps in use, I found IPA (£3.80, and in as good shape as this much-maligned beer gets) and Old Speckled Hen available, and a reversed Bath Ales Gem clip was also noted.

On 2nd March 2019 - rating: 6

Real Ale Ray left this review about The Birdcage

This one room bar we were told, is due to go under refurbishment. The interior was fairly non-descript on our visit and so were the choice of ales,GK IPA and luckily for us,there was Old Hooky on. The 1930s art deco building does attract you to this pub. This pub is a sister pub to the Sir Garnet. If you happen to visit the Grosvenor Fish Bar across the road, you can take your meal into the pub, where cutlery is provided.

On 16th February 2013 - rating: 7
