Olga Mathews left this review about Ship Inn

More restaurant than bar. Service is fast and friendly, even when very busy. The food is lovely, we thoroughly enjoyed the venison casserole, followed by vanilla dream with whisky sauce. If going for Sunday lunch, book in advance as it is very popular.
Restaurant is wheelchair accessible, limited parking is available outside the door & there is a large public car park nearby.
