ROB Camra left this review about The Pineapple Inn

Old school Robinsons pub, just outside the town centre. As you enter the main room is to the right, as is the bar. There's another small room to the left. It's quite a cosy space with lots of bric-a-brac around the pub. The floor is very uneven in places, so it's probably carpet over flag flooring. Fairly quiet during my late Friday afternoon visit with only 5 or 6 people in the pub, but they appeared friendly, as was the young guy behind the bar. Four handpumps on the go with Robbies Unicorn, Enigma, Dizzy Blonde and a seasonal whose name escapes me. I'm not a great fan of Robbies beer, but you have to drink it quite a lot of the time in Stockport, but my pint was a very decent pint of Enigma. It's a good locals pub that appears to be holding up under the direct competition from it's more famous neighbour across the road, The Crown.
