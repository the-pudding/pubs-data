Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Robin Hood

Tucked away in the residential area of Sherwood, this large suburban pub has been taken over by Greene King pub since my last visit almost ten years ago. The original building still houses the two-part bar which retains a pool table and dartboard, and the Hungry Horse restaurant section is located in a sizeable single-storey side extension. Patio terrace seating can also be found at the front, with another enclosed beer garden beside the dining area. IPA and Abbot (£3.25) available from two of the four handpumps.
