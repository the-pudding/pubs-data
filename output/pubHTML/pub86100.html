Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Bitter-Suite

Stretching the micro-pub definition a bit perhaps (given that it was formerly a 'normal' pub in the past), but this two-room establishment definitely has that ethos. Plainly furnished and decorated, but counter-less and with the 'cellar' being visible in a rear cool room. Five real ales - including the regular Bridgestrike from Burton Bridge (£3.50) - and four ciders advertised on the chalkboard. Excellent, and handy for the station too.

Gill Smith left this review about Bitter-Suite

What a great micropub this is not far from Lichfield City railway station. We had heard there was a beer festival, and found a list of 20 ales, 8 on stillage in the pub itself and 8 out in the yard area at the rear with 4 to go on. They also sell real cider gins and wines, and bar snacks, pork pies and cobs. There is no bar and they give table service. A very comfortable second room with tables and chairs plus seating in the rear yard area. First class drinking establishment. We enjoyed beers from Bath, Pershore, Flash House and Milestone breweries

Real Ale Ray left this review about Bitter-Suite

A truly excellent micropub this one and well worth a visit. The pub was busy on our visit and I thought I'd have to wait a while to be served, no problem there as the service was fast and efficient and the drinks were brought over to our table. Five ales and four ciders served from the cold room. The beer list on our visit was Roosters Cream, Mallinsons Komasal, Elliswood Legless, Burton Bridge Bridge Strike Bitter and Beowulf Dark Raven. I went for the Roosters and Mallinsons, both of which were superb and in excellent condition. Another added bonus to this micro was the choice of snacks, large handmade pork pies and scotch eggs, both of which looked delicious. There was also a choice of freshly made rolls, so I went for the cheese and onion, which was brill. Top marks to this family team who are running this friendly and welcoming micro.

On 30th July 2018 - rating: 10
