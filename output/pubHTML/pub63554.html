Harry Hancox left this review about Olde Coach House

Went here long, long ago, but now it seems it should be 'in a galaxy, far, far away!. My memories of a true olde world pub, open fire, top owner/landlord in a once great grub & a one time 10 out of 10 game pie, all now gone, nice wine cellar though and food looked good, but am sure service with snootiness is a winner.

Thuck Phat left this review about Olde Coach House

This used to be a very traditional village pub with wooden floors, beams, fires, fresh flowers etc. It has all been painted white and I overheard locals referring to it as 'The Clinic'. It seemed to take an age for the surly staff to arrive and the beer quality is poor with only Bombardier and Youngs Best as real options, neither of which was great when they finally arrived. Didn't eat but food looks good if expensive. Not one for the real ale connoisseur.

On 9th March 2010 - rating: 3
