Gill Smith left this review about Cider House (Monkey House)

We have often tried to find the Monkey Tree (as we have always known it), but failed. Yesterday we were on a coach trip round cider farms to collect cider for Birmingham beer festival, and this was our final stop, and the coach almost went past. It looks from the road like a farm, so not a hostelry, but wow. I am not usually a cider drinker, but I really enjoyed the perry served from what is basically a large hatch in the farmhouse wall. Dry and medium cider available too. We piled into what appears to be a large shed with a farm kitchen type table and seating round the wall on three sides, and a lovely warm wood burning stove. It was great, and a must if in the area and you can find it. There is a car park and toilets, plus a garden with seating which will be great in the summer.
