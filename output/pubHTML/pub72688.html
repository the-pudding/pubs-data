Alan Winfield left this review about White Swan

The White Swan is a large stand alone pub that is situated in the back streets but it is very close to the middle of fallowfield.
When i went in this pub it looked a bit dilapidated from the outside and once inside the pub was very basic with quite a few different rooms,the room i went in was very basic with a tiled floor.
This pub is a Robinsons tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Robinsons bitter and this was a really nice drink the other beer was Robinsons mild.
When i went in this pub it was what pubs were like years ago so it has probably changed a lot since then.
