Alan Winfield left this review about Crown Inn

The Rolleston Crown is a decent enough looking pub that is painted cream,once inside the pub had been done up in a more modern style with modern looking tables,but it still had a proper pub feel to it.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Black Sheep bitter which was a nice drink,the other beers were Bombardier and Greene King Golden Ale.
The pub was fairly quiet on our early Saturday dinner visit,but i would think it would fill up with diners a bit later on.

Pub visited 11/4/2013
