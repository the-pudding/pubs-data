Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Nick Davies left this review about The Castle

Nice village pub but not as nice and pubby as it was due to a recent gastro makeover, so expect the usual signs and symptoms: too few too-big tables with reserved signs on them mid-afternoon when few people are about, families with noisy kids out of control, and a long menu but no chance of a sandwich. This one does make a feature of its beer though, and is in the current GBG - I think the local branch rotates it with the Bell, and the Harvey's Best was spot on. Worth a look if you're in the area.

On 20th June 2013 - rating: 7
