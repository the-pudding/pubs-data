JohnWarks 'tache left this review about The Chequers

Visited this pub for family meal and wasn't disappointed. The food is very good, reasonably priced, the portion size large, and the Sunday Carvery also highly recommended! This is a proper old pub in a quiet location with friendly staff and real atmosphere. Well worth a visit! Staff so friendly, we left them a tip. Has a cracking log fire too

On 4th July 2011 - rating: 9
