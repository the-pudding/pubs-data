Graham Coombs left this review about Roman Camp Hotel

Small and well-kept roadside hotel, slightly more up-market than the average. Largely aimed at food trade, providing very nice but not cheap food all day, but retains a comfortable bar area with several real ales on offer. Extensive gardens to rear.
