Graham Coombs left this review about The Village Inn

A fairly-modern pub in a housing development area but unlike many of such shows signs of being well-run and despite the somewhat plastic interior is not unpleasant. Quite large and spacious rooms, and clearly a good community pub, with an extensive food trade. One or two reals ales on offer and Cask Marque listed.
