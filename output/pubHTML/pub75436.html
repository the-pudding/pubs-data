Peter Fryar left this review about Rose & Crown

A lovely quintessential Village Inn, with large gardens, a wide ranging menu of largely homemade food, Cask Ales, Wine List, serving food all day, and everyday. Beamed Bar, and large Restaurant. Monthly Music Evenings, and Fortnightly Quiz Nights, on Wednesdays. An oldfashioned Pub as they used to be, with a good mix of locals, and Food.

On 2nd August 2012 - rating: 9
