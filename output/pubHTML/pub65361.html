Graham Coombs left this review about Coach & Horses

Curious little pub on the main road with just one smallish room, so the toilets and a children's room are in a building across the car park. Friendly enough though, and at time of visit with Sharps' Own, Otter and two Exmoor beers on offer. Food seemed popular.
