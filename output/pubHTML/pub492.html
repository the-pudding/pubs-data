Alan Winfield left this review about Mechanics Arms

The Mechanics Arms is a typical looking town pub.
Once inside there is an L shaped room with the bar on the mid right side,the front area is carpeted and the rear a lino floor,there is bench seating and and chairs to the front and front left side,there is a darts board and TV to the left,the TV was showing Grand Prix,there is a pool table to the rear left.
There were no real ales on the bar or keg bitters,i had to settle for a drink of Fosters.
Not really a pub to go out of your way to do,but i am trying to every pub within the Bristol city boundarys.

Pub visited 29/7/2017
