Al Bundy left this review about Stanley Arms

Like the nearby Crofters, this too is a Sizzling pub. This one, however, is much better. Its a large 3 roomed pub with two rooms left and right and a rear room. The room to the right has a bar as does the rear room. There are 3 handpumps on each bar with a very pedestrian choice of GK IPA, Doom Bore, Hobgob and Bombardier on. Usual clutter of menus on tables. A traditional set of tables and chairs to sit on.

On 25th October 2017 - rating: 6
