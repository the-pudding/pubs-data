Oggwyn Great left this review about The Lowfield Inn

Large modern pub that seems slightly out of place in its rural setting , built in 2008 to replace the original pub demolished the year before , its very modern inside all light woods and comfy seating with several distinct areas .
Usual kegs and a decent selection of local beers from Woods , Six Bells and a decent Montys Sunshine , also Black Dragon cider on draught .
