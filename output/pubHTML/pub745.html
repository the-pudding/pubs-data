Cider Drinker left this review about The White Swan

First visit to this pub. Fabulous selection of ciders, 5 on draught and fridge of bottled to choose from. The beer drinker unfortunately doesn't fair so well. Only Doom Bar on tap and it was off when we visited. Usual selection of lagers available.

The bar was quiet whe we arrived, this was amended when the jukebox was turned on with very loud punk/garage music.

I visited the facilities before making a hasty exit and was faced with a rather offensive sign, offering direction to the male and female toilets, by way of Dicks and Flanges. Charming! Needless to say we won't be patronising this establishment again. Which is a shame as I would have liked to try some of the ciders.

On 28th March 2016 - rating: 3
