ROB Camra left this review about Hickorys Smokehouse

This is mainly a large, family friendly restaurant specialising in pulled pork, pulled brisket etc. There is however a small bar area where you can just have a drink and a good looking beer garden overlooking the river which is great in Summer. We've been here a couple of times recently and ate here on one occasion. The food is good, but expensive and there were a lot of kids in. The bar has one handpump alongside several lager pumps. They serve a changing Weetwood beer which has been in decent nick when we've called. Not a pub as such, but it can be very pleasant sat outside with a beer watching the world go by.
