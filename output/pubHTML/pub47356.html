George Howie left this review about The Hogshead

This pub is no longer a hogshead. Hogsheads were a Real Ale pub chain owned by Whitbread. The pubs were then sold to Laurel Inns who in turn sold some off to Greene King. This pub is now called the Old Town School and is now managed by the pubs arm of Belhaven Brewery who are now also owned by Greene King. The pub used to be very good but is now spoiled by the music being cranked up making conversation in the evenings a trifle difficult.
