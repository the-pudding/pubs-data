Oggwyn Great left this review about The Malvern Hills Hotel

Large landmark hotel at the end of the Malverns handily placed for the nearby hillfort , so popular with walkers .
The main entrance is through reception , there is a dining area in modern style to the right and the bar is to the left , through the bar is a conservatory dining room with spectacular views over the countryside , there is another function room downstairs.
Usual keg taps and a selection of real ales from Wye Valley and Ludlow , my Ludlow Gold was fine .
Service was very good despite the place being rammed , plenty eating but many just having a drink .
Worth a visit .
