Alan Winfield left this review about The Buxton Inn

The Buxton is a decent looking white painted pub that is in the middle of the gee cross area of Hyde.
Once inside this pub was quite smart,i had a drink in what was probably the lounge this room was very comfortable and i was quite happy having a drink in here.
When i visited this pub it was a Bass tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Stones bitter and this was a nice drink the other beer was Worthington bitter which was on handpump.

Pub visited 13/1/1996
