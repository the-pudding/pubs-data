Alan Winfield left this review about The Carlton Tavern

The Carlton tavern is a very large pub that is set well back from the road in its own grounds.
The main entrance is at the side of the pub and once inside the pub has one very large room the has got different drinking and dining areas on different levels.
There was a carvery to the left and most people were dining in this area this also had a conservatory added on the the end of the room,the pub was carpeted throughout and had a smart look to it and it was catering for family dining.
There was only one real ale on here this was Pedigree and this was a decent drink.
These sort of pubs are not really to my taste but it was popular with people dining.

On 7th March 2011 - rating: 6
