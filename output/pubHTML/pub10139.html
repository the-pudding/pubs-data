Soup Dragon left this review about The Black Dog

A terraced town centre pub that has character, in white render, with front facing gable and oriel window. There is a patio.

The interior is open plan. There is a long bar on the left hand side after entering from the main street. The general décor is white with support and ceiling beams, the room has a few areas, including a pool table area in cream and wood panel and a small sofa area which has a stone fireplace. There is some naval ephemera and prints on the walls. The service was fine and there was a mixed clientele. There was a TV with the sport on and the background music was general, but loudish. They did food, but I didn’t eat here so I can’t comment on that.

Beer; tap stuff with Ringwood Bitter and a decent Dorset Brewing Company’s Jurassic on handpull.

It was a decent enough place to visit and I would go back. It may appeal to the younger crowd on weekends.

On 17th September 2010 - no rating submitted
