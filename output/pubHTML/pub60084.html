ROB Camra left this review about The Bank Top Tavern

A change of landlord and a change of attitude since my last visit. The pub is now much more music orientated with a DJ at weekends playing oldies. It's one large room at the front, with another smaller room at the rear woth a pool table, both served by a central bar. 3 handpumps on the bar with Lees Bitter doubled up alongside Lees Dragonfire. The Dragonfire was a well kept pint. The clientele now seem to be similar to those who use Whittles across the road judging by my Friday early evening vist, which is no bad thing. A much improved pub.

On 5th June 2014 - rating: 6
