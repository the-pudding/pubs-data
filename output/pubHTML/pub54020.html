Alan Winfield left this review about The Hourglass

The Hourglass is situated on a very busy street.
Once inside there is a decent sized single oblong shaped room which runs to the rear,the room is bare boarded,the bar is on the right side the seating is a mix of tall tables and chairs and bench seating,there were TVs on showing the Chelsea v Man Utd game.
There were five real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Taylor Boltmaker which went down very well,the other beers were Jennings Cumberland,Wadworth 6X,GK IPA and Old Speckled Hen.
The pub was very busy on my early Saturday evening visit,i thought this was a decent pub to end my crawl in.

Pub visited 4/4/2015
