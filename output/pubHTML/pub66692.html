Quinno _ left this review about Peacock Farm

A converted farmhouse run by Hall & Woodhouse. Huge inside, with lots of character and sympathetic conversion features eg exposed brick, beams etc. Lots of internal fauna, the big palm tree especially caught my attention – sounds odd but it works. Some nice exterior seating too. The place is mainly aimed at hardcore diners though utilizing the place for a pint wasn’t an issue. Three ales on from the H&W stable, my Tanglefoot was in particularly good shape, if somewhat pricey. If you’re in Bracknell, this is worth seeking out if you can stand the walk.

On 22nd April 2015 - rating: 7
