Al Bundy left this review about The Lisbon

This is basement pub and situated in the gay part of town. Its a very ornate, Victorian inside and is a decent lookin pub which makes it worth popping in for a quick pint if you've never been before. No handpulls on the bar anymore so no real ale. Popular with the rainbow flag followers.

Real Ale Ray left this review about The Lisbon

A pub that has been frequented by Paul O Grady and Holly Johnson, so I imagine you get my drift. This is a basement pub and has an entrance on both streets. The heavily embellished ceilings in here is worth a visit, so too is the oak panelled room. There were two handpumps in here and sadly were not in use. So I settled on the Kronenbourg 1664 on draft. Had a game of pool with a couple of lesbians, I won.
