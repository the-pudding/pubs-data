Oggwyn Great left this review about Duke of York

Apart from one friendly local and his dog this place was full of Yorks 30 something trendy crowd and a few tourists on my Thursday evening visit , 12 handpulls in 3 banks of 4 , the banks at either end of the bar doubled up with Leeds Brewery beers , the middle bank had 3 in use with Cameron Strongarm ,Hawkshead Lager and a tasty Wylam Cascade .
Easily the busiest pub of the night , not my sort of place despite the decent beer selection.

Will Larter left this review about The Duke of York

I went in here soon after visiting the Eagle and Child, the new Leeds pub in High Petergate. As with all the Leeds Brewery pubs I've been in, this building too has been painstakingly, almost faultlessly adapted, if seen from an aesthetic point of view, but as a pub it is totally lacking in warmth and ambience. As with the new pub, there are 12 hand pumps, with Leeds's core range duplicated on a second set of four, though here the Midnight Bell gives way to a cider on one set. There are four guest beers. I don't rate the Leeds pubs much as pubs - perhaps they'll improve with age (or perhaps I'll become less fastidious, who knows).

On 26th November 2015 - rating: 6
