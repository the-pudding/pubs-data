Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Crescent Inn

Traditional backstreet local, partly opened out inside from three rooms. Sports TVs, darts, pool and a fireplace (with a bucket of coal to one side, but the fire is actually a photograph). Patio to rear. Two of three handpumps in operation, with Pedigree and Coach House Gunpowder Mild (£2.00) available when I stopped by.
