Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Jonathan Wilde left this review about The Bell Hotel

Surprised to still see this as a pub as when I worked in Old Sodbury in October 2007 it looked like it was going. It may well have in the interim years but I feared driving along Badminton Road towards it that it was going to be Indian Restaurant or something. How joyous it was to see it still retained as a pub. Oddly even though working just up the road I'd never ventured in due to the person I worked with saying it wasn't very nice and we prefered The Dog which was closer to work for our daily liquid lunches.

Anyway substantial refurb has occured in the interim period and it has a more foody feel as most places do now but it pleasant enough. Spotted about 3-4 ales on tap.

On 24th March 2012 - no rating submitted
