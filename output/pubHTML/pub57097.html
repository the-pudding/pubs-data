Aqualung . left this review about The Last Post (JD Wetherspoon)

This is an old red sandstone building that as noted below is on the right side of the square as you leave Gilmour Street station and was Paisley's main Post Office which has probably been shifted to a corner shop.
Inside it's a long room raised at the far end and an upstairs area I didn't go to.
The twelve hand pumps with only one unused seemed promising until it transpired that the two festival ciders and Kelburn Jaguar had all gone. This left the Scottish JDW Trio Of Doom, Jenning's Snecklifter, Stewart's Edinburgh Festival, a house beer, Hobgoblin and Purity Mad Goose. I gave up and went for a bottle of Oakham Green Devil.
I can't say I was impressed with this one but it is a pleasant building.

On 21st August 2017 - rating: 5
