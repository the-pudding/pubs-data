Aqualung . left this review about The Dolphin

As noted below this Hop Back tied house is a basic drinker's pub and was quiet on my Saturday late afternoon visit despite the Six Nations Rugby being on in the adjacent area.
The bar only had two of their beers on from the four hand pumps, Crop Circle and Summer Lightning. I went for the Summer Lightning (£3.20).
I was a bit disappointed with this one as I was expecting more from a Hop Back pub.

E TA left this review about The Dolphin

A basic drinkers' pub on the end of a Georgian terrace, currently operated by Hopback. There are 2 bars downstairs, and apparently there are rooms to let upstairs. A former skittle alley is now used as a function room. Recent refurbishment as removed unnecessary clutter, and the front bar is now a wood-floored drinking area, while the families' room at the back is more of a saloon. The bar had 4 pumps on it, but there were only 2 ales on draft, plus a selection of bottles. The barmaid was friendly and chatty, though there weren't many other customers in during our visit. The ales were Summer Lighting and Crop Circle. The Crop Circle was in excellent condition. A very valuable addition to a town crawl.
