Will Larter left this review about Bank Tavern

A building which the name and inn sign both imply was formerly a bank, but every effort has been made to make it look and feel like a traditional pub, with rough plastered walls, beams and exposed joists in the ceiling and some interesting B&W photos of Keswick and the surrounding area. The bar, which unusually can be seen through a window as you walk past in the street, has a full range of Jennings beers and some guests from the Marstons stable spread among its eight hand pumps. I usually call in here for a Sneck Lifter whenever I'm in Keswick and the beer is always in good nick and not overpriced for the area.

On 31st December 2012 - rating: 6
