Komakino . left this review about Two Willows

Right in the centre and already well-described below, this had a four ale line-up of Sharp's Doom Bar, Sharp's Sea Fury, Adnam's Southwold Bitter and Shepherd Neame Bishops Finger, augmented by two real ciders in Lilley's Mango and Old Rosie. Various large screens showed a rerun of an England ODI and muzak played in the background. Air-conditioned inside (the temperature was pushing 30-degrees C outside) some pretty young things had chosen to sit on tables outside in the shade and with adverts for DJ nights and cocktails, one suspects this is a haven for the "yoof" of WGC at weekends, so time your visit accordingly.
