Cecil Smith left this review about The Carriage House

The Carriage house has been extended since your picture of October 2007 to include several en suite rooms and a conservatory. Disabled access and toilet are available. It also offers a restaurant and bar,two outdoor patio areas,a children's play area and a caravan and camping site at the rear of the property which is serviced by toilets,showers and washing facilities.
Last month, together with other family members,I had an evening meal here. The menus are A la Carte,Turkish,Bar and Childrens. My Daughter and I chose from the Turkish menu and my Wife and Grandaughter from the A la Carte and Children's. Everyone thoroughly enjoyed their meals and we will certainly dine here again.
I can only really comment on the Turkish food as that what I chose and, having eaten in Turkey on several holidays there, I can say with some confidence that it is authentic in every way. The portions were generous and well presented. All in all,excellent value for the money.
There is also a good selection of beers and lagers to choose from.
They advertise a Karaoke night each Saturday and regular Belly Dancing and Turkish Nights.
Children are also welcome until 9pm.

On 2nd September 2013 - rating: 8
