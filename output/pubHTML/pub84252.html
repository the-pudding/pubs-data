peter ashworth left this review about Picturehouse Cafe Bar

The Picturehouse Bar is a Grade 2 listed building that was once a cinema and is now also home to a music venue called Band On The Wall although the 2 are separated and you can go in the bar without buying a music ticket.The Café bar has a simple reasonably priced menu plus cocktails and beer. I think the opening times are 5pm till 12am and 5pm till 2am on Fri and Sat
