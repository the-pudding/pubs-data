Graham Coombs left this review about The Green Man

The Green Man has the feel of a small country pub that has been totally subsumed by encroaching industry, surrounded as it is by overspill from Heathrow Airport, commercial motor premises and other unpleasantries. The half-timbering develops inside, with many rather low and hazardous roof beams. Clearly once two separate bars but now all knocked through, it retains numerous little areas and various nooks and crannies, with nice old furniture. Beers are from the GK range - at time of visit IPA, Abbot and Specked Hen - and seemed in fair condition. Outside there is a large garden area if you can put up with the aircraft and other noise. Note that the car park is pay and display, with the charge refunded against purchases. Nice enough if you happen to be nearby but not worth a special trip.
