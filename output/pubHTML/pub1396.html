Quinno _ left this review about Golden Farmer

Another GK locals outlet (there’s a few round here). This one is a 1970s Swiss chalet look building. Busy inside with a decent atmosphere, so long as you can avoid getting a headache off the mad interior colour scheme combined with over-loud music. Pool table spotted. Two GK ales, IPA and a pretty terrible Abbot, served up by a chap who had ants in his pants. Retreated outside to the somewhat careworn patio seating. Could do better.
