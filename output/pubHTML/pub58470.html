Alan Winfield left this review about Dog and Duck

The Dog and Duck is a decent sized roadside pub that is situated on a very busy road to the south of the town centre.
Once inside there was an L shaped room that was smaller than i thought it would be,the bar was to the rear left,the seating was normal tables and chairs,the room was carpeted.
There was a seperate room to the rear right which is now in use as a costa coffee outlet.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Wychwood Full Brazillian which went down very well,the other beer was London Pride.
There was background music playing and quite a few people having meals.
The pub was quite smart but it seemed more like an hotel bar than a proper pub.

Pub visited 21/6/2014
