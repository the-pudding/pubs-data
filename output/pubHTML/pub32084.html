Alan Winfield left this review about The Clive & Coffyne

The Clive and Coffeyn is a black and white fronted pub that is situated next door to the Sandbrook Vaults.
Once inside there was a single L shaped room which had a low beamed ceiling,the floor was bare boarded and the right side which extended to the rear of the pub was down one step.
The seating was bench type at the front which was quite comfortable with normal tables and chairs.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Sharps Doom Bar which was drinkable but pretty bland,the other beer was Bombardier.
This was a decent enough pub that was let down by the choice of beer on the bar,the pub was also almost empty on my mid saturday afternoon visit.

Pub visited 15/6/2013
