Oggwyn Great left this review about The Wrekin Giant

The first pub to open on the new Southwater development in Telford Town Centre , Southwaters aim is to try and make the souless out of town shopping area more like a proper town centre .

The pub itself is one large room divided by various wooded slated walls , think IKEA in 1975 .
Service from the young bar staff was fine , the one older member of staff was obnoxious , shouting at people if they dared even look at a table with a reserved sign on it , i think he may have been a Sailor with many people saying he was an Anchor .

Usual Hungry Horse cheap meal deals and very busy on a Friday evening although far to many kids shouting and running round for my liking .
Usual keg beers and 3 hanpulls with Old Spekkie Hen , Abbott and GK IPA which was suprisingly OK .
Not really somewhere i would normally head for , but it does its job.
