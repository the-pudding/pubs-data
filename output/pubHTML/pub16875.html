E TA left this review about Dover Castle Inn

A very busy roadside Shepherd and Neame pub with a lively local clientele. There are 2 large bars at the front and a large extension at the side serving formulaic pub menu food, which is the business's main effort. Both bars have open gas fuelled fireplaces, there is a redundant dartboard, gamings machines and a tv screen. Service was very inefficient, and it was clear that locals got priority, which I found a bit rude. Four ales on, I eventually managed to get a pint of Canterbury Jack which was in very good condition. Worth stopping for s snack and a pint if passing and you aren't in a hurry.
