Al Bundy left this review about Skipton Sound Bar

A novel little place. This bar is set in a record shop with the bar to the rear and vinyl records and cds for sale surround you. 2 handpumps on the bar with beers from Baildon on today. The walls are plastered with lp covers. There is a 'DJ' who plays records from the little counter to the right. Sadly it was awfully dull Pink Floyd nonsense on my visit. I did ask for Half Man Half Biscuit but it was not to be. A good place and I liked it, apart from falling asleep whilst Shine On You Boring Diamond was being played.

Lester Ford left this review about Skipton Sound Bar

A review from my visit in July 2016.

It would be rude not to visit all 4 of the Skipton micro pubs while staying here for 2 nights, so mid afternoon on Thursday the wife and I made our way to the Skipton Sound Bar.

As stated by another reviewer, this is a record shop with a dj playing all types of music, the small bar at the back of the shop has 2 hand pumps & a selection of bottled ales. One hand pump had Ilkley Pale on while the other had Wensleydales' Sound Ale @ 4.1% which was my choice. £3.20 a pint here, Early Doors is 60p a pint cheaper than this and the other micros in town.

A good mix of punters during our 30 minutes stay, good music playing and well kept ale...what a cracking idea for a Micropub...superb in my book and worthy of a visit

On 5th April 2017 - rating: 9
