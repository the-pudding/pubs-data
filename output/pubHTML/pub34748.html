Real Ale Ray left this review about The Artichoke

A very good rural pub and well worth a visit. You enter the pub into a long lounge and bar area, down a couple of steps and you see their tap room through some windows and turn left into the dining area. There is an inglenook fireplace, stone flagged floors and lots of horse brasses on display. Nine ales on offer, four on handpump and five from the tap room. I went for the Lacons Encore from Gt Yarmouth and Hook Norton Cotswold Lion.
