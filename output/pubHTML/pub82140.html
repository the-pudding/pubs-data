Alan Hurdle left this review about Rafters Bar@Holt Lodge Hotel

Rafters is a modern upmarket bar which is part of a large hotel and restaurant complex. Guest Real Ales are typically from small breweries like Purple Moose and Stonehouse. The hotel has a large attractive garden area.
