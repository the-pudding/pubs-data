Graham Coombs left this review about The Fintry Inn

Gorgeous little traditional village pub in remote area, which brews its own beers. Unfortunately I only had time to pop in for a quick sample, but the beer was great and the pub seemed excellent. Food looked good too. On the basis of a very brief visit, well worth returning.
