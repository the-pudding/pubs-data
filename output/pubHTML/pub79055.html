Steve C left this review about Bar des Voyageurs

Bar des Voyageurs is the landside bar at Luton airport. It’s not the biggest place and the bar in the right corner supports a few draught products. There are a couple of fruit machines to give an authentic pub feel, but that is where it ends. There is some seating outside where one can sit next to the hordes of travellers that are wafting down their overpriced Burger King meals.
Open from 6am until 22:00 and in my opinion is marginally favourable to Est Bar airside.
