Brian Llewellyn left this review about The Railway Tavern

Still going strong even though a few pubs round about have closed down. Real Traditional pub with a wide range of beers, lagers, ciders and at least one real ale that changes regularly. Still no food but well behaved Kids are now welcome in the lounge and it is now dog friendly. Live bands are making a come back most weekends and it is FREE entry.
