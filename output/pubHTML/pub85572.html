Reviews of The Old Coffee Tavern (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Al Bundy left this review about The Old Coffee Tavern

This pub, to me anyway, had the feel of an ex-Irish style bar. A large single room with high ceilings and wood throughout. A beer garden is to the rear. Situated just off the market square and cheaper than those there, minus the Wethers of course. Up to 5 real ales available. Nice enough without being great.
