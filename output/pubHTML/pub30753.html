David Errington left this review about The Old Reindeer Inn

Visiting from Huddersfield, I found this pub in The Good Beer Guide, and thank them for the recommendation. The cask beer was as good as they said it was. Being new to the brew I was offered a 'taster' (ask for one if you don't know the brewery). The Carvery was excellent and according to my partner 'the fish and chips are as good as ANY I've had in either Whitby or Scarborough.' (and she's eaten plenty in both resorts - don't tell her I said that!) Altogether a very enjoyable experience in a lovely pub. We shall definitely re-visit.

On 24th February 2010 - rating: 10
