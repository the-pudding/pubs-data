Graham Coombs left this review about Buchlyvie Inn

Good locals' pub with a good traditional feel, partly due to general lack of unnecessary modernisation. The smallish bar has some leatherette seating to one side, while to the side is another room laid up for dining. One handpump with changing guest ales, and the food sounded good but not sampled. Worth checking out.
