Old Boots left this review about The Green Dragon

Tiny tap room to side of bottle shop. It seats about 10 at a pinch with 3 or 4 more standing if they're thinnish. Decor of portraits with animal heads. 5 key keg taps of ever changing lovely beers.

Delboy 20 left this review about The Green Dragon

Tiny tap room attached to the well stocked bottle shop. There were 4 unusual beers on when I visited and I went for one from Tiny Rebel. If you are looking to try something different this is the place for you but check the opening times!

Danny O'Revey left this review about Green Dragon Ale House

Tiny bar in shop unit adjoining the bottle shop. Its purely a small room to show case 3 different beers via key keg system in connection with the shop next door.

Open Friday & weekends. One for beer fans
