Gill Smith left this review about The Angel

Community pub in the 2013 Good Beer Guide. They had Tetley, Taylor Landlord, Copper Dragon Golden Pippin and another, Black Sheep I think on so we chose the Golden Pippin and Landlord which were fine. I loved wandering round the pub chuckling at the saucy seaside postcards, loads of them in frames of 6 upwards adorning the walls. It was like going back in time when we were allowed to have a laugh without worrying about upsetting anyone's morals. Would have loved a day here to go round and see them all.

ROB Camra left this review about The Angel Inn

We've visited this pub on several occasions before, but we had a strange visit a couple of weeks ago. There's a horseshoe shaped bar and lots of bench seating around the walls, most of which is so narrow it's hard to sit on. It's very much a locals local. A couple of cask ales on the bar, including a decent pint of Copper Dragon Golden Pippen. The red wine is in small bottles, but it's Jack Rabbit which suits the other half down to the ground. They have a large sign which says that children are not welcome at any time. When we called in there was a birthday party on and the place was full of kids!! Poor visit this time round, but we'll probably call again as it's not usually this bad.

On 18th March 2011 - rating: 4
