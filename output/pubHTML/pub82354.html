Colin Walker left this review about The Bridge Inn

The Bridge Inn is a former coaching inn situated at the far western end of Linlithgow at Linlithgow Bridge. There is a public bar and a lounge bar restaurant. There is car parking at the rear. There is plenty of information on the wall pictures of a historical interest.
