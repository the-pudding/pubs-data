Reviews of Tap & Kitchen (Average Rating: 9 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Gill Smith left this review about Tap & Kitchen

What a brilliant place this is. Not far from Nene brewery shop this bar and restaurant is a delight for ale lovers. 10 keg taps and 8 cask which had 6 beers and a cider on yesterday. A notice said 4 thirds for £6 so tried 3 Nene brewery cask ales and 1 keg. They were placed in a small wooden box with the beer names entered on the side. All good. The bar itself has 2 main areas, one with seating for drinkers, and the other side set up for diners. A door leads out to the wharf itself with plenty of seating. It is a short walk to the Nene Valley brewery shop where there are fridges full of bottles and cans, plus more of their beer on gravity dispense.

On 2nd December 2018 - rating: 9
