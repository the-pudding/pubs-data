moby duck left this review about The Victoria

Opened out large roadside pub,wooden floor, normal tables, chairs and barstools plus some upholstered bench seating. three pumps with just Doom Bar available as well as Old Rosie Cider. Friendly landlord but the place was empty on a midweek afternoon.

Quinno _ left this review about The Victoria

Seems to have changed hands more often than a decimal day tuppence. When it's on form it's a nice little place, usually serving some decent real ales and worth making the time for if you're in the area. It's a big 1920's construction and is very much a locals pub. Low rating reflects the up/down nature of the place.
