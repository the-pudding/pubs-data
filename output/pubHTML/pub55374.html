Alan Winfield left this review about The Chequers Inn

The Chequers is a nice looking pub that was a short walk from the middle of St Neots.
Once inside there is a small square shaped bar room which is carpeted and has normal tables and chairs,the bar faces inwards,there is a small dining room up one step to the left,empty on our Saturday dinner visit and one more room past this to the far left.
There was one real ale on the bar,i had a drink of Shepherd Neame Spitfire which was a decent drink.
The bar room looked quite nice and was very busy with older drinkers.
This is a pleasant pub that is a very short walk from St Neots town centre.

Pub visited 4/6/2016

Nigel Sheppard left this review about The Chequers Inn

This is a lovely old coach house converted into a restaurant and bar. Still retaining much of the original old world charm and selling a wide range of well served beers from 3 pumps.

Well worth a visit on a cold winter night, sit beside a large open log burning fire and sample some excellent ales.
