ROB Camra left this review about Gasworks

New brewpub opened by the team that own the Dockyard pubs, which is fairly obvious when you look at the decor i.e. fairly minimalist with an industrial feel. I've been in here 3 times recently, so I thought I'd better get around to reviewing it. The brewery itself is to the right in its own glass area. Be aware that as you approach the bar the cask ales are all to the left hand side, partially hidden by a pillar. The centre of the bar is all craft keg at stupid prices e.g. £4.50 for a 2/3rds measure of Vocation. Only one of their own brewery beers available when I've been in, but there are 5 others to choose from. I've had a few in here and they've all been well kept. Cask ales are £3.50 per pint which is very reasonable for central Manchester. A very good addition to the Manchester beer scene.
