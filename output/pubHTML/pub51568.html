Colin Walker left this review about Gilmours Bar

GIlmours sits on the corner of the Main Street and hidden from view from the main traffic which passes the centre of Stenhousemuir by the B905 / King Street. This is the nearest pub to the superstore. The pub has a good-sized horseshoe bar, selling John Smiths and Carlsberg at £3.00p. The pool table takes up a lot of the open bar floor, although it does not encroach on those at the bar, customers sitting near the pool table have to move out of the way. The lounge has a separate bar. Parking is available on the other side of the Main Street from the pub in the superstore car park side.
