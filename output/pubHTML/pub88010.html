Jonathan Wilde left this review about Kings Steak & Burger Bar

The name defies what this place is. It's a pub with a stupid name trying to attract dinners when it isn't as it is branded as. Full of people drinking and two people slopped off to eat something while I was sat watching everybody just order more drinks.

When asked if we wanted food on entry as non locals it seemed if we said yes it would be an inconvenience over seeing the takings increase.
