Alan Winfield left this review about Friendship Inn

The Friendship Inn is a nice looking pub that is situated on the main road through Laughterton.
Once inside there was a single room which was carpeted and had a beamed ceiling,there was some comfy bench seating to the front,a TV to the front was showing the Man Utd v Swansea game,the rear left had a pool table and darts board.
The pub is a Marstons tied house,there were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Pedigree which was a nice drink,the other beer was Hobgoblin.
Food was available but there were no diners in on our Saturday dinnertime visit.
I quite liked this proper pub.
