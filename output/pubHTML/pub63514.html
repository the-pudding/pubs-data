Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Old Oak

Much to my surprise, this fairly basic corner now serves a real ale - a pretty ordinary pint of Youngs Ordinary (£2.80), but at least that is progress... A reasonably-sized bar with a couple of interesting features, but very much a locals pub so you might not feel too comfortable popping in as a stranger.

Steve C left this review about Old Oak

This is an unspectacular local's boozer with no real ale and only the usual suspects on draught. The Guinness was good and the barman was friendly enough, but there are better pubs in the area.
