Alan Winfield left this review about The Dolphin

I thought this pub was going to be a fizz house it looked very basic from the outside and it still had the Courage cockerel sign outside.
Once inside this small pub it was fairly basic but there was one handpump on the bar and this had Moles Mole Catcher on this drink tasted alright but was slightly too warm for my liking.
