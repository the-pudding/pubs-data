Graham Coombs left this review about The Surrey Arms

Pretty standard Stonegate clone of a Wetherspoon's, although not quite pulling it off. The large modern bar opens out into a brighter area with skylights behind, with a further separate "Games Zone" room behind that containing darts and pool, although the latter was covered in boxes so unclear how well used this is. Real ales available at time of visit were Ghost Ship, D*** B** and Twickenham Grandstand, which was in fair condition (Cask Marque listing). Overall, not unpleasant but wouldn't be my first choice of pub.

On 8th February 2018 - rating: 6
