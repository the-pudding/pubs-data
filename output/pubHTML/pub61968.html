Beer Belly left this review about Kanes

Dark windowless bar. Hardly open. Maybe open for big Football matches. Beer choice is small with silly price differences for anything decent. Charmless bar staff. A refurbishment moved the previously accessible toilets downstairs into a cellar and the removal of a smoking area around the back.
Lots of closures and unreliable opening times mean there is a void of open Pubs between The Varsity, Pier & Academy. And means this Pub is visited due to others being shut.

On 10th June 2016 - rating: 2

Quinno _ left this review about Kanes

Small two-floored drinking den with a teeny-weeny bar that serves lager, cocktails and spirits only – no real ale. Dart board and pool table downstairs. TV upstairs. Music often loud.
