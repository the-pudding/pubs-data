Al Bundy left this review about The Telford Inn

A pleasant pub at one end of the Pontycysstllle (or however you spell it) aqueduct, the other being The Aqueduct Inn on the other side. It's a multi-roomed pub with about 5 rooms. The bar is split between 2 rooms with 2 real ales available in the room to your right. These were Telford's Tipple by whoever and Llangollen Llangollen Bitter. As the previous reviewer said its by the canal basin so expect lots of Captain Pugwash's talking narrow boat and lots of tourists talking either Chinese or Japanese amongst other languages.

James Joines left this review about The Telford Inn

Pleasent pub next to the canal basen and a short walk from the aquduct.
Good fire burning and only down side the Real Ale was not available.
Food not cheap but big portions.
Ideal location to pick up a narrow boat.
Car Parking available although can get very busy in the simmer months.
