Lenny Mullan left this review about Port Logan Inn

This place has the rudest staff I have ever encountered. I was witness to the landlady shouting and shushing a BABY. This child wasn't crying but giggling. It was 3pm ....and the place was near deserted. It was easy to see why. Talking later to some of the locals and looking online this is not the first time that the service has been atrocious. These people give Scottish tourism a bad name.
GIVE THIS PLACE A MISS
