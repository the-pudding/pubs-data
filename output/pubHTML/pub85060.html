Mark Davey left this review about Quarter Bar & Lounge (London Bridge Hotel)

We stopped in the London Bridge towards the end of 2017 and popped into the Quarter Bar one evening. It is a very well appointed, swish hotel bar but has no real ale on offer. Having settled for a couple of Gin & Tonics, we left to find somewhere better.
