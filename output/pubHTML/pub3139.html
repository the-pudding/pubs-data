Alan Winfield left this review about White Horse

The White Horse is a stand alone pub.
Once inside the front bar runs across the front of the pub it is L shaped,the room was carpeted and had normal tables and chairs,there was a pool table to the right,there was seperate lounge to the rear but i did'nt look in this room.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Wadworth St George and the Dragon,this was a nice enough beer,the lounge had a different beer on but i dont know what it was.
The pub was far too quiet for my liking.
