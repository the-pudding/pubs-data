Real Ale Ray left this review about Warkworth House Hotel

The bar was situated on the left of entrance. This was modern in style with the bar counter along the rear wall. There were three handpumps with two ales on, I was happy to see they had Conwy Brewery Beachcomber Blonde, which was excellent. We visited the next evening to find Hadrian & Border Farne Island, another good beer and again well kept. This is a dog friendly hotel, so we did come across a lot of pooches around the place.
