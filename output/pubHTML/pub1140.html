Will Larter left this review about The Old Farm Inn

A cosy pub with a traditional front bar, with a very welcome solid fuel stove. The small bar counter had four hand pumps with Fullers London Pride, ESB and HSB available at the time of my visit. I went for the HSB, which was in good condition and helped to revive me after my bike ride from Cheddington (the fire probably helped a little, too). The bar manager (presumably no relation to the previous reviewer, below) was a young New Zealander and was enthusiastic and welcoming. This is a pub that I would be happy to visit regularly if I lived nearby.

Date of visit: 1st March 2016
