Alan Winfield left this review about Halfway House

The Halfway House was a fairly large victorian built pub that looked fairly grand in its setting among terraced houses.
Once inside the pub had been opened out into a single room which was fairly large,but it did have different drinking areas.
The pub was a Whitbread tied house when i went in it,there was one real ale on the bar which was Boddingtons bitter,this drink was ok but nothing special.
With this pub looking quite nice from the outside,the inside was a bit of a dissapointment.
