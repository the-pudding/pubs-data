Komakino . left this review about Los Reyes

Now a tapas restaurant that retains a nod to the former pub name (Los Reyes = The Kings) this does have a small bar counter where you can just grab a beer, albeit no cask stuff. Estrella Damm and three from Meantime - Pale Ale, Lager and Yakima Red (£3.00 for a half of the Pale). Not really geared up for our sorts and my visit saw them closing, but I was made to feel more than welcome, which is more can be said for many 'proper' pubs I've visited - suffice to say I won't be setting foot in here again.

Alan Winfield left this review about Los Reyes

Los Reyes is situated in the middle of Hitchin.
Once inside the bar is to the right of the entrance,there is a small area for drinkers which has one normal table and chairs and a pew seat,there is a room to the right which is set aside for diners and has normal tables and chairs.
There were no real ales on the bar,ii had a half of Estrella which cost £3,the pints were £6.
Crap pub and expensive drinks.
I would have liked to have done this place when it was The Kings Arms.

Pub visited 17/6/2017
