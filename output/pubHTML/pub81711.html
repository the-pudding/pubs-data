Quinno _ left this review about Beech Tree Inn

Not really a pub, more a restaurant bar to cater to the West Highland Way walkers and family outings. Main selling point is the lovely large garden with kids play area and menagerie/petting zoo. The main bar inside is pretty small and has no ales, but a good selection of bottles were spotted. Beware inflated prices, my sensible walkers choice of a can of Irn Bru set me back £1.75 – sensible only in the fact that they do not sell alcohol before 12.30!

Graham Coombs left this review about The Beech Tree Inn

Clearly aimed at families, walkers, cyclists etc rather than drinkers, this establishment has a small café bar, a large conservatory and a vast outside area complete with menagerie. No real ale.
