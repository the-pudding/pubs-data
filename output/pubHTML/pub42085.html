Gill Smith left this review about Pillar Of Salt

Popular locals pub with large screen showing sky sport, plus TV's showing the same. There was table football, game machine, juke box, pool table and darts there. They had Banks Mild and Bitter, Wychwood Hobgoblin and Ringwood Boondoggle which was just about ok I suppose. Car park and outside drinking area.
