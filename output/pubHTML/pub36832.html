Real Ale Ray left this review about Monkseaton Arms

A decent sized pub, hosting a busy patio garden area on our visit. The left hand side of the pub hosted a conservatory, along with lounge area and then a good sized lounge with bar. These areas were modern in décor, but kept with a traditional feel. I thought that was about it, until I found another large room on the right of the main door entrance, this was the original bar side, along with another bar counter.
Four handpumps serving TT Landlord, Purity UBU, Doombar and London Pride.

On 11th August 2019 - rating: 7
