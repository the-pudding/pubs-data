David Ross left this review about The Three Reasons

The slogan painted on the outside wall "Good Beer, Good Food, Good Times" explains the rather unusual name. I can only really vouch for the beer as I was only there for half an hour, but my pint of Guinness, costing £3.30, was fine. One guest ale, Puffin Ale, is on hand pump, with Tennent's and Carling lagers and Belhaven Best also available. The pub consists of one long, narrow modern lounge with a couple of TVs. Food is served daily until 7.00 p.m.
