moby duck left this review about The Prince of Wales

Basic pub catering for all ,drinkers,family's,food,and football,decent enough if you fancy watching the football with a couple of pints but not a destination ,I would suggest for the serious drinker.Usually three or four ales on and normally the usual suspects,i.e London Pride,Tongham Tea,Timothy Taylors Landlord, Courage Best,all drinkable but of average quality.this pub has its uses but I wouldn't go out of my way.
