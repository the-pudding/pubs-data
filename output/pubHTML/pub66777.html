Pub go'er left this review about Bluebell

Nice pub, reasonably priced beer and good food/meal offers. On the down side with only one small bar the weekends can get really over crowded. You have to wait a while to get served, and the regulars that sit at the bar are reluctant to move to let you through. Lots of seating area inside and good pool table and skittles room with regular teams an matches. The garden is spacious but in the summer looks a bit like a camp site.

On 29th July 2010 - rating: 6
