Aqualung . left this review about Belgrave Arms Hotel

Like the Bannockburn opposite this is a small bar that caters for tourists and the few locals in this remote place. It's a small U shaped public bar with pool and darts. There is a tiny snug on the left as you enter. The solitary hand pump was serving Salopian Shropshire Gold (£3.40) which was reasonably OK. They also do bottles of Harviestoun Bitter And Twisted (£3.40). Once again this place was OK but with the solitary real ale it's not really my sort of place.
