Kirsten Corrigan left this review about The Cock Inn

Just found this little gem recently and was pleased to find a decent menu and very reasonable prices. We went to the Supper Club which is once a month on a Thursday and had 3 courses for £12.95 - including delicious homemade desserts. I couldn't choose so they gave me a little of each! Definitely planning to go back for a Sunday lunch soon.
