Alan Winfield left this review about Smiths Arms

The Smiths Arms is a small back street pub.
Once inside this pub was very basic and it was like going back in time,i had a drink in the bar which was smallish and if a remember righ it had a tall bar,this room was very basic.
This pub is a Charles Wells tied house and there was only one real ale on the bar this was Wells Eagle bitter and this was quite a nice drink.
This is a locals pub but i was happy enough having a drink in here.
