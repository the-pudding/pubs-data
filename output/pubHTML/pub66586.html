Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Brodies

Modern bar / restaurant overlooking the former North Dock. Wedge-shaped interior with anonymous furniture and decor, with one small alcove up a half-flight of stairs. However, in any semblance of good weather, most of the customers are to be found out on the front patio area. No real ale, and only a limited keg offering, so opted for a bottled Spitfire (which came in at an eye-watering £5.55). A bit hidden away so it doesn't seem to get too crowded - even late afternoon on a Thursday or Friday - but still busy enough with people who don't seem to mind how much they are paying for a dull selection of drinks.
