Gill Smith left this review about The Peacock

A few years since we have been to this Chef and Brewer pub which was rather quiet with only a few people enjoying the early afternoon summer sun outside in the garden. There were 3 real ales available, Greene King IPA, Silhill Blonde Star and a pumpclip showing Peacock which is brewed for the pub by Greene King. Enjoyed the Silhill beer.

John Horton left this review about The Peacock

Pleasent popular Free House just south of city boundary in Warwickshire. Several very good real ales, good food. Has an odd dress code - no baseball caps. Large car park adjacent & very nice garden. Next to ancient Roman Road which is now a very narrow lane.
