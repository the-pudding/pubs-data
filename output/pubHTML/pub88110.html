Colin Walker left this review about The Springkerse View

The Springkerse View is a Greene King pub. On draught there is Foster's, Tennent’s, Amstel, Stella Artois, San Miguel Grundy, Peroni, Guinness & Belhaven Best. Ciders are Brothers Rhubarb & Custard, Kopparberg x 3 types, Thatchers Haze, Bulmers & Skal Rhubarb & Pink Grapefruit. Craft beers are Brew Dog Punk IPA, Hop House 13, Blue Moon & Old Speckled Hen Gluten Free. Scottish breakfast & Vegetarian Breakfast at £4.29 & go big for £6.29. There is also beer garden. Opening times are 09:00 to 23:00 daily and food is available 09:00 to 22:00. Plenty of car parking spaces in front of the building. Plus, great views towards the Wallace Monument. This is an estate that is still being further developed so one side of the building frontage is still on going.
