jimi allen left this review about Wavendon

Just been done out and very nice it is too cos it was starting to look a bit tired. Now selling Caffreys which was nice but no branded glassware for it (in the post apparently ....deduct one point lol). Lounge was packed out cos a wake was in so we ate in the little room. Don't know what Super Pints findings confusing about the menu. Lists what they sell and how much it costs and they don't have a specials board anywhere but barmaid says they're getting one (lovely woman who has been there for 30 yrs) Seen a few ijeets asked to leave and not return which makes for a nice atmosphere cos you're not sitting waiting for the place to kick off. Bar staff are canny; couple of them are a bit quiet. It's not cheap and they don't do offers on the beer so that tends to keep the ass*oles at bay which suits us.
