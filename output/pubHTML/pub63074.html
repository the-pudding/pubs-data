M O left this review about St. Clement's Working Men's Club

paid a visit a couple of times to this place, Found the interior to be very dated and uninviting. Having said that the service i received from the staff was pleasant enough although on one occasion it was tricky to get to the bar, due to people "hanging" round the bar (I know this isn't unique to this place). The regulars also seemed ok. The place just wasn't for me.
