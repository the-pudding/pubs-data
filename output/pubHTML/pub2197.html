Jane Clarke left this review about The Pheasant

Lovely traditional riverside pub. Emphasis on good beer,excellent service and high standards. No food; bring a picnic or order a pizza delivery and enjoy the fabulous garden. Comfortable ambience for ladies. (Female licensee!...) Well behaved dogs and kids welcome, on a lead and under control at all times. Several GSDs in residence; puppies from time to time. Regular customers are invited to help "socialise" the pups from the early days until they go to their new homes at 8 weeks old.

On 23rd January 2007 - rating: 10
