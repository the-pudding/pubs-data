ROB Camra left this review about The Elizabethan

A very large Lees pub that majors on dining. Inside are several areas all separated by glass partitions. Outside is a massive beer patio which can probably seat about 100 people. This is a massive draw in the Summer when it is a real suntrap. 6 handpumps on the bar but only 2 beers available Lees Bitter & MPA. The MPA was in decent nick and at £2.90 per pint a bit expensive for Less, but still cheap for around here. The whols place is a bit nondescript and despite the old brick exterior the inside looks like a new build. A pleasnt enouth place for a beer though.
