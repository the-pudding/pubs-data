Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The White Horse

Cosy single-bar terraced pub with bench seating around the sides, supplemented by some small tables and stools. Interesting old photographs on the walls. Up four steps and along a narrow corridor at the front, but a step-free rear entrance is also available. Four handpumps on the counter, offering Timothy Taylor Golden Best (£3.10) and Boltmaker, Leeds Pale and Jenning's Cumberland. NB - Opens at 16:00 during the week.
