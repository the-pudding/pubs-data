Komakino . left this review about The Pheasant Inn

A great country pub offering homemade pub grub and Donnington's ales x3 (BB, SBA and Cotswold Gold on this visit). A staple-shaped bar serves a roughly staple-shaped interior, with tables and chairs in front and either side with a pool table at the back to the left, and a sunken dining area to the right in what seems to be a newish add-on, although all the tables in the bar area had menus and condiments on them. A happy mix of local farmers at the bar, people (including young families) dining as well as solitary drinkers. Looks like it's had a bit of a makeover in recent years and some touches look a little too clinical (all wood panelling has been painted a duck egg green and the tartan carpet looks untouched by human hand) but this is a great find. And a pint of the BB and a packet of crisps came to only £2.75.
