Komakino . left this review about Revolution

This chain bar, eternally known as 'Vodka Revs' is located on Maxwell Road just off the High Street in Beaconsfield New Town and walking distance from the nearby train station. Another one of the questionable Pubs Galore entries, this is primarily a glorified cocktail bar, with an obvious on the clear Eastern European spirit, but does have a food menu and tables located the other side of the vast space between them and the rear wall-located bar were all set for dining. Predictably there was no real ale, but plenty of keg options such as Budvar, Mahou and Goose Island Four Star Pils along with some crafty stuff in bottles in the fridge. This place comes into its own in the evenings and there were various flyers and posters advertising 2-4-1 cocktails and Happy Hours along with a "Club Room" located to the left of the bar. One for the beautiful people of Beaconsfield (so not quite sure how I got in...)

On 30th April 2017 - rating: 2
