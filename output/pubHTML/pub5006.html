Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Waterfront Inn

Right beside Portreath's curious harbour, this pub is considerably more extensive than expected because the 'L'-shaped lounge bar / restaurant at the back is about four times larger than the busy main bar at the front. The appearance is all a bit seaside '70s, but that's hardly inappropriate here. There are also several interesting information panels explaining the local history. Two handpumps in each bar, offering three Skinners beers - Lushingtons, Cornish Knocker (£3.00) and Porthleven - plus Hobgoblin as a guest.

On 15th April 2017 - rating: 6
