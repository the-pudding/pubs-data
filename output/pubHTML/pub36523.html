Joyce Borland left this review about Northumbrian Piper

My friends and I went for dinner in December 2010 and wow what a change... I haven't been to the place for a few years and the minute we entered the property you could see it had improved... much brighter and the staff were happy and extremely helpful. We had meal that you would expect in a restaurant not a pub... Well done to the chef... No frozen reheated rubbish here. I would really recomend this venue for a truely lovely experience. WELL DONE NORTHUMBRIAN PIPER !!!
