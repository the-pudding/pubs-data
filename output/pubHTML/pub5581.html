Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Queens Head

Advertises itself as offer 'pub dining & rooms', so you probably know what to expect - an attractive refurbished hotel, with semi-modern furniture and decor in an upmarket lounge bar partly opened out for dining. Fortunately, however, there is also still a simpler traditional bar with a pool table and newspapers available to read. Two handpumps in each bar, offering Pedigree and Black Sheep (£3.00). Overall, quite a good mix of facilities.

On 3rd February 2013 - rating: 7
