Komakino . left this review about The Hampden Arms

Another rustic pub in the Chilterns wilderness that combines village pub and food pub well, although the space is rather confined, especially if the locals are in attendance. The bar is to the right, with the area to the left set for dining, a cramped bar area in front of the bar and a small anteroom to the right of the bar. Two ales on - Rebellion IPA and Chiltern Beechwood Bitter (£1.90 for a half and decent) with a further pump not in use.

On 8th March 2017 - rating: 6
