Komakino . left this review about The Bicycle Shed

A brand-new venture (October 2018) located next to a bicycle shop just before the retail confluence of Summertown. Styled as a vegan / vegetarian cafe / restaurant, this does have a distinct bar with seating and a dining area out back. Although there are craft keg beers (local ones at that) there are also three cask pumps which comprised West Berks Good Old Boy, White Horse Bitter and XT 8 Porter (£4.50 for a pint of the latter and decent). There's patio seating out front, although the Banbury Road isn't exactly an attractive vista. There were a few in on my Thursday arvo visit, so the hype seems to be working.
