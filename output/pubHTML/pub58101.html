Alan Winfield left this review about The Old Post Office

The Old Post Office is a large pub that i presume was converted from an old Post Office.
Once inside there is a large oblong shaped room which runs to the rear,there is a pool table to the front left,the bar is to the mid right and there is a decent sized raised area opposite the bar,the seating is mainly normal tables and chairs and bench seating with a few tall tables and chairs near the bar.
There were four real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Magpie Four For a Boy which looked like a decent drink,but tasted worse than doctors medicine,i shuddered every time i had a drink of it,the other beer were Robinsons Trooper,Hogs Back TEA and Pedigree.
The pub was empty on my pre 11am Thursday morning visit,but there was some decent background music the listen to.
The seating looked like it had seen better days.
The one plus point for this pub is that it opens before 11am.

Pub visited 2/6/2016
