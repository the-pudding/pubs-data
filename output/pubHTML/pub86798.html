Old Boots left this review about Boot & Rally

Odd shaped shop convertion into microbar. 4 pulls, 8 keg lines, two casks on neither of which I fancied, so an Atom Dark Matter on keg it was and very nice too. Bit of an odd shape - sort of a J with the counter in the long arm and some seating hidden away round a couple of corners. Lots of modern bench seating although the grey walls, Edison bulbs, cable tray and garden walls are still there. I like the Edinburgh style minimalism tables attached to some seating. B & W photos chart the renovation of the building. There's a lounge and the toilets downstairs.
