Old Boots left this review about Hop Lord's

Small bottle shop with 6 keg taps 2 large fridges of very interesting beers and seating on benches and stools along both sides. Not much to say it’s not a pub not even a micro pub but what it lacks in atmosphere it gains in the diversity of interesting beers.

moby duck left this review about Hop Lord's

Small bottle shop and taproom. Four keg taps serving on my visit, Plzensky Pilsner Urquell,Ridgeside Equator, Vibrant Forest Cambrian Root and Gipsy Hill Minnow, no real ale. Basic bench seating inside plus a table outside, two double fridges full of multicoloured cans and bottles with a small bar at the end of the shop. Decent enough small space but its just about the beer.

On 31st October 2018 - rating: 7
