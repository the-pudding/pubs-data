Quinno _ left this review about Old Waggon & Horses

The ‘unusual’ dark orange interior is actually pretty tiny belying the fairly large façade. The bar is situated to the right and the left is dominated by a pool table and fruit machine. As seems to be a Newbury speciality it was loud piped Chav music to the fore though the punters were fairly well controlled. Two ales on, a Brains rugby-themed one and Good Old Boy at a very reasonable £2.60 even if the quality wasn’t up to much. Friendly Irish (land?)lady behind the bar. Not exactly an experience but in Newbury it might be worth a look.

On 30th June 2013 - rating: 5
