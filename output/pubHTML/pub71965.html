Oggwyn Great left this review about Moody Cow

Friendly country pub , lots of exposed stonework in the cosy main bar ,there are two other rooms set for dining on large the other small and cosy .
It is very food led and eco friendly with most of the organic kitchen waste composted and used on the pubs own veg and herb garden , it also uses mainly local producers and they keep chickens as well .
Three changing beers on handpull a couple from Otter and Wye Valley HPA on my visit .
A decent pub and worthy of a longer visit , the food looked stunning .
