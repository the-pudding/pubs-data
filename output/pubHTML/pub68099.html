Ian Mapp left this review about Huntsman Inn

Hotel, restaurant and Bar. Looks like its had quite a make over recently as it was beautifully appointed. We went in the Walkers Bar. I ordered a pint of Down the Dragon from Greene King. And it was fowl. I am never sure with strange beers but surely the chocolate and chilli afternotes could not have left it tasting like vinegar. Unsure what to do, my mrs said I should take it back. I did and it was changed for a TT Landlord with no fuss. Which was perfect. Food was great, as was the service = so all in all, recommended.
