Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Molloy's

Not the Irish boozer you would expect from the name but instead a basic alehouse run by the Platform 5 brewery, featuring up to four of their beers plus Courage Best. I went for the Whistleblower (£3.25) which went down well. Apart from that, there is plenty of space around the 'J'-shaped bar for the advertised live music but also quite a bit rustic seating and a dartboard.
