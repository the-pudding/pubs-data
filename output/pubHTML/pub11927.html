Reviews of The Hanover Inn (Average Rating: 9 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Hanover Inn

Good-looking old pub beside the Old Harwich church, with a partially opened-out 'L'-shaped interior with the bar at the front, another seating area on the corner by the main door and more of a dining room further back. Very traditional throughout. Three of five handpumps in use, featuring two Green Jack beers - Trawlerboys and the fine Lurcher stout (£3.50) - together with Indian Summer from Box.

On 28th October 2018 - rating: 8
