Al Bundy left this review about The Phoenix Inn

A cracking pub just off from the town centre. Traditional looking outside and in. 2 rooms with the larger room to your left on entering and the smaller vault room to your right. Very pleasant inside the main room with 3 handpumps on the bar here and another 3, serving different ales, in the other. Therefore you have 5 real ales plus a cider. Well worth seeking out if you are in the area.

On 23rd May 2016 - rating: 8
