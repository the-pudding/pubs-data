Reviews of The Bull (Average Rating: 7½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Komakino . left this review about The Bull

A locally (or to use common parlance, 'community') boozer a few yards down the road from The Woolpack, set back from the road and hemmed in by housing either side. Two rooms - a Public Bar to the left on entry with standard wooden tables and chairs, dartboard and oche, jukebox, quizzer, TV and gambling machine. Lounge Bar down a small corridor and through a door, more cutesy and with some upholstered bench seats along with similar wooden tables and seating. There's also a decent-sized beer garden out back. Sharp's Doom Bar and a Harvey's Sussex Best which had travelled well (£3.70 a pint). My visit interrupted a game of Rummikub between the barmaid and the other sole punter to the background of a commercial radio station (Mix 96).
