Colin Walker left this review about The Slouch Bar

The Slouch Bar is one of the many basement located pubs on Bath Street. There is a large drinks choice with 9 beers on draught, 44 Bourbons, 4 Cognacs, 47 Gins, 17 Liquers, 48 Rums. There is a descent A la carte menu for food that is of very good value and a bar bites menu plus there are other menu option to try, check out www.slouch-bar.co.uk/
