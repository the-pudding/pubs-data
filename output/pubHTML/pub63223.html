Stephen Solar left this review about Valentine Arms

A lovely, traditional style pub with a comfortable atmosphere and the friendly bar staff greet you as you enter. Pedigree is the most common ale but there's a changing selection of guest beers plus a cider and a wide range of spirits. No food (but there's an excellent chippy round the corner). No parking available on site but side roads nearby if you don't mind a short walk. Highly recommended.
