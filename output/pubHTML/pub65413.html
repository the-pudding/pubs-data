Peter Edwardson left this review about Red Lion

A true classic, unspoilt rural pub. Basically just one room with a huge inglenook and a real fire in the grate. No food available. Just a small car park in front of the pub with no marked lines. Features in the 2011 Good Beer Guide offering Draught Bass, Worthington Bitter and a guest beer. The GBG and the Ordnance Survey give the location as "Dayhills".
