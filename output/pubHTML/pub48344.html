Colin Walker left this review about The County Barr

he County Barr sits at the top end of the Main Street in front of the old Tollbooth & the Stone of Mannan. Lots of lightwood panelling make the place very bright, a very basic thrills pub. There are plenty of Tennents beers on tap. There is parking available about the local streets.
