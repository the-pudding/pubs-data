Real Ale Ray left this review about The Scales

A traditional looking pub on the outside, but once through the doors you will find this to be very trendy pub. I would say that this pub had been recently refurbished as it was in tip top condition. The pub's layout consisted of an open plan room with lots of reclaimed timber cladding, colourful fabrics and feature walls in wallpaper, polished copper piping used for wall lamp fittings and a painted bar front.
The bar area was situated over on the right and had five ales on pump. The ale choice was all from Nationals so nothing over exciting there. The majority of the customers on this visit, had popped in for a coffee and a light lunch.
