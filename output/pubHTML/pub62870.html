Komakino . left this review about Three Steps

Light, bright, sports bar-esque and unsurprisingly, no real ale, so a half of Turkey's finest (Efes) it was. A Wetherspoons-style carpet runs throughout, football scarves are stapled above the bar, and there are two pool tables in a sunken room to the rear. The place was inhabited by a couple of clutches of local 'yoof' on a weeknight.

On 5th July 2016 - rating: 2
