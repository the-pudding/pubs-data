paul armer left this review about Fieldfare

Word of mouth is a wonderful thing ,having a fussy six year old and an even fussier 78 year old to feed we tried this pub and thouroughly enjoyed it not bad for a first visit,welcoming and friendly especially to families and the bogof offer on the menu is always welcome....
drinks are actually reasonable food well cooked and served bleeding quick which was a nice surprise as on a friday teatime in a packed pub we were expecting to wait for ever but 15 mins later! food!!!
pleasent beer garden and a small adventure playground topped off our evening, we will be going again!

Totally agree with Jonjo on this. If you are after a cheapish meal in a nice atmosphere with reasonably attentive staff then you wont go far wrong. Its not fine dining nor an old fashioned boozer. It has real ale which is always a plus in my book. One to take your partner to but not worth the diversion on a pub crawl.Mrs Phantom enjoyed the meal.
