Malden man left this review about The Oddfellows Arms

An old style community pub in the middle of Davenham village, diagonally opposite the more food-centric Bull's Head. It remains split into four square rooms, linked by an open corridor. The bar directly serves the two front and back rooms on the left as you enter. TVs showing football and rugby to an enthusiastic bunch on Saturday lunchtime. Various clubs and societies, especially golf with framed golf related pictures, a case of trophies and an honours board listing various captains etc. Some outdoor seating front and back but not today, it was very cold and wet. Fireplaces in each room but sadly not lit.
Weetwood Cheshire Cat, Pedigree and Sharp's Atlantic on. Quiz night and live music advertised.

On 25th November 2017 - rating: 6
