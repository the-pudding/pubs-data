Al Bundy left this review about Duke Of York

A large looking pub that's in dire need of a refurb. Its friendly enough but like most pubs in Eccles there's nothing to make you get a 2nd drink in the place unless you live here. Nice looking exterior, not so inside. No real ale.

ROB Camra left this review about Duke Of York

This is a very large multi roomed pub in the centre of town. There's a pool table to the left as you enter. There's a large bar with lots of bar leaners swapping banter with the barman. Sadly there was no real ale, so I had a half of Guinness. It's a very friendly pub, but the lack of ale made me move on.

peter ashworth left this review about Duke Of York

The Duke of York is situated in the centre of Eccles,with 4 drinking areas,high ceilings,three fireplaces and a collection of Victorian photos adorning the walls.After hearing about the refurbishment I was a little apprehensive that the pub would have been modernised,but no far from it,in fact in if you walk past the bar to the area on the right,it is like you have been taken back to the 1950's.Enjoyed my visit here, although I just found the Joseph Holt Black a tad on the cold side. Worth a visit
