Reviews of Duck Inn (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Ian Mapp left this review about Duck Inn

A tiny village and there is a C17th former coaching house. God knows what route it was on. Closed Mon/Tues and evenings only mid week.

It gets excellent reviews for the food on tripadvisor but we only popped in for a quick drink, mid way through a walk.

Only punters in at 7:10pm. Took the lovely landlady 10 minutes and three pints of foam to get a half of Wye Valley HPA. I think we were both grateful something materialised.

She made a real effort to chat to us and then three diners came in, to avoid the whole thing feeling like a visit to rarely seen relatives.

The HPA wasn't great - It could have been on for quite a while.

I would go back to check out the food!

On 28th June 2019 - rating: 5
