James Joines left this review about The George & Dragon

A light modern interior with a mix of stools around tables and comfy chairs/sofas.
Real Ale available Old Mill Bitter on my visit and two spare handpumps. Usual range of lagers plus Amstel and San Miguel on draft.
Food offering plus a pizza take away service.
Live music check with pub for details.
Large car park at front and a good size garden, childrens play area.
