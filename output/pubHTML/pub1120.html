Malden man left this review about The Wheatsheaf

We stuck our heads in briefly mainly as we wanted to get out of the rain but this pub wasn't for us, L-shaped room, loud boisterous clientele, pool table in the front corner and just the ubiquitous Doom Bar on. Karaoke and such rubbish advertised. Scaffolded outside yesterday but still open. BF's picture with three white vans outside gets it to a T. A definite no.
