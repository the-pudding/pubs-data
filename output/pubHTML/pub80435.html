Quinno _ left this review about The Brown Cow

A fairly charmless upmarket gastropub, with half the bar taken over for dining, meaning that most of the drinking-only punters end up standing outside. Just two ales on over three pumps - Wadworth 6X and a sub-optimal Twickenham Naked Ladies. Braying upper-middle class twits and their nouveau riche wannabe counterparts make up 50% of the punters. As Princess Ann’s Spitting Image puppet would say – naff.
