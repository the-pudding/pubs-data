Real Ale Ray left this review about The Old Restoration

A decent large corner pub that still retains the traditional inn feel about it. The pub is now owned by Butcombe Brewery who have slightly refurbished the pub to give it back some charm. Five handpumps with two from Butcombe, Liberation Brewery Sin Bin, Gower Brewery Up n Under and the fifth pump was a cider. The staff were friendly and chatty and our Butcombe Rare Breed was of the highest quality.
