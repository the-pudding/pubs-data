Philip Gaskin left this review about The Bell

I used to visit this pub quite regularly years ago and noticed it was missing from the site. There is a small public bar on the right hand side, with a restaurant on the left leading out to a covered patio with pond and waterfall. I have not rated it as I have not been there for some while.
