Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Sail Loft

Café bar located up a flight of steps behind and above the National Trust ticket shop by the harbour-front. No draught beer, but three bottled beers from Harbour Brewing in North Cornwall are offered; an Amber Ale (£4.50), an IPA and a pilsner. With no real pub on the island, this does at least offer some welcome refreshment to weary 'pilgrims' about to tackle (or having already tackled) the steep climb to the top of the mount.
