Alex Conway left this review about Victor J's

This is a small bar tucked down an alleyway in the centre of York. Inside it is hard floorboards and shiney table tops, the sort of place I hate. Beer wise there are two lagers Budvar (£4) and Becks vier (£3.95) as well as east coast ipa from green king which was £3.80 but not an ipa and really not to my taste much like most greene king beers, i wish i had gone for the budvar. There is also a limited selection of over priced bottled ales fro Yorkshire costing a painful £4.25.

There is additional seating upstairs that sometimes has live music playing but in my opinion not to a very good standard. This is really not a beer drinkers pub at all with stupidly high prices and has a very plastic dull bar atmosphere and the sort of people to match, really not worth a visit but they do have some beer so not the very worst york has to offer.
