Danny O'Revey left this review about The Gretna Inn

2 roomed pub on traffic island and opposite a shopping outlet village. Its very standard pub decor, 2 roome, though we were told the lounge is for diners only so the back bar is the drinking area but is mainly taken up with a pool table and dart board making it awkward for drinkers. There is some bench seating outside adjoining the island.

Very little choice of pub in this area and this one seems to be trying to cope with the locals and the weeding parties/tourists and sadly its too much and doesn't work. The space causes conflicts (like trying to reach the bar, or sitting next to a pool game. Add to this the lack of quality drinks (incl no real ale) and probably the most unwelcoming barman you will ever find you get an experience you would not rush back to.

On 27th July 2015 - rating: 3
