Colin Walker left this review about Doo'cot Road House

The Doo’Cot is aptly named with all its various open rooms, which are all suitable for different purposes. Such as function, private functions and relaxing rooms with large style armchairs all varying over two floor levels. Not all rooms are available at this time but the place is still moving in a forward open direction. The place sells of which there are plenty to choose from the Yorkshire based Samuel Smiths beers.

David Myles left this review about Doo'cot Road House

This Sam Smiths pub reopened on 20th April 2011 after being closed since May 2010 & a £2.5M refurbishment. 20 taps on in downstairs main bar, no handpulls. 2 other rooms downstairs with another bar upstairs. Dartboard in main bar. OBB at £1.88 a pint.
