Gill Smith left this review about Crown & Sandys Arms

Called in this pub as one of the three we were visiting in Ombersley. Lovely open fireplaces. A lot of tables set fot dining, but the real ales we had were good. Wye Valley HPA and Purity Mad Goose. Also on was Wye Valley Butty Bach. Lots of old photos on the walls. Large outside garden area both on grass, and a courtyard area.
