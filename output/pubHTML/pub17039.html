ROB Camra left this review about The Feathers

Newly refurbished and reopened. The floor is now all wooden pine look and there's some interesting stained glass. It was quite busy on my Friday late afternoon visit, but I suspect a lot of people were just having a look as it has only been reopen a week or two. Friendly staff and landlady (and landlady's mum). Sadly there's no real ale, you would have thought that they may have tried one or two to try and change their market, after all it closed as a keg only pub, so they need to try something different . I had a half of fizz and a pleasant chat and went on my way. I'll call in again next time I'm in Ashton to see how they're doing. It's good to see it open again and I wish them luck.
