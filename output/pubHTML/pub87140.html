Reviews of Hare On The Hill (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about Hare On The Hill

A small, brand new pub, owned by Ben from the Flyer in Rochdale. It's just one small room with a rather larger beer garden to the rear which will be a massive asset in Summer. Needless to say that the theme of the decoration in here is hares. There's lots of them about when you start looking. Only 5 or 6 tables, but they still do a limited range of food. Four cask ales served from taps at the back of the bar and the two and a half pints I had were in good nick as you would expect from the guy who runs the current Greater Manchester Pub Of The Year.

On 3rd December 2018 - rating: 7
