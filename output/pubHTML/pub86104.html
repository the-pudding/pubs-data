Old Boots left this review about Six Poor Folk

An ex-cafe now trading as a pub, it has a two level floor with the counter on a higher level by the side door, there are four pumps of decent locals Timothy Taylor Landlord (the only regular beer) plus one each from Vocation, Roosters and a foreigner from Wimbledon, some good (keykeg)as opposed to evil kegs and most beers were of a respectable strength, apart from the soft southern stuff. All are listed on a blackboard by the counter which is updated unlike some places. There's a varied selection of bottles and cans in some fridges opposite the counter. Wooden floors and leather bench seating in the lower section apart from two armchairs by the wood burning gas fire. A couple of barrels used as tables complete things. Pleasant landlord who is obviously keen on his beer. There's an upstairs room but not explored by me.

On 9th March 2018 - no rating submitted
