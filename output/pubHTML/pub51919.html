Alan Hurdle left this review about Owain Glyndwr Hotel

Old town centre hotel. Small olde world bar with a larger lounge area. The only "pub" open for a lunchtime drink in Corwen.
Meals served every day between 12noon and 9pm.
Real Ale is a recent addition to the Owain Glyndwr Hotel. The Theakstons Bitter doubles the availability in Corwen! The landlord has to sell Theakstons but intends to sell a guest beer as well if he thinks sales can sustain it. Real Ale is needed in the town for when the steam train arrives from Llangollen, as all Llangollen pubs sell it. Another point on the score for the ale!

On 28th June 2012 - rating: 7
