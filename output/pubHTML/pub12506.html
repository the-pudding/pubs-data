Alan Winfield left this review about The Bear Inn

The Bear is a largish pub that is in the town centre.
Once inside i went in a well furnished room and i think there may be more rooms in this pub but i did'nt look,there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Smiles bitter which was a very nice drink the other beer was Courage best.
This pub was very busy and there was also a few picnic tables at the side of the pub.

Pub visited 3 August 2002.
