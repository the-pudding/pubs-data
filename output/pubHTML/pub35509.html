moby duck left this review about The Three Horseshoes

A 17th century two bar pub,I didn't have time to investigate the back bar but could hear a pool table in operation,there is apparently also a pinball machine. in the front bar the floor is bare boards,a white ceiling with black beams, various seating and a TV screen show F1 practice on my visit. On the bar,three hand pumps, Doombar, Harveys Sussex Bitter and a Locale offering in Firebird Pacific Gem,which was a good pint.A decent pub.

On 2nd December 2015 - rating: 6
