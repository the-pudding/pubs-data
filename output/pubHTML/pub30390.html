Kevin Bradley left this review about Black Swan Hotel

This is a good traditional pub serving Marstons smooth and pedigree, also available on out visits at new year was Bank's bitter.
All of the beers were well kept.
We had the pleasure of an open fire and friendly locals.
The pub allows dogs and children in all areas. During our visits no food was available, not sure if they intend to start doing food or not.
We went on New Years day for a couple of hours, would have stayed longer but it was Kareoke night!!! however, we recommend as a tradtional pub with good company.

On 2nd January 2009 - rating: 8
