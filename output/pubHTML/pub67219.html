E TA left this review about No 69

Formerly Villiers, this small bistro-style bar (also referred to locally as Jayne’s Bar) with no real ale and a fairly unimaginative wine list. There is a main bar/reception at the front and a couple of smaller dining rooms behind. Food is of the standard bistro-style, while they will put out a buffet for private functions. All the tables were laid for diners, and I got the feeling drinkers were not welcome. Not sure this bar would go down well.
