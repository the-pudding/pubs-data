Gill Smith left this review about Hawkins Arms

Called in on Saturday while on a CAMRA coach trip of pubs on the old A30. This pub is open lunch time and evenings. It has an interesting interior with nooks and crannies and has a good food reputation. We enjoyed our Otter Bright and Skinnes Lushingtons here. The 85 bus running between Truro and Newquay stops close, but times need checking against pub opening times. 12-2, 6-11.

On 8th June 2017 - rating: 7
