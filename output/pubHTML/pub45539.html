Alan Hurdle left this review about Cain Valley Hotel

This 17th century coaching inn is one of the heritage pubs of Wales. The hotel has a wood panelled lounge, a public bar and a separate restaurant. The original 17th century staircase leads up to the hotel's accommodation. Real Ales are served and the lounge bar has its own good value food menu.
