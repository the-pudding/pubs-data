Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Kirklands Hotel

Eye-catching detached pub near the church, still offering B&B accommodation. Owned by Old Mill Brewery nowadays, the various seating / dining areas have been refurbished, but the main area around the bar and pool table has the character of a traditional local. Typical pub-grub menu. Three of four handpumps in use, offering the regular Bitter, Yorkshire Elixir (£2.95) and the stronger Black Jack.

James Joines left this review about The Kirklands Hotel

An Old Brewery Hotel with accommodation and a food offering.
3 Old Mill beers on hand pull in good condition in a large bar with a seperate dinning area and a small side room.
Lots of wood panelling and exposed brick work with a number of open fires for those cold winter nights.
Beer garden at the front and a car park.
