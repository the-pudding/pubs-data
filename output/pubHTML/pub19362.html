ROB Camra left this review about Royal Exchange Inn

Traditional corner local. The bar is facing you as you enter and there's a lounge area to the left. To the right is another room with a dartboard. This is another pub I used to play darts against about 30 years ago. It's a friendly pub in a blokeish, sweary sort of way. As the landlord/landlady live upstairs and have a dog I assume it's dog friendly as well. There's now a newly installed handpump on the bar which was pulling Sharp's Doom Bar. This is a fairly unusual beer for around here, unlike London which is awash with it. It was a good pint as well. I used to quite like coming here 30 years ago, I still do.

On 4th October 2013 - rating: 7
