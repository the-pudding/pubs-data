Will Larter left this review about The Dog & Bone

This is a lively back street local, situated mid-terrace but with a handy sign on the street corner pointing the way for anyone in doubt. The original two door entrance has long since been reduced to one, the former layout of public and lounge having long ago succumbed to the modern fashion of one room with a central bar. There are seven hand pumps, with four beers from Batemans including the Dark Mild, a guest beer (Black Sheep Imperial Stout) and two pumps selling cider and perry.

We enjoyed our visit here, but went on to call in at a couple more up the hill - in one of these, our previous visits were discussed around the bar, when we gave the Dog & Bone high praise, little suspecting that our interrogators were the landlord of this pub and his wife. I called back on my way to the station for another half, the intriguingly named Batemans Winter Chocolate Biscuit. Yummy!
