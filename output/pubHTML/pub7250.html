Oggwyn Great left this review about Three Horseshoes & Motel

Built as a farriers around 250 years ago the Three Horseshoes is now a popular dining pub with its own farm shop and even a small specalist chocolate factory , very foody the pub prides itself on locally produced seasonal produce , much of which is on sale behind the bar , the beer patio overlooks the chefs garden .
The interior is mainly given over to dining with a small area for drinkers , only one real ale a decent Pedigree .
I like it here its a bit different.
