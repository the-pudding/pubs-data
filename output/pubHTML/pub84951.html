Old Boots left this review about Venue Sports & Music Bar

Does what is says on the tin. It's open plan modern inside a stone skin. The counter is along the back wall with a pool table tucked in at one end. 16 keg founts in two banks, most are doubled up and all are national/ euro fizz brands. There's a solitary hand pull probably fortunately unused as it's not a cask beer kind of place. No music when I dropped in but all five big screens and the two small ones were in operation. There's also a dart board and a couple of machines for amusement. Seating on half round leather armchairs at glass topped tables or if you prefer at garden style tables and Chairs. There's something upstairs but I didn't look.
