Michael Wright left this review about Fox Inn

There was extensive flooding March 2014 and main A272, which is road to the pub, was closed in both directions at Bramdean, thus no passing trade for approx one month. The pub car park was underwater & pub sandbagged.

By April 2014 now closed & up for sale, car park still sandbagged but water has subsided from car park, A272 still partially flooded with traffic light control and pumping near the pub.

These events may be linked of course
