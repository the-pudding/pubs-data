Quinno _ left this review about The Cherry Tree

Characterful timber-framed Wadworth house with a few modern touches. Low beam interior with a rambling multi-room layout. Beer patio to rear. Selection of daily papers. Five ales on; Horizon (very good nick), 6X, Bishops Tipple and Henry's IPA, Irritatingly a guest (Bateman Valiant) was stationed round the other side well-away from being obvious as you enter. Nice place without overly exciting.
