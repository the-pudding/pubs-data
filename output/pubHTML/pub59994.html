Alan Winfield left this review about Flying Horse

The Flying Horse is a smallish looking pub that is on a narrow street in an old part of higher blackley.
There were a couple of rooms in this pub i went in a fairly basic bar but this was comfortable enough,this pub used to be a Boddingtons tied house and on my visit there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Boddingtons mild and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Boddingtons bitter.
I thought this was quite a nice pub and i think it is still open.

Pub visited 8/5/1993
