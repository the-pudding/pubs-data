Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Crown

Food-led Sizzling pub in the 'Northolt Village', with a slightly unusual arcaded facade. The interior has been extended with multiple seating areas, with pubco chain furniture and decor. Beer garden to one side. Three handpumps, offering Pride, Greene King IPA and Oxford Gold (£2.85). However, although hardly very inspiring, it is probably the pick of the limited number of pubs in the area. (NB - Review dates from August 2012.)

Eric Medcalf left this review about The Crown

Nice little pub rediscovered after 30 years. A village pub, off the beaten track but walkable from Northolt Tube Station. Clearly making a big effort to attract customers. Part of the "Sizzlers" chain with a wide range of well prepared food and real ale. Curry plus drink specials mid week. Had Adnams, Youngs and two others on the night I went. Plain clean interior with some interesting metal decorations and coal effect fire. Went in on a Monday night, looked like a poker game in progress.
Although plain from outside this pub is worth a visit - quite a village feel for this area
