Gill Smith left this review about The Rose and Crown

Called into this Marston's pub that was situated behind the bustling Christmas market. We chose the Lancaster Bomber which was OK and the other beer available was Pedigree. There were a few steps up from the bar area to the dining area which we found was strangely empty for a packed town and we could see a garden area with seating at the rear.
