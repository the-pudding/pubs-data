Will Larter left this review about White Swan

A smart modern interior is in contrast with the old fashioned external appearance of this market square pub. There's a small bar with three hand pumps, which at the time of our visit were serving Marstons Pedigree and two from the local Leatherbritches brewery. Toilets are upstairs - I didn't see a disabled alternative.

Date of visit: 5th July 2015
