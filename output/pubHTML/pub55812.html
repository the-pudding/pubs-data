Will Larter left this review about Platform 3

A railway themed bar within staggering distance of the railway station. This has to be the only pub I've been in where a miniature train runs along a shelf made from luggage racks. If that's not sufficient enticement, there are three hand pumps on the bar and a friendly welcome from staff and customers alike. Beers available at the time of our visit were Caledonian Deuchars IPA, Kelburn Red Smiddy and Loch Lomond Southern Summit. I called in on the way back to Dunfermline from Stirling, so as you can imagine I was a bit off the beaten track, but Linlithgow looks to be a very pleasant little town with a couple or three decentish pubs, so may be worth another visit sometime.

Date of visit: 20th March 2017
