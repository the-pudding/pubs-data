Russell Marshall left this review about The Old Bakery

I am employed by this fabulous pub but as there are no other reviews I thought I would let visitors know what we're about. We have 6 cask lines and 4 kegs lines. Although the keg lines tend to remain the same, our casks change on a weekly basis. We normally have a selection of Pale, Blonde, IPA, Dark, Red, Stout or Porters on offer. It's a small venue, tastefully decorated with a cosy and friendly atmosphere. We tend to refrain from playing music and have a selection of pub games for customers to play. We are dog friendly both inside and out and are currently working on a rear garden to compliment our fronted area of seating. We hope to see you soon!

On 2nd June 2017 - rating: 10
