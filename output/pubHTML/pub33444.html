Old Blue left this review about Millfields

Another hotel, restaurant and bar, but more of a pubby feel at the bar than most, and reasonably priced. The bar is decorated in red wallpaper with a gold flower print, and furnished with round wooden tables and upholstered chairs. Four handpulls on the bar are currently offering Timothy Taylor Landlord, Black Sheep Bitter, Thwaites Good Elf, and Tunnel Vision. My pint of Landlord (£3.30) was in good condition but served a tad too cold. The main courses on the specials board were priced at £7.50 each, with a curry night on Tuesdays and fish and chip night on Fridays. There is a pool table in the room behind, and the main restaurant is a separate area, a smart modern conservatory extension.

On 29th December 2013 - rating: 6
