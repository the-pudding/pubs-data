Quinno _ left this review about Hope & Bear

Currently being refurbished and rebranded as the Hope & Bear due to open early May.

October 2014
Formerly the student-oriented Upin Arms (and before that the rough-house Jack O' Both Sides), this large pub had a massive contemporary trendy refurb by M&B in late 2010 and has gone all upmarket - the interior is very tidy indeed, if somewhat dim. There's now lots of fancy expensive keg lagers (some quite rare ones too) along with five ales - which are usually well-selected but can be a little variable quality-wise which makes parting with upwards of £3.70 a gamble; get a taster first or go with a half. Service can sometimes be Spoons-esque if the bar is understaffed. Higer-end pub grub is available. With tighter quality control this could be a winner; at the moment it remains an intriguing enigma.
