David Ross left this review about The Gates Bar

A small, friendly local which, in spite of being a fairly ordinary pub, attracts a good mix of people, in terms of age, gender and football allegiance. Despite its compact interior, it manages to fit in occasional DJs and Karaoke singers, in the corner where the dart board hangs. The fenced outdoor section, to the left of the photograph, is more smoking area than beer garden, being tarmac covered. The upper floor looks like part of the pub, being painted in the same colours, but has lain empty for many years since it ceased to be flats.
