Old Boots left this review about George & Dragon

Pleasant 3 room village pub tied to Thwaites of Blackburn. Three pulls with Wainwright and Original, the third was empty but presumably might serve Bomber occasionally, Nutty Black would perhaps be too much to hope for. Thwaites kegs plus Wartsteiner, and a cider. Quiet but friendly place, the beer was a little lacklustre. Traditional comfortable furnishing, plain walls with some old photos of the village and locality, public-ish bar with open fire as you enter, a dining type room to the right and another bar at the back with a pool table.
