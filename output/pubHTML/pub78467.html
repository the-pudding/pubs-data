Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Piano Bar @ Holliers Hotel

Smallish but very prominent hotel in the old village, which has a reasonable bar to the left, opening out onto a nice garden area. The furnishing feels a bit 90's pvc-ish, but smart enough. A piano unsurprisingly provides the centrepiece and live music features regularly. The bar features a couple of handpumps, seemed to be London Pride and a local ale and in good condition. Behind the bar lies the restaurant and other hotel areas.

On 16th October 2017 - rating: 7
