Quinno _ left this review about The White Hart Hotel

Looks posh, enter through modern sliding doors. Punters a bit less posh than the other two nearby outlets. Nice front bar, room to right for dining less characterful. Football on the telly. Three ales; Hooky Lion, Adnams Bitter (decent) and Brakspear Ordinary. My Adnams came in a sticky glass. The weakest of the three we did that evening but a fair spot nonetheless.

On 30th April 2014 - rating: 6
