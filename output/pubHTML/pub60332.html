Dave Everitt left this review about Stix Bar

This is a very small and friendly Tapas/Med mix bar/restaurant. Fantastic staff, only been there for lunch suspect its got "regulars" and is treated like a pub as there are none in the new town.
Food is small dishes, order 8/10, its a nice change. Does sell a small but educated selection of beer as well as wine. I've been there twice in 2 months, by chance!
