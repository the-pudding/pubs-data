Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Queens Head

Quite a traditional building, with three former rooms opened out to form a 'L'-shaped bar. Refurbished with sparse dark decor and modern furniture. Pool table. Quiet. Unappealing little beer garden across car park. Live music advertised. Cask Marque accredited, but only one of two handpumps in use and that offering only a fair pint of Greene King IPA (£2.50).
