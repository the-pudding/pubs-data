Aqualung . left this review about The Queens Arms (JD Wetherspoon)

The Queens Arms is an original pub of the same name, albeit a fairly modern one dating from 1958. It's an L shaped room with fairly typical fixtures and fittings. It includes an extensive front garden / patio that actually includes some real grass. On a warm Spring Saturday this was packed leaving plenty of room inside.
The bar has ten hand pumps which had one unused, the JDW Trio Of Doom, Thornbridge Peverel, Spitting Feathers Old Wavertonian & Empire IPA, Peerless Red Wheat, Weetwood Oast House and Adnam's Two Brewers Irish Red.
I went for the Peerless beer which was in excellent condition.
I thought this was a good Spoons. It would have got an 8 with a more interesting building.

On 9th April 2017 - rating: 7
