Graham Coombs left this review about The Coach House

Set back above the road, this pub-restaurant is mainly notable for 'hot-stone' cooking, where your steak or whatever is served on a hot stone and cooks in front of you. The interior has been knocked through into one room but in separate areas, including a suntrap glazed area in the front. Furnished in a fairly modern style, but with odd traces of stonework round the edges. The small children's corner has a video screen. Has a couple of handpumps serving local beers, but at time of visit only one on and not in the best of condition. A fair number of tables at the front provide the outside area. Visit for the steak but not much else. Formerly the Coach House Hotel.
