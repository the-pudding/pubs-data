Ian Mapp left this review about The Baskerville Arms

Off the beaten track - we noticed the sign on the bypass offering real ales and pulled in after a day on the mountains.

One other punter.

Three real ales on - Butty Bach - a house ale and the one I went for - Otter.

Well looked after pint.

Photos at my walking blog - http://bit.ly/2awfDsQ
