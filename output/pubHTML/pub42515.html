Oggwyn Great left this review about The Swan Inn

Large Marstons Pub/Resturant doing cheap good value meals (2for£10) , inside its similar to many of these pubs that are popping up everywhere in the Midlands , open plan but with several distinct areas .
Fairly busy on my thursday afternoon visit , mainly with over 60s enjoying the cheap meal deals , the food itself is decent with large portions .
3 real ales available from the Marstons stable , Hobgoblin , Pedigree and an OK Jennings Cockerhoop .
Service was quick and friendly , not a drinkers pub but it does its job .

Marstons 2 for 1 pub eatery. Large carpark,small slope to enter make wheelchair access reasonable.Once inside it is not to cluttered but does have raised seating ares.Marstons and Banks ales The menu prices are ok if two of you are eating. Cannot imagine a single person eating at these prices but that goes for all 2 for 1 pubs.
A good place to visit
