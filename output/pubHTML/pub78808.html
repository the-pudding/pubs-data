ROB Camra left this review about Jam Street Cafe

This really is a cafe, with cafe style seating and menus but with 2 handpumps on the bar. I called in on a Friday afternoon at about 5:30 but the one person serving was far too busy cooking food to serve beer. "I'll be with you in a few minutes he said", "No you won't, because I won't be here" I said as I left. This is the trouble with this type of place, if they're cooking for a table of 4 they can't serve beer at the same time. De Nada gets the balance right, this place doesn't.
