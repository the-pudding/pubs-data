Alan Winfield left this review about The Beagle

The Beagle is a very nice looking pub that is in the western suburbs of York.
Once inside the main room is through the main entrance,this room is oblong shaped with the bar facing,there is a pool table to the right,the seating is very comfy bench type and chairs,the room is carpeted.
There is a separate pool room to the front left and a smaller room to the rear which had a TV on.
There was one real ale on the bar which was John Smiths Cask,this was a decent drink.
This is a very pleasant and comfortable pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 23/5/2015
