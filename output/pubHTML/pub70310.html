Alan Winfield left this review about Rose & Crown

This is a lovely looking pub with a small garden at the front with a few picnic tables on it ideal for a summers day.
Once inside the pub seems as it would have been years ago with small tables and stools to sit at there was only one real ale on the bar which was Marstons Pedigree i quite liked this pub and would have happily stayed for a few more drinks.
