Alan Winfield left this review about Jolly Miller

The Jolly Miller is an estate pub that is just off rochdale road.
This pub had a decent sized lounge and a more basic bar i had a drink in the bar and this was fairly busy.
This pub used to be a Greenalls tied house and it still had Greenall Whitley signs on the outside of the pub,there was only one real ale on here this was Greenalls bitter this was drinkable but nothing special.
I think this pub is still open.
