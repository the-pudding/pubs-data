Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Allotment

Backstreet local in a quiet area near the Grosvenor & Hilbert Park. It has been thoroughly refurbished, both inside and out, with generally light furniture and decor. Extra seating is provided in a conservatory extension, and there is a tidy beer garden to one side. Just one handpump on the counter, offering Ringwood Razor Back (£3.70) when I stopped by.
