OK pub if you stumble across it or happen to be camping down the road, but you wouldn't make a special trip to get here. When I stayed locally I visited here once then invariably drove in to Minehead for an ale. Pretentious and below average.

Paris Hilton left this review about The Exmoor White Horse Inn

The White Horse is full of the hunting, shooting, fishing types that frequent Exmoor; best not to mention that you are anti-bloodsports.

It has everything going for it, character, setting, landscape etc, but this pub could be a lot better than it is considering, so don't make a special journey. The beer was ok but nothing special; I can't even remember what I drank. Busy during summer weekends.
