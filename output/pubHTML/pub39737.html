Tel: 01798 342287 http://www.thewelldiggersarms.co.uk

A warm welcome awaits you!!

The Welldiggers Arms is a traditional English pub, full of old-world charm and character, set in beautiful Sussex countryside near Petworth. The pub offers a relaxed and informal atmosphere where you can enjoy a quiet drink or sample some of the fabulous food on the menu.

The pub offers a great menu of home-cooked, fresh food, much of which is sourced locally, and we serve real ale.

The bar is cosy and warm in the winter as the log burner glows and in the summer time you can enjoy the sunny garden with its wonderful views to the rolling South Downs.
