Helen Le Voi left this review about The Coastguard

Inflexible. Don't ask for anything off menu. Doesn't work if you have kids. Full adult portions or nothing. We paid £2.50 for some bread and got 5 tiny slices of French bread. 50p each!! Total rip off. Full portions for grown ups look good, but if you have special dietary requirements, kids or don't have deep pockets - avoid!

Manky Badger left this review about The Coastguard

I’d heard about the Coastguard some time ago, and this pub has been on my list of “pubs to visit” for quite a while. I suppose I should really have booked in advance.
The food was excellent, not cheap, but not as expensive as some places I’ve been to. But it’s not really a pub – more of a restaurant with a good bar. (I don't expect to have to book at a pub.)
Having said that, I’d certainly go back.

On 27th December 2010 - rating: 7
