Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about New Lanark Mill Hotel

Modern hotel bar housed in a former cotton mill forming part of the New Lanark UNESCO World Heritage Site. Tables set aside for dining, but there are also a few stools along the bar counter. No real ale, so went for the keg Caledonian 80/- (£1.80, half). Hardly exciting, and fussy service, but with nowhere else in the immediate area it does serve its purpose.

On 18th August 2013 - rating: 5
