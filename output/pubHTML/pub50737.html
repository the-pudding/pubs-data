Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Justinlees Inn

Large, food-led, outlet forming part of Orchid's All-Inns chain. Extended to the rear, with various seating areas around the central bar area. Light modern decor. Pool table. Three handpumps on the counter, but just Doom Bar (£3.00) available. All a bit dull.
