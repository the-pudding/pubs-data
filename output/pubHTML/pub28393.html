James Joines left this review about Black Boys Hotel

Hotel in town square with beers from Adnams and a food offering. My visit coincided with cycle racing and 2 beers were available from the cask outside the hotel with Southwold bitter at £3 a pint. Also tried a pleasent half of Fat Sprat on hand pull but at £1.80!!
