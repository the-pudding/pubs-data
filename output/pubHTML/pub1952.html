Quinno _ left this review about The Rose & Crown

A Greene King pub round the corner from the Edinburgh. L-shaped, a small bar to the right with the left being an elongated dining area which was surprisingly unkempt when you focussed in. The shade of red paint in here looked to me to be the same as that the Edinburgh, wonder if they clubbed together for a job lot? Points of note: horse racing on the TV and fairy lights strung across beams. It smelt of real fires but I only saw what looked like a gas one. Just GK IPA Gold available, pretty mediocre and died quickly in the glass, though Desmond reported that the lemonade was good. Very friendly service from both staff. Nothing to get particularly excited about here, really.

On 22nd April 2015 - rating: 5
