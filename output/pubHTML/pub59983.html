Rob Hunter left this review about Turing Tap

Visited mid December, Saturday evening. An interesting enough cask beer selection - two Christmas themed ales from Ilkley, a raspberry stout, and two ciders. I had the Holy Raspberry by Ilkley which was very pleasant. Unfortunately the same cannot be said for the service which was, frankly, miserable. Not helped by receiving an obscenely short measure that we had to ask to be topped up. With the exception of the doorman, who was nice and friendly, the rest of the staff we saw looked like they didn't want to be there. They weren't rushed off their feet either, so there wasn't even that excuse that they were overworked and harassed.

I'd go again for the beer, in the hope that the bar staff have either changed or are having a better day.

ROB Camra left this review about The Oxford

Many years ago this was a new build Hogshead when that chain was in its pomp. Now it's a bit like a small Wetherspoons with a very similar looking menu. There are 2 floors available for seating and the upstairs room has a pool table. There's a bar upstairs but it has no real ale and is only opened at busy times. 6 handpumps on the downstairs bar, but only 2 ales available. Hobgoblin and the rather more interesting Hogsback TEA which was a decent pint. Quite busy on my Friday afternoon visit, but this may have been due to a short torrential downpour. It's not great, but it's better than some of the other pubs in the area.

On 1st October 2015 - rating: 6
