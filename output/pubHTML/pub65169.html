Alan Winfield left this review about Marquis of Granby

The Marquis of Granby is situated in the centre of the village of Granby.
Once inside the pub used to have a few different rooms and we had a drink in the lounge which was a very nice room to have a drink in,this pub used to be an Home Ales tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Home bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Home mild.
This pub now has a good choice of real ales on including micro brewery beers it is also a favourite with Vale of Belvoir Camra members.
