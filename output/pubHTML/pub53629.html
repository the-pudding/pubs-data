ROB Camra left this review about The Horse & Jockey

A good looking part brick, part timbered pub on the main road. They have a sign outside saying "Try our range of cask ales". This didn't take long as they didn't have any. One pump unclipped, one with a turned around Hobgoblin Gold badge on it and one serving a real cider. Very disappointing. It's rather smaller than it looks from the outside with one medium sized room with a pool table to the rear and a huge TV showing daytime TV including adverts. It's quite nicely decorated, but in a very modern style, completely at odds with the outside. I ended up having a quick half of fizz. If they'd had any ale as advertised and it had been decently kept then this may well have been a 7.

On 13th July 2017 - rating: 4
