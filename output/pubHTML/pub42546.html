Jack Nicholson left this review about Blue Bell

Family and dog friendly free of tie village pub with pub games, sports TV, live music and a heated outside area. Recently refurbished to a high standard. Two real ales and a good selection of lagers. The car park, with CCTV, is not obvious as this is down a side street behind the pub.

Closing times are not set in stone.
