Graham Coombs left this review about Crown Hotel

large town centre establishment which felt more of a pub than an hotel, although it still offers accommodation, with different drinking areas arranged round an island bar and a pool room upstairs. Only draught ales on offer were Doom Bar and London Pride. Nothing especially wrong with the place, but it somehow lacked something.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Crown Hotel

Quite an impressive twin bow-fronted façade (albeit mostly hidden behind scaffolding at the time of visit). 'U'-shaped bar with a dining room in lower building to one side and a sports TV room to the rear. Some benches on front terrace a rear patio beer garden. Just Greene King IPA (£3.60) from the pair of handpumps, with a reversed London Pride clip also visible.
