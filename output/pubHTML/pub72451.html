Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Pheasant Inn

Attractive two-bar pub, with very traditional furniture and decor. Upper seating area beyond the public bar, plus a small dining room beyond the lounge. Patio seating out front (which faces away from the new main road which now runs behind the building). Two handpumps in each bar, offering Wylam Red Kite (which unfortunately went off during pouring) and TT Landlord (£3.30). (NB - Review dates from July 2012.)
