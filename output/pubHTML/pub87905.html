Huge barn of a place with chandeliers dotted around the ceiling. Bar to right as you come up the stairs. Mainly keg and very popular with the young. I will not be returning.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Cosy Club

New bar / restaurant housed in a large airy space upstairs in the former Corn Exchange - not only full of the usual Cosy Club random paintings and miscellanea but also including some large advertisements for old local businesses on the wall which look original - and altogether an impressive conversion. Two real ales available on handpump: Oldershaw Grantham Dark and Pragmatic from Beermats Brewing.

On 29th June 2019 - rating: 7
