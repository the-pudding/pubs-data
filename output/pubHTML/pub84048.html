Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Stable

New-fangled 'Pizza, Pies & Cider' house, with a square-ish bar at the front and a larger rectangular dining room at the back. Bare-brick walls, modern wooden furniture and industrial ventilation throughout, with a couple of small tables out on the pavement too. Features one cider on handpump, 12 more in boxes and some others available from the ten keg taps (which also feature a few beers, e.g. Five Points XPA, £5.50).
