ROB Camra left this review about White Swan

Another visit and another decent pint. It's nice to see that Pauline is still behind the bar she must have been here about 25 years now.

19/11/2010
Old stone built pub in the touristy bit of Hebden. The bar is to your left as you enter and there's another seating area to the right. It's another food led pub in the early evening with most people eating whilst I was in. There are 5 handpumps on the bar one serving a real cider. Inevitably they had Copper Dragon Golden Pippin on one of them which was a decent pint. Sadly they use the totally unhygenic and open to abuse return valves on all the cask ales. It's a decent pub that loses a point or two for the return valves.

On 31st August 2014 - rating: 6
