Quinno _ left this review about Hundred Acres

Another Bicester estate pub. This one is a single open-plan room with a raised area to the left. Looks like a recent refurb has occurred with gold and red leaf wallpaper, red pub carpet and mushroom painted wood. Usual estate pub punter entertainment - BT Sports, pool table, jukebox. No ale so half a Guinness sufficed. Later witnessed a toddler being sat on the bar where we had just been served, let’s hope the nappy was watertight. Needs some imagination here and a bit of oomph as there were only four other punters in on our Saturday afternoon visit whilst the White Horse, another estate pub in Bicester, was buzzing.

Komakino . left this review about The Hundred Acres

Another estate pub in the continually-expanding Bicester. This is also set on a retail complex, sharing a car park with a Tesco Express and a Chinese takeaway. A squarish room with the servery against the length of the far wall, this has a small, raised dining area to the right a mixture of tall tables with leather-backed chairs and a couple of sofas. A pool table occupies the area to the left and in a partial alcove behind the front entrance. No real ale available, so I had a bottle of Bud. Disappointing.

On 24th September 2017 - rating: 3
