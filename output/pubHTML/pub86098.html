Mark Davey left this review about Bendigo Lounge

Brand new place, part of the Lounge chain. No ale on the bar but a good selection of drinks and really good food. Its a bit of a cross between a Wetherspoons and a TGI Friday. Child friendly, open all day, a great use of an old McDonalds that closed many years ago.
