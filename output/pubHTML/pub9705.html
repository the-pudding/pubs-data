Aqualung . left this review about Greyhound Hotel (JD Wetherspoon)

The Greyhound is an original pub / hotel that in recent years has been added to the ever expanding list of JDW hotels. It's not one of their largest outlets and as noted below consists of a central bar area with several rooms surrounding it. There is a nice old tiled floor approaching the bar from a fire door that must have once been the entrance. There is a small patio at the rear.
The fact that the bar is so small is a problem as early on a June Saturday afternoon there were lots of people waiting to get served food and coffees although it did improve later.
The bar has just six hand pumps which had the JDW Trio Of Doom, Butcombe Gold, Dorset Brewing Citrus Maximus and Green Jack Gone Fishing ESB. I went for the Citrus Maximus (£2.49) which seemed in good enough nick but given the name was rather bland. I also tried their not so local from near Caerphilly Hallets cider (£2.69) which because of space constraints was not served from a fridge but apart from that was OK.
This was a decent Spoons especially compared to some of their duller shop conversions.
