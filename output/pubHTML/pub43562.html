Reviews of The Grey Horse (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about Grey Horse Inn

A good village pub facing the green, with a room either side of the small central bar. The interior is in traditional pub style, with odd bits of timbering and sundry random decorations. One side is more of a public bar with a dartboard, although not leaving much space when in use. The larger lounge side is slightly more comfortable and is tabled for eating, but not too committed to diners. Board games are available. To the back are a couple of upstairs rooms, one appearing suited to private dining and the other with a pool table. The beer range was fair, ales at time of visit being Barnsley Acorn, Marstons 61 Deep, Wainwright and of course Doom Bar, Condition seemed good. Food is corporate Punch Taverns stuff, quite adequate without being a major draw. Not much outdoor space, with a few roadside tables plus a smoking area at the back.

On 4th June 2019 - rating: 7
