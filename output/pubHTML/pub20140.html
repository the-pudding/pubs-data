peter ashworth left this review about The Balcarres Arms

The Balcarres Arms is an open plan pub serving food daily and has 4 cask beers on the go(The pint of Howling Wolf was spot on).The pub is quite well laid out with comfy seating,a couple of smart brick fireplaces and an outside drinking area and beer garden.On the walls numerous photos and prints show the pub and surrounding areas in times gone by including a photo of the Haigh Brewery which was a 5 storey building that stood directly behind the pub.
