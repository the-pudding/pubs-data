Graham Coombs left this review about Red Lion

What ought to be an imposing 3 storey building has been spoiled by a fire escape and other additions. The interior has been totally modernised with a bright feel and lots of light wood including what appears to be a brand new traditional bar, although the modern floral wallpaper is a bit of a distraction. A pool table and dartboard could be found at one end of the main room, although neither in use. At the other end is a dining room, with a rather unkempt garden area at the back. The food was a bit average but the draught beers were ok, being Bass and the local Mantle Cwrw Teifi. Needs to develop a bit of character and tidy up the rough ends but has potential.

On 7th July 2019 - rating: 6
