Tris C left this review about Phoenix

A very bland carpeted pub serving Black Sheep and London Pride.

Multiple plasma screens show both random sports - darts on my visit, joy of joys - or some inane music channel. There's something a bit Wetherspoons-ish about the place and most of the customers seem to be foreign tourists drawn from the local cheap hotels.

This really isn't my kind of place.
