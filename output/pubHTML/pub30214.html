S W left this review about White Horse

A traditional village freehouse pub with great food!
Recently refurbished, it has a growing reputation for a great choice & diversity of real ales, craft beers, wines and interesting quality
freshly-cooked food. All this in a cosy, friendly environment, with a beautiful south-facing beer garden modern seating on decking and grass.
