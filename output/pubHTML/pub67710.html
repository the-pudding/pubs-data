ROB Camra left this review about The Ship Inn

Two floored pub that seems to be totally populated by people called Julian & Emily, all of whom seem to think they own the place. Who knows? maybe it's a co-operative and they all really do own it. Apparently this is the "Handbridge Set" who meet here on a Sunday lunch. There were lots of interesting things around the walls and a roaring fire going in the grate. 4 handpumps on the bar dispensing some safe nationals plus a beer from the Three Tuns brewery. I had a pint of this which was in pretty decent nick. We then sat down and listening to the posturing, braying and hooting from the perma tanned blondes and their broods. It may not be like this on any other days and for the pubs sake I hope it's not. We won't be calling on a Sunday lunchtime again.

On 22nd November 2011 - rating: 4
