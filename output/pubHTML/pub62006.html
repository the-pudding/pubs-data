Alan Winfield left this review about The Sally Pussey Inn

The Salley Pussey's Inn is a roadhouse pub very close to Wooton Bassett,this pub has been extended over the years and the main entrance in now from the car park at the side of the pub.
This pub is now mainly food orientated with a large restaurant area with a carvery but there is still a drinking area and this was quite smart and had a modern look to it.
This is an Arkells tied house and there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Arkells 3B and this was a decent drink the other beers were Arkells 2B and a seasonal beer called Summer Ale.
This is not a drinkers pub its more of a pub to drive to have a meal and a drink.

Pub visited 8/7/2006
