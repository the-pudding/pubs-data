Colin Walker left this review about Caprice

The Caprice bar and restaurant is open from 9am for breakfast and throughout the day for lunch and dinner. The bar has wide varied range of Vodka such as Balkan 176, Broken Shed, and Cold River. A varied range of Gin, Malt Whisky such as Edradour, Glenfarclas, Springbank, Jura, Dalmore & much more. Some of the whiskies come in various ages. There are also many world-bottled beers such as Kirin Ichiban, Vedett, Viru & much more. The offer on the world beers is 3 for £9.95 and a free bowl of nachos all day and every day. There is also a heated beer garden to the rear. There is also a live DJ on a Saturday night. www. capriceprestwick.co.uk
