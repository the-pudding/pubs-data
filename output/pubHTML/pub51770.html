David Ross left this review about The Commercial Inn

A short walk from West Calder railway station, this is a traditional style pub with a semi-circular horseshoe bar and a friendly atmosphere, selling the customary range of draught beers. It makes a pleasant change from the modern bar/diners in the village's nearby big brother, Livingston New Town.
