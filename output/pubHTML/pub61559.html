Quinno _ left this review about The Bull

Large rambling foodie pub. Refurbished a few years back and now rather modern and lacking obvious character. Has three ales on - fairly mainstream choices (6X, Broadside and Courage Best) with the odd guest. The beers are usually too cold for me.
