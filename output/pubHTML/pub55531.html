Al Bundy left this review about O'Neill's

A large Oirish franchise. This one has recently had a facelift. Its a just below street level pub and is open plan. Its has 2 areas with the first area as you enter down the steps. This is where you find the curved bar. To right of the bar, down a couple of steps is the other area. This part has a stage for musicians. Lots of tellies. There are 2 handpumps on the bar but only Doom Bore is usually on. The rest of the bar is littered with shiny fizzpop taps. The place is popular of an evening. Not cheap either.

On 21st June 2017 - rating: 4
