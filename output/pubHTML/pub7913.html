Will Larter left this review about The Globe Inn

A striking street corner building in white picked out with black, and a pub sign depicting a vinyl record where the globe should be. On the bar were two hand pumps, serving Harbour Session Ale and Otter IPA. Service was a little slow, but the lass behind the bar seemed inexperienced and wasn't getting any support at a busy time - there were a few other Luton Town supporters here besides me. The beer was ok and I wouldn't mind coming back here another time.

Date of visit: 1st November 2014
