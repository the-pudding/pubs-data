Oggwyn Great left this review about Mytton Arms

Rural pub right out in the sticks down narrow country lanes .
There are two rooms to the front served by the same long bar , four real ales on from Hobsons and Three Tuns , my Three Tuns XXX was very good .
Suprisingly no food is served unusual in this type of pub .
Another good pub in the South Shropshire Hills .
