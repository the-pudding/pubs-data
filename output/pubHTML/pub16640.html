Robert READ left this review about Copperfields

I can't believe there are no reviews of this pub. I love this place. The carvery is fantastic value at just £3.69 and they are now doing a massive truck stop type breakfast for JUST £2.99. Nice bar and a good selections of beers and wines. Massive grounds and beer garden.. What are you waiting for?
Get down there and treat yourself and support this big pub so I can continue to enjoy it.
