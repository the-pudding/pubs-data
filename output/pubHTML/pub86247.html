Jonathan Wilde left this review about Walton Park Hotel (The Terrace)

Having being undecided on adding this a few years ago the local CAMRA branch went on a town tour and fully embraced this as a pub that's open to the public especially as this end of town is bereft of any places to drink.

The Terrace allows members of the public to enter without encountering anything awkward or hotel related.

As per website lovely views across the Estuary to Wales.

There is also more public bars within the building it seems so maybe need to return and visit the sports bar section of the Terrace which I clearly missed today.
