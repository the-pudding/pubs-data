Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Oggwyn Great left this review about Turtle Bay

Friendly Carribean Resturant/Bar , mainly geared for food although drinkers are welcome at the island bar in the centre of the resturant , very busy on my early Friday evening visit with a good mixture of diners and drinkers , the food is very good , i will recomend the goat .
Only beer on tap was Red Stripe but with a good selection of bottled beer including a very nice Jamacian IPA that matched the food and reggae music well .
Dont think i would pop in just for a drink , but would return for the food and atmosphere
