Komakino . left this review about The Bystander

A GK Hungry Horse outlet in a village in the Abingdon surrounds. This is a two-barred affair, with a public bar to the left with tall tables, TVs showing sport (or music channels) and a pool table and dartboard. A larger room to the right is the preserve of the diner. Plenty of meal deals as is to be expected from the chain, however, GK IPA was the sole ale on offer, so I politely declined.
