ROB Camra left this review about Anton's

Small bar on the seafront which used to be a chapel. Upstairs is a restaurant, but they're happy for you to sit in the bar for a drink or there's a few tables outside on the promenade. Just a couple of keg fonts with San Miguel and Tetleys Smooth, but I can see it doing very nicely in Summer. Closed Mondays in Winter and all through January after the 4th.
