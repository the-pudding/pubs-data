Real Ale Ray left this review about The Cock Inn

An impressive looking thatched pub, in a prime location as you enter from the top end of the village. I received a warm welcome from the manageress on my visit and was very chatty. I enjoyed the Woodfordes Nelson's Revenge which was superb and sampled the GK Londons Glory which was well kept.They also serve food.
