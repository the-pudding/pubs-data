patricia moloney left this review about The Crown Inn

Lovely country pub with huge garden and fish pond. Refurbished in early 2009 by Arkells Brewery.Was Taken over by Terry and Trish in March 2008. Serving good british fresh food.Carvery on Sunday Lunch from 12- 2.00 p.m.always very busy so wise to book.Bookings essential on Friday and Saturday nights also.
