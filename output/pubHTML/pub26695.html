Al Bundy left this review about Jacaranda

Now re-open and fully refurbished. A lot of the Beatles pap has been removed, though one corner still has photos, and the stairway that used to be in the middle of the room has been filled in. Real ale is available and you are still going to be hearing The Beatles for the billionth time. Comfy seats are now in place. Still an upstairs and a downstairs area.

Old Boots left this review about Jacaranda

Famous Beatle related bar, almost every vertical surface has photos, posters, signs or drawings, mainly of the Beatles, jazz muscians or film stars. Arranged over three floors I only visited the main bar on the ground floor, this serves the usual suspects beer and spirit wise but has a continental atmosphere. Reminiscent of Belgium or the Netherlands but with a Liverpool edginess.
