Old Peculiar left this review about Saddlers Arms

Quite a nice pub/restaurant, with a large beer garden. The drinking part is comfy with a pool table in the end room. The restaurant has a carvery which seems popular.
Two ales on sale, Saddlers (locally brewed for the pub [?]) and Otter ale which was served a bit cold but otherwise a good pint. Well worth a visit.
