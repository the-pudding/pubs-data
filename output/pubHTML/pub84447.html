Aqualung . left this review about Miners Return

This micropub is situated in part of the old Miner's Social Club. It has a fairly extensive seating area on the other side of the off road drive that it is situated on. Inside it.s a fairly typical rectangular room with the bar counter on the right at the rear. There is miner's bric-a-brac around the place and the seating is predominately low. I did notice that there were a couple of keg options on the bar. There was no TV or music.
Four beers were available, Tollgate California Steam & Old Rasputin Stout, Charnwood APA and Lincoln Green Hood. I went for the Charnwood APA (£3.00) which was in good nick. I thought this was another good micropub in this area.

Real Ale Ray left this review about Miners Return

Four ales and three ciders on handpump. I went for the Slaters Top Totty, which was superb, whilst Mrs A went for the Lincoln Green Archer. The pub has lots of mining memorabilia, a selection of beer tankards displayed along the wall near the front of the pub, there was also lots of hops hanging over the bar area. A darts board could be found near the entrance to the loos. So I had to do some careful manoeuvring when exiting the loos, as one of the players could hardly hit the board. The bar counter was painted T&G with a pine counter. The walls were wallpapered in a brick effect and the floor was tiled throughout.
