Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Paris Hilton left this review about The Royal Oak

This pub is now known as the Orchard Inn and I visited for the first time since the re-branding and decoration which seemed to comprise a lick of paint and some new wallpaper only

It must have been pension day when we visited as all the local pensioners were in taking advantage of the £4.75 carvery lunch. Not being a fan of stewed overcooked vegetables and dried up beef myself I did not join them for lunch. However, the beer selection was ok, Otter Ale, Tribute and Exmoor Gold; the Gold was fine. The garden has seen the addition of a patio area however being January was empty apart from the now ever-present smokers. Overall a slight improvement since the days of the Royal Oak, but I'm afraid I won't be rushing back.

On 15th January 2011 - rating: 4
