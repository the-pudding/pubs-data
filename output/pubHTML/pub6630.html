Alan Winfield left this review about The Blue Bell Inn

This is a decent looking pub that is on a quieter road in Kirk Langley once inside this pub seems quite olde worlde there were three handpumps on the bar i tried the Bass which was a decent drink there was also Marstons Pedigree and Old Speckled Hen,i would quite happily come back here for a few more drinks.
