E TA left this review about Word of Mouth

A hideous high street amusement arcade with a bar on the mezzanine floor, approached through banks of slot machines. There is a pool table, but that’s about the only normal pub feature aside from the bar counter. The clientele seemed to be exclusively teenagers, and most of those seemed to be of Far Eastern origin. There was no real ale on offer, nor indeed anywhere comfortable to drink it if there had been. I very much doubt I’ll be telling anyone to come here.

On 26th January 2016 - rating: 2
