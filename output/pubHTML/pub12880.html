Jon B left this review about Tudor House Hotel

another nice Tewkesbury pub, good real ales on the bar covering all styles. point deducted for over charging for a half pint. great for a quiet drink with its quaint booths (oh, it's called an alcove! thanks Gill!) though decor a touch on the dowdy side. a family were using one to play Monopoly! very much recommended if you're in town.

Gill Smith left this review about Tudor House Hotel

Very impressive black and white building on the outside and very clean and tidy inside with some smaller intimate little alcove seating. We chose St Austell Proper Job and Wye Valley Wholesome Stout which were fine. Rugby on the TV.
