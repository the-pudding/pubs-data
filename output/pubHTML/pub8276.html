Quinno _ left this review about Union Inn

Corner pub with a tidy-looking exterior, plenty of flora. U shaped inside, decorated in light colours with a dark laminate floor, plus an exposed stonework wall. Some tasteful prints and bits of breweriana adorn the walls. Pool, 2x dart boards, daily papers and cricket on TV. Four ales; Sharp’s Doom Bar & Special, Quercus Prospect and Betty Stogs (very drinkable) served up by a friendly barman. Beware the slippy tiles in bogs, almost did myself a disservice; perhaps I was blinded by the glittery wall. Decent little stop anyhow.

On 2nd August 2014 - rating: 7
