Soup Dragon left this review about Whittington Arms

A large detached brick build, with extension and some olive green and white timber - there is a large patio/beer garden - the place looks like a country club from the outside, rather than a pub.

The interior will be loved by some, hated by others. It is open plan and modern, with plain and different coloured walls; red, blue, beige etc, comfy seats, sofas and pouffes, arty sculpture and ornate mirrors. It isn't my taste, but many will feel relaxed in this kind of environment. There are a couple of wood panelled chimney hearths and a wood panel bar - the website will show you to help make your mind up. The service was friendly, the clientele mixed and it was seemingly popular.

Beer; tap stuff, with Peroni and Amstel and three handpulls; Adnams Broadside, Pedigree and a decent Timothy Taylor Landlord.

It isnt my kind of interior (best for me on a sunny day in the beer garden). I can't fault the beer, so best of luck to you!
