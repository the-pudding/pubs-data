Komakino . left this review about Pablo Lounge

According the website, Radiohead were from Abingdon and their debut album was called Pablo Honey, hence Pablo Lounge. Tenuous at best and this is in essence an eatery first with drinks coming second (much emphasis on their tapas according the A-boards outside), although there's a cocktail menu available. No real ale - Bath Ales' keg Dark Side is available as is their Gem in bottled version (£4.65 for the privilege and entirely poured into a glass for me). Not exactly a great addition to the Abingdon scene, but then again, no-one will class this as a pub, so safe to give this a miss.
