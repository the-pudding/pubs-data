Reviews of The Greyhound (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

moby duck left this review about The Greyhound

A nice looking country pub with a main unspoilt but modern feeling drinking room with wooden floor, black beams and white walls, a less traditional dining area is to the rear.A freehouse with five handpumps on the bar, there was a decent selection of ales on show, Bowmans Meon Valley,Irving Frigate, Hop Back Summer Lightening and Crafty Brewing Hop Tipple leaving one handle vacant. It was a nice pit stop.
