James Joines left this review about The Bradley Arms

Typical local with lots of Featherstone Rovers pictures. Large garden for the warmer days and a large car park.
Black Sheep, Theakstone and John Smiths avilable and one other beer had just finished.
Well worth finding and reasonable prices.
