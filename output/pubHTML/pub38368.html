Soup Dragon left this review about Spills Meadow

An unispiring 1960s house style detached brick building, with patio

The interior is open plan in white, red and wood panel. There are a few old photos and prints on the walls and there is Sky TV, a pool table and a dart area (though board may have been removed). The service was fine and the clientele friendly enough and mixed, its main problem as far as i was concerned was the level of the music on the jukebox - a bit overpowering.

Beer; tap stuff with a decent Banks' MILD on handpull, along with Jennings Cumberland Ale and Banks's Bitter.

A better than basic pub for its beer, but the real ale fans will go to the Britannia over the road
