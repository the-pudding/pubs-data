Richard Arwkrite left this review about The Dog At Harvington

Rustic styled eatery come pub.Lots of cottage style wooden furniture to give you the impression of dining in a farmhouse.Not many kids,single mums or push chairs to be seen. The prices of the food are reasonable and for a nice drive out one day. One or two ales to be had. Wheelchair access reasonable.
