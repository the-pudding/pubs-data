Al Bundy left this review about The Bridgewater

Another trad. arr Holts pub. A nice enough place with 2 rooms. I only went into the Lounge on the right so not sure what the other room is like. 4 handpumps in here all serving Holts Bitter. Its a fairly decent place and one or two benches outside make for a decent, if noisy and fume filled, spot when its sunny. There were 2 handpumps that I could see in the other room though there may be more.

ROB Camra left this review about The Bridgewater

Large corner pub with two good sized rooms. The left hand one is more of a games room with a pool table. The right hand one is more of a lounge with lots of seating all around the outside walls. There were some very loud and raucous regulars in this room on my Friday afternoon visit. This is a Holts's tied house with their Bitter & Mild on the bar. I had a pint of the bitter which was £2.24 in here, pretty much the standard price for the area. My pint was just about OK and turned out to be the worst Holts's beer I had all day on a crawl filled with Holts pubs.
