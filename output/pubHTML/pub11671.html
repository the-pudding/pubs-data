Family-run pub in Tiptree, Essex. Has a Large Beer Garden with Childrens Play Area Indoor and outdoor sports coverage (currently showing Wimbledon and the World Cup!!) Special Events including: Singers Karaoke nights Regular Happy Hours! Regular BBQs (Weather permitting) Pool Table - tournaments held at request Family fun days - bouncy castle and bucking bronco's! Catered events - wedding receptions, funerals, etc. Disco Every Friday Night from 8pm till Late
