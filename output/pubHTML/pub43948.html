Reviews of White Hart Inn (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about White Hart Inn

A large pub on the edge of town noted for its function suites for weddings etc. At the front is a small but good traditional public bar with a tiled floor, offering pool and darts. To the side is a larger comfortable lounge/dining room, with the suites behind. Draft ales were Ghost Ship and Old Speckled Hen, with ciders available also. Perfectly adequate.
