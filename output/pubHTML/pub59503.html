Gill Smith left this review about The Farmers Arms

Enjoyed our visit yesterday to this 2018 Good Beer Guide pub. The bar and main dining areas are in the original 18th century building with more modern extensions housing a games room and restaurant. Selection of cask ales and ciders available, and we chose Navigation Patriot and Gloucester Priory Ale which were fine. They also had 2 Wye Valley beers on and Butcombe Small World came on just before we left.

On 8th April 2018 - rating: 7
