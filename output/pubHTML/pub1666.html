Quinno _ left this review about The Horn

Despite addition of the lovely I Love Paella franchise in the kitchen, the ale in here is still dire - two of my three went back last visit.

Feb 2013
Perennially underachieving historic town centre boozer has recently had quite a makeover and is definitely improving. Three ales in middling condition, though be careful of their pricing policy which means you pay a premium for the pleasure of a half - £1.80 as opposed to £3.30 for a full pint...you do the maths.
