ROB Camra left this review about Guilty By Association

A cellar bar which is just one fairly large room. To the left are high stools and tables, to the right some seating that looks like car seats. There's no real ale here, but there are a couple of craft keg fonts. I had a half of Amstel at £2.20 and went to play on the AC/DC pinball machine. There's also a table football machine. I'll call in again if I fancy a game of pinball, but not for any other reason.
