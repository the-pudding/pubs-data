The Plough Hotel Larbert is family run by new owners who have refurbished the Plough Bar, which is SEPARATE from Restaurant, the bar T.V. shows BT SPORTS AND SKY SPORTS. The Plough Restaurant you'll find a variety of good wholesome home cooked food. and a children's play room. The Plough Accommodation - Seven refurbished bedrooms. En-suite Super King Twin/ Double - En-suite King Double - Super King Twin/Double * - Single rooms* - Rooms indicated* share 3 toilet rooms - 2 with showers. The Plough Function suite provides a friendly, intimate venue, what ever the event, Bespoke Weddings, Celebration Parties. The Plough car park provides off road parking free to guests.- web link www.ploughhotellarbert.co.uk

Colin Walker left this review about Plough Hotel

The Plough Hotel is right in the heart of the pedestrian precinct of Stenhousemuir of which the town is only 5 minutes drive from the more hustle and bustle town of Falkirk. The bar is situated at the hotels restaurant area, but there is separate seating to enjoy your drink away from all the eating. If all the eating and drinking gets too much, there are 4 standard bedrooms, 2 double, 1 twin & 1 single room available. There are 2 function room available has the capacity to accommodate 10 to 160 people.
