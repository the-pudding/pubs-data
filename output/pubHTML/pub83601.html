Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Mill Lodge

Table Table bar / restaurant located adjacent to a Premier Inn. Very food-led, of course, but there is some seating around the bar in addition to the various dining areas. Light, but anonymous corporate furniture and decor throughout. Patio beer garden at the back. However, whilst it having two handpumps was no particular surprise, the offered beers - We Three Kings from Shepherd Neame's experimental No 18 Yard Brewhouse and Yule Love It! from Thwaite's (£1.65, half) - showed rather more imagination than I was expecting.
