ROB Camra left this review about Brennan's

Sports orientated bar that still has The Beaten Docket written on the chairs and the windows. Standard range of Irish kegs on the bar plus one handpump that claimed it was selling Whitewater Belfast Ale, It wasn't it was selling brown sludge. To be fair the barman pulled the pint, had one look at it and said "Nope, I'm not serving you that". The bar is a fairly ornate affair with several large screens and an upstairs area that was closed on all 3 occasions that we visited last week. We watched the Man Utd vs Scalke 04 match here and ordered some pub grub. The fish, chips & mushy peas were good value with the fish looking like a dolphin in batter. The steak & Guinness hot pot was good as well. There's a real mix of people come in here and it can feel a bit rough and ready at times, but we liked it. Which is why we visited 3 times.

On 8th May 2011 - rating: 7
