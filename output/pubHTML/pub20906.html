Alan Winfield left this review about The Man At Arms

The Man at Arms is one of those pubs i really like the look of,it is situated next to the village green.
Once inside there is a small room on entry,this had a few tables and chairs and a real fire roaring away,the main room is a long and narrow bar which runs across the front on the pub,the seating in this area was bench type with wood topped tables on metal stands which looked like they came from the 60s,there was a real fire in this area also roaring away,the right side is for diners and this area runs to the rear of the pub with a raised area at the rear with another real fire on the go.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Shepherd Neame Spitfire which was a nice drink,the other beer was London Pride.
I enjoyed having a drink in this pub.
