ROB Camra left this review about Hare & Hounds

Fairly traditional, two level pub owned by Thwaites. Thwaites is not my favourite brewery, but the Wainwright I tried here was in excellent condition, they also had Thwaites Bitter on handpump. I didn't eat here, but the food looked good and is certainly competitively priced compared to the nearby pubs. Plenty of bar stools round the bar and some high tables and stools around the place. An area at the top half of the pub has 4 tables which were set up for dining although people were eating in other areas. It's a bit more foody than it used to be when I came in here regularly a few years ago, but it's still a good pub and is definitely the pick of the Bamford pubs.
