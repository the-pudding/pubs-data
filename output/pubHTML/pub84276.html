Komakino . left this review about The Charlton Kings Club

As Helen mentions, flashing your Camra card gives you free entry and although rather club-like the main bar area is, a regular of Butcombe Bitter and three rotating guests - on my visit, well-kept halves of Titanic Lifeboat, Bingham's Hot Dog Chilli Stout and Thornbridge Crackendale - at a bargain £4.43 in total, means this is worth a double-up with Cheltenham's other lauded club, the Cheltenham Motor Club. I was the youngest in here by a country mile and got a few stares from the locals, but the above plus friendly service to boot means this is a welcome alternative to the frenetic Royal the other side of the churchyard.
