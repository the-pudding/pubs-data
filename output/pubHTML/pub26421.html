Reviews of Molly's Chambers Add Review see review guidelines

Al Bundy left this review about The Old Colonial Inn

Has now reopened as a pub again called 'Molly's Chambers'. The place is, at the moment a basement bar and is still being done up whilst I was in. The bar is a L-shaped bar with, at the moment 3 unused handpulls. There are a selection of unconventional keg beers available. There is to be a kitchen when it is fully operational and to the side a beer terrace is still being done. Whether the upper area is to be opened I don't know. The pub itself is hidden away in a rather run down area which is mainly warehouses and business units. The Wirral Motor Museum is opposite so you will see old trams parked outside. As the place was still being kitted out it was a bit of a mess but it is open.
