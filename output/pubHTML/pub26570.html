Al Bundy left this review about The Clubmoor

A large 2 roomed pub that looks nice outside and isn't too bad inside. The room to your right is the larger lounge area with a traditional style interior. To the left is a small bar which is sparsely decorated. No real ale. Its opposite the, now sadly closed, Dockers Club. My old school used to be visible across the field opposite but sadly Anny Comp got knocked down years ago.

19/7/14
Now open again.
