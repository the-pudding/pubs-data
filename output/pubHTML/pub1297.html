Komakino . left this review about The Star

A Chef & Brewer restaurant / pub in this drive-through village set back from the road and decorated in the usual C&B white and grey. The bar counter faces on entry and serves a largish bar area in front and to the left and right. The room then snakes towards the back right and behind which is a dining-only area (tables had wine glasses on them). The bar area has a lowish, rendered ceiling, exposed beams and a mixture of tiles and boarded floor. A good turnout of early evening drinkers on my visit, and although I stuck to a soft drink, the options of GK IPA, Abbot Ale and Sharp's Doom Bar meant I wasn't missing out.

On 25th September 2017 - rating: 3
