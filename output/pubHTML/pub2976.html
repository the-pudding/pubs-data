Will Larter left this review about The Beehive

With the appearance of a back street local, it's disappointing to find that this pub has taken the gastro route. Admittedly there are four hand pumps on the bar, serving a cider and three beers, but the strong smell of food upon entering tells you all you need to know. Service was by pleasant young women in aprons, which may float someone's boat, and to be fair the place was doing a roaring trade in pub food and the beer selection was not unimaginative, but I have to say this pub is not for me.

Date of visit: 6th December 2015
