Alan Winfield left this review about Bricklayers Arms

The Bricklayers Arms is a fairly large bare brick built pub that is set sideways onto ordsall lane.
Once inside the pub had a couple of rooms that we noticed and we had a drink in the bar which was a decent enough room to have a drink in.
This pub is a Joseph Holt tied house and there were two real ales on the bar we had a drink of Holts bitter and this was a really nice drink,the other beer was Holts mild.
We thought this was quite a nice pub and we were happy enough having a drink in here.
