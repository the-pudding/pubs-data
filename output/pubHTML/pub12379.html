Pete Simms left this review about The Kings Head

Friendly and popular pub with great real ales as well as the usual beers & lagers. When the weather is good the beer garden at the side is very pleasant. Even has free wifi ! My favourite pub whenever I get the chance to visit Bishops Cleeve.

Helen Iwanczuk left this review about The Kings Head

My visit to the attractive village of Bishop's Cleeve was a fleeting one on this occasion, so I wasn't able to sample the delights of The Kings Head. Situated near a round a bout next to a VERY busy main road, the Kings Head it never-the-less an attractive c17 thatched pub - one of only a handful of thatched pubs in Gloucestershire.
