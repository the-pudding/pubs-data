Graham Coombs left this review about Coach & Horses Inn

Pleasant country pub on road junction, particularly noted for splendid views to rear, best enjoyed from beer garden. Inside has a plush yet cosy feel, decorated with brass musical instruments. Beers from Robinsons; food home-cooked and very good.
