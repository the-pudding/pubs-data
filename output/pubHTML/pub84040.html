Komakino . left this review about Beefeater Uxbridge

A bright, modern Beefeater bolt-on to a Premier Inn just off the main centre of Uxbridge. Cask ales were Fuller's London Pride and Wychwood Hobgoblin Gold along with bottled versions of Sharp's Doom Bar, Purity Pure UBU, Shep's Whitstable Bay and a couple of BrewDog offerings, so fair play to them for making the effort at least.
