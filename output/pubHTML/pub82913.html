Reviews of Harvester New Square (Average Rating: 3½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Harvester New Square

One of the chain's smaller 'Salad & Grill' outlets, with just a tiny general seating area by the minimal bar and all the other tables laid up for dining. However, it also has a pavement seating area out in the square catching the sun for most of the day. Just Carling, San Miguel and Stowford Press on tap, with a few bottled and canned beers and ciders in the fridge.

On 22nd April 2019 - rating: 4

Graham Coombs left this review about Harvester New Square

A modern plasticky thing on the edge of the pedestrianised area. The tiny bar has keg Carling, San Miguel and Stowford Press only. A pub by technical definition only.
