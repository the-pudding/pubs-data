Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Beech Tree

Substantial Ember Inn, with a large number of seating / dining areas in an overall 'L' shape. However, on the hot sunny day when I visited, most of the customers were sat outside at the front patio tables. One could say that the semi-modern furniture and decor is (as expected) all a bit corporate, but the interior is pleasant enough (and quite a good example of its type). A very pleasant surprise was to not only find a total of ten handpumps but a different beer on each. I opted for the Acorn Barnsley (£3.45) but there were a good range of styles from breweries as far apart as Harviestoun and Exmoor.

On 3rd October 2016 - rating: 7
