Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Two Pointers

Proper village pub with the main bar in the centre, the 'Games Bar' at the north end and a single-storey extension housing another seating area and a function room located up a short flight of steps at the other. Traditionally furnished, but lighter decor than one might expect. Old '2 Pointers' glazing in the front door. Three of six handpumps in operation, offering the regular Black Sheep (£3.65) plus Wainwright and Old Speckled Hen, with a reversed clip for the other regular (Landlord) noted.
