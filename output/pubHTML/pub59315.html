ROB Camra left this review about The Duke Of Buccleuch

An old pub that has been broken out into one large room. There's a pool table and a dartboard in what were once separate rooms. No evidence of any food being available during my Friday, early evening visit. It's a fairly nondescript pub with a couple of large screens. Quite a few people in though. Two handpumps on the bar and my pint of Pride Of Pendle was in decent nick.
