Quinno _ left this review about The Red Lion

Characterful gastropub, nooks and crannies and quite well decorated. Impressive green foliage exterior looks enticing. Two pumps (take some finding) Brakspear Bitter was off leaving an adequate Doom Bar as the solitary choice - why do gastros so often take their eye off the ball at the bar?? Meantime Lager on keg and Sierra Nevada in bottles a much better bet. Less expensive than expected though but otherwise, pretty forgettable.

On 14th September 2015 - rating: 5
