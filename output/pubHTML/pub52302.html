Graham Coombs left this review about Wynnstay Arms Hotel

Well-run, tidy old hotel with a large and comfortable lounge to the front, and a livelier large bar to the rear. Across the lobby is a snug library and restaurant area, while there is a big function room at the back. Beers only from Robinsons but a very good selection from the range and Cask Marque listed. Food seemed competent. All in all, a useful establishment.

Al Bundy left this review about Wynnstay Arms Hotel

A large ivy-clad hotel. There are two bars in here with the main lounge area being a smallish comfortable room with the servery dispensing a couple of real ales on my visits. The other bar room has a pool table and dartboard plus TV screens for sport. Outside at the rear is a car park and beer garden beyond that. There are 3 handpumps and all were Robinson's beers. Ruabon has 4 pubs by the station and they are pleasant places to have a drink.

On 30th July 2016 - rating: 6
