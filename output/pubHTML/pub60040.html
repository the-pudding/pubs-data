Al Bundy left this review about Revolution

One of the down sides of having to go into pubs I've never been in before is having to go into these rubbish Revolution places. This, like all the others is rubbish. Split into 2 with a central bar there are 2 dull areas to sit on either side of the bar. Minimalist fixtures and features this is a fairly industrial canteen like place. The area to the left has one of those stupid table football games which sums the place up. No real ale, and even if it did it'd still be crap. I hate these places.
