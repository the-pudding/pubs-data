Sharon Sandford left this review about Kings Arms Inn & Restaurant

This pub is a little gem! As well as the olde worlde feel with all the beams & white walls, it serves real ale, has an excellent & reasonably priced wine list, delicious home-cooked food & attentive, friendly staff. Well worth the visit!
