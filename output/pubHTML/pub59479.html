Chris Sawle left this review about The Bridge Inn

Great, hidden pub in tiny village on a dead-end lane: the road crosses the adjacent bridge for the car park and promptly becomes an unsurfaced green lane beyond! Necessarily a little foodie. Great local perry on tap. Nice streamside beer garden with chickens roaming free. Small campsite attached.

On 9th October 2013 - rating: 8
