Alan Winfield left this review about The Clifton Inn

The Clifton is a back street pub that is awaf from the centre of Swindon and not near any other pubs.
This is an Arkells tied house and once inside the pub had one room that was well furnished and comfortable,there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Arkells 3B and this was very nice the other beer was Arkells 2B.
I quite liked this pub.
