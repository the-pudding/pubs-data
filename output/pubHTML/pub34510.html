E TA left this review about The Bear

An historic ale house, dating from the 17th Century, ruined. The exterior is attractive enough, but inside it looks and feels like a low-grade works canteen. The single long conjoined bar/dining area lacks appeal and has been stripped of most of its character. The burgers look unappetising and dry, and they don’t justify the prices. It was noisy, the staff were nowhere to be seen and there was no real ale. I left feeling a little grizzly and probably won’t be back.
