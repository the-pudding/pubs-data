David Ross left this review about Macdui's

Despite the Scottish name, Macdui's has the feel of an American style bar/diner rather than a British pub and was mainly populated by people eating on both the Friday and Saturday nights I called in. You can just have a drink, however, and the Cairngorm Brewery Trade Winds and Wildcat real ales, both costing £3.80 a pint, were both in good condition. Carlsberg and San Miguel lagers, Caledonia Best and Guinness are also available on draught. So is Somersby Cider with Strawberry and Rhubarb, which sounds rather unappealing to me, although I'm not a cider drinker anyway.

On 26th September 2016 - rating: 6
