Paris Hilton left this review about The Anchor Inn

I quite like this out-of-the-way village pub and often visit on a summer afternoon for a couple before lunch. The Landlord always sells fairly local beers eg, Branscombe, Otter, Palmer which make a pleasant change from Cotleigh, Exmoor etc, and no sign of the ever present Doom Bar. It has a large garden and smaller terrace which is a great sun-trap in the summer, real log fires in the winter. There is a small snug behind the main bar and a larger restaurant-type area which is often busy on a Sunday.

On 15th January 2011 - rating: 6
