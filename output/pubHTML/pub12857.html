Aqualung . left this review about Berkeley Arms

This is an old pub that is a Wadworth tied house, the first one I had visited for many years. it consists of a room at the front with a smaller room at the rear all with wooden floors. It was quiet on my Tuesday afternoon visit but in December the whole of Tewkesbury seemed dead.
There were hand pumps in the front and rear bars with three ciders at the back and four Wadworth beers at the front namely 6X, IPA, Swordfish and Old Timer. I went for the Old Timer (£3.50) which was in good nick but very much had that distinctive Wadworth taste that rather puts me off their beers. Nevertheless this seemed a pleasant enough place and the landlady was very friendly. It's GBG 2017 listed.

On 7th December 2016 - rating: 7
