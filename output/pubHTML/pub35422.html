Please Note: This review is over a year old.

moby duck left this review about The Three Mariners

A side street pub presumably of some age given its irregular external shape.Inside there is one long room set into separate areas most of which is low ceilinged with ancient looking wooden beams.To one end there is a small upstairs lounge area, there's also a separate function room to the rear of the pub.Five handpumps on the bar and all were in use, Doom Bar and Coaster from Sharps,Bath Gem, Ringwood Fortyniner and Truman's Swift. All in all quite a decent pub.

On 19th March 2017 - rating: 7
