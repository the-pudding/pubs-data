Alan Winfield left this review about Navigation Waterfront

The Navigation Waterfront is a modern bar in an old waterside building.
Once inside there is an oblong shaped room with the bar on the right side,the room is bare boarded and has a very low ceiling,so i was very carefull when walking to the bar and back.
The seating is normal tables and chairs and a few settees.
There was a decent choice of real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Newark Best which was ok but a bit on the bland side,the other beers noted were Everards Tiger Bitter,Deuchars IPA and Bombardier.
The pub has views of the River Trent but not the locks but of dredgers sonot has good as the Waters Edge.
Background music was playing.
This pub seemed more intimate than the Waters Edge but i did prefer the Waters Edge to this one.
