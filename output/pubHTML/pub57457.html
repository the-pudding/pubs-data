Old Blue left this review about The Bulls Head

Dropped in here for a pint on Monday. It is on a corner, with a very nice garden to the side away from the road. We found the pub itself quite bland however, and the ale selection of Greene King IPA, Young’s Special and Pride exceedingly so. The barmaid was delightfully friendly, and brightened up our visit, so extra marks for her.

On 10th May 2012 - rating: 6
