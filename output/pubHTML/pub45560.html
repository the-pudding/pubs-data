Aqualung . left this review about Y Llew Coch

This is a locals pub that retains a large Banks's sign on the exterior. Inside it's a wide rectangular room where a pool table wastes a lot of space over to the left and there is a small group of low tables and chairs to the immediate right. The bar is to the rear and has two very obtrusive TV screens above it showing sport. Other furniture was all high or medium height. I managed to find a solitary low table and chair around the back next to a door with glass panes that were all cracked except one which was boarded up. It was fairly quiet at around 12:30 on a snowy February Wednesday.
Although there are three hand pumps all were unused and despite the external sign there was no keg Banks's Mild. I had a pint of Marston's Smooth Crap (£2.40) which really was a crap drink.
Rather than translate the English heraldic Red Lion name into Welsh perhaps they should have given it an original Welsh name. I would suggest Y Twll Cacha.
With the obtrusive TV screens and pool table plus no real ale it gets the score of shame.
