Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Phantom

It's a Scream (as in the pubco), so the worldly wise will know what to expect. Despite being quite a nice-looking historic building from the front, the interior has been stripped of almost all its character to just leave basic decor in garish colours and some graffiti-style illustrations on the toilet doors. Two pool tables, a 'Max Grab' machine like you might find in a seaside amusement arcade and music videos on the TV screens. It does also have outside seating in a patio area at the car park end. No real ale, so just keg Tetleys (at a remarkable £1.25 for a pint) and a few bottled ciders in addition to the usual lagers and Guinness. Very much aimed at the student population (except for a small area rather implausibly laid up for those looking for a festive easting experience).

On 21st December 2010 - rating: 4
