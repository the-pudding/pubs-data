ROB Camra left this review about Laverys

Very ornate gin palace with an island bar in the main room. There's a back room which is used for live sport and also as a disco later at nights. They also have a pool room and a bistro. The main room is full of TVs as well as the back room. There are several small booths on the left hand side . The right hand side is mainly dining tables aimed at larger groups. We called in one lunchtime when it was fairly quiet with just a few business people in having lunch. Standard array of Irish kegs on the bar. It was warm outside so I had a pint of Harp which was OK. No idea what it's like when there's a big match on or the disco is in full swing, but it's very pleasant at lunchtime.
