Oggwyn Great left this review about The Crown Inn

Two room pub opposite Shrewsbury Abbey , the bar is pretty plain with bench seats around the sides , the lounge is slightly plusher , there is also a small outside area , lots of pictures of old Shrewsbury Town Teams on the walls , this was once the nearest pub to the old Gay Meadow ground , and a few brass plaques comemerating some of the old regulars including Shrewsbury terrace legend Anti Tank .
Usual keg taps and a couple of handpulls , only Bass on though it was pretty good .
Its OK in here if in need of a little TLC
