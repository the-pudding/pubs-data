Alan Winfield left this review about The Sarah Mansfield

The Sarah Mansfield is an unusally named pub that is in the very small village of Willey.
Once inside the pub looked nice and smart,the bar was to the right and there was a seating area to the front right,the front left looked more like a dining area,there was a larger area to the rear right which had a pool table in it,the pub was part carpeted with the rest tiled,the seating was very comfortable bench type with small stools,the ceiling was beamed which gave the pub a cosy feeling on a cold day.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Brains bitter which was a decent drink,the other beers were Bombardier and GK Abbot.
The pub was fairly busy and had a good atmosphere,i quite liked this pub.

Pub visited 16/3/2013
