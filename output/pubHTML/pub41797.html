Alan Winfield left this review about The Dolphin Hotel

The Dolphin is a large victorian pub that is close to the old Swindon Works which is now a shopping area.
Once inside the pub was quite smart with bare boards and comfortable seating,this pub is an Arkells tied house and there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Arkels 3B and this was a very nice drink there was also Arkells 2B and a seasonal beer also from Arkells.
I thought this was a very nice pub and i was quite happy having a drink in here.
