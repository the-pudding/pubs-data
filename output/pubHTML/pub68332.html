Oggwyn Great left this review about Bull Inn

Recently reopened and refurbised , the bar is to the right of the main entrance , the room being divided by an enclosed stairwell , there is a curtained off dining area to the left and a conservatory style modern room tacked on the back , the floor is tiled with brightly coloured seemingly random tiles .
Usual keg taps and five handpulls , only two on during my visit with Deuchars and Pendle Blond Witch .
Service was friendly as were the locals .
Its decent enough in here , maybe a few more ales on to improve it
