Graham Smith left this review about The Bowyer Arms

Large Greene King pub. Focus on dining but there is a large public bar area with pool table, etc. Large garden at the rear. Pub is adjacent to Radley rail station. One train an hour to Oxford or Didcot. On my latest visit real ales available were Greene King IPA, Old Speckled Hen and Old Golden Hen. Comfortable pub popular with locals. Service at the bar can be a little slow at times but pleasant enough staff.

On 16th August 2012 - rating: 6
