Gill Smith left this review about Salutation

Caught a 3.2 bus here from Kinoulton and found this to be a fantastic pub. The bar is a large island serving from 2 distinct seating areas. Most tables laid for food, and plenty of people eating here so we joined them. There are 3 beers on either side of the bar, Bombardier, Abbott and Castle Rock Harvest Pale on the one side, and Theakston's Lightfoot, Robinsons Unicorn and Tetley Gold on the other side. These were the changing guest beers and were in good condition. Pub was friendly and comfortable.
