Old Boots left this review about The Green Dragon Hotel

Probably the best pub in Bedale for beer and friendliness, it's not badged outside but all the cask beers are from the Marston's stable, Ringwood, Mansfield and a very nicely kept Hobgoblin. The single bar shows signs of a recent renovation in the modern, old style with comfortable upholstered benches, stained glass over the counter and in a divider , a bit of panelling, stools and cast iron tables. The woodwork is stained in old pine and the décor limited to a few arty photos. Inevitably there was musak, machines and a big screen showing sport. There is a large dining room to the rear in a conservatory and a beer garden. The mixed bunch of locals were friendly and the staff enthusiastic, which with decent beer makes this a pretty good pub.
