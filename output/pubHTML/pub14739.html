Karina Bowen left this review about Rose & Crown

nice atmosphere, pity about staff attitude, When I phoned to book a table for 9.40 on a Friday they informed me the kitchen closed at 9.30, despite the website clearly stating the kitchen closed 10pm fri + sat nights. When I asked if the website was wrong, the attitude was quite frankly very off hand- I had to ask this twice - and got a terse 'yes'. Pity as it's a great location and not a plastic pub, like so many chains.

Quinno _ left this review about Rose & Crown

A quintessential English pub in the visual stakes, covered and surrounded by greenery in a semi-rural location. Popped in off the back of its GBG listing though it’s more restaurant than pub really. Not a huge amount to shout about beer-wise (Deuchars IPA, Pride and a guest) but the half I had was fine. Large garden to rear though unexplored due to the impending dusk. Two real fires.

On 10th June 2009 - rating: 6
