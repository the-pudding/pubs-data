Hugh Jonesj left this review about The Craven Heifer

Four of us visited on 12th Dec. 2010 and all enjoyed the Sunday carvery, good value at £7.95. Guest beers included Black Sheep, Moorhouses and the one we sampled Hetton Dark Horse, which was very acceptable. Good value, atmosphere and service, at weekends probably as well to book a table. Would recommend.

Hugh Jones
