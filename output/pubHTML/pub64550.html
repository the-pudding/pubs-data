E TA left this review about Desvins Cafe & Wine Bar

A station servery claiming to be a wine bar but managing to be nothing more than a place to sit and consume over-priced, second rate goods. Staff are inefficient and unhelpful, and none of them had English as a native language. Food was bland and pretentious. The beers were the usual megakeg offerings plus smoothflow Speckled Hen. The latter was predictably over-chilled and unsatisfactory. I shall be back for breakfast croissants but nothing else.
