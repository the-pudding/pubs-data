Alan Winfield left this review about Golden Cross Inn

The Golden Cross is on a very busy street in the middle of Cirencester.
When i went in this pub it was a traditional pub that was unaltered.
This pub is an Arkells tied house and they have spent a lot of money doing this place up,there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Arkells 3B which was a very nice drink the other beers were Arkells 2B and an Arkells seasonal beer.

Pub visited 3 August 2002.
