Denis Iliffe left this review about Roebuck

My wife and I went in for meal on the 3rd February.
Told to sit on a table for 2 up a corner as window seat were only for parties of four and they couldn't afford to change it. We felt like second class customers. I was front of house for a 16th century restaurant for four years and wouldn't have been in business for long treating customers like that.
We left and will not set foot in the place again and would recommend everyone else to do the same.

On 3rd February 2013 - rating: 1

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Roebuck

Very new, food-led, edge of town, Marstons outlet. Low building with a large 'L'-shaped bar and various seating areas. Patio and grass beer garden to one side, with a childrens play area to the rear. Pedigree, Sneck Lifter and Boondoggle (£3.00) available from two banks of four handpumps. Overall, probably does what it does pretty well, but distinctly anonymous.

On 3rd October 2012 - rating: 6
