Reviews of Mill Street (Average Rating: 3 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Quinno _ left this review about Mill Street

Oakham’s ‘plub’, done-out in blues and greys. The front bar has a decoration *feature* of tiles that have been pulled off the wall, for some bizarre reason. To the rear is a foody conservatory, served by an open kitchen. Three pumps with nothing on, just a Doom Bar clip turned, so I went for the rather leftfield option (in the circumstances) of Kozel, which tasted a bit odd, my companion’s Estrella was much better. One for completists.

On 2nd August 2018 - rating: 3
