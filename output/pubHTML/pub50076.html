Colin Walker left this review about The Burnside Hotel

The Burnside Hotel has a lounge and a very large bar. The bar has Sky sports and has the larger gantry to have a better choice of drinks. Although both bar and the lounge are connected. There are a variety of entertainment evenings from live music to quiz nights and live sporting events. There is a good choice of meals from their varied menu. There is parking is available in the hotel grounds to the left of the hotel.
