Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Bay Horse

Attractive pub, opened out inside to form an elongated 'U'-shaped layout. Very traditional furniture and decor with plenty of memorabilia. The locals were mostly clustered around the bar, but there is plenty of space including two separate dining rooms. Background music. B&B. Beer garden. Just one handpump, offering Old Speckled Hen (£3.35), but a very pleasant place to stop mid-way round a long cross-country walk. (NB - Review dates from August 2012.)
