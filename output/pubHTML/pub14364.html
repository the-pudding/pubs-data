Komakino . left this review about The Three Tuns Hotel

A good-looking building in a good-looking village. Under GK's auspices, IPA, TT Landlord and a GK summer brew called 'Bermuda Joe's' which was actually really good - light, hoppy and fruity - surprised me in a good way. It's been modernised inside as you'd expect, but in a fairly sympathetic manner. Not an award-winner, but comfortable, cosy and a credit to its village.

On 7th August 2017 - rating: 6
