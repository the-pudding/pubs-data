Soup Dragon left this review about Desi Star Inn

A cream terraced Banks' pub, that carries the names of the Desi Star and the Seven Stars

The interior is open plan and in yellow and mainly pine wood panel, like the bar itself. There is quite a bit of plant greenery inside. A part of the pub doesnt seem to be used, apart from having a punchbag machine. The main pub has a pool table and a TV, that wasnt on at first, then turned on for Gunfight at the OK coral with Kirk Douglas. Tis resulted in the radio, which was supplying the music being turned off. The service was fine, the clientele a couple of locals, just the one being drunk!

Beer; all tap stuff, with Spekky Hen on tap, as well as a decent enough Banks's MILD.

Sorry, nothing for real ale drinkers - so go to the Pretty Bricks around the corner
