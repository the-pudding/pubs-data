Edith Gregory left this review about The Royal Exchange Inn

Having previously been a frequent visitor to the Royal Exchange in Hartpury a few years ago, I was extremely disappointed on my recent return to the area and visit to find the pub a completely different place. The food leaved a lot to be desired and I found the welcome uninviting. There are other pubs in the area with better food, and a much much better friendlier welcome and atmosphere. This may sound harsh, but I feel it is true.

On 19th October 2008 - rating: 4

Judith Burrington left this review about The Royal Exchange Inn

You have to try this place. The food is very very good, plenty of it. Value for money too. Dogs are welcome - there is a very very large garden at the rear. Also just started a small caravan site for upto 5 touring vans and tents as well. Can't rate it too highly. Sunday roasts too and a big open log fire - brilliant

On 8th September 2008 - rating: 8
