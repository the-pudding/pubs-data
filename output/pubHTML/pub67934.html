ROB Camra left this review about 34 Church Street Bar & Grill

The name has changed, but the ownership stayed the same, despite them claiming they were leaving and having a leaving do etc, very naughty. They now have 2 handpump which were serving Cumberland Ale & Hobgoblin last night. The Cumberland was OK.

19/04/2010
This place is owned by the guys who were finalists in Britains Got Talent a while ago. They bought it with their winnings. They're often not here themselves as they take their show touring, however one of them was behind the bar last Friday. Pretty impressive he was as well, chucking glasses and bottles etc around, whilst making drinks. It's a typical modern bar, somewhat at odds with the old stone exterior. It's all wood and comfy chairs although there are a couple of stand up tables. A couple of TV's dominate the room and there's also a restaurant upstairs. Fizz only on the bar of course and pretty expensive it is too for Littleborough, £3.50 for a pint of Peroni. It's OK for Littleborough which needed a bar like this, but it wouldn't stand out anywhere else.

On 28th September 2013 - rating: 6
