Reviews of The Crown Hotel (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Alan Hurdle left this review about The Crown Hotel

17th century town centre pub. Recently redecorated by new landlords but retaining wood panelled interior. Meals served Fri, Sat Sun lunch + evenings (except Tue & Wed). Real Ale is 1 changing guest beer from a single handpump, but availability is now very irregular!!
If the cask is off, don't buy half of Guinness. It's £3 for a half! (£3.60 a pint). Another point docked for this.
