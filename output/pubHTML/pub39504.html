Reviews of Wilkes Head (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

paulof horsham left this review about The Wilkes Head

Currently a CAMRA regional pub of the year, so an obvious destination for us towards the end of the day. As a result of it being "towards the end of the day", I can recall little more than a good quality pint of Adnams IPA and food readily available.

A pub in a location like this is clearly going to have a strong emphasis on food, but there's still a good pub feel. Definitely worth another visit.

On 11th February 2013 - rating: 7
