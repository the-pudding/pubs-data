Graham Coombs left this review about The Troll Cart (JD Wetherspoon)

Vast modern Wetherspoon's in purpose-built building, complete with hotel, in shopping area and opposite the bus station. Fittings and facilities exactly as you might expect, except that is does include quite a large patio garden including a covered section. Beer range seemed fair, with a few locals alongside the usual stuff. As mentioned in earlier reviews, it does seem to get busy and was absolutely heaving on a weekday lunchtime.

Aqualung . left this review about The Troll Cart (JD Wetherspoon)

This is an old school JDW dating from the mid nineties. It's a wide room with not much depth, typical furnishings and was busy at around 11:30 on a June Saturday morning.
The bar has ten hand pumps which had the JDW Trio Of Doom, Adnam's Ghost Ship, Woodforde's Nelson's Revenge, Grainstore Rutland Beast, Hobgoblin, Wolf Sirius Dog Star & Trawlerman's Best and Wild Weather Choppy Waters. I went for the Sirius Dog Star (£2.39) which was in very good nick. This seemed a decent enough Spoons but probably worth avoiding in the summer months.

On 29th June 2016 - rating: 7
