Steve C left this review about Gemini Beach Bar

Gemini Beach Bar has been renamed Brighton Music Hall and is a bar on the Brighton seafront. The interior consists of a bar area to the right and some seating in a separate area to the left. The major draw is the plethora of seating out the front on the beach and the seven days a week live entertainment during the summer period. During my visit on a recent Friday afternoon there was a solo singer who was interacting very well with the crowd. Standard draught is available from the bar and the service was friendly enough.

I thought that this was one of the better beach bars.
