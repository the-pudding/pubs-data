Graham Coombs left this review about The Quaker Wood Inn

A reasonably respectable modern pub tucked away in the housing estates, in the 'Flaming Grill' chain. The interior is divided up into a number of different areas with some parts aimed at family dining and others dominated by sports or gaming machines. Someone shows some enthusiasm for beers though, with a sign promoting the real ales. which were a couple from GK along with Tetleys Cask, and in good condition (Cask Marque listing). Could be much worse.

On 28th November 2017 - rating: 6
