moby duck left this review about The Crooked Well

A backstreet corner pub now gentrified, leaving little of any pub character it may have once had, internally it has ore of a hotel lounge feel.Two hand pumps with just one in use, the solitary beer was Camerons Ahopalypes Now and on a blisteringly hot day the quality was struggling. Not a pub I would choose to revisit anytime soon.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Crooked Well

The Crooked Well is a corner pub that is just away from the busy road running through the middle of Camberwell.
Once inside there is a decent sized single oblong shaped room which runs the width of the pub,the bar faces on entry,the floor is bare boarded,the seating was a mix of different types,there was a very long settee to the front left,one tall table and chairs in the middle of the room with normal tables and chairs elsewhere,the right side was set up for diners.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Doom Bar,this drink was decent enough.
There was some very quiet background music playing.
This is seems more of a gastro type pub than ordinary boozer,not really my sort of pub.

Pub visited 13/9/2014
