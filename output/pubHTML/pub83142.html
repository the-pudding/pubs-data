Steve C left this review about No 1 Middle Street

No. 1 Middle Street is a small bar that was empty when I visited just after 20:00 on a recent Saturday. The lone draught pump badged with Estrella was off so I was left with a small bottle of San Miguel which set me back an eye watering £4.50. No wonder it was empty! It is open until 3am and advertises itself as Brighton’s elite urban night, which translates to Brighton’s bloody expensive night. I was charged less in the Grand Hotel!
I enjoyed the chilled dance music and noted that there is an upstairs ‘lounge bar’ and that live DJs are advertised. I’ll not be setting foot in here again.

On 1st September 2016 - rating: 1
