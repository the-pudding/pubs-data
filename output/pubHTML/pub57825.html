Paris Hilton left this review about The White Horse Inn

This pub has re-opened for a couple of years, but still serving the same three beers, namely Otter Bitter, Exmoor Ale and Cotleigh Tawny. They did change one beer to something else once; it was, yes, you've guessed it, Doom Bar.

Having said that, it is a very lovely old village pub with beer garden, restaurant, skittle alley, log fies etc etc
