Real Ale Ray left this review about The Orchard

A decent enough modern style bar with two handpumps and a range of ten craft keg beers located from a small bar at the rear. Wishbone Flux and Goffs English Pale was the cask choice on our visit. Things were going ok, along with a couple of fellow drinkers, until a Granny brought in her two granddaughters dressed as fairies who ran around the place screaming and hitting us all with their wands. The barman didn't say a word, so the Granny sat there with a pint of craft lager, oblivious to the carry on. Unfortunately the kid's wands didn't make themselves disappear, only us adults.

On 25th February 2019 - rating: 6

peter ashworth left this review about The Orchard

This microbar is situated in the new market hall but unlike some market hall bars this can be visited when the market has closed.There was 2 cask beers on offer plus on the back wall behind the bar 10 craft ales on tap.On the left is a blackboard displaying the breweries,beers and strengths of the craft beers.There is also an option of you sampling a third of a pint.
Opening hours Sun till Thurs 12 till 10pm Friday and Sat 12 till 11pm
