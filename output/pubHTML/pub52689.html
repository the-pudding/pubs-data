L Paul left this review about The Crows Nest

I ate here with colleagues one lunchtime in June 08. Arrived at the dirty looking bar where the 3 staff seemed too busy gossipping to be bothered to serve us. My cajun chicken arrived sizzling on the hot plate and looking pretty good. A generous portion but the plate from the 80s was def a health hazard and the sauce was spitting everywhere. Ate a few chips to start - soggy and brown - bad start. Cut right throught the chicken breast to find it was purple and RAW through the thickest part. Waitress apologetic but bar staff couldn't give a hoot. Seemed surprised I wanted a refund!
Terrible service and disgusting food. Cheap and nasty, I'd suggest you avoid this dirty chain pub like the plague.
