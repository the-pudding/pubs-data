Jon B left this review about The Empire

a quite nicely done little one roomed pub. but it’s lacking in any real character or decent beer - I tried the Coast To Coast craft fizz which disappointingly tasted of nothing. the handpumps have now gone. I can imagine it being a really sweaty place when it gets busy as it isn’t very big at all. I doubt I’ll ever be in here again.

Al Bundy left this review about The Empire

Its not too bad a pub when it hasn't got karaoke/crap singer on. But sadly it has a lot of these on. There used to be a large collection of Beatles memorabilia dotted around but this has been scaled back and there is now a large collection of Merchant Navy ship pictures. 2 unused handpumps but these haven't been used for decades.
