Graham Coombs left this review about Railway Inn

A really old and atmospheric pub with some really old and atmospheric locals! Tucked away just off the main street (at clock tower) this friendly local has two rooms, liberally strewn with railway memorabilia and othe detritus. But it is the range of beers and especially ciders and perries that is its main draw and has secured various awards. Definitely a classic from a dying breed.
