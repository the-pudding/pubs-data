Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Oxford

Basic community local, but the landlady was friendly enough and the atmosphere was fine. Features a 'L'-shaped bar and a small snug at one end, and plainly furnished and decorated throughout. No real ale, so opted for the keg Courage Best (£2.60).
