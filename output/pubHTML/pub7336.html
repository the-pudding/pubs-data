Alan Winfield left this review about The Mundy Arms

The Mundy Arms is a large pub in the marpool area of Heanor,this used to be a Kimberley tied house and it is now a GK house.
This is a family dining pub that is fairly smart inside and there are three real ales on the bar i had a drink of GK Abbot which was ok there was also GK IPA and a seasonal beer.
