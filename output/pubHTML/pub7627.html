Graham Coombs left this review about The Lamb Inn

Very much a locals' pub up a hill out of town with a good range of beers (at good prices) and traditional pub food. The inside probably didn't benefit from being knocked through into one large L-shaped room, with a bar on one side and a pool table round the other. There is also a dining room and a garden at the rear. A bit overwhelmed with TV football on a Saturday afternoon but pleasant enough.
