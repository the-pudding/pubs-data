Alan Winfield left this review about The New Inn

The New Inn is a nice looking stone built pub that is just up cemetery road from the Albert.
Once inside this is quite a small pub,there is a very small cosy lounge to the front,this room is oblong shaped,carpeted and has very comfy bench seating and small stools,quiet background music was playing,there is a small narrow bar to the rear which is far more basic,there is a darts board in this room.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Youngs London Gold which was a nice drink,the other beer was Bombardier.
The lounge was quite busy in this very pleasant pub.

Pub visited 13/12/2014
