Quinno _ left this review about The Tailrace Inn

Fairly modern bar with few real distinguishing features. L-shaped, part carpeted and part tiled with curtains, a set of U-shaped fabric banquettes and the usual black and white local pics. TV sports, jukebox and awkwardly positioned pool table. No ale and literally nothing of interest on the bar, so a bottle of Harviestoun Bitter & Twisted saved the day, though the pitted glass was less impressive. WiFi.

Accommodation offered and in contrast to the failings of the bar it was very good, with one of the few proper working showers on the whole route. As a pub though, it offers little of interest compared to some of its competitors in the village.
