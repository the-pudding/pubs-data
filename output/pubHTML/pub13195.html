Quinno _ left this review about The Old Ford

A nice old building adjacent to the station which unfortunately houses a rough old pub. Looked like someone tried to refurb it to a decent standard 15-20 years ago but it has gone to seed. Layout is a long single bar with a separate raised room to the left hosting a pool table. Large screen TV showing some old gumpf through knackered speakers. The fitted trophy cabinets with glass fronts hold no trophies just assorted crap. Three pumps with just Bombardier on which was well past its best. Sweary regulars and a landlady who seemed to be taking too much lip for my liking. Oddly there were flowers on the tables but that was the equivalent of polishing a turd really. Despite my train not being for another 20 mins, I secreted my barely-drunk half and sat on the platform instead – I’d seen enough.

On 16th September 2017 - rating: 2
