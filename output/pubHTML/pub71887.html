Will Larter left this review about Shoulder Of Mutton

This is the sort of pub that probably derives 90% of its income from weekend food, so finding a decent beer here was a bonus. Having had my fill of Bradfield Belgian Blue in three out of the four previous pubs, I went for the Timothy Taylor Boltmaker, which was in excellent condition. There's a real fire here too, a solid fuel stove that had the doors open, providing a welcome glow.

Highlight of the visit, though, was this exchange at the bar: (Barman to colleague) "Can you serve this dude?". (Me) "Not sure I'm a dude." (Barman) "You are."

Date of visit: 10th December 2017
