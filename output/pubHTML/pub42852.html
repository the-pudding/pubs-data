Will Larter left this review about The Old Peacock

It was very busy at the time of my visit: not very surprising as I was among a crowd of over 16 thousand attending a Leeds Rhinos game at the stadium across the road. I could see numerous hand pumps but most of them were without pump clips. Some Osset beers were listed on a blackboard, and I had a half of their 1919 Peacock 4% in a plastic drinking vessel, which is apparently normal for match days. What must it be like for Leeds United home games? I guess I'll never know. The beer was nothing special, but at least there was no exploitation or profiteering involved, as I was charged £1.70 (cheaper than the city centre)and the frothy beer was topped up without prompting.

Date of visit: 8th February 2018

aleand hearty left this review about The Old Peacock

It has been reported that the lease of The Old Peacock, opposite the LUFC ground, has been taken over by Ossett Brewery. The pub is currently undergoing a major refurb and should be open by the start of the new season. Nine real ales are promised. Should be good news for football fans...assuming you can actually get through the door.
