john mcgraw left this review about The Oatlands Chaser

After a marathon walk from Walton on Thames railway station I found this pub a fairly decent drinking hole.One part is a pub like place and the other part is more of a restaurant.Two of the usual suspect Young's and Fuller's were on with the Mary Jane being in very good form.Easy to knock with the Surrey set being in attendance but on the whole not a bad watering hole.
