Colin Walker left this review about Pitfirrane Arms Hotel

The place is a family run business, which is situated, in the small village of Crossford about a mile from Dunfermline. There is live music, karaoke and various entertainment in the bar and function suite. On a Sunday there is a quiz night which is the longest running quiz in Fife.
