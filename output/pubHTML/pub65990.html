Quinno _ left this review about The Angel

Nice and posh in here. Neo-Classical touches on what looks like a solid Georgian building. This is a large hotel with a rear bar and plenty of upmarket rooms to explore/lounge in, including one with a bloke playing a piano on our Saturday evening sojourn. Some pleasing internal architecture to admire, including (it says here) nice dado railings. The bar room where we ended up (‘Foxhunter Bar’, I think) was a shades-of-brown effort, with some nice half-wood panelling. Three ales: 2xWye Valley brews and Rhymney. I had a nice enough drop of WV Bitter, though it needed topping-up. Relatively quiet when we first got in around 9 but rammed 20 minutes later. I quite liked it here.

On 17th February 2014 - rating: 7
