Alan Winfield left this review about The Black Horse

The Black Horse is a decent looking pub that is on the corner of a road junction.
Once inside the bar faces in a decent sized L shaped room that is carpeted throughout,there is bench seating and chairs to the front,the room extend down the left side which is larger than the front area,there are normal tables and chairs and bench seating here.
There is a pool table and darts board to the front right.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Deuchars IPA,this was a decent drink.
Background music was playing.
I though this was a pleasant and comfortable pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 29/7/2017
