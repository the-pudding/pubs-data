Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Eastham Ferry Hotel

Substantial Marstons hotel overlooking the former eponymous landing stage (abandoned in 1929). However, you can still get some idea of what the place was like in its heyday from the scale of the pub's facilities. Quite a grand central bar with various seating areas around it. Patio terrace out front with some good views. Lots of 'Two for One' meal deal banners and posters. Three handpumps, offering Pedigree, Banks's Mild and Boondoggle. Service a bit unfriendly, though. (NB - Review dates from September 2011.)
