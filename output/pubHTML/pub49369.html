Colin Walker left this review about The White Gates

The White Gates sits on the Dunfermline to Alloa the A907. The pub is very easy to miss and by the time you see the place you are going past. The place is a typical country local. There is nothing fancy about the place, just down to earth people passing the time of day. A sign shows that there is a restaurant but I did not have time to sample. There is parking available down the left side of the pub at the rear.

On 25th March 2015 - rating: 10
