Debbie Pettit left this review about The White Horse

The original owners are now back in the White Horse and it has been smartened up considerably. A real ale is now in place and there is only one fruit machine. The back garden has had an overhaul and flowers make this smoking area a bright feature. A new shelter with tables and chairs is also being updated in the rear garden. Most weekends now have a live band of some sort and all sky sports are being shown. I was in there this weekend and both high profile football matches were being shown sunday at 4.00pm after the singer had finished and there was also another tv showing the rugby at the same time !!. Everyones needs were catered for and the atmosphere was very friendly as were the bar staff.

On 12th March 2013 - rating: 8

E TA left this review about The White Horse

The White Horse is a Victorian end-of-terrace local in the crowded residential area which used to support the paper mills. It consist of a single bar running the depth of the building, a tiny back yard and a small covered smoking area to the side. Reception was friendly, the decor is tired and the multiple gaming machines somewhat depressing. The lack of real ale means I probably won't be back in a hurry.

On 1st August 2012 - rating: 5
