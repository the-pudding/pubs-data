just had a lovely week in walton,visited a few pubs but definately best by miles is The Bath House-modernly done up,warm(was a trifle chilly out),lovely staff(especially Simon and Martin),good food and very reasonable prices(cheapest in walton)-we were made to feel like regulars and they even went out especially and got our preferred drink-which they didnt normally stock-add to that a great juke box,darts nite9with free chilli) and kareoke and all in all was top class! well done!! look forward to return next year xx

Jan May left this review about The Bath House

Simply the best pub in Walton and for miles (and miles) around! We visit Walton a couple of times a year and the Bath House is always our pub of choice. Nice location on the seafront, FANTASTIC BEER PRICE, great atmosphere, very friendly locals and staff and the guv'nor very welcoming. Can't fault it. We've had some great evenings there and even some great afternoons sitting outside in the summer. 10/10! Jan and Billy

Adam Smith left this review about The Bath House

Not enough good words in the English dictionary to say about this Fantastic Pub/Restaurant. Let's start from the beginning. My first experience of The Bath House was some 3 years ago, and was outstanding back then and even more so now. For a locals pub (I'm not a local I'm from Basildon) everyone makes u feel welcome all the locals and barstaff talk to you and make it a very homely place to be. I always make sure I go to The BathHouse when in the local area. Outstanding value for money on food, I mean where else can you find A massive steak burger and chips (not for the faint hearted) for about £6.00 and having the reassurance that the proprietor is a chef, not only that the chef he employs caters for your every need with no complaints, and is also the best chef in Essex, my wife has the seafood cocktail on a bed of fresh salad which always goes down a treat and this is for little over a £5. Some people may be put off because the price and may take the impression "Cheap & Cheerful" well the only true statement of this is "Cheap" but Dave & Lenny's menu is designed to give you the best food without ripping you off, and they do this just superbly. As for the drinks Monday - Friday £2.00 a pint day & night (selected drinks) is outstanding. I would like to take this opportunity to say thanks to everyone at The Bath House for making every visit a pleasant, friendly and superb Culinry experience. Adam & Sherry
