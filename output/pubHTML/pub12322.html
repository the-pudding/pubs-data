Komakino . left this review about The Bell Inn

Primarily a roadside eatery, this has a decent bar area to the right on entry (nice leafy carpet) which had the Trojan Horse of Sharp's "Bell Ale" accompanied by Dartmoor Jail Ale (OK) and Weston's Old Rosie as a real cider. A small, flagstone floored eating area with tables dolled out to the left and a higher ceilinged, restauranty bit area beyond the bar. Clutch of locals around the bar made getting served a tad trying, and a Spotify playlist in the background made itself known with intermittent adverts.

On 14th April 2018 - rating: 4
