Steve C left this review about The Illustrious Bar and Cafe

Illustrious Bar and Café backs onto the toilets in the centre of a satellite walkway. I think that most are seen to be hurrying past on the way to their gates. Five draught products to enjoy if your flight is delayed at the last minute and you can’t be bothered to walk back to the terminal. This was my least favourite of the five airside drinking venues.
