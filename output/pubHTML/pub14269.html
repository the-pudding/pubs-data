Richard Arwkrite left this review about Starting Gate Travel Inn

Situated on the outskirts of the city the pub /restaurant is a Beefeater with a name for good food. Its handy if you are staying at the adjacent Travel Inn. Access for a wheelchair from the car park is reasonable to the cluttered bar area and a w/chair lift is provided for entry to the restaurant. A real ale or two are available but a short taxi ride can get you to the excellent pubs that Hereford has to offer. However the Grandstand has everything the weary traveller may require.
