moby duck left this review about Hiver Taproom & Shop

Another newish entrant to the Bermondsey Beer Mile, this one sells beers that make use of honey in the ingredients, there was only a choice of four beers, I went for the IPA which was rather bland tasting not helped in any way by the plastic (or plastic substitute) glass.
I wasn't inspired to plan a revisit.
