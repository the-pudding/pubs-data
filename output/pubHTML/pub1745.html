Quinno _ left this review about The Six Bells

Down to two ales the other day, both national brands (think I had Courage?). There's a nice grassed beer garden out back with a jumbo weeping willow but you get the feeling that they could try a lot harder in here.

October 2014
Small but decent local that serves up to four ales in decent if sometimes uninspiring condition. Nice rear garden. A hangout for the local Morris Men - you might get a go on their wooden sticks if you're lucky.
