Olga Mathews left this review about The Blue Anchor

A busy and popular village local. The pub is all no smoking.
Beers on offer include Manchester Rambler & Doom Bar.
Wide choice of food, children's menu, veg option and specials board. Servings are generous, most dishes are offered as regular and smaller portions. The liver & bacon was delicious.
Large garden to rear with seating and children's play area.
Car Park to side.
