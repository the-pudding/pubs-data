Reviews of The Outside Chance (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Quinno _ left this review about The Outside Chance

Out of town outlet with an inviting exterior lawn. Small, tight, multi-area’ed interior with a faint l-shape and some full-length wood panelling. Homely set-up, I liked the grandmother clock and it was clean and tidy throughout. Large grassed beer garden looked inviting. Split of punters between the drinkers (mainly watching the footy) and the diners. Nice atmosphere prevailing. Three Waddies ales, I forget to note what I had but it went down without any problem. Quite a good little place, though not really walkable from Marlborough centre unless you’re a seasoned foot-plodder.

On 11th May 2014 - rating: 7
