Philip Gaskin left this review about The Talbot

This is not the sort of place we would normally visit, but as the site did not have any photos, and it is a few miles away, we tried it on a warm spring day. The usual corporate plastic look and feel. No real ales available, but the prices of some of the keg offerings seemed competitive. It is a large place, so there is a separate bar area, and the place was clean. We do not plan to return.
