Old Boots left this review about Manor House

Two room hilltop pub on the main A68 road. It has a dining room to the left and a bar with five pulls to the right. Guests from Morland, Bateman, and Sonnet43 when I visited. Beer quality was excellent. Exposed stone walls and tasteful pictures among a plethora of chalkboards as decor. Traditionally furnished in both rooms it's fairly food led.
