Alan Winfield left this review about The Engine Inn

The Engine is a nice looking pub that is one of three pubs that are within sight of each other.
Once inside there is a single U shaped room with the bar facing,the room has comfy bench seating and small stools and is carpeted,there is a darts board to the rear right and the TV was showing Sky sports football updates.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Woodfords Wherry which went down well,the other beers were Brew X1 and Wadworth 6X.
This is a comfy and pleasant pub to have a drink in.
