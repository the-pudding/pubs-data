Komakino . left this review about The Beech House

Located on a parade of shops either side of road leading to the train station, this establishment proclaims 'food and drink' on its green facade and to their credit, they have three handpumps albeit from the Fuller's stable (LP, Oliver's Island and the 6 Nations-themed Front Row), although the Oliver's was reversed. The Front Row wasn't cheap at £4 for a pint, but fair play to them for leaving an area out front for those who just want to pop in for a beer.
