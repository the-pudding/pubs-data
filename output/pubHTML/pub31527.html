Komakino . left this review about The Beehive

A large, two-roomed Wadworth house, partially hidden from the road by all manner of foliage in front of an extensively decked area out front. They seem to have got the vibe right for the town as the right-hand room is open plan and bare floored with a glut of TVs showing a Premier League game on a Sunday afternoon; the room on the left-hand side is left for contemplative drinking and eating and has a beaten, dark-wooded bar in front and extensive carpet throughout. The three ale line-up consisted of 6X, IPA and Cooper's Vice. It's never going to win any awards, especially as it's a Waddies house, but if you could swap the Siege's ale range for here, you could be onto a winner...

On 2nd November 2016 - rating: 4
