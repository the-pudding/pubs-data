Real Ale Ray left this review about Oddfellows

A good well run ale pub this one and well worth a visit. This is a one room pub with a beer garden. The barmaid was very welcoming and chatty. There were three ales on handpump and a further three on the back of the bar. I went for the Gundog Brewery Top Gun and Milestone Olde English Ale, both were top notch. The pub has a good relaxed atmosphere to it and the locals were friendly.

Alan Stobbs left this review about Oddfellows

Visited Oddfellows last night as Sunday is quiz night and found a choice of eight real ales on show. Three on the bar and five on an addition to the back wall which can accommidate up to six beers in pins. Tried Derwent Mutineers and Sonnet 43 blonde beer both in very good condition. If you are in the area 2nd to 6th May watch out for the beer festival in the beer garden out the back.
