Alan Winfield left this review about The Old Swan

The Old Swan is situated close to the main shopping area.
Once inside there was a lounge to the right which was carpeted and had some leather settees,there was a dining area to the right and a small bar on the left side of the pub.
There were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Taylor Landlord and this was a very nice drink the other beers were Sharps Doom Bar and GK IPA.
This was a decent enough pub to end our crawl in.
