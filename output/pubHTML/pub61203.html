Graham Coombs left this review about The Notter Bridge Inn

Refubished pub on stretch of old road off A38, by River Lynher. Seems to be mainly aimed at food trade, but served a very nice pint of Otter, with usual Tribute etc also. Smallish bar area, with rather too much furniture wedged in, with a larger restaurant area and conservatory. Also tables outside at front. Has its own little zoo (entrance charge). Large car park at rear.

On 5th July 2014 - rating: 7
