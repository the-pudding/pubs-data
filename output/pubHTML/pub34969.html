Reviews of Cock & Pye (Average Rating: 5½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about Cock & Pye

A small and very historic exterior hides a larger and fairly tedious pub behind. There is one long room which widens out at the back, with a bar to the side and a small patio behind. Ales on offer were IPA, Abbot and Hogs Back TEA. At least it remains a pub, with few others in this corner of town.
