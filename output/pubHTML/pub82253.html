Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Bessemer Arms

Pub/restaurant housed in the newer buildings on the 'down' platform of the Bluebell Railway's Sheffield Park station. Surprisingly basic seating / dining area, but the counter has been made to look more traditional. A canopy has also now been erected along the two external faces which adds a bit more character. Two real ales on handpump: the 'house' Harvey's Halt and Arundel Castle (on special offer at just £2.50).
