Alan Winfield left this review about The Swan Inn

The Swan is a really nice looking white painted pub with a thatched roof.
Once inside the main bar was T shaped with the bar facing,there was a nice stone floor and a beamed ceiling,there were a few tables and chairs near the entrance,the main seating area was to the right,this had normal tables and chairs and a real fire on the go,there was a dining area up one step to the left.
The pub is an Everards tied house,there were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Everards Beacon bitter which was a very nice drink,the other beers were Everards Tiger bitter and Amberella an Everards seasonal beer.
The pub was very busy with drinkers having a good time,we had to sit on stools at the bar,all other seating was in use.
This is a very pleasant pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 30/11/2013
