ROB Camra left this review about The Top House

Open again, but may not be for long on this showing. There's some out of place comfy sofa's at the front of the pub. A pool table against the wall towards the rear and there's also a quiz machine. I called in on my way back to the station at about 7:30 on a Friday. Most of the people in here appeared to have been on the beer for most of the day. There's no real ale in here, so I had a half of something fizzy. The locals seem friendly enough though as a couple of them moved over to allow me to get to the bar. I doubt that I'll ever visit again though.
