Al Bundy left this review about The Irby Mill

Nice but small country pub. Though it does sell eurofizz lagers you're not going to get any throw-it-down throw-it-up scallies in here. You are, however, likely to get irate dog owners giving you dark looks cos youve just stood on their dog thats hiding under tables/lying in the way. Has 5 or 6 real ales for strokey-beard Camra bores.
