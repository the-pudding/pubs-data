Alan Winfield left this review about Kingsdown Inn

The Kingsdown is a stand alone pub on the corner of a road junction and it is the closest Arkells house to the brewery so it could be classed as the brewery tap.
Once inside there was one room that was well furnished and very comfortable,there was also the full range of Arkells beers on the bar i had a drink of Arkells 3B and this was a very nice drink the other beers were 2B, Kingsdown Ale and an Arkells seasonal beer.
I thought this was a decent pub to have a few drinks in.
