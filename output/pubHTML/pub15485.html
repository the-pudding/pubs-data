E TA left this review about The Red Bull

The Red Bull is a Shepherd and Neame country pub, an old family run community pub with a carpeted floor, low beams and a mix of tables and sofas. There are two bars and a separate eating area, whiel one corner has unobtrusive gaming machines and a tv. The staff were very friendly, and the greeting was warm. There were 4 pumps on the bar, 3 with Sheps clips and one reserved for guest ales, though there was no guest on when I came in. I had a pint of Spitfire which was in perfect condition. A decent village pub in a lovely area, worth stopping for lunch or having a quiet pint in the evening.

Traditional village pub with low ceilings and lots of wood (created over time from at least different buildings). Friendly atmosphere, and several comfy chairs. Four handpumps (with three on at time of visit), bur beer offering spoiled slightly by the Spitfire which was just starting to turn.
