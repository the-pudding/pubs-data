BobOs . left this review about The Red Lion

Excellent pub with excellent ales and a warm welcome. The pub stands opposite the old brewery which is now Brewers Quay (where the welcome isn't as warm, or the beers as well-kept). I had a pint each of the Bombardier, FortyNiner, JD1742 and Chesil - all of which were top notch. The food was good value with decent portions and reasonably priced, although the menu consists of ordinary pub grub and could do with a bit of inspiration. Well above average.

Manky Badger left this review about The Red Lion

A fine ale selection, and a smashing area outside to sit and watch the world go by. Occasionally there's a band outside, and the bar staff are friendly and welcoming.
There are other pubs in Weymouth that the cognoscenti will suggest you visit, but this place is better than those...
