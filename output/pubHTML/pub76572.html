Richard Ewington left this review about The White Cross Inn

A warm and friendly little pub built in the eighteenth century tucked away in Groes Wen Village. Real Ales, Real fire, real people and a real good feel. facilities include pool, darts, function room, Sky TV for matches. Anual Real Ale Beer festival in April. Quizez, Beer-Bellies club and music nights a regular thing.
