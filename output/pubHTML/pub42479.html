Gill Smith left this review about The New Inn

We had a job finding this pub in Sinton Green as it is tucked away, and I think we explored all the roads round the green in the dark before finding it, but it is a real community pub with friendly landlord and the Wye Valley HPA was first class. 2 other handpumps with Pedigree and Marstons Burton Bitter being the usual. There are 2 rooms bar and lounge, and a lovely roaring fire in the bar area. When I came back in after taking the photo, a cat dashed in and went right round the room under the long seat along the wall to claim it's place by the fire. Homely.
