Quinno _ left this review about White Hart

A pretty average Youngs pub really, a bit of 'chain bar' sanitisation inside. Decent seating outside, a saving grace at least. Only three ales on (Ordinary, Special and Bombardier) which isn't great for a pub this size and two of them ran out quickly without being replaced. Not cheap either.

Preferred the Four Horseshoes over the road - whilst their wasn't beer not overly superior it had a better pub atmosphere. But neither really convince.
