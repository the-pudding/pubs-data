Ian Mapp left this review about Thatched Tavern

Looking forward to my visit after a long walk from Evesham.

Signposted as 13th Century - its a timber framed, thatched (obviously) pub. Lovely welcome as I entered.

Green king ales on show - the IPA, an Abbot and a Sharpe's Atlantic. Not the most inspiring of choices, but the Abbot was in superb condition.

Nice pub.

Trip blogged at http://bit.ly/2hcW8ce
