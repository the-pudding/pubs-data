Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Marine

Narrow-fronted pub, with an elongated bar squeezed in between a few side tables supplementing those in the slightly wider area at the front and conservatory restaurant at the back. Plenty of decor, but no obvious nautical theme. Rear patio beer garden. Reasonable bar food menu. Friendly atmosphere. Pride, Abbot and some very respectable Harveys available from three of four handpumps in operation.
