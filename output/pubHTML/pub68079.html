Oggwyn Great left this review about Plume of Feathers

Recently re-furbed pub on the Much Wenlock to Shrewsbury road , inside the old multi-room interior has been knocked out to make a large L shaped room , the older part of the pub is to the right of the entrance with a large open stone fireplace showing the age of the pub , the lefthand side has a modern conservatory extension tagged on and is more a dining area .
There are the usual keg taps and four handpulls with Salopian Shropshire Gold and a decent Ludlow Gold , the pulls are split two each side of the L shaped bar and i only noticed the other two on the way out and did not note the beers .
