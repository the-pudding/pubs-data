A B left this review about The White Swan

Excellent village pub, divided into a small bar and large restaurant; the food is excellent, the staff are friendly and personable, and the beer is great. Woodforde, Oakham and Adnams are regular beers plus a single guest ale pump. German lager - Bitburger? and Harry Sparrow Cider also available on tap.

On 20th December 2012 - rating: 8
