Komakino . left this review about The White Hart

A gastropub in a village just off the A420 Oxford-Swindon road. There's a nice bar area with large, open stone fireplace (albeit with a modern stove burner inside), a smattering of tables and chairs, quarry-tiled floor and a bar offering Charles Wells Eagle, Sharp's Doom Bar and the local Loose Cannon Abingdon Bridge. There's a higher-ceilinged dining area to the left of the bar with beams galore. Lots of awards on display regarding the food, but fair play to them for still operating as a pub at least.

On 20th November 2017 - rating: 6
