Reviews of The Crown Tavern (Average Rating: 2 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Quinno _ left this review about The Crown Tavern

A small sort-of micropub in the modern and covered shopping arcade. Exposed brick interior, dart board and football on the telly. Bizarrely, the barman wouldn't give me the Wi-Fi code, much to the amusement of the cackling locals. One ale on, GK London Glory, which wasn’t up to much. It’s not often I feel genuinely unwelcome in a pub, but this place managed it.

On 2nd August 2018 - rating: 2
