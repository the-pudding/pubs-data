John Horton left this review about The Malt Shovel

If pubs were graded on the view out the back then this would be up with the best. Well presented local reasonably priced real ales, good food. Large 17th century building with bar & lounge areas. Large gardens - yes more than one. Large car park. When in the area this is my pub of choice.

On 3rd September 2008 - rating: 10
