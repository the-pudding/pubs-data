James Joines left this review about White Horse Inn

A fine traditional pub with a garden at the front. Good size main bar with 2 real fires and a second bar with exposed beams. Johns Cask and a guest (Black Sheep on my visit) available in this fine building with 2 bars and a covered smoking area.
Now with a food offering served outside when weather permits and childrens play area.
Car parking available at the front.
