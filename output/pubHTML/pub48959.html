Colin Walker left this review about The Dreadnought

This former named Halfway House pub is now called Dreadnought is a pub in Leith, (Although nearer to Newhaven) in the North side of Edinburgh that was recently named 1st in the February 2018 City of Edinburgh Pub of the Year in the Campaign for Real Ale (Camra) awards. Here is what was on the beer / ale menu in my early 04/08/18 visit, Deca Gram Mono American pale ale, Brass castle Brewery Northern Blonde, Two by Two Amarillo Mosaic, Two by Two Choc Cherry Porter, Straight to Hell Lager, Samuel Smiths Cider Reserve, Pilot Blonde, Pilot Mochaccino Stout, Brass castle Disruptor Cider & more on tap.
