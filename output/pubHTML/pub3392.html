Al Bundy left this review about Red Lion

A nice country pub not far from where and close to there. A Vintage Inns place. Very comfortable interior with an L-shaped bar. 3 real ales of which one was off, one was foul and one was ok. £3.40 + for a pint. Beer garden out front. No pond outside anymore.
