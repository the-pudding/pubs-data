Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Quinno _ left this review about The Green Dragon

Wadworth pub in a pretty village. Tastefully done out internally to a modern but cosy standard with green the predominant colour over the various rooms. To the rear is a small but nice grassed garden with flowers and a chicken coop. Four ales on; Wadworth 6X, IPA, Swordfish and a decent pint of Batemans QED. Expensive here - a pint, a half and a large lemonade and lime clocked in at £9.70. A decent stop. 6.5

On 29th October 2017 - rating: 6
