Gill Smith left this review about The Five Alls

Called into this pub which features in the 2019 Good Beer Guide while on a tour of Gwent pubs. It is very much a rock music pub with a juke box, live music and open mic nights. Friendly bar staff served our Hydes Flying Flanker which was fine. Traditional pub with games, pinball machine and pool table.
