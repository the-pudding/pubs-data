Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Hog & Hop

One of several ephemeral food / drink stalls on Folkestone's harbour arm, but this one has cask beer from Gadd's (Seasider) and Pig & Porter (Skylarking, £1.90 half). Wonderfully relaxed atmosphere on a nice summer day, sat on the rustic seating, listening to a very proficient trio on a temporary stage, and generally watching the world go by.
