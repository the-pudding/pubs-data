Alan Hurdle left this review about Giler Arms

23/09/2011
A welcoming country inn signposted off the A5 near Pentrefoelas. A lounge, public bar and games room are served from a central bar. Real Ale (mild and bitter) from Bathems brewery. The pub is in the 2012 Good Beer Guide and has been awarded the Mild Pub of the Year by the Abercolwyn branch of CAMRA. Closed Mondays and lunch times except Saturday and Sunday.
01/04/2017
Now open every day from 6pm. No longer owned by Bathems. Beers now from Purple Moose and Conwy breweries.
