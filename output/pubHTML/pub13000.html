ROB Camra left this review about Hope & Anchor Inn

Small, friendly, noisy, locals local. One roomer with standard tables & chairs. Quiz machine in the corner on what looks like a small dance floor. Music played way too loud, but the people in the pub seemed to be enjoying it. No cask ale, so I had a half of Carling. It's not for me, but it does look to be a successful local.
