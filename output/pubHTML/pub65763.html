Lee Shaw left this review about Cock O' The North

I've had the pleasure of samplings this taps ales on a few occasions and extremely tasty they were, it has a good atmosphere and a large seating area outside in the garden which also houses a small campsite so ideal for those dont wanna go home nights....they also do carry outs of your fave ales in handy containers. Tis well worth a visit but stick to halves that way you get to taste the numerous micro brewery ales on offer.....Enjoy..
