Alan Winfield left this review about Revolution

Revolution is a large pub /bar that is on the apex of two roads in the middle of Derby.
Once inside i went in a large room at the front this room had windows on either side and the bar was facing,there were raised leather bench seating to the right and american diner type seating to the left there were three small tables and chairs in the middle of the room which is where i sat,i noticed on my way out that there is a smarter room that is carpeted and had more comfortable seating to the rear of the bar.
There were no real ales on the bar and i had the choice of Tetley smooth flow or Guinness i went for the Guinness and this was probably the worst pint of Guinness i have ever had,i wish i had gone for the Tetley smooth crap it would have been better than the Guinness.
This is not my sort of pub but with only a few left in Derby to do i had to go in it.
