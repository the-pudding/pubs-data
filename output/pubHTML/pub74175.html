ROB Camra left this review about Sleepers

A very pleasant and nicely decorated cafe bar. A few tables and chairs outside for those sunny days, this definitely wasn't one. Very busy on our Thursday afternoon visit with most of the tables being taken up by diners, although drinkers are welcome. The bar is to the right on entering and if you look closely, almost hidden by menus, there's a handpump. This was pulling Roosters Yankee which was a good pint. Ms CAMRA had a glass of red wine which she also thought was good. It's a very nice pleasant place and whilst not really a pub we were happy enough sitting in here. We may well eat here at some time in the future.

On 9th January 2017 - rating: 6
