Ian Mapp left this review about The Howard Arms

Very handsome country freehouse with emphasis on food. Outside has lots of certificates - inlcluding cask marque (expired) and Sawdays special places.

Set in a pretty location in a lovely village. Most tables set for dining. Four real ales on Ubu, Doom Bar, Hook Norton and Landlord. I may have simply made the wrong choice but my landlord was hazy, on the cusp of vinegar and with small bits of detritus floating in the head. Shame, as it had a lot going for it.

You may be luckier.

Visit blogged at http://bit.ly/2mDHUpL
