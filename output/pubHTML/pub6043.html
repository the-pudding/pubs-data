Will Larter left this review about Thin White Duke

More of a cafe or bistro than a bar, with waiter service at tables predominating at the time of my visit. There are a couple of stools at the bar and two hand pumps serving Lancaster Citra and Loweswater Gold. I didn't stop for a drink.

Date of visit: 19th April 2016
