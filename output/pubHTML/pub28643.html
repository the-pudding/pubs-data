Steve of N21 left this review about Royal Oak

The Royal Oak is a welcoming Free House situated on the B1132 between Bungay and Norwich.
The bar supports 17 real ale hand pumps with a good selection of beers from regional breweries from all over the country. Then there is usually an additional 2 ciders and real ales from gravity.
