Southern Man left this review about The Falstaff

Large two storey split level estate pub built in 1968 on the northern edge of Plymouth and with a surprisingly unspoilt interior comprising the large Lounge bar on the right (mostly used for events) with views over the rolling South Devon hills and the Public bar on the left. It is very much a community pub with only one Real Ale - Doom Bar!
