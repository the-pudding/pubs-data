Will Larter left this review about The Well

Now open again with a smart grey paint job on the exterior and rough or recycled timber on the walls inside. Goes back a long way, so could hold quite a few customers if they turn up. Cocktails prominently advertised, but there are also a couple of hand pumps, with beers from Tring Brewery. I had the Side Pocket for a Toad, which was in good condition but took forever to pour because of a faulty line. At £1.80 for a half, this was the most expensive beer I've ever had in Luton. I'm told that some regulars from the now closed English Rose have found their way here - I don't imagine many would bother to cross the road from the Bricklayers Arms.

On 15th February 2017 - rating: 6
