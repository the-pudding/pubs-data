ROB Camra left this review about The Pavilion

This is mainly a restaurant, but there's an area for drinkers including an outdoor terrace overlooking the attached bowling green which is a real suntrap. Quite a few of the locals from Birtle Brook use this as their local. We call in here a couple of times a year for a meal and a game of bowls. There's no real ale of course, but they do serve Peroni on draught, plus a few bottles and a large selection of wines.
