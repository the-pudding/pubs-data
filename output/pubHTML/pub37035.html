Reviews of Red Lion (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Ian Mapp left this review about Red Lion

Small hamlet with a scattering of houses, this does have the enviable position of being a short walk from the Burton Dassett Country Park.... downhill to the pub and uphill back up!

Seems to have had a chequered recent past of closures and reopenings... It's a simple enough single room pub, offering food and two hand pulled ales - Purity and an Xmas one from wychavon brewery.

Needs punters. Two in on a later Friday lunch time at Xmas doesn't bode too well.

On 28th December 2018 - rating: 6
