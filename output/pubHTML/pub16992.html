Aqualung . left this review about The Commercial Hotel (JD Wetherspoon)

This is an original pub and hotel although it's not currently one of the JDW chain of hotels. Inside it's the usual open plan layout but split into areas with the bar at the rear right and a raised area over to the left. I came here on a Wednesday morning for breakfast and a pint so it wasn't busy.
The bar has ten hand pumps which had one unused, two ciders, the JDW Trio Of Doom, Naylors Lancashire, Coach House Gunpowder Mild, Three Bs Stokers Slake and one called Accy Bees which may have been a house beer.
I had the Stokers Slake which was in good nick and went down well with the breakfast.
This seemed quite a reasonable Spoons to me.
