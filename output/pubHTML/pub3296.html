Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Mill @ Upton

Attractive main road pub, extended and refurbished (both inside and out). The light, modern furniture and decor in the various seating / dining areas within the split-level interior won't be to everyone's taste, but to my mind works quite well. Holt's Bitter, IPA and Two Hoots, plus an excellent Bootleg Urban Fox (£3.25) available from a total of six handpumps. Yes, it's a food-led establishment by a Premier Inn, but actually a decent place for a pint too.
