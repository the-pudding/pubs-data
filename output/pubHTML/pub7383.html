Will Larter left this review about The Soldier Dick

We walked here along the canal tow path from Bugsworth basin and the Navigation Inn - a very pleasant walk. There are five hand pumps in the bar and a friendly welcome, but the four changing beers mentioned on WhatPub have now become just two: Courage Directors and another mainstream beer. There were also three keg beers. Sorry, craft ales. I tried the DE14 Flight Suit orange pale ale (which is brewed by Marstons), but was not terribly impressed. We were a little disappointed with the beer range but happy enough with our trip out here. (It was about to go downhill, as we waited 40 minutes for our bus to Chapel en le Frith, but that's another story.)
