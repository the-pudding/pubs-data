Alan Winfield left this review about Ashley Brook

The Ashley Brook was a fairly modern pub when i visited it as the pub was built in 1990.
This is a bare brick built pub and once inside the pub has got two decent sized rooms,i had a drink in the bar which was quite comfortable with bench seating,the lounge looked quite nice and was carpeted.
This pub is a Joseph Holt tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Holts bitter and this was a really nice drink the other beer was Holts mild.
I quite liked this pub and it was a good addition to this area of Salford.
