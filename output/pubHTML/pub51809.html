Guy Hannay-Wilson left this review about Blue Peter Hotel

The warm Yorkshire welcome you will get here from Ian and Ruth Murray will be memorable! I tasted a superb pint of Rooster IPA which was pulled superbly. Ian changes his beers on his 2 handpulls regularly and evidence of this is found in the very many pumpclips. Great home cooked food and great locals who are always up for a natter. Try and catch the Red Squirrels in the Beer garden....but do so quietly!

On 28th June 2008 - rating: 10
