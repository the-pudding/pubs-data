Reviews of The White Horse (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Al Bundy left this review about The White Horse

A nice country pub in a small village just outside Ruthin Town. The pub is basically 3 rooms with a lounge, a pool room and a dining area. Comfortable fixtures and fittings make for a pleasant place
Outside seating front and rear. There was just the one real ale on from Conwy on my visit. The food is apparently good but I didn't eat so I'll take a locals word for it.
