Old Boots left this review about The Spread Eagle

A Marstons house with their range of beers on six pulls plus national kegs on a low T-bar. The small front bar is furnished with traditional wood furniture and has clean, neat décor with bareboards and a bit of exposed brick. A few old Marstons ad's brighten the walls. To the back is a larger lounge/diner with a darts board. There are screens and machines as expected. The pub has a nice local feel, possibly as it's in a less touristy part of the walled city.
