Reviews of House des Löwe (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about House des Löwe

A small, friendly bar right next to the Rochdale canal. It's one small room on two levels with a hotchpotch of furniture. We were greeted by a friendly barmaid on our Saturday, mid afternoon visit, but we were the only ones in. No real ale, but a couple of craft keg pumps. It's probably lively at night and weekends as there's a DJ rig and you can book an hours slot on the decks to play your own music on Tuesdays. It's a nice little place and we'd happily call in again. Still no-one else in when we left though.

On 20th June 2019 - rating: 7
