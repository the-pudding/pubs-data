Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Boat Inn

Large family-orientated pub in a fine location overlooking Kielder Water. Various seating areas inside, but if the weather permits the well-kept patio and grass beer garden is the place to be. Just two handpumps, but at least the beers are local: Wylam Turbinia and Magick (£3.25). Overall, does what it does pretty well. (NB - Review dates from August 2012.)
