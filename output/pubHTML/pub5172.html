Gill Smith left this review about Rising Sun Inn

Visited this pub as there was a beer festival on, and although up quite a steep hill leading out of the city, it is a friendly locals bar, and we enjoyed all the ales we tried. It appears very open plan, but the drinking areas are separate, and the bar area is at the front with the other areas having tables set up for dining. The raised area at the rear leads out to a sun trap of a patio garden where they had the beer festival stillage set up.
