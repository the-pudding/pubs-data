BobOs . left this review about The Bottle Inn

On my last visit it was good to see four local ales on - the Summa That (Branscombe Brewery) and Norman's Conquest (Cottage Brewing) were both excellent. Also on were Otter Ale and Otter Bitter. The food was enjoyable although the menu is not extensive, the portions are not over-generous and the prices are very steep. Just better than average - the prices let it down.

On 11th July 2011 - rating: 6

Albert Steptoe left this review about The Bottle Inn

Once a busy thriving pub it has now gone downhill so far it needs a jump start to get it going again.
Run of the mill food at London prices to accompany the lousy Beer, hopefully the Landlord who lives off the premises will get the message in 2008 and leave.
With the right Landlord this could be a thriving pub for Local and visitors alike.
If it states "Pym" over the door keep driving.

On 2nd January 2008 - rating: 2
