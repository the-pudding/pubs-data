jim Matthews left this review about The Wheatsheaf Inn

Lovely looking pub in a lovely location.It's a bit upmarket serving excellent food and superb well kept real ales.Cosy inside with log fires in winter,and a large well used seating area outside which gets busy in the summer.Situated on the edge of the village it has a large car park,staff are very pleasant.Rooms are available.Bus stop outside.

On 29th March 2015 - rating: 9
