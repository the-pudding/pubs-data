Ian Mapp left this review about The Green Dragon

Right on the rather nice village green and next to the pub. Dining area and separate lounge bar with rather comfy sofas. Four real ales on - Adnams, two from Purity and my chosen Hobsons Best. Really good pint. £3.40.
