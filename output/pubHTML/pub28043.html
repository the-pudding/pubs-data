Real Ale Ray left this review about The Horseshoes

A proper traditional pub, you get a friendly welcome and the manager enjoyed a good old chat when I was sitting at the bar. Beer very well kept and served straight from the cask,the cold room is just behind the bar area. I enjoyed the Earl Soham Victoria Bitter, Crouch Vale Brewers Gold and Adnams Bitter. Will defo call again.
