Steve C left this review about Sip & Stone

The airside Sip and Stone Bar and Kitchen is a typical airport venue that takes advantage of the captive crowd with inflated prices. The bar area is sandwiched between an open kitchen and a coffee bar, all of which face an abundance of tables and chairs. There is no cask ale here, but there are plenty of keg options (Amstel, Birra Moretti, Heineken, Tiger Beer, Cute Hoor IPA, Murphy’s, Guinness and Orchard Thieves Cider). Pub grub mains are available from the kitchen. I opted for a £13 fry-up which wasn’t worth the money. Burger and chips came in at an eye watering £17.50.
I found the staff to be polite, but next time I fly out of Belfast International I think I will have my lunch in the adjacent Burger King.

On 4th March 2019 - rating: 4
