Alan Winfield left this review about Fox Inn

The Fox Inn is in a really nice looking village that is fairly close to Stow on the Wold.
Once inside this pub had a bar area which had a stone floor and normal tables and chairs and there was a small raised area to the rear which was carpeted and a dining area at the back of the pub,there was another dining area to the right this was in a seperate room.
This pub is a Donnington tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Donnington BB and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Donnington SBA.
The toilets are well worth a visit to see the saucy postcards and jokes on the walls.
We thought this was a really nice pub in a lovely setting and well worth the visit.
