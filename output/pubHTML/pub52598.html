Graham Coombs left this review about The Ostrich Inn

Busy village inn handy for castle and priory ruins. Rather lovely old building has been refurbished, retaining much charm, but perhaps just a shade pretentious. Worth a visit though. A couple of ales from Greene King, including a house-badged one and good -lloking food, although not sampled.

On 26th August 2013 - rating: 7
