Alan Hurdle left this review about Ye Olde Bulls Head

25/05/2012
Comfortable L-shaped beamed lounge and separate public bar. Real Ales served are Greene King IPA and Hobgoblin. In The Good Beer Guide until 2011.

08/10/2016
Just reopened after a short closure for refurbishment. Décor now much smarter and comfortable. One large wall decorated with ultra large scale old ordnance survey map of Bala.
