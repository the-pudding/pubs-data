Keno G left this review about Driftwood Beach Bar n' Grill

Unfortunately I did not have the same experience as Colin. The poor quality of the food was only matched by the appalling service received. The chicken was colder than our drinks, evidently straight out of the freezer, served with a stale baguette. Not only were we denied bluntly when we asked for it to be warmed. When I requested the manager for a refund, he responded by shouting aggressively, making it clear that we were not going to receive any refund as the menu did not specifically stated that it had to be warm. Ludicrous!
Avoid at all costs!
