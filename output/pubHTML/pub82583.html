Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Cotton Mill

Modern Marston's pub / restaurant with Grill & Pizza branding, and generally indistinguishable from other such outlets from the same chain (with the usual semi-modern furniture and a small patio area out front). Still, it does do real ale from the two banks of handpumps: Pedigree, Hobgoglin and Young's Special (£3.20).
