Reviews of The Mytton & Mermaid Add Review see review guidelines

Delboy 20 left this review about The Mytton and Mermaid

Substantial and listed coaching Inn / hotel on the banks of the River Severn. The emphasis is on food but there are a few comfortable seat in the bar area. Also a decent choice of beer with Doom Bar, Shropshire Gold and Hobson's Old Prickly available on my visit. The Gold was fine. A decent place for a quick stop and I was happy to have visited.
