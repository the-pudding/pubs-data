Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Doric Arch

If you are looking for a decent pint near the station, but want to avoid the usual crush at the Euston Tap(s), this Fuller's pub (formerly the Head of Steam) is a reliable bet. The bar is housed in the first floor of a very undistinguished block near the bus stops, with a split-level arrangement that makes the most of a fairly limited amount of space. Semi-traditional furniture and decor mostly comprising railway memorabilia. On this visit, I found four of the brewery's beers, six guests (including the excellent Pheasantry Best Bitter, £4.40) and a cider from the total of 15 handpumps.

On 25th June 2017 - rating: 8
