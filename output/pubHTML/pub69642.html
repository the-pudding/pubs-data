Alan Hurdle left this review about The Bridge

The Bridge is close to the River Elwy in the lower part of St Asaph. It was closed in 2011 for major repair and renovation after the December floods and in danger again in 2015. Most of the pub is now given over to restaurant service with limited opening hours of 5.30pm to 9pm on Thursday to Saturday. Possibly due to the limited hours, Real Ale was discontinued in 2019. Drinkers are still welcome and actual closing time is likely to be nearer 11pm as diners will be still there.
