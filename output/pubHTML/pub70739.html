Colin Haslam left this review about Pi (Chorlton)

I ventured in here a while ago and was pleasantly surprised. Now normally, I'm one who begrudges paying over the odds for a pint,but on this occasion, I had no objections to paying £4.76 for a pint of really nice Dutch beer,the name I can't remember.Yes,this place is a bit pricey but the sheer range and quality of ales and lagers on offer make paying that bit extra more than worth it in my opinion.I shall definitely be calling in here again in the not too distant future.

Al Bundy left this review about Pi (Chorlton)

Amalgamated to the the places of the same name in Altrincham and Mossley Hill and just as small as those places. 5 handpumps on the bar dispensed 5 on this day. A small outside area is available but most people will bevvy in the small interior.

Small double levelled moody cafe bar, lit with dim red lights and candles. The bar is at the top end, all wood floors. Four real ales of good quality. Bring a torch
