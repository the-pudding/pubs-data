Steve of N21 left this review about Three Mariners Inn

Fairly busy pub set up for large groups of vertical drinkers. Basically a one roomed pub with a central bar area, a few wooden tables dotted around the sides, a pool table on one side of the bar, and a small dance floor section with disco space to the back. Plenty of chrome lager and Guinness taps on the bar, but no real ale that I could see.
Plenty of flat screens dotted about and I can imagine this is a great place to watch the footy or rugby, and I believe it may also have a late licence on weekend nights.

On 29th August 2010 - rating: 5
