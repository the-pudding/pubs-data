Steve C left this review about Tin Tins

I arrived at Tin Tins as it opened at 17:45 on Friday afternoon last week, which was in fact 15 minutes before the advertised opening time. The interior consists of a small bar that faces some seating and the front, floor to ceiling, windows. To the right is a small DJ corner where music is played until the early hours. Behind the bar are a couple of small seating areas and a death trap flight of stairs that lead down to a dingy toilet. Do not expect to wash your hands!

The bar supports a couple of premium and standard draught options and there is an extensive ‘bomb’ menu. On the wall are a couple of Tim Tin posters that advertise drink deals. Food is not available.

If Tin Tin was real I imagine that he would appreciate the attitude of the friendly barmaid, but the loud pop music and gloomy atmosphere would probably have him reaching for his long tan coat.

On 5th August 2015 - rating: 3
