Reviews of Lucien's Bar (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about Lucien's Bar

A large and friendly coffee lounge bar in a shop conversion with a sort of Scandinavian feel from the areas of modern wood panelling and dark wood tables. It extends back some way, with a nice covered patio at the back and a surprisingly large lawned garden behind. The bar area is to the right side and boasts a dartboard. Three handpumps were dispensing Wold Top beers: Bitter, Gold and Arcus Stout, which seemed well-kept. Not a conventional pub by any means, but very pleasant and with some nice beers.
