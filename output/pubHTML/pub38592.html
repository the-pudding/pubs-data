Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Tap & Tandoor

The former Sadlers Arms is now firmly established as a self-described 'Indian gastro-pub' and was doing very good business when I called in early on a Wednesday evening. The layout doesn't seem to have altered, but the character of the place certainly has (and for the better in many ways). No real ale these days, but a pretty good selection of (mostly) craft beers plus a cider from 12 taps on the bar-back, including Charlie Don't Surf session IPA from Alphabet Brewing (£3.80). I will also add that the service was both friendly and efficient.

Delboy 20 left this review about Tap & Tandoor

As the name suggests Indian food and craft ales! Quite an interesting choice of beers on tap and a variety of bottles. The food looked and smelt excellent although we didn't have time to eat. I went for Hopwork Orange - an American pale ale - which was excellent. I hope this place does well as it is a little different and well worth a visit.
