Will Larter left this review about The Avenue

A Sizzling chain pub, which is not my cup of tea normally but this one had an atmosphere that grew on me to a certain extent. Maybe it's just that most of the customers, like me, were on their way to the rugby; though obviously they were nearly all Hull FC supporters, and there was only one identifiable Leeds Rhinos fan. Not me: I'm a fan but I wasn't identifiable as such as it was much too warm for a scarf.

On the bar are five hand pumps, with both Bass and Greene King IPA doubled, and a wheat beer from Sharps. I went for the safe option: Bass. It was in pretty good condition and I was pleased to note that the beer here was a lot cheaper than my last one at Pearsons.

On 20th April 2018 - rating: 6

Alan Winfield left this review about The Avenue

The Avenue is a large pub in a suburban shopping area in west Hull.
Once inside this pub there were two large rooms i went in the front room that ran across the front of the pub this room was carpeted and quite smart with some bench seating normal tables and chairs and a couple of tall tables,the pub was very busy with different age groups probably because there are no other pubs near this one.
There was also a smart bar at the rear of the pub this had a pool table in it.
There were three handpumps on the bar with Black Sheep bitter, Bass and one unused i had a drink of Black Sheep biter and this was a decent drink.
The pub had a decent feel to it and i was quite happy having a drink in here.
