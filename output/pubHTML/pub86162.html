Reviews of The Assembly (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Rob Hunter left this review about The Assembly

The Assembly opened a few months ago and offers 8 craft keg taps with an interesting and ever changing selection. I visited on a Friday afternoon in May and the chap behind the bar was friendly. I was the only customer in at that point, but as a fairly small establishment I imagine it gets rather busy at weekends. The Assembly has recently had a cask hand pull installed, with the promise of a cask ale coming soon. At the time of my visit I had a Choco Choc Choc porter by Mad Hatter, which was superb. I'm normally all about the cask ale but this place is worth a visit even before the cask ale makes an appearance. The Assembly is an Untappd Verified Venue and active on Facebook and Twitter, for those wishing to check out the beer menu before making a visit.
