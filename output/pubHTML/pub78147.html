Please Note: This review is over a year old.

ROB Camra left this review about The Banyan Tree

Typical cafe bar under an apartment block. Lots of dining tables and chairs. A pair of sofas near the door and a small raised area with leather look seating. There are tables and chairs outside on the pavement with a lovely view of a building site. Nice. No real ale of course but several lager taps. A half of San Miguel was £2.10. I'll not be bothering to walk out here again.

On 4th July 2016 - rating: 3
