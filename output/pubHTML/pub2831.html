Nigel Sheppard left this review about The Crown Inn

This pub is attached to the Premier Lodge across the car park and is where you would come for your meals if staying there. having said that it is very pleasant inside with great food and a nice outdoor seating area. There is a good selection of beers and a couple of pumps rotate, however dependant on how busy they have been determines the beer quality, you occasionally get a stale one.

On 10th October 2007 - rating: 6
