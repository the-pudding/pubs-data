Soup Dragon left this review about The Woden Inn

A nice detached brick built corner pub, with a rear beer garden

The interior is open plan with a pool table area and dart board. The decor was mainly white and wood panel. There was Sky TV and a karaoke or music system set up, though exactly for what i cant say, as we visited at 1pm. The decoration was supplied by a few old poster reprints and some jugs and ornaments. Service was fine and the clientele small.

Beer; a basic kind of tap pub really, with an OK Banks's MILD.

Nothing really to write home about. There is no real ale to attract that kind of clientele, though there was keg MILD for me. One of many basic pubs in the Wednesbury/Darlaston area - there are far worse than this though. If you are happy with the Fosters/John Smiths kind of thing, try this place, it will be ok.
