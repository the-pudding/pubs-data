Alan Hurdle left this review about Leyland Arms

A fine old stone building opposite the church of Saint Elidan just outside the village of Llanelidan. Real Ales from Thwaites + a couple of guest ales. Meals served Tuesday to Sunday lunch and Wednesday to Saturday Evening. Pub closed Sunday after 18.00 and all day Monday. (June 2013)
Edit: (June 2014) Under new management since May 2014)
