Graham Coombs left this review about The Mill Race

Not quite sure of the history of this pub, but exterior is not very publike. Inside has a very modern feel and concentrates on upmarket locally-sourced food, but retains a good bar area with the obligatory tall tables and stools. Beers sampled were Wye Valley Bitter and Butcombe Rare Breed, both in good condition.

On 26th November 2014 - rating: 7
