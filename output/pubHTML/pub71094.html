Quinno _ left this review about The Yorkshire Rose

Upmarket place. Oddly, a (presumed) extension to rear has a crazy paved floor. Posh dining up steps to left in a separate room with nice leaded glass wall. Pumping music was rather incongruous given the attempt at sophistication elsewhere. Decent grassed rear garden. Two pumps, Pride and a turned clip which showed Rebellion IPA. My Pride was a really short measure and needed a prompted top-up, the eventual half was so-so. Budvar and Estrella of interest on keg. The Plough next door is a better bet.

On 21st July 2015 - rating: 5
