Alan Winfield left this review about The Riverway

The Riverway is an estate pub that was built in the late 70s when this area of the meadows was redeveloped.
This pub used to be a Shipstones tied house with two rooms,there are still two rooms in this pub and real ale has been on the bar on occasions but not always.
This is very much a locals pub except on match days when football fans call in on the way to watch Forest or Notts county.
