Gill Smith left this review about Angel Inn

This is a large hotel on the main road, and is a 2010 entry in the good Beer guide.
Sat on seats by the road with well served beers, Wylam Collingwood and Cumberland Corby Ale.
Feels more of a hotel than a pub.
Didn't like the chunky chairs round the bar as I couldn't reach the barmaid with my money, or get my beer off the bar without a struggle. Must be a nightmare when busy and chairs being used.
