Real Ale Ray left this review about The Bulls Head Inn

Took a taxi from Bakewell to visit this pub, which was only a fiver. Well worth it, as the food is excellent and not over-priced. Enjoyed a selection of Robinsons Ales on handpump. Staff and owners very friendly. This pub is set in a picturesque village, so will definitely call again.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Bulls Head Inn

The Bulls Head is a very nice looking pub that is set well back from the road it is one of two pubs in this really nice looking village.
Once inside there are two rooms the room we had a drink in was very comfortable and had a cosy feel to it.
This pub is a Robinsons tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Robinsons bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Robinsons Hatters mild.
I thought this was a very nice pub and i would have happily stayed here all day.

Pub visited 4/5/2002
