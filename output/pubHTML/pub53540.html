ROB Camra left this review about Duke of York Hotel

A Robbies house that has had a recent refurbishment. The room to the left is painted a horrible grey colour and appears to have a dance floor area which is a bit tatty underfoot, but the bar area is nice enough. There's another area to the rear with a pool table. There's a small room to the right with nice copper fireplaces. It's very dog friendly as there is a water bowl set out for them. The toilets were very smelly and the beer wasn't a lot better with my Dizzy Blonde being just about OK. Loads of TV screens all around the pub. I really didn't take to this place at all. Apparently they have accommodation. but nothing could persuade me to stay here.

On 18th October 2017 - rating: 3
