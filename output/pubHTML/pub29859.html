Andrew Longdon left this review about Plough Inn

Rather surprised at earlier comments - perhaps it has changed hands. I called in here with my girlfriend on a cold February night and was pleasantly surprised by the warm attention we received. The food was very good in terms of both quantity and quality and the bill was very reasonable.

Thoroughly recommend - although I'm sure a certain Mr Winner would disagree ;-)
