moby duck left this review about White Horse

One long single bar set out for eating one end and pool table at the other, stools at the central bar and some tall tables opposite this,three beers on ,Courage Best,Otter Amber and Exmoor Gold, The Beer quality wasn't great.Housed in a nice old building and not too spoiled inside but I think this pub needs to decide is it a pub or a restaurant,I feel the atmosphere it should have, suffers due to the juxtaposition of the two.

Old Boots left this review about White Horse

Best described as a down market dodgy local's pub, the punters dump their coats all over the place and a few give you the evil eye, the service was a bit off as well. Three pumps grace the long counter with Doombar, a drinkable if pricey Courage Best and a spare. It's one long space with the usual games stuff at one end and eating at the other, drinking is done in the middle at mainly tall furniture. You probably wont be surprised to learn it belongs to Enterprise and is for sale.
