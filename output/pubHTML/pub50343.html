Lawrie Tweed left this review about The Taverna Lounge Bar

"The night is not over til the fat lady sings" Morecombe and Wise used to say. And so they say in Rothesay, not a town with a thosand night-spots! So by 10 p.m. everyone about moves to this tiny bar (a bit like drinking in a tube train,) and sure enough a dear old thing croons away to a computer machine that thankfully adds angel choirs, a full orchestra, and loads of echo. This is real ol' entertainment, so NAFF that it's hilarious and enjoyed by all for it's awfulness. This really needs filming...the place was so packed during our visit I couldn't get a photo!
Hilarious beer in cloudy glasses too.

On 15th April 2008 - rating: 10
