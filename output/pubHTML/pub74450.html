BobOs . left this review about The Monkton Court Hotel

Monkton Court Hotel is an old manor house sited alongside the A30, just outside Honiton, in The Otter Valley within the Blackdown Hills. Very comfortable surroundings, excellent welcome and service, beers are very well kept and the restaurant offers excellent meals at reasonable prices. Above average and well worth calling in if passing.
