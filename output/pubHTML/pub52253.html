Alan Hurdle left this review about Poachers

Being on the bank of the Llangollen Canal close to the Welsh/English border, this pub gets very busy on warm days. The tables along the canal bank are extremely popular. Meals are served all day and Real Ales are available from the Marstons range.
There is a queuing system at the bar for both meals and beer! Any unsuspecting stranger who walks straight up to the bar, suffers the wrath of the queue.

On 16th July 2017 - rating: 6
