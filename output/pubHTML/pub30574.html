Peter Carr left this review about The Feathers Inn

Great pub, Friendly staff ,clean, good log fires,No loud music ,4 real ales on tap at my visit.(30/12/08) The bar meals were excellent. (Local food and 3 of the local real ales.) My friends and I will return. Hence 4 out of 4.
