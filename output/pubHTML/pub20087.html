Graham Ainslie left this review about Bay Horse

Great pub ! Food is awesome, and reasonably priced. Good cask ales. Entertainment, poker nights, and an outside area to die for. Sports on tv all over the pub including a drop down screen in the vault.
Couldn't wish for a better set of staff too - very knowledgable.

BEN UNSWORTH left this review about Bay Horse

The Bay Horse is my local pub which i used to use most weeks, but over the past few years the pub has declined and we stopped using it mainly because of the poor quality of the cask beer.However in the past year we have tried again and found the pub has vastly improved and there is a choice of four casks and i must say in good condition.The food is first class and prices are very reasonable,as are the beer prices.Iwould have marked the pub higher but at times when we have gone we have found them cleaning the beer lines and there has been no cask beers on sale and this has been mid afternoon,a big mistake.

On 8th November 2010 - rating: 7
