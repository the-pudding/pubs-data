Graham Coombs left this review about The Plough Inn

A cosy and snug old pub, with a series of connected rooms with a glorious mixture of furniture, including some comfy sofas. Definitely felt like the sort of place to be on a winter evening. Several good Yorkshire ales on offer and the food sounded good, although not sampled. Well worth the short diversion off the main Hull road.
