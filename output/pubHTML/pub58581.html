Gill Gillard left this review about The Board Inn

My husband and I couldn't fault this welcoming inn; the hosts and bar staff were very friendly, our room was superb (we had the four-poster bedroom with dressing room/mini lounge)and the food was excellent. The inn was full, and all guests had brought their dogs, which is another reason this is a wonderful place to stay. A big thank you to Karen and Alistair, we'll definately be returning!
