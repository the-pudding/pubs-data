Soup Dragon left this review about The New Inn

A detached white renderd and white painted brick build, with a beer garden area.

The interior is open plan, with red and white wallpaper and dark red walls, with decoration provided by displays of photographs of regulars over the years and some trophies. There was a stone fire place with a real fire blazing. There was pool, darts and freeview TV. Service was friendly, there was one other guy in.

Beer; well, nothing really. A few lagers and Tetley smooth. The Ansells MILD wasnt on, though i could have had a can of Banks's. I had a coke and left.

A non-entity of a place, with no beer choice. Shame, if they did a decent real ale, or the Ansells, i would come back, i just remember last time i was in the Ansells was pretty vile.

On 15th October 2009 - no rating submitted
