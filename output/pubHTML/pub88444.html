Reviews of Runaway Brewery Tap (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Rob Hunter left this review about Runaway Brewery Tap

Limited opening hours - midday to 8pm every Saturday. No cask beers but eight of their own Runaway beers on keg. I assume cask beers would simply not be viable in a place that opens once a week.

It's a bit of a tricky one to find but you basically enter the gates to a small industrial area and then Runaway is in the last arch.

The bar is upstairs, cash and card are accepted, there are board games and stuff available too should you so desire.

It's a shame they aren't open a little bit later as we only ever have time to have one drink before last orders; there's usually a couple of groups in at closing time but not sure how busy it gets during the afternoon.

It's a small unpretentious place, you get to speak to the brewer, he knows his beers so will answer any questions you have and is happy for you to have a taster before making your mind up. The beers have never let me down so that's the main thing for me.

On 30th September 2019 - rating: 7
