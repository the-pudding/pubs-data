Jonathan Wilde left this review about All Stars Sports Bar

After adding this and visiting today this is a members bar but given the sporting facilities of gigantic screen e.g 1 1/2 times the size of my bathroom/kitchen, 10 pool tables, american pool tables and snooker tables for the £1 temporary membership it's worth retaining given it's in a popular tourist resort where from visits to most pubs sports isn't always the first consideration.

Gigantic open plan room with small roof terrace containing all the above mentioned sports tables complete with monster booths for watching the mammoth projector screen.

Has Doombar so real ale box ticked along with food, quiz night and everything you want in a pub. I sense the membership fee is a licencing bypass/cash in on tourists etc but it's worth paying it in my opinion.

If local to Weston yearly membership is £8 and again worth it to watch anything remotely football with an atmosphere.
