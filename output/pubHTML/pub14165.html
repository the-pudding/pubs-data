Quinno _ left this review about Jolly Farmer

A bland, split-level GK local with recent corporate refit. Inhabited by the locals, the order of the day here is karaoke, quiz machine, awful evening TV and darts. Five pumps with four on - probably two more than will realistically sell here - GK OSH and IPA, Tim Taylor Boltmaker (vinegar, taken back but not off) and Exmoor Dark (decent nick). There’s not any need to venture outside the city centre for this one unless you’re a completest. 4.5

On 1st February 2017 - rating: 4
