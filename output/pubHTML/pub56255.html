Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Pitcher & Piano

Location is the plus point here, particularly the lower-level Waterside Bar and terrace right by the canal side. Mind you, if you want a real ale, you'll need to order this at the main bar upstairs (i.e. at street level) where there are four handpumps on the bar-back - although only Cocker Hoop was available on this visit. Otherwise, anonymous modern furniture and decor throughout.
