Sarah Angus left this review about Three Horseshoes Inn

The pub has been transformed over the past 18 months,by the new owners Jane & Paul. The food is fantastic and the staff are friendly which makes for a great atmosphere. The Sunday lunches are amazing I have never seen Yorkshire puddings like it - they are huge! It was a pleasure to sit in the beer garden during the summer, the hanging baskets were a riot of colour. Definitely worth a visit.
