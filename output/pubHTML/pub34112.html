Alan Winfield left this review about The Queens

The Queens is a large pub that is situated on the northern edge of Rawmarsh.
The doors on the left side were locked,but a door tucked away to the right was open,this lead me into a decent sized room with the bar facing,the room was oblong shaped and divided by a part wall,the pool table was in a smaller area to the left of the wall,the seating was bench type around the edge with three small tables in the middle of the room,a TV was showing day time programmes.
There was lots of boxing memorabillia on the walls with lots from Nigel Benn including signed shorts.
There were two pumps on the bar with one unclipped,this left Black Sheep Golden Sheep which was a decent enough drink.
The very large lounge to the left was not in use on my visit,this room ran to the rear of the pub.

Pub visited 31/5/2014
