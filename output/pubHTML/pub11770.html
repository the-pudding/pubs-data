Real Ale Ray left this review about The Black Lion

This 14th Century coaching house is well worth a visit. The pub is very popular for food, whether it is their bar snacks or dining in the restaurant area. The manageress made us very welcome, as did the locals. This is a GK pub, so was a bit stuck when it came to ordering, we tried the GK Bohemian, which was pretty good.
