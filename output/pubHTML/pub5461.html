Aqualung . left this review about Number Twenty 2

This is a long narrow room that is clearly a shop conversion with the bar on the right. It was quite busy on my Saturday afternoon visit and it was difficult to see how many hand pumps there were let alone the beers that were on. I did spot two mainstream beers Adnam's Bitter, Jenning's Cocker Hoot and three Village Brewery beers which are a bit of a fraud as noted below since they are brewed under contract at Hambleton. I found one called Leamside American Pale Ale which I went for but was well overpriced at £3.50. It was a good pint in excellent condition. I didn't really take to this place as it seemed rather trendy and was expensive but would almost certainly return for the beer choice alone.

On 12th May 2015 - rating: 7
