ROB Camra left this review about Tib St Tavern

After a refurbishment the former Bar Centro reopened as the Tib Street Tavern in May 2012. The layout is pretty much the same as Centro used to be, but it's been brought up market. Up stairs are a some comfy seats along the left hand side as you enter along with some dining tables and chairs. Downstairs has some horseshoe shaped comfortable seats. The downstairs area used to be a bit of a forgotten part of the bar, but they've made a good job of it now. There are 3 handpumps on the bar selling Hobgoblin and a couple of changing beers from the Marston's stable. The large screen was advertising Dunham Massey beers, but these may just be bottles as they weren't on the handpumps. We've called in twice recently and both the pints I've had were in good nick. It's now worth a visit again.

On 21st January 2013 - rating: 7
