Alan Winfield left this review about The Wheatsheaf

The Wheatsheaf is a decent looking pub that has got quite a long frontage,there were three doors at the front of the pub i went in the one to the left which took me into a smartish room and was the sort of room i like to have a drink in.
This pub is a McMullens tied house and there were two real ales on here i had a drink of McMullens Country and this was a very nice drink the other beer was McMullens AK bitter.
I quite liked this pub.
