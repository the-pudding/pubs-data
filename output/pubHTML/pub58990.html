Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Bishops Finger

The compact façade and interior of this Shepherd Neame pub have both been refurbished but the decor is still quite traditional although the furniture is modern in style. Several sports TVs. Extra seating at a few small tables out on the pavement and in the reasonable patio beer garden out back. Three of four handpumps on when I visited, with Master Brew, Whitstable Bay (£3.55) and Bishops Finger available.

On 24th August 2014 - rating: 6
