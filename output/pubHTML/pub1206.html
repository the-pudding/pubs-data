Bucking Fastard left this review about Ship

Now converted to a Carvery ,yet still possible to drink in the front bar area.The refurb has changed the vibe and the interior is now full of sofas and low tables,rather like drinking in grans front room.Three handpumps ,one reversed,another dispensing a real cider and the third supplying a very indifferent pint of Old Speckled Hen.I didnt bother with the carvery which during the week is priced at the extreme value end of the market.
