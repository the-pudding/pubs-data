Quinno _ left this review about The Green Man

Managed to blag our way in on the ‘soft launch’ opening day of this out-of-the-way place that has had a massive modern refurb and now has what can be best described as an omega-shaped bar. Walls are a mixture of whites, greys and exposed brick. Rear garden with kids play area. Attractive white tiled bar back. Six pumps with five ales - Bombardier, Pride, Doom Bar, Ascot Green Man, Ascot on the Rails with Westons Perry on the other pump. My half of Ascot was pretty lifeless, hopefully bottom of barrel after a successful day? Won’t leave a mark but would like to check it out again.
