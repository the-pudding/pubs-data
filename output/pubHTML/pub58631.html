Graham Coombs left this review about Cross Keys

Refurbished but retaining a pleasant feel, the friendly pub has an emphasis on food but still plenty of room for drinkers. It has 6 handpumps with usually 4 good local ales on offer - Cask Marque listed and very drinkable. Food also very good. Large car park at rear.

Alan Winfield left this review about Cross Keys

The Cross Keys is a decent side pub that is close to York racecourse.
Once inside there is a fairly large single L shaped room with the bar facing,the seating is two picnic tables to the right,plus some normal tables and chairs to the left and bucket type chairs.
There were four real ales on the bar,i had a drink of York Guzzler which went down very well,the other beers were Leeds Best,Copper Dragon Golden Pippin and Taylor Landlord,i thought this was a good choice of real ales.
Background music was playing and food is available,i did'nt see a pool table or darts board but i did'nt have a really good look round.
I prefered this pub to the one just down the road.

Pub visited 23/5/2015
