Peter Rydings left this review about The Warrington Arms

One of the old fashioned pubs they have in this area of the country with the outside walls tiled.
Its a bit hard to get to you drive past on the main road and theres no turn of for a bit then you go through a maze of terrace houses but worth the trouble to go.
It was nice and clean the decor was like pubs were years ago but sadly no hand pull but well worth a call

Alan Winfield left this review about The Warrington Arms

The Warrington Arms is a decent looking green tiled pub,that looks very similar to the White Horse which is just up the road,the pub was also opposite the now closed Major Wilds pub.
Once inside the pub had been opened out but still retained different drinking areas,the seating was a mix of comfy bench type and normal tables and chairs.
When i visited this pub it was a Bass tied house,there were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Keg Bass Special which was not that special.
This was a pleasant enough pub for a quick half.

Pub visited 20/12/1997
