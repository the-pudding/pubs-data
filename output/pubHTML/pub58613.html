Alan Winfield left this review about The King William

The King William is an estate pub on the western edge of York.
This pub had one room that ran across the front of the pub there was a raised games area to the left with pool and darts.
There was a John Smiths cask pump on the bar but they could'nt be bothered to pull any beer off so it was fizz beers only in here i had a choice of fizzy Worthington or even worse John Smiths smooth crap so i had a Guinness,the pub was very busy so it must be popular with the locals but i wont be coming here again
