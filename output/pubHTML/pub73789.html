Nick Davies left this review about Olive's Bar

Public bar of the Gloucester Hotel with separate entrance on Gloucester Road. It's the usual sterile hotel bar in what could be quite an atmospheric setting if they used a bit of imagination. With an early evening happy hour and Sky Sports advertised outside it's half heartedly trying to compete with the local Punch outlets and indeed if you don't want real ale you'll get a far more civilised time for much the same price. The Bombay Brasserie, one of the best Indian Restaurants outside India, is part of the same hotel.

On 5th May 2011 - rating: 5
