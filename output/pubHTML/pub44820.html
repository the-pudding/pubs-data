Soup Dragon left this review about Cambria Inn

This is a Victorian corner pub, in yellow render with black paintwork. It appears to originally have been a cottage-styled pub, but by the 1920s it seems to have taken the building behind. The pub has had most of its interior walls knocked out, but there is a back room that i didn't really see. The front room is in white artexy decor, with red tile floor and bench seating. There is a TV which had the footy on and there was no music, although there is a jukebox. Mersyside footy and other sporting pictures are on the walls. The service was fine and a few locals were in. Beer; no real ale, so i had Brain's Dark Smooth which was fine enough. Nothing really for the real ale fan.
