Alan Winfield left this review about The Great Northern

The Great Northern is situated on the busy Leeds road.
Once inside the pub has two separate rooms,the main room to the front is square shaped,there is a mix of seating types in this room,all taken,there is also a smaller square shaped room to the rear right,the seating here is bench type and small stools.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Tetley Cask,this went down well.
There were TVs showing the Man Utd v Liverpool game.
The pub was packed on my late Saturday afternoon visit with a mix of age groups,all drinking.
This was a decent enough pub to end my Bradford crawl on.

Pub visited 12/9/2015
