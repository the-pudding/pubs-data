Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Queen Victoria

Attractive and welcoming pub set just a short distance back from the pier end of the seafront. Partly opened-out at some stage to leave an 'U'-shaped bar with another small seating area off the side of one of the branches. Also has a patio terrace at the front (and noticeably more civilised than that on the rowdy 'wine bar' next door). Five real ales from the Marston's stable on handpump, including EPA, Banks's Bitter (£3.25), Pedigree and Forty Niner.

ROB Camra left this review about The Queen Victoria

Good looking pub that won Marston's Pub Of The Year for 2012. Popular with the older end of the market during my visit. Hobgoblin, Banks's Bitter, Pedigree and two changing ales from the Marston's extended stable on the bar. I had a couple of pints of Wychwood Wychmyst which was in very good condition. It's definitely one of the better pubs in Llandudno and I'll certainly visit again if in the area.
