Nick Davies left this review about The Crown Inn

Pleasant local in a small village just off the main road. It's a two room eighteenth century building, with a traditional public bar and comfortable lounge, and a largish garden to the rear. Though not elaborately decorated there's a fascinating collection of Guinness memorabilia. Ansell's bitter; and Liberation Ale - from Guernsey - guesting when we called. All clean and tidy and friendly service, it's well worth a visit.
