Reviews of Hart's Head Inn (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Rob Hunter left this review about Hart's Head Inn

Visited earlier this month. At the bar are six pumps for cask ale but unfortunately three of these were off. The remaining offering consisted of Pheasant Plucker by Bowland, Mary Jane by Ilkley and Tetley Cask.

The pub is effectively one main room surrounding the bar in a horse shoe, though there is the feel of separate sections. There is a room to the right set out for dining.

Seemed fairly friendly and a group of locals were in chatting to the young barmaid.

Water is out for the dogs, and a jar of dog biscuits.

There is a small courtyard and car park to the rear.

On 20th February 2019 - rating: 6
