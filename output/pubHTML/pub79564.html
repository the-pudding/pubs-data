Jonathan Wilde left this review about Old Badger Inn

Further to the below review I would concur with all but the well kept beer as I don't drink real ale, although there was a wide selection and surely taking pump clips off due to selling out is a good sign.

However after a visit tonight I found the 3 bar staff seemingly busy while actually achieving little in the serving stakes. To top it off while serving me, eventually, the person stopped after the first pint to serve a local then continue with the second drink I'd ordered. Thankfully they took my money first so the delay was reduced.

In all the pub is nice the a central bar area with seating in the front with L shape seating to the left hand side leading into a garden area. After sending my friend to locate a seat we ended up in what I would consider the Board Room as it was a partitioned area(by-fold doors)obviously open due to being busy which does lead you to feel removed from the atmosphere of the pub however it was a decision made by others so not a negative against the pub in all honesty.

I would visit again however maybe not on a Friday night when your face seems to be the factor of how the bar staff make the who's next decision.
