Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Stile Bridge Inn

Roadside pub located some distance from the nearest villages, but the car park is usually full most evenings and at weekened lunchtimes. Painted a rather alarming eggshell blue outside. Eclectic decor in the two front rooms which have been joined to form the main bar. There is also a small back bar with the sports TV, a dining room beyond, covered decking area to the side and patio beer garden to rear. Good selection of draught and bottled ales, with six handpumps featuring a mixture of local offerings (such as Kent Brewery Cobnut and Hot Fuzz Brewery Hawaiian) and things from further afield (e.g. Blakemere Gold) at reasonable prices for the area ranging from £2.90 to £3.40. For non-ale drinkers there are interesting continental beers including Curious Brew, Rothaus Pils, Brugse Zot and Kaltenberg Hell available on tap. Regular evening music events plus 'Bridgestock' festivals held on some bank holiday weekends. Justifiably Maidstone and Mid-Kent CAMRA pub of the Year 2012.

On 8th September 2012 - rating: 8
