Aqualung . left this review about Carrick Stone (JD Wetherspoon)

This is set on the North Eastern corner of Cumbernauld Town Centre which is nothing more than a hideous modern shopping centre. There is an entrance from the shopping centre and one from the car park outside it. What Pub says it is on two floors but I completely missed any staircase as the toilets are downstairs. The room I found was roughly square with a raised area on the shopping centre side and the bar on the side next to it. There was a mix of the daytime brigade and shoppers.
The bar has just eight hand pumps which had two unused, two available soon, Abbot, Doom Bore, Strathaven Claverhouse Red and Broughton Spring Ale. I went for the Strathaven beer (£1.99) which was in excellent condition.
This isn't the worst shopping centre Spoons I've been to but in general it seemed a below par JDW.
