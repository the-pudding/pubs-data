Alan Winfield left this review about The Iron Horse

The Iron Horse is on an estate that is quite smart with bungalows.
This pub did'nt look that appealing from the outside but once inside here the pub was well furnished,smart and had a warm feel to it,this pub is a Greene King tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of GK IPA which was ok but nothing special there was also Abbot on.
This is a decent pub for the locals in this area of Wroughton.
