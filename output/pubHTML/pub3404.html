lively pub on Chester's market area. it was quite friendly with a fun atmosphere on a Saturday evening. better than expected real ales with Ubu and Cheshire Cat plus two dull choices. not a great pub by any means but worth popping in.

Al Bundy left this review about The Shropshire Arms

I've never been a fan of the 3 pubs by the Tourist Info place and this place is as average to dull as the Dublin Packet and the Coach House. The single room is slightly cramped and the choice of real ale is usually dull. You can sit out front on the pavement should you so wish.

ROB Camra left this review about The Shropshire Arms

Small L shaped, one roomed pub, has been modernised to remove all its character. The same could be said of the barman. Only a couple of regulars in on my Thursday lunchtime visit. There are 3 handpumps on the bar, but only Bombardier was available and this was only OK. Not a place I'll be visiting again with so many better pubs in Chester.
