John Horton left this review about Tudor Arms

Nice pub near to Wildfowl Trust. Just down the road from the local Youth Hostel. Regularly stop here on way home from the West Country. A few miles off the M5 but worth it. Well kept local real ales and good food (though a little over priced). Car park adjacent.
