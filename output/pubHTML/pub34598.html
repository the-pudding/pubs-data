E TA left this review about Ye Olde Rose & Crown

As described by others below, this former inn, still a Joule’s outlet, has a good pub feel to it and was a pleasure to visit. Food looked good, the young staff were pleasant and the locals welcoming. They run weekly quiz and folk nights. I had pints of Joule’s IPA and Pure Blonde, both in perfect condition. An excellent pub and a definite for inclusion in a crawl.

Quinno _ left this review about Ye Olde Rose & Crown

Traditional pub feel but with modern fittings, kitted-out by Joules after purchase in 2011. Two bars serving two rooms, the front room is square and cosy with plenty of wood trim and padded booth bench seating. The rear part of the pub is an unusual tight zig zag layout, cold and less welcoming, with ITV on the telly to an audience of nil. Rear patio noted and pub is dog friendly. Five pumps with four Joules beers on; my Pale was extremely good, though the down-at-the-mouth bar lady wasn't in the mood for a convivial exchange. A few better class locals in which gave the place a relaxed feel. A nice pub and good beer but the glum service let it down, I felt like the stranger in town. 7.5
