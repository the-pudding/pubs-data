Jeff Storey left this review about The Eden Bar (Peterlee Lodge Hotel)

It is a Labour of love to be the first person to write a review of the Eden Bar...in its various guises the Norseman has with the Gamecock been my local since 1976...Julie and her bar staff are warm friendly people..resident DJ Steve does Disco and Karaoke on a weekend...as well as Eden Bar there is a function suite downstairs and a nightclub Vibe..previously known as Joe Cools and the Dance Factory on what used to be the bar area...the drinks are varied and reasonably priced and problems within are rare due to well trained doormen...I speak of it as a regular customer as opposed to a Hotel visitor, but I know the Hotel facilities are the best in the area

On 23rd November 2010 - rating: 10
