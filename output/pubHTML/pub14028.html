E TA left this review about The Shepherds Crook

A Victorian town pub in a residential area close to Fratton Park. The single amorphous, carpeted bar is formed from knocking through two smaller rooms and is dominated by massive TV screens showing football. There were various football memorabilia on the walls, including several signed photos of PFC players past and present. There are gaming machines, and they host regular social events including quiz nights, live music and a weekly meat raffle. Children were kicking a football around inside, which made walking to the counter a little hazardous, while the jukebox was minimally annoying. Two ales on, Doombar and Razorback, both in reasonable condition. Despite its proximity to the stadium and its almost overwhelming fixation with football, it's very much a locals' pub, and although the welcome was genuine enough, I didn't feel terribly comfortable, so we got the flock out of here.

On 5th July 2017 - rating: 5
