Jon B left this review about Britannia Hotel

A community local. it's owned by the same people as the Borough in town but is smaller and nowhere near as corporate as that place. It's done in a contemporary style with sofas etc and seems to serve an awful lot of pizza. popular with academics and trendy people so it's naturally a bit irritating, but worth a visit if you're in this part of town.
