Jon B left this review about Hogarths

this chain seems to be springing up at a rapid rate in the main drinking areas of northern towns. they are sort of Victorian gin palaces (basically drinking barns with vintage interiors)) in the Amber taverns group. they also offer very cheap and well-kept real ale. not bad and at least better than the vast majority on this strip.
