Colin Walker left this review about Silver Birch

Don't be deceived by it's bland / social club exterior. The inside is all modern looking with some wood effect. The only pub in the nearby visinity, so has a lot of locals. On the main road from Crossgates to Cowdenbeath. A bit quieter drinking the the main pubs in the local town, Cowdenbeath.
