Alan Winfield left this review about The Green Room

The Green Room is a bar that overlooks devonshire green.
Once inside there is a smallish L shaped room,there was a small serving bar near the entrance,the seating in the bar area was a mix of normal tables and chairs and settees,there is a bare wall which divides the room,the left side away from the bar is smaller.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Bradfield Farmers Blonde which went down well,the other beer was Bradfield Ye Olde English Ale.
There was a DJ playing vinyl records on a twin deck,with music from the 80s,the sound quality was great,the music playing was even better.
There was a really nice laid back atmosphere even with the music playing at a loud volume that i like.
This is the sort of bar i could settle in all day,i was very tempted to come back here even though i was on a pub crawl.
I really enjoyed my time here, well worth a visit.
