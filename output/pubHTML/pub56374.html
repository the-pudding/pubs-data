E TA left this review about The Cerdic (JD Wetherspoon)

An obvious cinema conversion as described by Aqualung. Of the 12 pumps there was one dry, the remainder had Doombar, Ruddles and Abbot (of course), plus Exmoor Stag, Chesil and Cotleigh Tawny Owl. There were 4 boxed ciders. It's not the greatest JDW, scruffy and unappealing from outside, noisy and malodorous inside. I wouldn't make an effort to come here other than to use my CAMRA vouchers.

Aqualung . left this review about The Cerdic (JD Wetherspoon)

The Cerdic is a conversion of a small cinema which is obvious from the exterior but internally due to a false ceiling at the front is only really recognisable as such at the rear. The open plan room is split level with some lower seating to the right. The furnishings are fairly standard.
The ten hand pumps had the JDW Trio Of Doom doubled up, one unused, Dorset Brewing Durdle Door & Chesil and Otter Dark. I went for the Otter Dark (£2.19) which was in excellent nick.
This one isn't one of the better JDWs but is a long way from being the worst.
