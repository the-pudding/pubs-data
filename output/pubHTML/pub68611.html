Colin Walker left this review about Barrasford Arms

Unspoilt splendid 1870's Country pub located in the heart of Barrasford village. Top chef Tony Binks has won several culinary awards. The pub is located at the heart of Barrasford village in the North Tyne valley. It regularly hosts quoits tournaments, Hunt meets, darts finals and vegetable competitions. Friendly bar complemented by good ale and customer banter. The nearby former railway station is used by Scouts but the bar has several railway pictures from yesteryear that adds to the pub ambience. Courtesy of https://whatpub.com/pubs/TYN/4108 & http://www.barrasfordarms.co.uk/
