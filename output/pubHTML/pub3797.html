ROB Camra left this review about The Bears Paw

Looking at Frodsham you wouldn't think it would be a chavvy mess on a Saturday night, but it is, especially in Summer. This place tends to get them all in (and outside). Having said that the beer range and quality have been improved over the last couple of years. Not a place for the faint hearted at weekends but it does sell a decent pint.
