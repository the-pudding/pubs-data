I L left this review about Kazbah

Nearly walked straight past this bar but then noticed the Cask Marque plaque by the entrance. not you ordinary bar by far. Walks are rough cast render and except for a few leather sofas in the front the rest of the seating generally consists of small stool or Pough types along with small low tables. a restaurant adjoins the bar. However there are three hand pumps and at time of our visit had Hogoblin, doom Bar and Betty Stogs ales on. The Betty Stogs was very nice. Friendly staff here swell, so well worth visiting even if it doesn't look like a regular pub.

On 30th October 2010 - rating: 7
