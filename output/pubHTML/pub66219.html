Alan Hurdle left this review about Golden Lion Inn

Excellent village local but welcoming to all. Separate Bar and Lounge/Restaurant areas. Real Ales from Holts (rare in this area - landlord collects from the brewery) plus other guest beers. Accommodation and camping available. Closed all day Monday and weekday lunchtime.

On 16th August 2011 - rating: 9
