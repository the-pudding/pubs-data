Reviews of Bottle Grove (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Bottle Grove

Craft beer shop, with a very well-chosen selection of bottled and canned beers for sale - either to take away or to drink in at one of the four small tables. However, there are also four draught taps behind the counter, offering strong IPAs from Burnt Mill and Franklin's, Gun (Parabellum ?) milk stout (£4.50) and the Mango Sour from Chorlton.
