Alan Winfield left this review about White Horse Inn

The White Horse is a very nice looking pub situated on the main road through Eton Socon.
Once inside there is a narrow L shaped room to the front which is divided into different areas by walls,the seating is bench type in bays and the floor is part tiled and wood.
There is a separate dining room to the rear left.
There were three real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Adnams Lighthouse which went down very well,the other beers were Theakston Old Peculiar and Jennings Snecklifter.
We found this to be a very pleasant pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 4/6/2016
