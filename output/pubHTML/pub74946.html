Neil Williams left this review about Ye Olde Inn

Now under new management, this old style family run pub offers well kept real ales and a number of different ciders.

The food is excellent and there is plenty of it and the Sunday lunch is not to be missed.

It has a great beer garden and backs onto a cricket/football field with a small park where the kids can run around.

Not to be missed.

On 22nd November 2011 - rating: 9
