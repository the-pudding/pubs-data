Quinno _ left this review about The Wheelwright Arms

A battered Greene King pub in the pub desert of Lambourn. Just Morlands on pump and it was appalling, I tipped it away. The barmaid had a PhD in idiocy but at the least the locals seemed ok. Some equine ephemera as to be expected but otherwise terrible and I made good my escape.

On 27th October 2014 - rating: 3
