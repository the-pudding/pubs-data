peter ashworth left this review about Hop Inne Bier

The Old Bier Shoppe opened just before Easter 2013 and is housed in a former card shop and described by the Ormskirk Advertiser as " A quirky Victorian style beer shop for the discerning drinker"and has been brought to life by the owners of the Hop Vine Inn at nearby Burscough.
The pub is a one roomed affair with the oblong bar being at the far end of the room,with to the right the complete full length of the wall has smart glass cabinets showcasing the large range of bottled beers on offer (around 50 European bottles)On the left hand side cut off keg barrels finished with wooden tops to provide handy places for you to rest your pint.There are 3 cask beers on plus 6 continental draughts plus a Belgium beer style menu complete with tasting notes.
The opening hours are 11am till 11pm 7 days a week and this fine conversion is certainly worth a view if you are ever in the centre of Ormskirk.
