Alan Winfield left this review about Kasbah

When we went in this pub it was called the Redan,this was a decent looking pub that is quite close to the sea front.
Once inside the pub had a few different rooms and we had a drink in the room at the front,this was a fairly smart room that was comfortabe enough.
There were no real ales on the bar and we had a drink of keg Courage bitter and this was a pretty poor drink.
This was a decent enough pub that was let down by having no real ales on.
