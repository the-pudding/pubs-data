Komakino . left this review about The Old Swan

Up in the sticks, roughly between Great Missenden and Wendover, this is an old style classic country pub classing itself as pub and restaurant, and sating both camps perfectly - two clear areas: dining to the left in a narrow, down-stepped area to the left of the central entrance and the remaining, staple-shaped area around the bar is a nice haven for enjoying a pint or two. There's nice olde world dark beams, a semi-inglenook fireplace and a flagstone floor which give off a rustic vibe and polite service and a couple of locAles (Rebellion IPA and Chiltern Pale Ale) were a welcome site. Definitely worth a look-in if you're in the area.

On 13th May 2017 - rating: 6
