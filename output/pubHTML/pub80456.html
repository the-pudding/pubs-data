Reviews of Wine Library (Average Rating: 10 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Colin Walker left this review about Wine Library

The Wine Library with over 99 percent of the gantry covered in wine. There is Peroni & Tennents lagers on draught. There is a small selection of spirits available. Gin tasting & wine tasting nights. There is food available such as Cured meats, Cheese boards & Seafood platters. Plus soup and sandwiches. The Wine library sits on a prominent corner site which should help with custom.
