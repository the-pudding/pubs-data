peter ashworth left this review about The Grape Vaults

This is a small grade two listed pub that that is unspoiled, friendly and a down to earth local and i can see why it is regarded by most as Leominster's best real ale pub.There was 4 cask beers on when i visited and i also liked the furnishings and the old bare floor boards.

Tris C left this review about The Grape Vaults

I came here one afternoon on my birthday around 1973 and remember a landlord with a spade beard who presided over a small pub with quite a traditional interior and lots of knick-knacks with a bar to the left wall. What I do recall were a pair of ship's port and starboard lanterns mounted on a shelf above the bar - we debated their relative position, in that they were correctly positioned with regard to the barman looking ahead but were wrong from the perspective of the customer.
That's about all I remember, but from the outside at least, this appears to be one of the more attractive options in the town.

Will Larter left this review about The Grape Vaults

Still the best of the real ale pubs in Leominster in my opinion too. Service was good, the atmosphere friendly. Five hand pumps available, with three Ludlow beers plus Woods Shropshire Lad and Swan Gold; all from local breweries.

Date of visit: 18th April 2017

A further visit on Sunday 30th July found the pub closed, a fact that is not mentioned on Facebook or WhatPub, just on the door of the pub. The old ways still the best?
