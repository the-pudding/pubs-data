Steve C left this review about The Cavendish Arms

It was pretty quiet in here during my week night visit, but the barman was very friendly and the locals liked gossiping about people that weren't there.

The pub has Sky Sports and a pool table and there is a disco and karaoke every weekend.

Greene King IPA is available alongside a standard draught selection, but unfortunately my Guinness wasn't the best.

I wasn't impressed with this place, but it was very quiet and I could see the potential for a good craic so I think that I'll reserve proper judgment until after another visit.

On 11th September 2009 - rating: 6
