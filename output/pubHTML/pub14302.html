General Staal left this review about The Seven Stars

Lovely pub. Modern refurb of an ancient pub following a fire in the early 2000s. The layout at the front looks like it is original, even with the modern refurb. The restaurant to the back looks much more 'modern'.

A warm, friendly pub. Food was excellent and filling, but not cheap. Mrs Staal and I thoroughly enjoyed the food.

Three ales on - Three Shires Hafren, Pardoe's Old Swan and Wye Valley Bitter. All three were excellent. But, the Hafren was sublime.

A lovely pub.

On 30th March 2016 - rating: 8

Real Ale Ray left this review about The Seven Stars

A very inviting looking pub, with a splendid interior, with low beam ceilings and timber floor. The pub consists of two rooms, the main bar in the front with various seating and a dining area beyond the bar. We called in on a Saturday afternoon and found it to be a locals pub. The customers gave me the impression of pretending to be Ledbury's elitist set and all were very clicky and unfriendly. Three ales on handpump from Marstons.

On 29th March 2014 - rating: 5
