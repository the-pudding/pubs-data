Old Boots left this review about Uxbridge Arms

A corner pub a little way out of the town centre but worth dropping into. In contrast to the usual Burton style it’s a single L shaped room with an interesting ceiling painted with cards and dominoes. Three hand pumps with the inevitable Pedigree plus a guest from Dorset’s Wyre Piddle in good nick, the third was unclipped. The short arm has a piano as well as the more common machines, the longer arm has a video juke box and the seating here is laid out refectory style which is unusual for a non-food pub. The atmosphere is friendly like so many Burton pubs and its worthwhile to visit and not just to escape the all pervasive Pedigree.
