Newly opened bar and bottle shop, sandwiched between Teignworthy brewery and Tuckers Maltings. Featuring three cask ales and four on keg. Note the limited opening hours:

Thursday - Friday: 5pm - 11pm.
Saturday: 12pm - 11pm.
Sunday: 12pm - 10pm.
