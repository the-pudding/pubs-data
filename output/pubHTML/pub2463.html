Komakino . left this review about The Swan Hotel

A tired old building inside and out, this has a central, horseshoe-shaped, wood-panelled servery which offered Sharp's Doom Bar as its solitary ale. A stone bar apron follows the wraps around the bar, a green-patterned carpet is the flooring and upholstered bench seating runs around half of the periphery. There's also a pool table in an extended area beyond the bar and B&B accommodation is available.
