BobOs . left this review about The Rest and be Thankful Inn

Nice comfortable country pub/hotel at the main crossroads in the village of Wheddon Cross, in the heart of Exmoor National Park. We stayed for a very nice long weekend and can recommend the warm welcome, excellent menu and well kept ales. Our room was excellent and we had a good view of Dunkery Beacon. Overall not cheap but good value - above average.
