Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Bulls Head

For an Ember Inn, this one certainly appears to be (from the street) at the traditional - if still very much food-led - pub end of the spectrum. Of course, the interior has been thoroughly remodelled and modernised, and there isn't much character left in the various seating / dining areas around the central servery. A few wooden tables can also be found outside by the entrance. Only five from a total of nine handpumps were in use, dispensing Ember Pale, Purity Pure Gold (£3.15), Pedigree, Otter Ale and Old Rosie cider.

On 25th August 2018 - rating: 6
