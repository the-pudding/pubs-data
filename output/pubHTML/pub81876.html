Quinno _ left this review about Baravin

Newly-opened bar specialising in cocktails and pizzas. Purple Moose ale (Madog, Glaslyn) available in bottles as well as Hallet's cider. I plumped for a half of Ankerbrau keg. Nice sea views. Modern internal décor in the oval bar won't suit all, quite London craft bar-esque. The white metro tiled bar back looks ok though.

On 6th June 2014 - rating: 5
