Reviews of Endbutt Hotel (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Al Bundy left this review about Endbutt Hotel

A large, sprawling Greene King gastropub. The pub is a decent looking place and inside is a fairly typical modern pub. Open plan and sprawled around the central L-shaped bar. As you'd expect the tables are cluttered with oversize menus. 6 handpulls with GK IPA, Old Speckled Hen, Old Golden Hen and Tim Taylor Landlord on. Plenty of tellies dotted around and a pool table to the right of the bar.
