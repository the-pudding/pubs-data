Neil Coomes left this review about The Bell

THINGS CAN ONLY GET BETTER!

We went there on Saturday evening for Karaoke and was pleasently surprise as we use to be regulars in there until the Polish nights started!
it is now clean and bright with friendly ENGLISH bar staff and smiley happy bouncers to keep out the trouble!
Saturday was the Army/Navy rugby match at Twickenham and the pub was full of the supporters and the atmosphere was warm ,fun and comfortable with no looking over your shoulder!
The Bell has had its problems in the past but now there is new management taken over and the pub is in the process of a redecorate, and after a long chat with Chris the new Manager who also does the Karaoke he said the new kitchen will hopefully be up and running soon selling an English menu and he is trying to make it a Famly friendly place.
Please ignore all the negitive posts about the Bell and give the new management a chance,
we will going back and making this our regular again!!!

Steve C left this review about The Bell

After spotting, and being unable to read, the signs in Polish that are strewn over the outside of the pub the next thing you notice is the sign in the window stating that anyone found in the possession of drugs or WEAPONS will be barred. I would have thought that would be obvious. The landlady was friendly enough, but I found the aged locals and the general atmosphere to be very depressing.

There is a pool table and a dart board on the way to the toilets, which had no lock on the door. The other side of the pub has a glass serving area that looks into the kitchen. The draught selection is very basic and, like most pubs in Hounslow, there is no real ale.

I'll not be returning here again.
