Gill Smith left this review about The Tinners Arms

Called in on Tuesday when I was starting a walk along the coastal path to St Ives from Zennor and enjoyed a half of Sharps Zennor Mermaid. A good beer but it was £3.60 a pint which I felt was a little steep. The pub itself is charming in a great location.

Jenny Lawrence left this review about The Tinners Arms

Brilliant location for a pub. Food OK, beer good. Welcome a bit offhand. Booked in a party of 10 which I thought was good for business in these difficult times. However, they were not particularly bothered one way or the other. Not a place I would bother going back to. If you ignore the disadvantages the location is magic.

On 31st October 2008 - rating: 4
