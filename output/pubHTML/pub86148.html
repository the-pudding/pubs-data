Reviews of The Bard (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Old Boots left this review about The Bard

Square single room with a rear counter holding six pulls and another six kegtaps, all fairly local breweries although Thornbridge and Tigertops were represented. All casks were £3 a pint. Grey walls and laminate floor otherwise as described. Wines and spirits also sold so not quite a traditional micro but still no food, except bar snacks, or electronical junk. A couple of daft quotations painted on the wall but I did like the one pictured - a Pubs Galore outing?

Al Bundy left this review about The Bard

Situated on the bottom corner of High St this is a decent little micro. The single room is sparsely decored and the bar is on the rear wall. Seating is along the walls and front leaving a bare area in the middle. 6 handpumps on the bar with Melwood Riddle Me This?, Foxfield Inverse Pale, Black Edge Dark Mild and Melwood Stanley Gold on today. 6 taps for crafty beers are also on the wall behind the bar. Its one of the better pubs in Prescot, if not the best.

On 17th February 2018 - rating: 7
