ROB Camra left this review about Mexx

Definitely a small Mexican restaurant, but drinkers are welcome. In fact at 17:45 on a Friday there were more drinkers than diners. There was no real ale on this visit, but the attractive and chatty barmaid told me that they have been used recently. I just had a half of Brahma, which was a very reasonable £1.60. I quite liked it in here, so we may well call in for something to eat in the near future.
