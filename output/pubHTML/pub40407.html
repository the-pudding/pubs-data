Steve C left this review about The Spring Gardens

Spring Gardens has a U-shaped bar with seating to the right and a pool table and dartboard at the rear. Both televisions were off during a recent Tuesday night visit and I didn’t see any live sport advertisements. However, there were advertisements for bingo, a meat raffle and a disco, but no specific dates or times. There was some low background music playing, I didn’t see a jukebox.
Standard keg is available alongside Birra Moretti and there is no food. I found the barman and the three other customers to be very friendly and I enjoyed my visit, even though the pub itself is nothing special.
