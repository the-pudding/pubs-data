Graham Coombs left this review about Wensum Lodge Hotel

Small hotel in attractive old mill, with restaurant, function rooms etc. Quite a large bar, with lots of dark wood, opening into the other parts of the hotel including a games room. No particular reason to visit unless eating or staying there.
