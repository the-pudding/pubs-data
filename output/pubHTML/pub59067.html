Steve C left this review about The Pembroke

The Pembroke is a large Smith and Jones pub with a long bar that stretches the length of the left hand wall and faces raised seating found up the right hand wall. There is also some seating around the entrance and a line of high tables up the centre of the pub. I found the décor to be quite bland and the numerous plasma screens showing nothing in particular added nothing to the non-existent ambience. Six Nations rugby was being advertised and I was unaware that Sky Sports was shown until I saw the chalkboard in the gents that advertises upcoming Premiership football games.
The bar is stocked with standard draught and supports three hand pumps. One of these was unused leaving Bombardier and Courage Best as the ale options. Very cheap pub co food is available daily until 21:00 and no under eighteens are permitted.

If the wooden floor was to be replaced by carpet and all but one of the plasma screens was removed this could be a Wetherspoons. There are better pubs nearby.
