Richard Byford left this review about The Wheatsheaf

Had a very enjoyable visit on Thursday lunchtime. The pub has an excellent restaurant with an extensive menu. The Adnams bitter was well kept and the other beers on offer are Broadside and Green King IPA, plus the usual non-descript keg bitters and lagers.
This is a pleasant pub, attractively decorated inside and has three non-smoking areas plus the public bar where smoking is currently allowed. It was very busy with senior citizens dining at the time I visited. There is also a decent sized beer garden. I recommend this as a pub well worth visiting.
