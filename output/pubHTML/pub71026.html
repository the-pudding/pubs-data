Oggwyn Great left this review about Three Fish

Large recently built Beefeater on Mere Park Buisness Park on the outskirts of Newport , its all very generic in here , to the right of the entrance is the main dining area with diner style back to back seating , to the left is the more bar like area with comfy seating and a few high stools and tables and a large tv showing news 24 . There is a large outside patio to the front and a decking area to the side .
Beers are the usual keg taps and two handpulls with Pedigree and a deecent Hobgoblin , service was friendly .
Its a typical Beefeater so you know what to expect .
