peter ashworth left this review about The Juniper - TJ's

Until a couple of years ago this was part of the Duke of Wellington pub next door but has now been converted and they are two separate places. The bar is quite spacious with chairs and tables in the centre, a room to the right and on the left booth type seats.There is also a graffiti wall on the right hand wall to add any comments.
The high bar is straight ahead and is very small and is more of a serving hatch than anything else.As you would expect with the bars name there is a decent amount of gins and cocktails but disappointingly no cask or keg beer.There is a moderate amount of imported bottles although a tad pricey (£4.00 for 330ml)so I opted for a 500ml bottle of cider for the same price.
