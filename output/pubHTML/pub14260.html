Richard Arwkrite left this review about Rose & Crown

A Green King pub/diner in a nice suburb of Hereford.Over the years its qualities have varied but at the moment its not a bad pub. It has three ales on tap Ruddles, Abbot and something else.The public bar has pool tables and has a younger clientelle. The lounge/ diner attracts all ages but during the day are mostly older and quieter folk.
the number of tables have been reduced making it much more wheelchair friendly.
A power cut prevented me from trying the food. I have put the pub back on my list as a place worth visiting.
