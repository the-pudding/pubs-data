Lucy Hall left this review about The Thorn Tree Inn

The Thorn Tree Inn is a wonderful new gastro pub located on the A610, near the M1. It is newly refurbished with country views and wonderful locally sourced food cooked fresh onsite.

The food and service here is fantastic I highly recommend it. We have adopted it as our new local!
