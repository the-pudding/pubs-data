Reviews of The Fat Boar (Average Rating: 6½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Aqualung . left this review about The Fat Boar

I turned up here early on a damp Wednesday afternoon to find it very food orientated. It looked like I was the only one just visiting for a beer.
It's basically a wooden floored single room with the bar on the left and split into areas.
There are two hand pumps which had Stonehouse Off The Rails and Salopian Lemon Dream. The Off The Rails had finished but the Lemon Dream (£3.70) was in good nick. I wished I had noticed the substantial CAMRA discount which obviously has nothing whatsoever to do with it appearing in the 2019 GBG.

Al Bundy left this review about The Fat Boar

This pub has been extended. To the rear there is now an extra room and the beer garden has taken over what was the car park. Very nicely done but the pub still leans more towards food than beer. Still 2 handpulls for real ale.

13/6/15:
Reopened. Totally refurbished inside, and a bit of a mess to be honest, with a main influence on food. Set on two levels the ground floor houses the bar and open kitchen. Upstairs there is more seating. Two real ales available with the possibility of a 3rd. On outside area out back was still being worked on on this visit. A bit pricey but at least its new.
