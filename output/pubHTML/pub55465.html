Graham Coombs left this review about The Coach & Horses

Large pub near river bridge, handy if changing buses in the adjacent bus station. One large room, with smaller areas to the front and a narrow part behind, all fairly spartanly furnished but pleasant enough. 3 or 4 real ales available including guests, seemed in fair condition.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Coach & Horses

The Coach and Horses is a medium sized pub that has got one room.
This pub probably caters for the younger customers in Tadcaster there is a pool table and large screen for watching sports,the pub was fairly comfortable and there was one real ale on here which was John Smiths cask this was a decent drink.
