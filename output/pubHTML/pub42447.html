Oggwyn Great left this review about Half Way House Inn

Food led pub just off the main Worcester - Malvern road , the small bar counter is straight infront as you enter , to the right is a small dining area , to the left is the lounge/dining area with a mixture of sofas and dining tables , the pub appears to be in the middle of a gradual refurb .
Usual taps and a couple of handpulls , only one real ale from the local St Georges brewery , this is mainly a food pub (food has a good rep judging by all the clippings of reviews from local papers and magazines)service was friendly .
One to visit for food more than drink
