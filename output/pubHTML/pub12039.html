Essex Gourmet left this review about The Bull Inn

Under New Management as of 2010, The Bull and Willow Room, situated in the country village of Great Totham in Essex, has been beautifully restored to suit the needs of the 21st century. Step through their doors and you will find a fine-dining restaurant, a gastro pub and a little snug where you can savour a pint or watch the big match.

The food is fantastic, well kept ales and an excellent wine list. Very well kept gardens and an idyllic country setting. Really the best of all worlds.

On 16th September 2010 - rating: 9
