ROB Camra left this review about Mist Bar & Lounge

Called in here for the first time as I'd been told that they now sold cask ale. They only have one handpump serving Deuchars and pretty ropey it was too. I ended up drinking San Miguel. There are several high tables and seats near the large front windows. To the left there's a couple of sofa's and to the right there are a couple of booths. The food is very cheap and cheerful especially at lunchtime, £3.95 for sausage & mash plus about 4 more options. Not exactly top quality but good portions. We may call in again for one as it was pretty friendly, but I don't think I'll have the cask ale.
