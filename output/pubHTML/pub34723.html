Philip Gaskin left this review about Horse & Groom

This is a small pub. There is a bar area at the front, which leads to another smaller bar area at the back to the left. Further left there is a pool table and door to the car park. The barmaid was friendly and welcoming. Adnams Bitter, Broadside and Ghost Ship on draught. Worth a visit.

On 18th October 2011 - rating: 7
