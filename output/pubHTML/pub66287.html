Will Larter left this review about Queen of Bradgate

A typical town centre drinking barn on a shopping street with other examples to compete against, including a 'Spoons. Which was inevitably busier than this was. The bar, with just one person serving at the time of my midweek mid-afternoon visit, has eight hand pumps, but only three ales and a perry were available. The beers were Navigation Nippon, Sharps Doom Bar and Everards Tiger. Having just had a Tiger at the Old Horse, I went for the same beer, but it was not in such good condition.

Date of visit: 27th January 2017
