Will Larter left this review about The Wheelwright Arms

A one-roomed town centre local with all of six hand pumps, but nothing to suggest that any more than two are in regular use (i.e. no beer list, no pump clips on the walls, etc). The two that were on were Greene King IPA and Fullers London Pride. I had the latter, which was OK, but Pride has changed recently and is not the beer it used to be.

There's a pool table in the centre of the room, and there was a darts match in progress when I was here. The TVs had Sky sports on with the sound down, and there was music on instead. Old team photos adorn the walls; I saw Liverpool, Tottenham, Arsenal, Nottingham Forest - and had to look quite hard before finding the Luton Town photos - very strange.

Clive Thompson left this review about The Wheelwright Arms

A good locals pub that looks to be owner run. I think it is a relatively recent refurb of a previous incarnation. A small bar with a football theme including many photos etc. showing Luton Town FC. Pool table good value at 60p a game. The London Pride was also good value at £2.40 but was served a bit warm. Bed & Breakfast accommodation is advertised.

On 15th December 2008 - rating: 6
