Alan Winfield left this review about New Crown Inn

The New Crown is situated on Newton Heaths main shopping street.
The pub looked like it had had a modern front added to it giving it a drab and uninviting appearance but once inside the pub was quite smart and very comfortable,there was bench seating and the pub was carpeted.
This pub was a Marstons tied house and there was one real ale on here this was Marstons bitter and this was a decent drink.
I thought this was quite a nice pub to have a few drinks in.
