Bucking Fastard left this review about The Canute

This is one of the better Smith & Jones outlets,given the choice of real ale from six handpumps.There are even tasting notes written on a small chalk board,and the condition of my Ossett Citra was fine.The interior is split into different levels but the chain use the same designers as 'spoons,it's virtually an identikit.However the numerous screens cover sports with the volume up,so if you want a sports bar atmosphere with some real ale ,this place may work for you.National real ale brands rarely inspire me,I feel there are better pub alternatives close by.

Thuck Phat left this review about The Canute

This is the first Smith & Jones pub I've been to and Mr Later, below, is spot on when he refers to it as a high street drinking barn.
Two things stuck out from the norm, firstly that they do have Sky Sports and do show live games on the myriad screens around the place. Unlike 'Spoons with their relentless Sky Sports News. Secondly, there's a fair selection of ale offered.
On our visit the beer choice was Ossett Citra, which was good, Pedigree Diamond, Ringwood Boondoggle, Morland Original, Bombardier and Olde Trip. Unsurprisingly dominated by the nationals but saved by the Citra.
You know what you're getting here and if you're lucky it may include a decent pint of something interesting.

On 16th June 2012 - rating: 6
