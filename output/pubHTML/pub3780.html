Reviews of The Queens (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about The Queens

This has recently been refurbished by Robbies to within an inch of its life. It's now featureless and characterless. There's a small lounge area to the left and a further one up a couple of steps to the right. There are two dartboards, one with a very impressive surround. Local radio playing over the sound system, I hate that, listening to adverts whilst drinking your beer. One positive is a sign saying no under 18's allowed. Unicorn & Dizzy Blonde available on the handpumps. The Dizzy was in decent nick (for Dizzy and at £1.50 very reasonably priced.

Al Bundy left this review about The Queens

A fairly standard pub. Its an open plan place and fairly modern inside. There are 3 handpumps with Robinson's beers on. There are better pubs around but while this place isn't rubbish its probably not the place for real ale aficionados.
