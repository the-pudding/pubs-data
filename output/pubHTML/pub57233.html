Soup Dragon left this review about Elisabeth Arms

A large detached modern and somewhat squat and dull looking white painted brick and white board Sizzling chain pub. It has a patio and trees outside with lights on!

The interior is open plan and, as usual for this kind of place, at bit soulless. The decor is wood panel and white, red, plum and there is an olive colour ceiling. There is little in the way of interest through pictures or ephemera, but there are a few mirrors and there is a pool table. Mainly foody, it does have a few sofas, as well as a wood pile like the Ember Inns - quite why, i am not sure. The background music was fine, service very good and the clientele mixed and it was busy.

Beer; tap stuff with Banks's MILD, and a decent Banks's Bitter on handpull with an untried Pedigree.

The service and beer were fine, the food looked alright, i just find these pubs uninspiring
