Colin Walker left this review about Gladyer Inn

The Gladyer Inn (Pronounced Glad Yer In – (Glad You Are In)) has the appearance of a prison camp building from the outside and has ruined the look of the frontage of the hotel. The inside gives a much better rendition of a local pub. Although the place is attached to the hotel it does not sell the beer at hotel prices and they are similar to the other local pubs. Ignoring the outside the pub has a pleasant enough atmosphere. The place is filled with locals and those staying at the low cost hotel. There is parking available at the front of the hotel.
