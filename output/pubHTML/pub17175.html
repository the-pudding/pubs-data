peter ashworth left this review about The Gamecock Inn

Has recently undergone an extensive refurbishment and must admit was quite impressed. From Monday to Saturday a carvery operates, offering a good meal in pleasant surroundings, at a price that is excellent value for money (on Sunday the carvery still on but is at a higher price) Sometimes there can be a lengthy queue but it is well worth the wait.
