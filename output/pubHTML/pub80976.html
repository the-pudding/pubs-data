Graham Coombs left this review about The Old Warehouse

A tiny bar with a vague attempt at a Mediterranean feel, including a large mural. There is an even tinier patio at the rear acting mainly as the smoking area. No real ale; mainly lagers and ciders on keg with John Smith's as a token bitter.
