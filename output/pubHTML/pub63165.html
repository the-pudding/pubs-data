Ian Mapp left this review about Thwaite Arms

Well, initially we were concerned it would be closed. Midday on a Friday and door bolted. Indeed it is in Winter Time - but April is Summertime. Opened at 12:03pm.

Tiny unspoilt bar, where the landlord and landlady make you most welcome before going back to tending their garden!

Theakstons in good condition.

A great experience.

Photos at my walking blog - http://mappiman.blogspot.co.uk/2016/04/150416-day-5-yorkshire-dales-inn-way.html
