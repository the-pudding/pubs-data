Alan Winfield left this review about Plumbers Arms

The Plumbers Arms is a back street corner pub that i liked the look of and it is the sort of pub i enjoy going into.
Once inside i went in the room at the front which was fairly small but it was comfortable enough and i had a decent atmosphere to it.
The pub had free house signs outside but the only beers on the bar were from the Bass stable,i had a drink of Bass and this was a very nice drink the other beer was M&B Brew X1.
I thought this was a very nice pub to end my Worcester crawl in.
