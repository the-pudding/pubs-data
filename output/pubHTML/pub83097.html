Colin Walker left this review about Wallace Well Farm

Wallace Well Farm is part of the Farmhouse Inns group and was newly built in the autumn of 2014. The pub name comes from the well that William Wallace reputed to have used before his capture at Robroyston There is an outdoor patio area / beer garden for when weather permits. There is a good choice of drinks on draught such as Budweiser, Carling, Stella Artois, Tennent's, Belhaven Best Guinness, Aspall Suffolk Cider & Strongbow Cider. The place is open daily 8am to 11pm. The bar is open from 11am to 11pm. There are meals available daily from 8am to 9pm and a carvery available from Mon - Thu: 11:30am to 8:30pm, Fri - Sat: 11:30am to 9pm and Sun: 11:30am to 8:30pm. There is plenty of parking around the building.
