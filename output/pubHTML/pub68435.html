E TA left this review about Bridge Bar and Eating House

As one would expect from an airport bar in England, it's a soulless business whose aim is to provide fast food and drink for people waiting for planes. That said, I was grateful to have the opportunity to grab a cooked breakfast and wash it down with a pint of Doombar before disappearing off to Portugal where decent beer was (as I suspected it would be) not to be found. The Doombar was colder than it should have been but it was nonetheless drinkable. Worth popping in for that last relaxing drink before boarding your plane.
