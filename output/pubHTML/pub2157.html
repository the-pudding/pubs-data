Komakino . left this review about The Fountain Inn

A two-roomed pub which seems to be 25 years in the past with its yellow and orange colour scheme, lino floor and cans of K Cider in the fridge. There's a Saloon Bar to the right which houses a pool table and a larger Lounge Bar to the left which had a throng of locals watching the denouement to the 2016-17 Premier League season. Two ales available - Fuller's London Pride and St Austell Tribute: the latter's price of £3.30 meaning there's at least one pub in the village where you can still get "3 for a tenner".

On 23rd May 2017 - rating: 3
