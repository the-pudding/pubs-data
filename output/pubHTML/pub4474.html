Alan Winfield left this review about Cutty Sark

The Cutty Sark is next door to the Falmoufh Townhouse.
Once inside there is a large square shaped room with the bar on the right side,the floor is bare boarded,the seating normal tables and chairs,there is a smaller separate room to the rear which is square shaped,there are bucket chairs and tables in this room.
There were two real ales on the bar,we had a drink of Skinners Cornish Knocker,which was a nice drink,the other real ale was Sharps Sea Fury.
Background music was playing.
It was almost empty on our Sunday afternoon visit.

Pub visited 4/8/2017

Gill Smith left this review about The Cutty Sark

Called in today and found they had a Hook Norton beer on. They had to change the first drink as it hadn't been pulled through, but it was fine after that. Friendly staff enjoyed chatting to customers. There was a pool table and darts, and they host live music. There is a lovely beer garden out of the back and a comfortable lounge with settees and TV.
