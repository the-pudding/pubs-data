Steve C left this review about Malones

Malones is a live music pub with minimal seating and sets playing most evenings. During my visit on a recent Monday evening a band was setting up on the stage to the right of the entrance. The two plasma screens and projector were showing muted Sky Sports whilst some background music played. A standard keg range is available from the small bar, including four Guinness fonts. I found the staff to be very friendly, but I’m not a huge fan of live music so will probably never return.
