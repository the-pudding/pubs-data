Al Bundy left this review about The Friendship Inn

A large very well presented Hydes pub. Lovely inside with a Victorian look. Mainly open plan it is split into 2 areas. There is a central U-shaped bar. 12 handpumps on the nar with one for cider. A range of Hyde's beers on including Bitter, Pale, Lowry and another I've forgotten. Other beers available were 2 from Beer Studio, Black Country Chain Ale, Charles Wells Globe Hopper and reversed Brimstage ale. A proper looking pub unlike the Wethers opposite.

On 13th September 2017 - rating: 8
