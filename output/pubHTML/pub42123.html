Anonymous User left this review about The Round Of Gras

Badsey is an important asparagus-growing area. 'Gras' is the local word for the seasonal delicacy, which is only on sale locally from Easter (weather permitting) until 21 June. Naturally, the pub features asparagus extensively on its menu.

See how attractive the old sign was!
