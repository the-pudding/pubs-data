Quinno _ left this review about The Wishing Well

Nothing changes. Which is how the regulars like it. Says 'ales' on the exterior sign but that's a lie.

March 2011
Little Irish pub on the Oxford Road by Reading West station. Surprisingly modern both inside and outside - inside looks like it came out of the Wetherspoons interior refurbishment catalogue. No real ale.
