ROB Camra left this review about Beef & Pudding

Recently refurbished restaurant and bar. As you enter the restaurant area is to the left with the drinkers area to the right, divided by metal display shelving with a large screen TV attached. All very nicely done with a painting of a cow on the wall and some comfortable seating. 3 handpumps on the bar with 2 on, serving Phoenix Spotland Gold & Cottage Deltic with Moorhouses's Pendle Witch turned round. At £3.60 the Spotland Gold was expensive, but in decent condition. I'll no doubt be back here as the other half will love it.
