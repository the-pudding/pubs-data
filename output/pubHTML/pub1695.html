Quinno _ left this review about New Inn

Now re-opened under its old moniker, the New Inn. Real ale available from Rebellion, heavily carbonated but came through a pump nonetheless. Still heavily food oriented. Worth a visit for the extraordinarily fit brunette barmaid.

October 2011 - as Old Devil
Foody pub that has three ales - sometimes good, sometimes howlingly bad (with a scowl if you try and get them changed). Closed at 10:15 on a Thursday night last week when we drove past, so assume business isn't looking good??
