Andy Ven left this review about The Bowling Green Inn

Pub/restaurant with laminate floor throughout and rough textured internal walls painted in a cream colour gave the feeling of it being a clean place. There were 3 rooms, one being part of an extension to the original building structure with a doorway through from the lounge, and a beer garden to the rear. There were a handful of people in on Saturday lunchtime. Service was friendly and efficient.

Cask ales were Martson's Pedigree and Sharp's Doom Bar.
