Graham Coombs left this review about The Haywain

Large and busy open plan family-eating sort of pub on roundabout by retail area, but benefits from a good range of beers from Marstons including Banks, Pedigree and 49er, achieving Good Beer Guide listing at time of visit and certainly seemed in good order. Food includes lots of 2 for 1 offers and its own pizza oven. Very good of its type.

On 22nd November 2014 - rating: 7
