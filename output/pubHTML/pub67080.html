Alan Winfield left this review about The Bank Corner

The Bank Corner is a newish pub in the middle of Alsager and i think it is a Smith&Jones pub.
There are quite a few tables and chairs at the front of the pub to watch people and traffic go by.
Once inside there is the usuall layout of tall tables and chairs in the middle of the room with comfier seating at the front and round the sides.
There were three pumps on the bar but only one was in use and this was Titanic Lifeboat which was a decent drink though slightly too warm.
