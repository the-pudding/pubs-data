Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Hillfield Farm

New-build Farmhouse Inns 'Dining & Carvery' outlet hidden away in a business park, with unusually defined 'adult' and 'family' dining areas. Just a little general seating around the central bar, but there are also some patio tables at the far end. Anonymous furniture and decor throughout. Standard menu, but thankfully some real ale too, with Greene King IPA, Abbot and Doom Bar (£3.30) available from three of four handpumps.
