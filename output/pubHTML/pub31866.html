Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Quinno _ left this review about The Fleur De Lys Inn

Traditional interior with some cosier modern touches. Flagstone floor, with some carpeting. Candles on tables, log fires, food. Very friendly locals. Three pumps, two local ales - Loddon Hoppit (pretty good) and Vale Red Kite (reported good). Accommodation offered. Musical accompaniment from a small woodwind orchestra practising in the house next door. Decent grassed garden and Aunt Sally pitch. Nice little place, preferred the more pubby ambience of the George but this one is certainly worth popping into.

On 30th April 2014 - rating: 7
