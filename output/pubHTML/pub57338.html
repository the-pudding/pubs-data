Thuck Phat left this review about The Red Lion

An identikit chain with the twin advantages of an attractive exterior and good location.
The Red Lion is central to Stratford, has a decent sized garden and overlooks the canal and river.
I have eaten here in recent years and the food was neither awful nor memorable. Standard food service delivered I'd guess.
Our recent visit found Sharp's Atlantic and Pedigree on and I have never found anything other than nationals or large regionals here which has tended to lead to Stella on warm days.
Fine to sit outside with the family on a nice day but that's as far as it goes.

Bucking Fastard left this review about The Red Lion

A rather lifeless Table-Table chain pub ,with a full gastro makeover inside and few original features.There is a decent sized beer garden which backs onto the towpath of the Stratford Canal ,but the busy one way road system dominates the view meaning it is neither relaxing nor quiet.
Just two real ales on during my trip Pedigree and Sharp's Atlantic,which didn't excite either.
