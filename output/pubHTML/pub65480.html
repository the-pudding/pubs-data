Steve Thorpe left this review about Ben Nevis Inn

After a very strenuous walk up Ban Nevis and a far quicker descent, the image of a boozer at the bottom is a sight to behold! The day I visited was a day when they had ran out of real ale so had to do with some fizzy lager rubbish. Still, having trekked the big hill for 7 hours, even that tasted good! It is a 200+ year old building and houses a wood burning stove.

On 2nd September 2010 - rating: 6
