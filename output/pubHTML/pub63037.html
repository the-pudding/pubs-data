Komakino . left this review about Golden Cross

The only pub in the roadside village of Saunderton, this sates most camps offering pub grub, a three ale selection and retains a two-room layout in an era of glasnost. A narrow room which had a pool table and a central table with diners occupied the right-hand room, with the left-hand room being slightly larger and L-shaped and possibly more geared for dining (all tables had menus). GK IPA, Butcombe Bitter and Wye Valley HPA were the ales on, and as this is possibly better-judged on a Friday or Saturday night rather than a weeknight, this is another one mentally-noted for a revisit.

On 8th March 2017 - rating: 4
