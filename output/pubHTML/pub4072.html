Old Boots left this review about The Bay Horse

A large dining pub, very popular Sunday lunchtimes, no doubt with folks from Teesside. Old looking but extended with a restaurant area at one end. Modernised interior with old style furnishings and old photo decor. Four pulls of Cameron’s / Marstons beers and the usual range of UK kegs. I was there on a Sunday lunchtime and it felt a bit ruthlessly efficient and slightly regimented but these places aren’t my cup of tea anyway. It may sound harsh saying that but keeping up that pace and efficiency does need very hard working professional staff, which it has. Beer was ok but not sure it warranted its place in the GBG 2019.
