Helen Iwanczuk left this review about The Ridge

The Ridge is a large estate pub in Wyman's Brook. I used to live in this area, so I'm a little bias when writing a review. The pub has improved quite considerably since it was The Haymaker. It is quite spacious inside, with comfortable modern furnishings. Sports fans are catered for with a dart board and pool table.There is a lovely view of Wyman's Brook if you sit in the recent extension at the front of the pub. Or you can sit outside on the patio, and watch the world go by. Best of all, it is very near Cheltenham Racecourse - ideal for race goers! There is a car park, at the back of the row of shops near by.

On 6th March 2011 - rating: 6
