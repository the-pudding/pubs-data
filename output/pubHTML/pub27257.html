Alan Winfield left this review about The Vaults

The Vaults is a very basic looking corner pub and one of only a few left on this stretch of road.
Once inside there is a decent sized oblong shaped room with the bar to the rear,the room is carpeted,the seating is bench type and chairs,there was a TV to the front right showing music videos,there is a separate pool room to the rear which is down one step,there is bench seating in this room.
There were no real ales on the bar which was no surprise,i had a drink of John Smiths Extra Cold which was cold and crap.
The pub was fairly busy with older drinkers.
I love doing basic pubs like this,this one did'nt dissapoint.
