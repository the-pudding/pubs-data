Barbara Walker left this review about Beaconsfield Arms

Despite being newcomers to the Pub trade Andy and Toni have made a real impact on this Pub and the Village. With their enthusiasm, hard work and warm welcome the Pub is a delight to visit. They are always behind the bar to greet their customers and Tone sometimes in the kitchen. Beer always in top condition: Adnams, Woodfords Wherry among others. Good food and a quality selection of wines.Darts nights, snooker, supporting the local football team just a sample of the activities. Sunday lunch!! great.
My husband and I have to travel 4 miles to get there but have made it our local.\a proper traditional Pub despite the hard times that all country pubs are having at the moment. We thoroughly recommend this wonderful Pub.

On 22nd February 2008 - no rating submitted
