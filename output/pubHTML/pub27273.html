Al Bundy left this review about The Viking

As noted it is owned by a TV dinner-maker. A large open plan pub with a central island bar. As you would expect food is high on the agenda and there are 5 handpumps on the bar with beers being Wainwright's, Tiny Rebel Cwtch, Liverpool Organic 24 Carat Gold, Brimstage Trappers Hat and Liverpool Craft Quokka being on today. As you enter there are 2 copper tanks for Budvar beers. To the rear is a pleasant beer garden which has a small boat and a boules thingy. Its slightly on the expensive side.

18/2/15
This is now called The Hilltop.
