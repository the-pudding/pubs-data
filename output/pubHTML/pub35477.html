Reviews of The Prince Regent (Average Rating: 4 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about The Prince Regent

A couple of old Watney real ale signs adorn the attractive frontage, but the inside is a pretty ghastly throwback to the worst of the 1990s refurbs, with characterless (once-)modern seating, some hideous imitation stone wallpaper and a restaurant area pretending to be a cafeteria. Two handpumps both claimed to be dispensing London Pride, although it tasted suspiciously gassy to me. A selection of screens showing different sports and the food specials sounded cheapish, but not much other reason to visit.

Quinno _ left this review about The Prince Regent

Looks alright from the outside but the oblong interior has been stripped mercilessly and is now a thunkingly dull bar-by-numbers with laminate floor, poorly done three shade painted walls and a bar top made of sparkly floor tiles. Two pumps with one ale on, Pride, which was pretty drinkable – a surprise. The barman somehow decided that turning off the 6 Nations match between Scotland and Italy just as the Italians were mounting a comeback was a great idea. Pretty poor really but at least the ale was alright.
