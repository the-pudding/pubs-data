Aqualung . left this review about Castle Hotel

As noted below this isn't really a pub but a poncy hotel bar open to non-residents. It was full of braying English visitors on my Friday evening visit. The bar has five hand pumps which had a cider and Conwy Dawson's Dark, Welsh Pride, Clogwyn Gold & Honey Fayre. I went for the Dawson's Dark (£3.30) which was in good nick.
It's not my sort of place but deserves credit for stocking four beers from the local brewer.

Gareth Williams left this review about Castle Hotel

Can get crowded and deservedly so! A popular fairly upmarket place to meet and greet which is bright and clean. Staff welcoming and cheerful-they take pride in the place. Very well stocked bar with well kept local brews available. Food is good and well presented. Not really a pub but a pleasant hotel bar where non-residents are welcome.
