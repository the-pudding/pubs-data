Hayley Evans left this review about Tafarn Y Garreg Inn

I ended up lost around this area and came across this pub. The landlady and her family were fantastic, kind, friendly and hospitable. I had a meal there which was great and the landlady, Rhianedd kindly loaned me the use of a caravan for the night, free of charge. How kind! I cannot recommend dropping in this pub enough, not only for the brilliant hospitality but it's a great place to begin various walks too, especially since you can leave your car on their car park for a small fee. The pub has been restored and is steeped in fascinating history and Rhianedd and her family plan to make further developments regarding possible camping sites etc so keep your eyes posted: this will be a great place to base yourself for visits to the mountains, hills and surrounding beautiful landscape. A pleasure to end up lost there! I will be returning. Kind regards to the wonderful landlady and her wonderful family.
