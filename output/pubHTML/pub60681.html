Graham Coombs left this review about Thirsty Farmer

Large village inn with equally large bar areas. Dining area at rear but most of pub given over to food anyway. A couple of fair beers on offer and kept well(Cask Marque listed). Pleasant enough. Note the lettering on the outbuildings which shows it was once the New Inn, and spot the former Old Inn opposite.
