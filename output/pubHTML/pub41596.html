Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Bryn Williamson left this review about The Red Lion

I have fond memories of this pub, when it was a truly great establishment. It still is a fantastic building, in an awesome setting, but sadly it's let down by the standard of the food and drink, and by the miserable staff.

It can get incredibly busy, especially in summer, and you can't help but feel its owners see it as something of a cash-cow. It has the potential to be a great place again, but at the moment the emphasis seems to be more on belting out below average and overpriced food and ale.

On 12th June 2008 - rating: 4
