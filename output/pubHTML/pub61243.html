Alan Winfield left this review about The Bunters Bar

Bunters Bar is massive inside and has entrances of two different streets.
There is a large oblong shaped room with other rooms off it,one has four pool tables,there was a large screen in the main room showing sky sports.
There were four real ales on the bar,we had a drink of St Austell Cornish Best,which was a nice drink,the other real ales were St Austell Tribute,Bath Ales Jem and Bath Ales Prophecy.
Not really our type of pub,but the beer was decent enough.

Pub visited 14/8/2017

Gill Smith left this review about The Bunters Bar

Called in for a drink here as it is featured in the 2010 Good Beer Guide. Large airy pub with more than one bar area, the others being for evenings or events I expect. A phone box and large screens can be found inside. Conservatory feel to parts of it. Sell local ales, and we had Skinners Betty Stogs and St Austell Tribute, both of which were in good condition.
