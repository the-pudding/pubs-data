Malden man left this review about The Railway Vue

The pub is now in the 2010 GBG. Four on, two in each bar so you need to go through to check what is on. Black Sheep Bitter and GK IPA in the lounge and Adnams Bitter and Camerons Fireside in the bar at the time of our visit.
Two screens in the bar for sport, and the pub is very convenient for Histon FC.
The Guv'nor is a staunch Stoke City fan, this is reflected throughout the pub. Note the system in the gents, with the "Fast Lane" urinal for imbibers of 5 pints or more. Highly entertaining stuff.
Currently easily reached by bus from central Cambridge incl station, but will eventually be a stop on the new Cambridge high speed busway.

On 4th February 2010 - rating: 6
