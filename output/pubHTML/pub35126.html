Reviews of The Dukes Head (Average Rating: 8½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about The Dukes Head

I have come to the conclusion that an immaculately-kept exterior means that the pub is 'foody',and this is certainly the case here. The two sides are not labelled, but the right-hand side is slightly more of a drinkers' area, with fairly traditional furnishings, although still much used for food. The left hand side is more modern in style but primarily a restaurant/dining area, opening through to modern extensions behind. Large garden to the side and a monthly nightclub in the outbuildings. Beers included some interesting and well-kept local ales while the food, although undoubtedly of very high quality, seemed pricy and not over-generous.
