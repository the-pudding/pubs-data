Bill Walker left this review about Red Lion Inn

A warm friendly local bar, more like a clubhouse, really, with accent on sports, particularly RU, soccer and cricket. Sky sports always available.
Speciality restaurant, with high quality fare and extensive menu very well rated in the area. Serge and Jules preside. Need to book well in advance.
