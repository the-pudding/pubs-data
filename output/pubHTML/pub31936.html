Alan Winfield left this review about Jolly Sportsman

The Jolly Sportsman is a decent looking stone built pub that is situated on a corner of a crossroads in the middle of Eynsham.
Once inside there is a small L shaped room to the front,the floor is wood tiled,the seating normal tables and chairs and a few pew seats,there was a TV showing football,there is a separate lounge to the rear.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Doom Bar which was a decent enough drink,the other beer was Taylor Landlord.
This is a decent locals pub that was really busy on my late Saturday afternoon visit.

Pub visited 16/5/2015
