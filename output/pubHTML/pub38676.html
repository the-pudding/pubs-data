Real Ale Ray left this review about Red Lion

Pub No. 2 on our Vintage bus tour. A decent pub this one, where a lot of thought and care has been used to bring the interior up to date. The bar has been fitted using pine timber and has been cleverly stained to resemble solid oak. This is an L shaped pub with lots of comfortable seating areas. They have even finished the rear patio garden to a high standard. Five hand pumps, I went for the Salopian Lemon, which was a well balanced and good tasting beer.

Old Peculiar left this review about Red Lion

Recently purchased by the Furious Pub Company and reopened following a £150K renovation and facelift, the pub now has Salopian beers and several guest ales from as far away as Scotland and Belgium. It is proving very popular; having a queue at the bar at 4pm on a weekday is a problem many landlords would die for, but it was worth the wait.
A far cry from the jaded place it once was, a visit is a must.
