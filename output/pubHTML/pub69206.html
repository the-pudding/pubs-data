Alan Hurdle left this review about Imperial Hotel

This is one of those pubs that look better on the inside than expected from the outside view. It is very large but split up into many different areas. The walls are decorated with mirrors and many local historic photographs. It was very quiet early evening on a Wednesday, but with its central location, I imagine the atmosphere will be quite different on a weekend night.
Real Ales available were Courage Directors and Wells Bombardier with the latter on sale at £2 per pint.
