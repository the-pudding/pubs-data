Real Ale Ray left this review about Golden Fleece

A decent enough one room pub this one, with friendly staff and locals. The pub consists of a nice display of terracotta floor tiles running from the entrance to the back of the pub. A comfortable seating area on the left, with newly upholstered, jade in colour banquette seating running across the front windows and stools along the bar. The bar was finished in mahogany and has all its original features. There were two handpumps with Bank's Bitter and Mild.
