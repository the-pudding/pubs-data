Quinno _ left this review about Hurley House Hotel

The ‘one I missed’ on my Berkshire 100% challenge in 2015 as it was closed throughout the year, this one was massively refurbished and opened in July 2016 as a ‘boutique hotel’, shedding its former moniker of the Red Lyon in the process. Clearly a huge amount of money has been spunked on the place, they probably spent more on the bar fittings than I earn in year. The garden is quite impressive, but unfortunately that was where most of my appalling Rebellion Smuggler ended-up. To be fair, I was half-expecting it...

Laura Hayes left this review about The Red Lion

The area surrounding the pub is steeped in history - Queen Elizabeth was imprisoned in nearby Bisham Abbey and it was here that Henry VIII first beheld the unfortunate Lady Jane Grey.

After a busy day observing many of the local attractions you will be happy to sit and enjoy a delightful meal full of freshly prepared ingredients such as Slow Cooked Shoulder of Lamb, daily fresh fish dishes and Homemade Pie of the Day. The menu at the Red Lyon boasts several fantastic dishes as well as a great choice of fine wines and cask ales.

The Red Lyon is a stylish pub with a contemporary twist. Features include roaring open fires, low beam ceilings and several cosy but chic areas to relax in.
