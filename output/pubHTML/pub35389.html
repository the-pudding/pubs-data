Real Ale Ray left this review about Royal Oak Inn

This large 16th century pub is right next to the church. The main bar faces you when you enter with a dining area to the right and a pool table area on the left. There was also a good outside seated area in the front. This was a busy pub with drinkers and also attracts a lot of diners. Enjoyed a couple of games of pool, the downside for me was that the Earl Soham Victoria was far too cold.
