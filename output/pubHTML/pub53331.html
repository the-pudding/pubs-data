moby duck left this review about The Regent

Still spoonsesque but with better service, some decent background music and live footie. Housed in an interesting building, a former cinema there were a few decent beers on from the likes of Titanic, Twickenham, a couple of micros I now forget backing up the more ordinary Abbott and Doom Bar along with a couple of ciders, my Adnam's Ghostship was in good form despite being a tad on the cold side.
Not really my cup of tea but not too bad for this type of pub.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Regent

Now sold from Wetherspoons to Stonegate. Very little change apart from the menu and addition of permanent background music. Still retains a good range of beers and quality seems to bearing up ok. For the moment at least it remains a pleasant enough place to enjoy good beer at a reasonable price.
