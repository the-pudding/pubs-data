Alan Winfield left this review about Coopers Arms

The Coopers Arms is a decent looking pub that is fairly old,the pub is now in the middle of an estate.
Once inside the pub had two rooms with a decent bar and an olde worlde style lounge which was very comfortable.
This pub used to be an Home Ales tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Home bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Home mild.
I think this pub is still up and running but i dont know what beers are now on here.
