Will Larter left this review about The Dunkirk Inn

This is an isolated roadside pub with a real friendly atmosphere - the landlord went out of his way to greet us, and when I dropped my return train ticket while paying for my beers, sought us out and made sure our day wasn't spoilt. It was quite busy in here, with a dining room to the left and a public bar to the right, and a further small room further right where a pool table was hidden away behind the fireplace.

There were three real ales available: Wychwood Hobgoblin, Tetleys Bitter and Jennings Cumberland, and the three of us had a different beer which we drank without complaint. This was our second pub of a Denby Dale crawl, and we had another walk back to the centre and a bus to catch, otherwise we'd have stayed longer. There was a lad of about seven playing pool who would have beaten me if I'd taken him on, so it's just as well we had to hit the road.

On 30th December 2012 - rating: 6
