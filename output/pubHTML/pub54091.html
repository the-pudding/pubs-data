Alex Conway left this review about Burns Hotel

Classic Sam Smiths pub with the usual selection of beers including their only cask beer Old Brewery Bitter (£1.80). This is accompanied by Alpine lager (£1.73) Double four lager (£2.00) Taddy lager (£2.10) Reserve Stout (£2.20) as well as also featuring Sovereign Bitter, Dark Mild, Pure Brewed organic Lager and organic wheat beer. The OBB on my visits is usually nicely kept but as in all Sam Smiths pubs the occasional poor pint does squeeze through, but for £1.80 a pint who is complaining.

After the recent refurbishment this pub now looks very clean and presentable with lots of cushioned bench seating and nice armchairs. At the very reasonable prices they sell their beer for it is no wonder a pub right in the heart of the city center like this is so popular.
