Quinno _ left this review about The White Horse

Posh hotel bar in aged building, parts dating circa 1500 if the plaque is to be believed. There’s a lovely set of galleried doors down the side entrance (which presumably led to old stables). The bar (to the left past reception) is obviously quite modern and loungey in style, modern (read bland) with rugs and mushroom shades on the walls plus piped music majoring on muted trumpets. Small selection of board games atop a pouffe. Surprisingly there were two ales and both local as well - Flack Double Drop and Upham Punter. My Flack was an odd one, in seemingly decent nick but odd-tasting. Noted that it came in an oversized glass and was also a similar price the Old House at Home. You’d have no real reason to seek this one but at least somebody’s making an effort at the bar.
