Alan Winfield left this review about Cast

This bar is part of the Nottingham playhouse complex which was built in the 60s and is now a listed building.
Once inside here there is one largish room which is quite smart and comfortable.
A few years ago there used to be a good choice of real ales on here but the choice is'nt as good now with only a couple of real ales on.
This pub is hidden from the main road and its nice in summer sitting outside here.
