Tex Icecubes left this review about Graham Arms Hotel

Been here a few times.
Just one real ale from the local Geltsdale brewery. On this occasion it was served in a murphys glass which is just the way I like it.
Usually about a dozen locals in on an afternoon (good for the area) mainly smoothflow and lager drinkers. One or two diners (meals reasonable), there is even a separate dining room for non minglers.
There is an enormous mirror, weekend entertainment and a dartboard.
The one beer is always decent. All in all in all its a good place to stop off. Kind of place to enjoy a sleepy summer afternoon.
