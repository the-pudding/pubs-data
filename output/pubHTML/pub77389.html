ROB Camra left this review about Cheshire Cheese

A small two roomed locals local on the edge of the town centre. Has a large sign on the door saying "No Travellers". Often has live music on at weekends and there's a small beer garden to the rear. There are 2 handpumps, but usually on Black Sheep Bitter is available. The pint quality can be variable depending on when you visit. I don't normally drink the real stuff in midweek.
