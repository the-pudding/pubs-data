Andy Ven left this review about The New Inn

Multi roomed brewery tap including pool room, bar/lounge and restaurant. On handpull there were the brewery’s own ales plus one guest. There were a fair number of mature diners in during my lunchtime visit but it was strangely quiet, like a library. Not a bad place at all if this was your local.
