Alan Hurdle left this review about Odfellows

I am not sure what to make of this pub compared to other pubs in the town. It is quite a large old pub selling three cask ales from micro brewers. Trade was quiet on my very brief visit prior to catching a train to Oakengates. It is an ordinary sort of pub and very handy with the station entrance right outside the door.

Aqualung . left this review about Odfellows Wine Bar

This is a pub that has been given the bare floorboards and large wooden tables treatment, making it resemble one of the trendy pubs in Hackney or Stoke Newington but without the inflated prices. The beer selection was limited compared to the other GBG pubs in town. It consisted of Oddfellows Shifnal Pale Ale, Salopian Shropshire Gold & Oracle and Batham's Best.
There was also a low ABV cider called Merry Monkey. Apparently the Oddfellows is brewed somewhere on the premises and I ordered it (£2.40) but sadly it was a bit cloudy and smelled of vinegar.
It was changed without question for Batham's Best which was superb.
This place would be worth another visit just on the basis that it's only a few yards South of the Station but it would be good if they could get the home brewing side of things sorted.
