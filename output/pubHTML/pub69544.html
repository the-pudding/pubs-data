darren howard left this review about Sidmouth Arms

We stumbled across this fantastic pub last week when we stayed in Taunton.

I cant recommend enough that you try the food! Its absolutely fantastic and the servings are huge so make sure you're hungry.

Everything they service is locally produced and sourced, even down tot he ice cream which is made anout 2 miles up the road from the pub.

The staff are incredibly frienldy and nothing at all is too much trouble. I was even show the skittle alley which is over 100 years old! Its great and i was pretty good too :o)

This is definitely a pub thats well worth seeking out. Theres loads of parking too.

You wont be dissapointed!

On 14th May 2010 - rating: 8
