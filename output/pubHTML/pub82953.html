Reviews of The Compleat Angler (Average Rating: 4 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Komakino . left this review about The Compleat Angler

Situtated by Marlow Bridge and named after Izaak Walton's 17th Century book, we were here for the 'Sindhu' Indian restaurant. Primarily a hotel, this has a functioning hotel bar (The Walton Bar) open to non-patrons whose outside terrace offers a pleasant view over the Thames and its weir, as well as All Saints Church. As mentioned below, no real ale except bottles of local brewery Rebellion's Red and Blonde. Some nice oak panelling and furnishings means this is an ambitious detour from the hit and miss pubs of Marlow town.
