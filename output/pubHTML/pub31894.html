Komakino . left this review about The Extraordinary Hare

Another Oxfordshire re-opening - this roadside pub had been closed for a number of years. Resurrected by Star Pubs, it's had the obligatory country pub makeover, but is none the worse for it. The Carpenter Gothic facade remains with a grey paint job and there's a mishmash of styles inside. Three ales were all nationals - Fuller's London Pride, TT Landlord and Wychwood Hobgoblin Gold - a pint of the latter £4 and decent enough. A couple of screens showed an England ODI and A BT Sport programme respectively, both on mute, with some random muzak in the background, which bizarrely included Madonna's "Dear Jessie". A fair few in during this visit, so a welcome return of what seemed a lost cause.
