moby duck left this review about The Inn

A bit of a strange place this one,it feels a bit like a very posh shed conversion,wooden flooring and the decor is rather low key and clinical,lots of tables and chairs as food is a big thing here but also a distinct drinking area around the bar with barstools.The service was very friendly.Three beers on Tribute and Pride along with a decent pint of Thurstons Horsell Gold. Not really my kind of place.
