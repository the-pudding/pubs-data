Steve C left this review about Marine View Hotel

This is the downstairs bar of the Marine View bed and breakfast that is bigger than I thought it would be. There is a dartboard at the rear along with a bar billiard table that was covered during my visit last Saturday evening. The bar is to the left of the entrance where standard draught products can be purchased including keg London Pride and Old Speckled Hen. Pub grub is served and the mains are priced around the £8 mark. There is a plasma screen that was turned off and I saw no Sky Sports signs, but there was some background music playing. There was only one other customer, but he and the landlady were very friendly.

I wouldn’t make a point of visiting here again, but I would be happy to pop in if I was staying in one of the rooms above.

On 29th September 2012 - rating: 5
