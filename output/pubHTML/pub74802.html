Alan Hurdle left this review about Brookhouse Mill

17th Century flour mill (powered by the nearby River Ystrad) until early 1900's, then making ceramics until the 1960's.
Now an attractive beamed bar with several restaurant areas.
Real Ales served from local Welsh brewers Conwy, Facer's, and Great Orme.
