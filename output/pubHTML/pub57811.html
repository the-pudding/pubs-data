Nigel Rowe left this review about The Natterjack

An excellent hostelry that I have visited many times over the past 30 years. Formerly The Railway (alongside the former Somerset & Dorset station) then The Silent Whistle, The Natterjack is a superb pub. A very enthusiast beer lover, the landlord keeps his beers in tip-top condition. Bath's Gem and Butcombe Bitter are the regulars, with one guest ale making for a great session. Really good pub food adds the icing to the cake. A charming country inn. Well recommended.

On 25th April 2012 - rating: 10
