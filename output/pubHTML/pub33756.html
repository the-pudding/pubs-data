Rosalinde Smith left this review about The Anne Arms

New owners took over this pub earlier in 2012 and they have done it proud in terms of food, service, and welcome! They hosted nearly 40 people in the conservatory who had been on a Treasure Hunt, single-handedly served us at the bar without fuss or fluster, and provided a superb Carvery. Some had other meals - Lamb Shank excellent!There is a good sized garden and great play area for children; all enclosed so is safe for kids likely to run onto a road. A really pleasant time - thank you!!
