Komakino . left this review about Chequers Inn

A pub / Indian restaurant which thankfully keeps the two separate, with what is more than a bar out front and a separate eating area out back. Like some of the pubs around here, some exposed stone, but also overloud MTV music from a large screen and real ale limited to Doom Bar x 2, albeit at £3 for a pint. Very friendly barmaid made the stay bearable, but still not a destination venue by any stretch.
