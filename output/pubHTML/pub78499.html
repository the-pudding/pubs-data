Steve C left this review about Tangerine Bar

I paid a fleeting visit to Tangerine Bar on Saturday evening when it was just starting to fill up with the younger inhabitants of the area. There are a couple of bars that both serve up standard and premium draught products alongside spirits of varying potency. There is a DJ booth and I imagine a late license every night. I decided to leave when a stag do turned up and started to sing along to some inane chart music.

Not a place I will be rushing back to, but I will mention that the staff were very friendly.

On 2nd October 2012 - rating: 4
