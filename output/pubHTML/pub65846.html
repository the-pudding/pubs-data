E TA left this review about Drift Bar

A small, cramped one-room former shop overshadowed by both Slug and Lettuce and Wetherspoons. Staff were pretty flippant, the décor is tired and the customers were mostly human flotsam. No character, no atmosphere, no real ale, no reason to drift back in.
