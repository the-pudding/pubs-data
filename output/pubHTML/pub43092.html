Alan Winfield left this review about Castle Inn

The Castle is a stone built pub about 5 mins walk from the middle of hillsborough.
This pub is quite smart and comfortable inside and was very busy as sheffield wednesday were at home and the fans in here seemed quite friendly,the one thing that let this pub down there was no real ale on the bar only John Smiths smooth crap so it was a Guinness in here.
I would happily come back to this pub for a few pints of Guinness but it would be a lot better if there was a real ale on the bar.
