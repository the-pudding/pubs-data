Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The John Gilbert

Large food-led Greene King pub, with a variety of seating and dining areas around the bar and outdoors. Bright modern interior. Very busy on a Saturday night, but with a 'curfew' for children at 21:00. Surprisingly good choice of real ales from ten handpumps, with many offerings from smaller local and regional breweries (e.g. Blakemere Hit & Run) in addition to the inevitable IPA and Old Speckled Hen.
