Danny O'Revey left this review about The Crane & Dragon

Now just called The Crane.

Large L-shaped room with high ceiling, this has an ok modern feel. Dark green walls and lots of comfy chairs, it looks looked after.

Seems to be themed food nights, and the emphasis appears to be on diners.

Overall an ok place, but marked down by me for having no real ale. Keg Tetley's Smooth is available
