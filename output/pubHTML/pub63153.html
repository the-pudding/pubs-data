Alan Winfield left this review about Buzz Bar

This pub used to be an Home Ales tied house with four rooms.
This pub was knocked about in the early 80s and it had one large lounge type room.
The pub closed down and reopened under the name of Buzz bar this pub does not have any real ale on and only opens in the evening.

Update: This pub has changed names yet again and is now Called Buzz Bar again,why not revert it to its original name of the Prince of Wales.
There are very restricted opening times of only 8 till 2am on Fridays and Saturdays.

E TA left this review about The Chameleon

Previously The Buzz Bar, and before that The Prince of Wales. It's a single bar pub with a gopping carpet, trying to be a nightclub and failing. The original building is impressive and occupies the corner of the high street and the main road out of town, but the current business isn't worthy of the edifice. Decor is awful, staff are indifferent and customers are mostly oxygen thieves. There is no real ale. I don't intend to return.
