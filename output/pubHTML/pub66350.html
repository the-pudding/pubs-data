E TA left this review about Wahaca

A subterranean bar/diner with a Mexican slant, though they do sell other meals. The décor and internal construction seem to be largely from recycled building materials and railway sleepers, and the overall feel is that it's very yellow. Staff were pleasant, but there was no real ale or in fact any reasonable beer at all. If over-priced tequila cocktails and trendy food is your thing, then this will suit you, but otherwise I'd avoid.

On 29th January 2018 - rating: 3
