Gill Smith left this review about Wellington Inn

Called into this 2018 Good Beer Guide pub yesterday It is a multi level pub from a sloping car park. There are 2 entrances from the car park, if you enter the top level there are a few steps but the lower level takes you into a lounge, but there are steps to get to the bar area from here. Dining areas to the rear of the pub. 3 cask ales available. Goffs Tournament, Gloucester Gold and Lakehouse Citrus, and we enjoyed our beers from Lakehouse.
