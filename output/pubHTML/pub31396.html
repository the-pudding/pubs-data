Gill Smith left this review about Red Lion

Busy roadside pub with seating on the front. Cannot remember what the ales were unfortunately as we were on our way back from a good session at Banbury beer festival and just wanted a meal. Excellent fish and chips enjoyed in the early evening sunshine outside. Atmosphere appeared to be good amongst the crowd outside who appeared to be pub regulars.
