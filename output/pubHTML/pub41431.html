Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Swan Hotel

A fine old hotel, dating from 1500 if the frontage is to be believed, with a sizeable three-part bar / dining area to one side at the back. Given a semi-modern makeover at some stage, which doesn't grate too much thankfully. Thai-influenced menu, and live music events advertised (although it appears all rather sedate at lunchtime). Three Greene King real ales on handpump: IPA, Old Speckled Hen and the 'house' Best Bitter (£3.80).

On 27th December 2015 - rating: 6
