Real Ale Ray left this review about Black Bull

A surprisingly early opening time for this rural pub at 11am. I would say this was due to its position right next to Etal castle, which does attract many visitors in the summer. The pub had a comprehensive lunch time menu on our visit. There was only one ale on the bar, which was Flying Scotsman from Caledonian. The novelty factor here was the collection of witches hanging over the bar. One of the locals decided to bring one back as a souvenir from a trip to Prague and hence started this trend. The pub has the main lounge and bar in the middle, a small snug on the left and a pool table on the right.
