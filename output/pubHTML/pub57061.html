Aqualung . left this review about The Sussex (JD Wetherspoon)

This is an old school JDW shop conversion consisting of a large low ceilinged room with the bar on the left and an eating area to the right.
The bar has two sets of five hand pumps with the JDW Trio Of Doom but the Doom Bore turned, Exe Valley Devon Glory doubled up, Daleside Old Legover, Hafod Moel Famau, Marston's Help For Heroes, Big Bog Bog Standard and Wychwood Jester Jack. I went for the Big Bog beer (£2.15) which was in excellent condition. The reasonable beer selection made up for the rather dull interior.
