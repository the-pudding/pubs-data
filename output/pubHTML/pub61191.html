Graham Coombs left this review about The Queens Head

Friendly unspoilt locals' pub, with one main room around the bar and a smaller lounge behind. Beers at time of visit included Doombar and Courage Best. Large beer garden at rear - small car park but ample parking on road.

On 3rd July 2014 - rating: 7
