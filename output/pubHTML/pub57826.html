darren howard left this review about White Hart Inn

Having just returned from another fantastic week in Taunton i decided to review some of the places we visited.

The White Hart Inn is in the gorgeous little village of Corfe, Nr Taunton in Somerset.

Its a beautiful little pub serving very tasty home made food. The menu is great, the servings are very generous and the owners are very frinedly and welcoming.

We've visited the White Hart a few times now when we stay in Taunton as its very close to the campsite we use. We've never been dissapointed and are happily working our way through the menu.

I'd absolutely recommend you visit the White Hart Inn and if theres anything you do when you visit you MUST try the home made lemon meringue pie!!! It'll blow your socks off! Its absolutely amazing.

On 14th May 2010 - rating: 8
