Alan Winfield left this review about The Bradgate

The Bradgate is a very large pub in an upmarket village.
Once inside the bar was facing and this was a very large room that ran across the front of the pub there was also another large seating area up a couple of steps to the rear of the pub,the pub was quite smart and comfortable and was geared up for people dining but as drinkers we were made just as welcome.
This pub is an Everards tied house and there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Everards Beacon bitter and this was a nice drink the other beers were Everards Tiger bitter and Original.
This pub was ok for a quick drink but it is more of a dining pub.

On 26th June 2011 - rating: 7
