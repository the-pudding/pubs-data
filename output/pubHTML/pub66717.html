Will Larter left this review about The Queen

An oddly shaped building, or rather a pair of buildings knocked into one, with changes of level for both customers and staff between the two main rooms served by the central bar. Fake beams hide whatever work was necessary to keep the ceiling from falling in during this remodelling, but the interior has got a charm of its own nevertheless. On the bar just two hand pumps serving Sharps Doom Bar and Kelham Island Easy Rider. The beer was OK, though nothing to write home about, and service was friendly, but I would struggle to talk myself into a return visit to Mosborough just for the pubs (and I'm not aware of any other attractions).

Date of visit: 3rd March 2014
