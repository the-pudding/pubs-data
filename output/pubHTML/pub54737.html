Blackthorn _ left this review about The Crown Inn

A fantastic, unspolit country pub in a lovely rural location. True it's a little tricky to find, but only a two minute drive from the A38 so worth checking out.

Beers on this occasion were - Hewish IPA, Batch Bitter, PG Steam, Butcombe, Bass, Palmers IPA, Gem, Tribute and Pitchfork. Also four ciders on tap, Thatcher's Traditional, Thatcher's Dry, Mendip Magic and a pear.
