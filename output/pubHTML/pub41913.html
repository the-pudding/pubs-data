Soup Dragon left this review about White Hart Hotel

A terraced hotel, restaurant and bar on the High St, the lower half is in Cotswold stone, the upper half is in a cream-ish rendering, with dormer windows. There is a patio area at the rear.

The interior I believe also had a dining room, but I only really saw the main bar and lounge/dining area, which was in cream, with green carpet, with general prints on the walls. There was a dart board and a TV (not on during visit) giving the hotel bar a little more pub feel than you may have expected. The rest of the room did have some comfy seating – looking a little more like I would have pictured a hotel bar. The service was fine and there was no other clientele.

Beer; tap stuff, with a couple of Arkells beers on handpull; the 3B was well kept and fair enough, but the Kingsdown Special was a decent pint.

Happy I popped in to try the Arkells, it was a brief visit. The Red Lion is the must visit place in Cricklade.
