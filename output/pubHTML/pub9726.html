Alan Winfield left this review about The West Point Tavern

The West Bay is up a narrow street and very close to the beach.
This pub offers accomodation,the room i went in was fairly small and had a nautical decor,this is a Palmers tied house and i had a drink of Palmers best bitter which was a decent drink there was also Palmers 200 and Dorset Gold.
This pub was fairly quiet when i was in here but it would get busier later with people dining.
