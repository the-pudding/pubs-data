Colin Walker left this review about Cornhill Inn

The Cornhill Inn and annex section called Hardies Bar has live sports on TV and at various time live acts. The place can be booked for functions. In the Inn there are 2 courses Tuesday to Friday only £7.95! Served 12:30 pm - 2:30 pm and 5 pm - 8 pm. Plus there is a Burger night every Tuesday only £8.95 per person! Which you can choose from a chicken burger or a beef burger this also includes a pint, a glass of wine, or a soft drink. Curry night every Wednesday only £8.95 per person! Which you can choose from chicken jafrezi, tikka, rogan josh or the pubs own curry with poppadoms, this also includes a choice of drink. Steak night every Thursday only £24.95 per couple! Again there is a choice from two 8oz rib eye steaks or two sirloin steaks served with chips or potatoes. The price includes a choice of drinks.
If this all seems too much, stick to the Hardies Bar section, which is very modern and relaxing with Belhaven Best on tap.
