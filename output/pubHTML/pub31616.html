Komakino . left this review about The Greyhound

A gastropub in the Henley surrounds owned by Anthony Worrall-Thompson, the marques in the car park give you an indication of the aimed-for clientele here, and the 'Crazy Bear'-style interior and low lighting will have something for Sunday Magazine readers to die for. On the ale front, there was Rebellion IPA along with Ringwood's Boondoggle and Razorback (the latter £2 a half) and the front garden is a decent escape from the inside pretention.

On 1st September 2016 - rating: 4
