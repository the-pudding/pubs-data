Soup Dragon left this review about The Pegasus

This is a detached and rather charmless looking 60s estate pub with a patio area. The rather square looking central building has extensions around and is mainly in undressed brick, with a little white render. The interior has a couple of rooms, but the larger was completely unoccupied at my visit and i didn't really see it as the lights were off. The smaller room is a bar room, with a pool table. It is in white, with a bit of wood and has several TVs around (not on), likely for the cricket as several flags were displayed on the walls for various nations.There was no music. The service was fine and the clientele, though small, was cosmopolitan, with everyone knowing each other. Beer; usual keg stuff, no real ale - i had a shandy. Nothing for the real ale fan, one for the locals - aptly named the Pegasus, as mine was a flying visit.
