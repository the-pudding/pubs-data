Old Peculiar left this review about Windmill

Not a 'real' pub as there's not a bar but it does have a pool room. Comfy lounge area with tables, sofas and bar stools plus a restaurant area (food good, but a little pricey). 3 handpulls, Tribute and Doombar being served, plus ciders and lager. Worth a visit if you're in the area.
