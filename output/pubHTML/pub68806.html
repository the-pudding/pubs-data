Graham Coombs left this review about New Inn

Excellent and well-kept old thatched inn, perhaps a little too dominated by food, however serving some good local beers and ciders in good order. A long room running beside the bar, with a daintily-furnished dining room to the side. Large and tidy beer garden at rear. Mind the low doors, especially if visiting the toilets!
