Alan Winfield left this review about The Crown Inn

This is a decent looking pub that still has two rooms.
When you enter this pub there is a small lounge to the left i dont think this has got a bar,if you go to the right there is a largish room that was probably being done up on my visit.
There was a couple of unused handpumps on the bar so it was a choice of three keg beers i had a drink of Stones Bitter there was also Worthington and John Smiths smooth crap on the bar.
The tennants were new to this pub so hopefully they will get real ale back on the bar.
