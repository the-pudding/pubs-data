Alan Winfield left this review about Mount Inn

The Mount Inn is in a really nice looking village and it is up the hill at the end of the village there is no through route here.
Ths pub is built with cotswold stone and looks quite nice from the outside.
Once inside the bar area was facing this had a stone floor and a large fire place that had been used recently,there was a lounge through an open doorway this was carpeted the seats near the windows had great views over the village and further this room was carpeted.
We had a drink in the bar as the lounge was full with diners,this is a Donnington tied house and there were two real ales on here i had a drink of Donnington SBA and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Donnington BB.
There was an outside drinking area that was fairly busy and this had the same great views as the lounge.
This was a really nice pub in a great location with great views and well worth a visit

On 16th May 2011 - rating: 10
