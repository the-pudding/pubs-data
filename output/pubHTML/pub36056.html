Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Marquis Of Granby

A bit of brewer's Tudor about the exterior, which could easily be mistaken for a large house if it were not for the open car parking area in front of the building. Inside, it comprises a long bar in two parts: a 'L'-shaped public bar with pool table, etc; and a semi-modern lounge bar extended into a conservatory. Substantial covered smoking area to rear. Six handpumps in total, but only Pride (£3.10) available.
