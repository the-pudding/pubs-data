Chris Sawle left this review about Cornewall Arms

Quite possibly the best pub ever. No jukebox, telly, any ambient sound.....chairs ranged along both walls, an original bar skittles takes pride of place. Open fires at both ends of this one-room pub. Excellent local cider. Come and drink like you were in the 1920s, share your day with other villagers in the quiet. Words fail me as to what a gem this pub is. Great, grassy beer garden adjacent to the medieval church with carved animals of various descriptions scattered about. Amazing.
