Alan Winfield left this review about The Swing Gate

The Swing Gate is a well kept pub that is in the Ecclehill area of Bradford.
The pub has two separate rooms,i had a drink in the room to the left which turned out to be a very comfortable lounge,this room is oblong shaped and part carpeted,the seating is very comfy bench type and small stools,plus a few normal tables and chairs,there was a TV to the front showing music videos.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Tetley Cask which went down very well after my long walk uphill in the rain,the other beer was Theakston best.
I found this to be a very pleasant and comfortable pub,one i could settle in for a good session.

Pub visited 12/9/2015
