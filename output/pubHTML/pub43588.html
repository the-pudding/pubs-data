Graham Coombs left this review about Lighthorseman

Slightly run-down pub with accommodation on the southern fringe of York in what would once have been an impressive Victorian building. The main bar room is very much for the locals, with a games room containing a pool table behind, a small dining room and a fairly basic outdoor area behind, functional enough but all having seen better days. Beers mainly from Thwaites were Lancaster Bomber, Wainwright and Lil Bewdy with a guest from Wadsworths.

Alex Conway left this review about Lighthorseman

Large Thwaits pub up from the sting of pubs near the barbican center. There are four hand pulls available with lancaster bomber and wainright being permanents adn on this occasion a Thwaits seasonal plus a guest o forgot. the wainright was in very good nick here but the price seems a Little steep at something around the £3.40/50 mark, my friends Coors Light was £3.80!
This pub was very busy with regulars as this is not really on the tourist trail and was very lively. I really enjoyed having a drink here and would gladly return as they sell good beer in a nice environment.
