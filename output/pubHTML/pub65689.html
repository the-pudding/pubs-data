ROB Camra left this review about Gardeners Arms

A very neat and well kept pub about 2 miles North of Hull city centre. Lots of buses pass nearby though on Beverley Road or Newland Avenue. The front room is nicely wood panelled and has a matchwood ceiling. The rear room is almost a separate sports bar with 5 pool tables. There's a decent sized patio at the front for when it's sunny, as it was during our visit. 7 handpumps serving Greene King IPA, Tetleys Bitter and 4 changing guests alongside a real cider. My pint was in very good nick. It had been about 4 years since my previous visits, but I'm pleased to say that it's still as good as ever this time around. Listed in the CAMRA GBG2015.

On 18th September 2014 - rating: 8
