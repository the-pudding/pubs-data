Graham Coombs left this review about Flanagans

Clean and light modern bar on the hill down to the pier, with a nice terrace overlooking the sea. mainly furnished with dining-type tables, but equally pleasant for drinking, and with several sports TV screens. A local ale in the form of Goddard's Starboard was sampled and was very drinkable.
