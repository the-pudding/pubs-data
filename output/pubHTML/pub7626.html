Tim Beer left this review about Hunters Lodge Inn

Fantastic wayside In for holiday makers with large easily accessed car park, children and dog friendly.
Real Ale is usually Otter Ale but they also carry a good varied range of Keg Beers and Lagers.
Wide ranging Menu from Butties to Steaks.
