Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Slow and Easy Hotel

Substantial roadhouse, comprising a 'L'-shape bar with various seating areas and a large side room where live music and other events are held. Accommodation. Large beer garden to one side. Greene King IPA and Directors (£2.75) available from the five handpumps.
