ROB Camra left this review about Molly's Yard

Bit of a misnomer this one. It claims it's a brewery & restaurant but College Green beers are brewed at Hilden brewery, so it's not a brewery at all. It is definitely a restaurant though as they wouldn't serve us a beer unless we bought at least a basket of bread. There's a yard area on the left as you enter and then a small dining area inside, also on the left. There are 2 handpulled beers on the bar but I don't know what these were like as we refused to buy the bread! This "pub" is in the GBG2011, it shouldn't be. It also shouldn't really be on here, but as I've added it, put a picture on and reviewed it, I'm not going to tell Conrad :-))
