Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Rose & Crown

Nothing like the old-fashioned boozer it used to be - the original layout has been opened out to create a 'U'-shaped bar. Also refurbished with modern furniture and decor by Baskerville Taverns since my previous visit nearly six years ago, and now more food-led than before. Just Harvey's (£3.90) from the three handpumps.
