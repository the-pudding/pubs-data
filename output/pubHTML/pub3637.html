Alan Winfield left this review about The Railway Inn

This pub as the name suggests is very close to Mobberley train station.This was an ex Greenalls house as can be seen on the pub signs.
There were four real ales on here i had a drink of Tetley bitter which was a decent drink there was also Greene king IPA, Greenalls bitter and Jennings Cumberland on the bar.
This was decent pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 13 October 2007.
