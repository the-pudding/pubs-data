hondo . left this review about The Royal Mile Tavern

Located centrally on the royal mile. A long narrow pub with quite a dark interior and a raised seating area at the rear. A mixture of brewery and Edinburgh memorabilia on the walls. 4 real ales plus keg beers and food served.
