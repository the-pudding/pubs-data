Alan Winfield left this review about White Lion

The White Lion is a really nice pub in a small village.
When we went in this pub it was a Shipstones tied house and there was a couple of their real ales on the bar we had a drink of Shipstones bitter and this was a very nice drink.
The pub was quite cosy and comfortable inside and we were happy having a few drinks in here.
