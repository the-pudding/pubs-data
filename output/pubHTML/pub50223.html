David Ross left this review about R&B's

This is a typical modern bar/diner, with most people eating on the Friday night I visited, although you can just have a drink. It's roomy, brightly lit and family friendly, but rather bland and unexceptional. Drinks were on the expensive side, with a pint of Guinness costing me £3.90. Also on draught were Hop House 13, Peroni, Tennent's and Heverlee lagers, Caledonia Best and Strongbow ordinary and Dark Fruit ciders.
