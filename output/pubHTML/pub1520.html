Quinno _ left this review about The Cock Inn

Suburban GK local. Interior has clearly had a going-over in the last few months but was well-done, tidy and retaining the traditional feel (such as the working wood-burning stove) without being one of those mocha-brown efforts. Pretty local clientèle, mainly the lager and sports type. One solitary ale, a highly impressive drop of GK IPA (yes, I know). Pool, darts and two TVs (rugby with sound on one, mute footy on the other). Not necessarily one to float the boat but certainly a good example of what can be done with a working mans local to bring it into the 21st century as a pub and not as a house.
