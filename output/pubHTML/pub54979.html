Oggwyn Great left this review about Tarrington Arms

Largish pub on the Ledbury , Hereford road , its a bit dull to look at but has been cheered up with hanging baskets , you enter through a pillared porch into a small hall , to the left is the main bar with a pool table and large TV , to the right is another smaller bar with pew type seating around the sides and a few sturdy looking tables and chairs in front of an old fireplace and large mirror , this room leads to a long room with old leather sofas at the front and a large modernish dining room to the rear . Thereis a large beer garden to the side of the pub . 3 hanpulls on Wye Valley Bitter and Butty Bach which was OK , service was friendly enough though it was very quiet on a Thursday Lunchtime , only an elderly couple eating and 3 teenage girls playing pool , The place is a bit of a mish mash of styles , i think its being gradually done out , OK if your in the area

On 22nd April 2010 - rating: 6
