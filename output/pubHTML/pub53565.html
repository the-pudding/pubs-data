Real Ale Ray left this review about George & Dragon

A handsome looking building that was purposely built for Clarkes Reddish Brewery back in the day. This is now a GK pub and I found the interior to be adequate, but nothing to write home about. It was quite featureless internally with the bar and lounge area being one large space, with a side room, where a group of teenagers were salivating watching one of their pals having a mixed grill. Five handpumps with disappointingly, only two on and it was Saturday evening. The choice was GK IPA or Robinsons Dizzy Blonde.

ROB Camra left this review about George & Dragon

This is a massive pub. It's all one broken out room but with lots of different ares. There's a pool table, dartboard and lots of TV screens. There's a small function room to the left as you enter. Futher to the left is an outside beer patio which is a real sun trap. 6 handpumps on the bar, but only GK IPA, Robinsons Trooper & a real cider available. The Trooper was in decent nick. Quite busy on my Friday, late afternoon visit. Lots of high vis jackets and a large group of painters having a loud, post work drink. This is a workingmans local in a high income area.

On 14th December 2015 - rating: 6
