Komakino . left this review about The Six Bells

A quaint Brakspear country pub situated on Warborough's green (a previous moniker was 'The Six Bells on the Green'), popular with the Midsomer Murders set. A gravelled patio out front, with small bar to the left, some bench seats and standard tables opposite and a dining area set off to the left, but partially hidden. Brakspear Bitter and something called Golden Duck on the pumps - the latter £4.10 and a super pint. Busy on a Tuesday evening and a collection of classic cars were assembled out front created a nice backdrop.

On 14th July 2017 - rating: 6
