Gill Smith left this review about Bridge Inn

Called in while doing a walk from Wheal Busy to Portreath as it is close to the Coast to Coast mining trail. If you are not into walking there is a car park, and the 43 bus runs there from Camborne, Redruth and Truro. There were a couple of real ales on and I selected the Coastal Poseidon which was a well kept ale.

lyppy lyppy left this review about Bridge Inn

The Bridge Inn is one of the best oubs in Cornwall. Its a basic, good old fashioned pub with proper pub grub - burger and chips, egg and chips, but also sells jacket potatoes and has a good specials board. Food prices are cheap, but the food is worth more. If you want poncy food with salad dressings then this isnt the pub for you!
