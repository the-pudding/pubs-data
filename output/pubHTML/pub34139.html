Alan Winfield left this review about The Ship

The Ship Inn is situated in the middle of Greasbrough and close to the half dozen or so shops that are close by.
Once inside there is a single square shaped room,the serving bar divides the two sides,the left side had a single bench seat to the front and a pool table,the right side had bench seating to the front with very little seating elsewhere.
There were no real ales on the bar,the keg option was John Smiths Extra Cold Crap which lived up to its name.
There was some decent background music playing quite loudly,the pub was also quite busy just after 12 on a Saturday dinner.
This pub was let down badly by its lack of any real ales.

Pub visited 22/3/2014
