Old Boots left this review about The Allenheads Inn

A very decent country pub. It has two bars plus a games room, the pumps are in the right hand room, four pulls but this time three working; all with blondes, one each from Mordue , North Blyth, and Great North Eastern Brew Co (no me neither). It's a fairly plain bar to the left, mainly being a dining room, but the room to the right has all manner of stuff hanging from ceiling mostly woodworking related. Apparently there used to be a lot more, so many that you couldn't see the ceiling. One corner has photos of aircraft carriers and naval aircraft. There's a pool table and darts board in the games room to the left, this one has fishing rods and skis decor. There is also free wifi which is very useful in this signal free countryside.
