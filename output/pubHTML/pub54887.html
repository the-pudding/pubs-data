ROB Camra left this review about Bulls Head

This is a multi roomed pub that could be quite smart. Sadly it isn't at the moment as there's tat all over the place. For example, there's a dartboard with two more on the floor, storage containers on the seating at 5 on a Friday, wires all over the place which doesn't look very safe. There are 2 handpumps on the bar, but only Doom Bar was on. I asked for a half, but could tell it was off from the smell even before the barmaid finished pulling it. They didn't have anymore available even though they had been delivered another 9 on the Wednesday as no-one had bothered to rack it. Total incompetence all the way. This won't be open much longer unless something changes.

Bulls Head left this review about Bulls Head

The Bulls Head is a Local Friendly Pub on Knowl Street in Stalybridge Where Everyone is Welcome. We Have a Wide Selection of Different Beverages on Offer. We are open 7 Days a week, We Open at 4pm on Weekdays and 12pm on Weekends & Are Open Till Late Every Night. We Have Two Rooms With Seating Areas and a Bar, Jukebox, Fruit Machine, Games Machine, Toilet Facilities & an Outdoor Area With Seatings areas and Cover the Sit Under.

We Have Many Events on Offer, Including Sky Sports in HD on 3 Flatscreen TV's and a Large Projector Screen for Fantastic Viewing & a Great Atmosphere. We Also Host a Monthly Open Mic Night on the FIRST THURSDAY of EVERY MONTH Which Have All Been Very Successful & Include Some Great Acts From Some of the Local Musicians, It's a Great Night So Come Down and Enjoy the Beer & Music. We Also Host Other Music Events Such as Irish/American Folk Nights, Different Bands From the Area, BBQ's, Psychic Nights and Many More.

To Keep Up to Date With the Latest Events Join Us on Facebook at http://www.facebook.com/TheBullsHeadStalybridge

We are a Friendly Pub With Friendly Staff and Everyone is Welcome

We Look Forward to Seeing You There.
