Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about 'Smiths' of Smithfield

Multi-storey venue, with the canteen-style main bar at ground-floor level, a (mostly bottled) 'craft beer bar' on floor 1, the dining room on floor 2 and a restaurant / terrace at the top. Fairly basic semi-modern furniture and industrial-look decor in the areas I explored. Trendy enough to be ridiculously pricey, though, with a Camden Hells from a dull keg-only selection coming in at £5.60...
