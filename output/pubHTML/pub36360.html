Andrew Kane left this review about The Badger

i've visited the badger many times and thought the food has been very good, however i had lunch today with my wife and two small children (12th sept 2010) and although my tradditional sunday roast dinner was very nice when we were given our dessert it was covered in balsamic vinegar, wouldn't be too bad if our dessert was a salad but mine was chocolate brownie and the kids had ice cream. we were given replacements but we weren't given an appology or refunded any money wich is the very least i'd of expected. apparently the guy who my wife took the food to just laughed and said the chef will be embarrased about it. not very proffesional! we probably will go back one day but wont be getting any desserts!

On 12th September 2010 - rating: 5
