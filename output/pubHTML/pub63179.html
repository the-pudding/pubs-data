Graham Coombs left this review about Outgate Inn

Unexciting-looking roadside inn, which is actually quiet pleasant. Inside there is one large room of three areas, reasonably comfortably furnished, with bar at end. Outside areas too. Robinsons beers selection in fine form. Food proved to be genuinely home cooked and very good. Note car park tucked away in turning opposite.
