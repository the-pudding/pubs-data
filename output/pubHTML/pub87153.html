Reviews of Beer No Evil (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

paulof horsham left this review about Beer No Evil

This is more craft beer off-licence with taps than small bar with off-sales. There's 4 beers on draught, plus a bunch of fridges (one featuring some some top-quality Belgian ales). Prices are as you would expect from the new wave of craft keg brewers.

While the owners are enthusiastic, friendly and helpful, and have clearly built up a following in the fairly short period of trading, I found I couldn't get on with the place at all. It felt a bit too hipster-trendy for my liking, but maybe Worthing's just changing faster than I am.
