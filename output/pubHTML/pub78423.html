ROB Camra left this review about Red Zebra Wine Bar

Tiny wine bar next door to the cathedral. There's only 1 draught beer which was Erdinger, but there's a massive selection of wines by the glass or bottle. There's a very small bar area and another small room to the right as you enter with 4 or 5 high tables, all candle powered of course. I believe there's also a cellar room and a Morrocan themed room, but we didn't visit those. We had a glass of red wine each which was good quality, although certainly not cheap. This was my penance for taking Ms CAMRA to several real ale pubs first. :-)) It's a pleasant enough place, but it wouldn't be my first choice in the area.

On 7th March 2013 - rating: 5
