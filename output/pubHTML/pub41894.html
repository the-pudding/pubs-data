E TA left this review about The Rose & Crown Hotel

A former hotel/inn with 2 large bars and a games room with a pool table, the Rose and Crown is at the top of a steep hill so is a welcome rest after the climb. The interior is mostly "rural" but is spoilt by an ugly fireplace which is wrong for the building. There were 2 ales on, both from Arkells. I had a pint of 3B which was drinkable but nothing special. I didn't try the food but it looked to be good value for money. This is a working village pub which is worth dropping into if passing, and I would include it in a country crawl, but I'm not sure I would go out of my way to spend an evening here.

On 29th September 2010 - rating: 6

IAN BERRY left this review about The Rose & Crown Hotel

Went there for Sunday lunch with a party of 11. The staff were very hospitable and we were not rushed. A very popular place but still didn't feel overcrowded. There was quite a choice from the menu and it was well cooked, plentiful and delicious. Highly recommended.
