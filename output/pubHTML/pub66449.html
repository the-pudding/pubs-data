John Ogbourne left this review about Palmer Flatt Hotel

The Palmer Flatt Hotel is a idyllic hotel with picturesque views. Our hotel site dates as far back as the Crusades, when it stood as a hospice, which was allegedly run by the monks from the Knights Templar. The Palmer Flatt Hotel is situated on the outskirts of Aysgarth village in Wensleydale. Our hotel provides a bed and breakfast service with 10 en-suite bedrooms all with colour TV, central heating and tea/coffee making facilities. We have a large restaurant that will seat over 60 people, which also provides a Sunday Carvery.
