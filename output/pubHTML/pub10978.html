Will Larter left this review about The Wick Inn

A fashionable bar on a street corner, which has presumably been a pub for a long time but felt like a recent conversion from other uses. I immediately felt out of place, as this is a fashionable place with loud music and prices to match: my beer here, at £2.30 a half, was the most expensive I had in two days in Brighton and environs. There are four hand pumps with Old Rosie cider, Sharps Doom Bar and two local beers. I went for the Gun Strong Pale Ale, which didn't make a lasting impression on me. It was lively at the time of my mid-evening visit, but I'm not sure it would have been better in the afternoon.

Date of visit: 22nd March 2018
