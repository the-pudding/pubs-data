Reviews of Kings Arms (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Gill Smith left this review about Kings Arms

Visited this thatched inn that is in the 2019 Good Beer Guide yesterday and found a very busy friendly local. There was a group of mummers in the pub and their play which was performed in the bar was hilarious. The men were all in costume, and the play itself was brilliantly funny. We enjoyed our Grainstore Ten Fifty. Proper locals pub.

On 2nd December 2018 - rating: 8
