Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Holly Bush Inn

Cosy little inn, almost indistinguishable from its neighbouring terraced houses. Square bar with a small dining room behind the fireplace. Accommodation. Two local beers on handpump - High House Farm Nel's Best and Tyne Bank Monument Bitter (£3.10). Restricted opening hours, so probably best to contact first if travelling from a distance. (NB - Review dates from July 2012.)
