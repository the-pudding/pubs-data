Tina Tricker left this review about The Blackbirds

Visited Blackbirds on Father's Day last Sunday 17th June 2012 at 14.00 after it was recommended by a friend who is a native and regular customer from Flitwick.
Had a great meal for 4 of us. Food was good and the bar/kitchen were very friendly and helpful.
I did book and glad I did. will hopefully visit again when in the area.
Mousey, Ipswich, Suffolk.
