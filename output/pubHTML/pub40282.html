Lindsay Webb left this review about Magnet Hotel

magnet hotel is a pub/b+b/food house which is situated on the main road to castleford near junction 32, magnet is a lovely pub and its true what they say 'once in drawn for life' the pub consists of a beautiful lounge which is very warm and welcoming even on those cold nights you can sit beside the open fire watching the flames roar as you try one of our guest ales or our range of lagers. even if you fancy a stretch of legs you can ave a game of pool watch football matches or even rugby in our sports bar with tvs in every angle and a fire in there too. maybe if your thiknig of having a party,wedding,funeral why not take a look at our function room which caters for everyones needs it seats 150 ppl for buffet 100 ppl for a sit down meal menus are always avaiable as we cater for every function that comes through with reasonable prices as well. or maybe you cant be bothered going home or have relatives that are travelling from afar why not pop in and ask about our b+b services. if ure hungry why not grab a bite to eat off our menu or even carvery which is served mon-sat 12-8 sun 11.30-7. we also do special nights with food. tues thurs pool night fri quiz sat entertainment
