Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Queens Head

Old inn, given a semi-modern makeover so less interesting inside than one might expect. Opened out at some stage to leave a 'L'-shaped bar with further tables on a narrow strip behind one of the original walls. Also has a rear patio area. Doom Bar, London Pride and Wadworth 6X (£3.80) available from three of four handpumps when I visited.
