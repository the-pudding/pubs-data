Soup Dragon left this review about The Old Court House

This is a detached Victorian pub, with patio area. Formerly called the Court House pub. It may have been the former Petty Sessions Court, but was a pub by 1880 at least. It is a cottage-styled pub with bay windows flanking the central entrance and is in white render, with some black paintwork. The interior is a U-shaped-ish room. It is in coffee, red and wood, with a white ceiling and a wood floor. There is a dart board and a pool table up one end. There are lots of TVs, but only one was being used for the very loud karaoke that was on. A few prints and Baggies memorabila are on the walls. The service was fine, the clientele mixed and i found, unfriendly. Beer; usual tap stuff with Banks's MILD. No real ale. I had a shandy. My verdict - not a great experience.
