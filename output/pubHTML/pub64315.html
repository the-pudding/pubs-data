Gill Smith left this review about Wildmoor Oak

Busy country pub that was our last port of call last night and it was packed as there was a party going on. We bought our Weatheroak St Udley real ale and made our way to the restaurant area as there was a little more standing room at the bar. It is a 2012 Good Beer Guide pub and the beer was fine. Looking at the meals coming into the restaurant, it is one I would love to go back to when it is not crammed full. There appeared to be a lovely garden and adverts were up for the themed nights. They do Caribbean nights and also comedy, quiz, chinese buffet and cheese and wine nights.
