Will Larter left this review about King George

Pleasant sub-urban pub which is busy on match days as it is only 100 metres from Altrincham's Moss Lane ground. At least, it's busy when my team Luton Town are playing here. There were three JW Lees beers on hand pump for my latest visit, which finished 1-0 to the Hatters.
