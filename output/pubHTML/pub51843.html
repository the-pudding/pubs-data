Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Alan Hurdle left this review about The Black Lion Hotel

A large comfortable food oriented pub close by the River Elwy. I was not expecting anything special, just average pub meal. I was totally surprised at the quality of every aspect of the meal from the starter, taste of the vegetables and quality of the sauces. It was, and still is, the best meal I have had in a year and a half in Wales.

On 15th September 2011 - rating: 9
