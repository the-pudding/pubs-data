ROB Camra left this review about The Kingston Hotel

A small, one roomer, roughly L shaped. Very much a locals pub, many of whom were watching the horse racing on the TV. Lots of B & W pictures of old Hull on the walls. There's also a quiz machine and a dartboard. 4 handpumps on the bar all from Marston's. The Help For Heroes was in decent nick though. A bit rough and ready is this one. I'll probably not bother again.

peter ashworth left this review about The Kingston Hotel

The pub looks quite large from the outside but on entry you find it is a small one roomed affair with bare floorboards and a limited amount of awkward looking high tables and chairs.Although there was 3 cask beers on and the landlady was friendly enough I cannot envisage a return in the near future.
