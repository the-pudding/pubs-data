Michael Woodley left this review about The Eagles Head

This is a great little country pub near the Grizdale national park you get a very warm from the Rob And Rebecca Bruce who are the owners there is always a log fire on for them cold winter days and nights which makes it very cosy. There is a great choice on the food menu which is local produce real ale on tap very good wine list which is not over priced same with the food which you get plenty of which is piping hot when you get it And I must highly recommend this establishment and dogs are very welcome has well.

On 13th November 2010 - rating: 10
