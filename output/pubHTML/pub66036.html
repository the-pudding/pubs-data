Alan Winfield left this review about The White Swan

The White Swan is a decent looking white painted pub that is situated in the middle of Spondon.
When i first went in this pub it was a Bass tied house witha decent drink of Bass on the bar,things went downhill over the years and the pub went keg only.
The pub now has real ales on the bar with Pedigree usually on plus a guest beer.
