ROB Camra left this review about Harewood Arms

No idea who Tamara Smithe is, do you Phil? And no I'm not the landlord, Phil is along with landlady Yvonne.

This is a very neat pub and there's a bit of a contrast between the outside and inside. The outside is a fairly standard brick built pub. The inside is all wooden beams and panelling with a flag floor. Lots of nooks and crannies with an eating area to the rear left. They also have a pool table, dartboard and a jukebox. Greenmill brewery is in the cellar which is run by Phil and his son Matt. That's the reason I was here, to brew a beer for Rochdale Beer Festival with them. Very successful it was too being the joint winner of Beer Of The Festival. Whilst brewing a beer there are a couple of times when all you can do is wait, so we waited in the bar. There are 6 handpumps for ale and a two more for real cider. All the beers on the bar were from their own brewery, I would have preferred to see a guest on there as well, but you can't have verything. The Greenmill Northern Lights was in very good nick and we tried several of the beers just to be sure. It's a very good pub and is now the only one left in Broadbottom. Long may it continue.
