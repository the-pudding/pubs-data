Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Smoking Goat

Very inconspicuous side-street cellar bar that is very easy to walk past (as I did first time). Once located, it is gratifying to see that they have avoided the Gothic look; instead, the furniture and decor is relatively light and modern (with a few twists such as the milk churns for bar stools). Two cask beers on handpump: Fyne Ales Jarl and the local Ayr Jolly Beggars (£3.50, but rather disappointing).

On 16th November 2017 - rating: 6
