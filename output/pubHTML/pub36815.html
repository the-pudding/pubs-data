Real Ale Ray left this review about The Briar Dene

Another well run Fitzgerald pub. This is a large pub with a very ornate interior. There's lots of seating in the main lounge split up by partitions, columns and archways. Also there is a separate bar area with pool table. Eight handpumps on our visit, I went for the Wylam Turbinia and Three Kings Brewery Tour de Force. Food served 11-3.
