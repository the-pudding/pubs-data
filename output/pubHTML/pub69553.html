Clive Thompson left this review about Carousel Lounge

A strange little place that has the air of a hotel lobby bar. Comfortable seating, art deco theme, low background music and a feeling that you should be talking in hushed tones. Five handpumps, four in use which were Eagle IPA Youngs Ordinary, Masterbrew and Cairngorn Trade Winds, decently priced at £2.80.
