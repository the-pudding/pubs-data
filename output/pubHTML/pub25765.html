Al Bundy left this review about B Lounge @ The Bridge

This place is now just called The Bridge. It a long narrow pub that is bigger than it looks from outside. Wooden floored throughout with the bar to your right as you enter. Very modern feel to it despite it trying to look older. 3 real ales on the bar with Hopback Summer Lightning, Wainwright's and Hobgob on. Has 2 Athenian helmets above the bar. Why? No idea.

ROB Camra left this review about B Lounge @ The Bridge

This pub is part of a chain of only 2 pubs, both in Manchester, the other is the Brunswick, hence the name. Sells Theakston Best and Old Peculiar plus Thwaites Bomber. The beer is usually OK but not first rate and often they only have one beer on. We call in here because it's a comfortable pub and the other half likes the wine. If you apply for a free B Lounge card you get 10% off at both pubs. There's a restaurant area at the rear. The whole place gets full of suits around 5 - 7. It's a pretty average place all round.
