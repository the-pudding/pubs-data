Oggwyn Great left this review about The Plough Inn

Rambling country pub , multi-roomed and traditional in feel , its layout is basically square with a central bar serving the different areas , a traditional tap room , snug , snug , sidebar and dining room , lots of wood and beams around .
Six handpulls with beers from Theakstons , Robinsons , Maltsmiths ? and Weetwood .
Busy on a Tuesday lunchtime , service was very friendly and the food looked good .
I liked it here worth a visit .
