Reviews of The Fuggle & Nugget (Average Rating: 7½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Will Larter left this review about The Fuggle & Nugget

A comfortable and relaxing micropub with no bar counter, beers being poured in the cold room and brought out to customers at their tables or in the absence of tables, to where they happen to be standing. Beers, ciders and keg offerings are listed on a blackboard, with real ales identified by their respective pump clips. It was quite busy here before the game, with Burton and Luton supporters mixing amicably. I liked this one much more than the Last Heretic near the station, and that's not just because we had yet to lose the match at the time of my visit here.

Real Ale Ray left this review about The Fuggle & Nugget

Some micros have a small bar with a few handpumps, other trends are to have a cold room where the beer is straight from the cask on stillage. This micro uses the latter way of serving, which in my opinion is ok, but not the best of methods. Firstly the beer always seems to be served flatter and if you're sitting near the cold room, the constant opening and closing of the cold room door, becomes irritating. Plus if the pub is busy, service does takes a lot longer, rather than three pulls on a handpump, which is far quicker.
The quality of the beer here was top notch, but served too cold. We went for the Great Heck Chopper, which was ok when it warmed up and we also tried Half Moon Brewery Mr Pickering. Three ales and four ciders to choose from on our visit.

On 19th January 2017 - rating: 7
