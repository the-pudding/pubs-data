Quinno _ left this review about White Hart Inn

A dash across the countryside at speed limit-bothering pace in order to catch this one before afternoon closing, after seeing it named Lincolnshire CAMRA pub of the year whilst perusing one of their magazines in the previous pub. Odd on approach – it really doesn't look much from the outside. Inside is a large place in an L shape with red carpet and trim, with wood panelling and a proper fire. Five ales on over two banks, with some left-field choices. We managed to grab very good halves of Rudgate Choco Vanilla and Yeovil Quad. As the only customers (shame), we had a few minutes to speak to the licensees who were nice people. I liked it here, shame we didn’t catch it at a busier time. You should make a diversion to try it. 8.5

TERRY DALES left this review about White Hart Inn

I visit this great little pub every couple of weeks or so.
Landlord Mick is a real ale enthusiast,usually 4 or so different ales available every week.all very well kept.
Good homemade food served in the comfortable lounge.
Real coal fire in the basic bar.
Pub is well worth a visit if you like your real ale,dont't ask for John Smith's.
