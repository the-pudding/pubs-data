Isabel Goodwin left this review about The Burlton Inn

Had a really enjoyable evening at the Burlton Inn last week, good food, good service and nice wine list. My lamb was fantastic whilst my partner had a more simple, but equally delicious fish and chips. The chocolate mousse we shared for dessert was gorgeously chocolate-y and not too heavy.
Thoroughly recommend this country pub, just a shame it was quite quiet!
