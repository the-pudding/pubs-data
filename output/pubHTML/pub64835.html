Komakino . left this review about Hanwell Arms

A NuMarstons set-up in the confines of a newish housing estate on the northern fringe of Banbury, there's little to set the world alight here, as we've all seen the generic interior, food-led emphasis, Marstons portfolio (Pedigree, Burton Bitter, Hobgoblin, Hobgoblin Gold and Jennings Queen Bee) and heard the bland Maroon 5-esque background muzak time after time. Unless you feel the need to 'tick' this one off a list, I wouldn't bother.

On 20th April 2016 - rating: 3
