Steve of N21 left this review about Swan Hotel

The bar of this four star ivy clad hotel with its prominent position in the cotswold tourist trap village of Bibury is open to non residents . The Swan bar is modern and comfortable but is what it is and therefore has all the ambience of an airport waiting lounge. Usual keg offerings from the small bar which did have two hand pumps although only one was in operation dispensing a just about passable Deuchars IPA. Probably convenient if you are staying here but fortunately the Catherine Wheel at the top of the hill provides a much better pub experience.

On 21st June 2019 - rating: 5
