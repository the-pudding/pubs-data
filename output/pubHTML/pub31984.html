Komakino . left this review about The Blenheim Buttery Inn

Somebody must have got excited and clamoured to add this to the site as it formerly had the word 'Inn' in its name. This is actually, in essence, a cafe / tea room which is licensed to serve alcohol - so with no handpumps or keg taps, you can have a bottle of Becks, Kronenbourg or San Miguel. I didn't bother.
