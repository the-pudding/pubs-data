David Ross left this review about Ingrams Bar

Ingrams is unusual in still having a separate bar and lounge, the latter on a slightly higher level than the former. As the bar was tiny and crowded, I had a pint of McEwan's 80 Shilling, costing £3.20, in the quieter lounge. Guinness, Belhaven Best, Tartan Special, Tennent's lager and Strongbow cider were also available on draught. The lounge is only marginally less basic than the bar and, overall, this is a fairly ordinary pub with little to commend it.
