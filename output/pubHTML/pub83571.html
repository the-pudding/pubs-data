Gill Smith left this review about Barbridge

We quite liked this bar with it's background music playing from a juke box. 4 real ales from Kinver, Marble, Green Duck and Slaters. 4 Key keg beers and we chose Beatnikz Kentucky Stout which was not too bad once the extreme cold wore off. Cider drinkers will love this place for the selection of 6 available. Variety of seating with sofas, cinema seats and high chairs round beer barrels.

Aqualung . left this review about Barbridge

This one isn't a proper micropub as it had music playing and does some mainstream keg products. However, it does have a glass cabinet that I assume is chilled and will hold at least eight casks. It's a roughly square room with a red tiled floor and mock graffiti.
There was a Green Duck tap takeover about to happen on my visit so the choice was from Duck & Dive, Madness, Duck Under and Sitting Duck. I went for the Duck Under (£3.00) which was rather warm and possibly not ready..
I thought this place was OK but wonder if there's a question mark over beer quality especially as it's GBG listed.

Danny O'Revey left this review about Barbridge

Shop conversion with urban gritty theme, graffiti, music pictures etc. Couple of barrel tables in the middle of the bar. There is some high seating to the front window, though the view is only of the adjoining concrete multi-storey. Nice, different, but the beer quality was only ok.

4 real ales
