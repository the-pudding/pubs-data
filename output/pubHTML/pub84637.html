Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Jerusalem

Cellar bar and kitchen that I assumed was new as I had never noticed before but which has apparently been open for nearly 20 years. The dark, rectangular bar has the counter and kitchen along one side, facing which is a slightly raised 'L'-shaped dining area which also extends across the back. The main seating area between these is full of basic wooden tables in a mixture of sizes (some quite long). Also has a rear patio back up at ground level via some rear steps. No real ale, unfortunately, so I opted for a keg Caledonian Coast to Coast pale ale (£4.70).
