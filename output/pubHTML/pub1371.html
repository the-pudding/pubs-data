Quinno _ left this review about The Dukes Head

Greene King pub, staple shaped interior with little internal stylings of note bar a pool table. Much more earthy than the other two Sunningdale pubs. Three ales on, GK IPA, GK St Edmunds and Black Wolf IPA (pretty naff). I wouldn’t feel the need to revisit.
