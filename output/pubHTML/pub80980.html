Gill Smith left this review about The Queens Head

Really enjoyed our visit to this micropub which features in the 2019 Good Beer Guide. Staff and locals friendly, and with no TV conversation rules ok. Ales on offer were Zulu Alpha Evolution, Hopcraft Goldings Balls, Hopcraft/Waen Pamplemoose, Grey Trees Azacca APA, Evans Evans CWRW, and TWT Lol Tuti Fruiti. There was a massive blackboard on the wall with the beer list, and a brilliant map of Wales with the location of the breweries being served marked up. Great place.

On 11th March 2019 - rating: 8
