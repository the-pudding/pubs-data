Real Ale Ray left this review about The Original Oak

A promising looking pub on approach after just getting off the bus from Leeds. The interior definitely lived up to it's name with original oak panelling to be seen everywhere. I also especially liked the quarry tiled entrance, which was finished in three colours cream terracotta and black tiles. The pub also had some wallpapered walls, which was traditional and suited the interior of the pub. I noticed a decent looking room to the right of entrance which looked comfortable along with the fireplace. The central bar served both rooms and the lounge side had an elaborate looking brass handrail along the perimeter of the bar front, close to the counter top. This pub unfortunately lost a couple of points, due to the ale choice, which on our visit was Doombar and GK IPA.

On 6th November 2017 - rating: 6

Alan Winfield left this review about The Original Oak

The Original Oak is a decent looking stone built pub.
The front door was not open so i had to pass the doormen to the left.
Once inside there is a large single room which extends to the rear,there is also a small bar room to the front left which has comfy bench seating and chairs,the main room is bare boarded and has some high bench seating.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Leeds Best which went down well,the other beer was GK IPA.
Another pub that was packed on my late Saturday afternoon visit.

Pub visited 16/7/2016

david hammill left this review about The Original Oak

Made my way here for a beer and a burger before the rugby. When we arrived we discovered half the pub was shut.on entering the bar that was open the queue for service was beyond a joke so we walked out again and entered a nearby pub where we got served virtually straight away. Surely the management of this venue must have been aware that a local derby rugby match was scheduled to take place on a hot sunny summer's day resulting in a vast number of people being in the area. A missed chance for a big financial kill on their part!
