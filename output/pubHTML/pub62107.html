Aqualung . left this review about Snowdonia Parc

This pub is set on the main road just South of Waunfawr but the Welsh Highland Railway station is at the bottom of the garden.
It's quite a big pub and inside there is a room to the left that includes a pool table followed by the main bar area which also has a room off it with pool and darts. As I was about to leave I noticed there is a further room with more hand pumps behind the bar. It was quiet on an October Wednesday afternoon.
This pub brews their own beer (Snowdonia) and this is the only place I've seen them. There were three available on my visit, Cwrw Gwyrfai, Gold and Carmen Sutra all priced at £3.05 and all in good nick. They are old school beers that are fairly modest but good value for the area.
Although this pub is fairly unexceptional in that it's largely modern and open plan it's worth a look for their ales.
