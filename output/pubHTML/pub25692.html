Al Bundy left this review about Hennigan's Sports Bar

Classing itself as a 'sports bar' this is a small bar that looks uninviting but was quite friendly on this midweek afternoon visit. Pictures of sporting people compete for wall space with televisions. A staicase to the right of the bar goes somewhere but I dont know if its for toikets. No real ale.

ROB Camra left this review about Hennigan's Sports Bar

A small narrow bar that goes back quite a bit further than you might think from the outside. The bar is down the left hand side and the walls are covered in TV's including one behind the bar. It's a keg only pub now and I had to have a half of something fizzy. Friendly enough in a boisterous kind of way. I'll not be bothering with this one again.

Alan Winfield left this review about Hennigan's Sports Bar

When i went in this bar it had'nt been open that long,the bar is situated in the main shopping area of Levenshulme.
Once inside the pub was fairly small and i had a drink in a room at the front i am not sure if there were any other rooms further back.
I was surprised to see one handpump on the bar which had Boddingtons bitter on this was a fairly decent drink.
Not really my sort of place but it had to be done because it was there.
