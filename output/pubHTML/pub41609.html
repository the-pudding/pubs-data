Jonathan Wilde left this review about The White Hart

A pub that proudly promotes no food and after a chat with the landlord after getting spotted taking a photo it is a pub that bucks the trend and is thriving, building trade on being what a pub should be the focal point of a village giving people a place to relax and enjoy.

After the landlord let me in for a view of the place I will return and update my review on what it's like when actually open but it's one I'm looking forward to.

It is an evening opener so plan your visit to begin post 5 o'clock.
