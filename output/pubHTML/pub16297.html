Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Chequers Inn

One of my favourite pubs - the sort of place every village should have. Very attractive white weatherboard facade, with plenty of flowers and some patio seating under umbrellas. Very traditional interior, with a rather cramped bar counter but usually well staffed. Decent sized beer garden, and the small oast to one side is sometimes used to house stillages for beer festivals. Other events throughout the year. Three of four handpumps were working on my last visit, with Adnams Southwold, Wadworth Farmers Glory and Dark Horse Birdie (£3.60) available. Overall, well working seeking out.

On 1st October 2012 - rating: 9
