Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Old Courthouse (JD Wetherspoon)

Housed in fine old building in an imposing position looking down towards the River Bann bridge and the town centre on the far bank. A particularly grand space is found just inside the front entrance, with various seating areas on two levels beyond. Nothing very local beer-wise, but you won't find anywhere else between Belfast and Londonderry offering five real ales.
