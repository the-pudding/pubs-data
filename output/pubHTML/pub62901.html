ROB Camra left this review about Time Zone

Long, thin bar with a DJ rig at one end and a pool table at the other. Lots of disco lights on the ceiling and it advertises itself as a late bar. It's also open from 9 in the morning, which makes for a very long day. There are lots of Man Utd & England flags all around the bar, but apart from that it's pretty featureless. The barmaid was friendly, that at least was a saving grace. It's keg only of course and the 6 or so punters who were in were in their late 50's and early 60's and of the ffffing every other word type even though the barmaids 12 year old was in the pub. I'll not be calling here again.

On 2nd July 2014 - rating: 3
