Komakino . left this review about Dog House

Just off the A420 Oxford-Swindon road, this is one of GK's more upmarket eating houses, as befits a building of this stature at least. Some nice woody touches here and there and a bar with accompanying bar area for the solo drinker. Four ale line-up comprised GK IPA, Old Speckled Hen, Abbot Ale and H&H Rockin' Rudolph (it was approaching that time of year...)
