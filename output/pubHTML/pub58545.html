John Horton left this review about The New Inn

So impressed by this place that all I can remember about it is the loo - and I was sober. My companions remember a little more though, so here goes. Large rambling 18th century coaching house - comfortable. Copper Dragon real ale - average. Food - they had some but menu ordinary. The crisps were good though. Village is said to be the starting point of the best route up Ingleborough - passes Gaping Gill on the way to the summit.

On 11th July 2008 - rating: 4
