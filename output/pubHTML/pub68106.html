Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Oggwyn Great left this review about Grove Inn

Very dining led pub , the main entrance from the carpark is around the back , the first thing you notice is two large fridges full of heart attack inducing cakes , the pub is an open plan L shape with the shorter part of the L being the bar area , all tables are set for dining .
Outside is a large beer garden partially decked .
Usual keg taps with 3 handpulls , 2 doubled up with Robinsons Dizzy Blonde the other with a decent Salopian Shropshire Gold .
