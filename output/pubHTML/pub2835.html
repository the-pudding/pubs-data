Alan Winfield left this review about Eaton Oak

The Eton Oak is a decent looking pub that has lots of ivy or something similar growing up its walls.
Once inside there is a large square shaped room with the bar on the right side,there is a slight raised area to the front right,the seating is normal tables and chiars,the rear area of the room is carpeted and the tables had knifes and forks on them,so probably more of a dining area.
The pub is a Charles Wells tied house,there were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Eagle IPA which went down well,the other beer was Youngs Special.
This was a decent enough pub to end our crawl of St Neots and area,it is also very handy for the A1.
Sadly the pub was almost empty on our Saturday afternoon visit.

Pub visited 4/2/2016
