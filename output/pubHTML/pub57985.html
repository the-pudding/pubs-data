Phantom Pigeon left this review about The Ben Jonson

Unfortunately, there were not a lot of customers, (Sunday lunchtime!) which, bearing in mind some of the reviews on other sites, I found this quite surprising. In turn, this meant that there was a lack of atmosphere, and the two bar/waiting staff seemed to need to take charisma pills, which didn't help.
The food was good. Lovely piece of roast beef, but the fish cake was more cake than fish. However, the Village Idiot was on fine form.
A return visit? The Ben Jonson would not be my first choice.

On 21st November 2011 - rating: 6
