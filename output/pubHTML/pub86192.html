Komakino . left this review about Miller and Carter

Steakhouse chain serving the newish Aylesbury Vale development heading out of Aylesbury towards Waddesdon. This has a standalone bar area to the right on entry, but no real ale was spotted - three lagers (Estrella Damm, Peroni and Pravha) were the only beers I could see.
