Alan Winfield left this review about Malt N Hops

The Malt N Hops is a small pub that is on the busy king street.
Once inside the pub had a single room that was quite nice,when i first visited this pub it was a Burtonwood tied house and there were two real ales on the bar,with both Burtonwood bitter and mild being nice drinks.
The pub is now a free house and it stocks micro brewery beers,the landlady in Darcy's said the pub is well worth a visit if you like real ales.
