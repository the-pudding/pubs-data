Quinno _ left this review about Alto Lounge

The ale has gone from here, as appears to be the case in most of the Loungers outlets.

July 2012
Modern cafe bar style thing designed for the Caversham Yummy Mummy and her latte-drinking friends during the day and the young professionals during the evening. To be fair, it's pretty good for what it is. Serves up a determinedly middling Bristol Beer Factory Toga Man on handpull, with Pipe Smoker Stout on keg. The food isn't too bad.
