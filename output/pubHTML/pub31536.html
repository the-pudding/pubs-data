Komakino . left this review about Red Lion

At one time three cottages, now knocked through this is the most characterful of the remaining pubs in Chinnor with its low ceilings and beams, original quarry stone tiles, tiny bar, separate, small Public Bar room and large brick inglenook fireplace in a former dining area at the rear. The best choice of ale in the village with Wychwood Hobgoblin, Loddon Ferryman's Gold and Rebellion Humbug on this visit (the three ales rotate). The size of the bar, means it can seem rather crowded if there's more than five in, and a large TV screen above another fireplace, mid-pub (showing Sky Sports Soccer Saturday on my visit) can detract from the ambience.

On 19th December 2017 - rating: 6
