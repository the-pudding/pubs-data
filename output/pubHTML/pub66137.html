Graham Coombs left this review about Lion Inn

Quite excellent old stone pub in picturesque village with some good ales and ciders on offer but mainly noted for its deservedly popular food. A fairly compact bar serves two areas, with that to the left being more of a restaurant. various special events, including ale and cider festivals. Cask Marque listed.
