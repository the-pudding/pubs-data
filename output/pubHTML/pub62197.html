Quinno _ left this review about The Black Lion Inn

A nice rural stop-off offering a lovely view over the valley and a warm cosy interior. Mainly frequented for meals, it was fully booked when we rocked up for a pint so it must be pretty good as far as nosh goes. One real ale on tap (the brand I have forgotten) which had an irritating metallic taste. Worth a stop to take in the view and I imagine the warm snug interior would be most appealing on a cold winters night. Loses marks for poor ale quality sadly.

On 6th July 2006 - rating: 6
