Will Larter left this review about The Victoria Inn

An old building in local stone in this quiet village - full name Kirkby in Malhamdale - with the date 1840 above the door, and an ancient sundial above that. Once inside it's a multiroomed pub with the bar in the room to the left, and the room to the right featuring a pool table and Sky Sports on tv.

On the bar are six hand pumps (see photos) with three from Timothy Taylor, two from Westgate Brewery (this is Clarks of Wakefield rather than Greene King of Bury St Edmunds) and also one from Naylors. My half of Boltmaker was in good condition but the pub lacked atmosphere, probably because I caught it at a quiet time of day.

On 19th August 2017 - rating: 6
