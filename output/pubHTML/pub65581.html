Real Ale Ray left this review about The Vaults

The landlady was very welcoming on our visit and their beer was in good condition. There were four ales on handpump, TT Landlord, Purity UBU, Salopian Shropshire Gold and Salopian Lemon Dream. I went for the Purity which was one of the best pints of UBU in a long time, so obviously they know how to look after cask ale. The only downside for me was the lack of natural light on such a sunny day. The interior reminded me of a Working Man's club, or something along those lines.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Vaults

Unpromising-looking venue that looks closed until you see the times on the 'Unlocked' sign on the door. On closer inspection, the façade has been repainted fairly recently, and the interior is tidy enough too. Mix of semi-traditional furniture in the various small seating areas, with decor including some stuffed fish in trophy display units. Six handpumps on the counter, dispensing Landlord, Sharp's Atlantic, Purity Pure UBU, Salopian Shropshire Gold, Butty Bach and Old Rosie cider.
