Steve of N21 left this review about Grove Hotel

This is another hotel in St Davids that has opened itself up to the public to take full advantage of the visiting crowds. And the Grove is fairly well located to do this being dead opposite the plush information centre and just down from the main car park where the tourist coaches park.
Externally it’s a decent looking old stone building that now has a large conservatory dining room bolted onto the side.
The original main building houses a fairly comfortable lounge seating section with a fair amount of leather sofa seating and then the large conservatory section to the right is set for dining.
There is a decent food menu, although we didn’t eat and being a Brains house there were three hand pumps including Brains SA and Reverend James and a third one that was off on our visit.
Comfortable enough place for a pint and the menu looked good and reasonable if you intend to eat.
But if all your after is a pint, I’d still recommend the more traditional pubs further down the street by the Cross.

On 22nd August 2010 - rating: 6
