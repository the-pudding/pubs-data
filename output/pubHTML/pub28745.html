Real Ale Ray left this review about Robin Hood Tavern

This GK house was the first watering hole we encountered when walking down from the station. This traditional pub had friendly staff and a good atmosphere. The pub consists of a bar area as you enter and an L shaped lounge. It also has low ceilings and plenty of bric-a-brac dotted around the walls. All the usual GK ales on offer and also serves food.
