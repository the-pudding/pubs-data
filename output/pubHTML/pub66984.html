Graham Coombs left this review about Dragonfly

Modern purpose-build eating house on edge of industrial estate, in Vintage Inns group. Actually not bad of its type - the food was well above average and it seems to have changing guest ales (Cask Marque listed) as well as particularly helpful staff. Also has quite a nice outside area, albeit a bit close to main road. A reasonable place to eat and quite tolerable for a drink.

Pete Snelling left this review about Dragon Fly

The Dragonfly is well known in Newport and across Gwent as a good country pub. we are easily accessible from Newport and Cardiff. There is always a warm welcome with roaring log fires, freshly prepared food, a good selection of real ales and a wine menu for you to choose from.
