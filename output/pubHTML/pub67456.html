Alan Winfield left this review about The Fair Maid

The Fair Maid is a largish pub at the far end of the main shopping street in Cottingham.
This pub was smart inside and it was also quite busy,there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Bombadier which was ok but nothing special the other beer was Courage Directors.
