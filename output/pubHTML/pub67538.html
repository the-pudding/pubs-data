Alan Winfield left this review about Lockoford Inn

The Locoford is a decent looking white painted pub that is situated in the North Chesterfield area.
Once inside there was a large square shaped lounge,this room had mainly normal tables and chairs,but there was a bench seat and a pew type seat,there was a small area to the right which had a food servery in it,unused on my Saturday afternoon visit.
There was a small seperate bar to the left which had a pool table in it,there was a TV on showing Sky Sports football score updates.
There was only one real ale on the bar which was Courage best,this was a decent enough drink.
This is a pleasant enough pub,i was happy enough having a drink here.

Pub visited 29/3/2014
