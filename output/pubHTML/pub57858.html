Jonathan Wilde left this review about The Bowlplex

Further to all the below negativity this place is now a Hollywood Bowl with an emphasis on bar and dinner on the signage, I assume due to its proximity to the cinema next door and trying to tap into the pre film clientele for food and drink.

As referenced it won't attract real ale drinkers and probably shouldn't be listed but there is a definite separation between bowling and a bar in the building especially given the multiple pool tables are within this area I suspect to detract from having unsupervised striking kids ripping the cloth off them.

On my Friday afternoon visit there were a fair few people drinking and not wearing clown shoes so further suspect it attracts normal people wanting a drink or food prior to watching a the latest films.
