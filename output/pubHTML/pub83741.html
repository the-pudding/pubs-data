Real Ale Ray left this review about Gallagher's Ale House

Sister pub to the Beer Engine in Skipton. This micro pub is a lot bigger than their first outlet in Skipton, and comprises of two areas, the main bar with glass fronted cellar at the rear and a front seated area as you enter. Five handpumps with five ales, which were Oakham Citra and Oakham Scarlet Macaw, Hambleton Stallion, Dark Star APA and Titanic Plum Porter. I went for the Oakham Citra which was in good shape. There was also a choice of four ciders, at £3.60 a pint. To get here, we got the Keighley bus from Skipton bus station, which only took 15 mins.

On 8th November 2017 - rating: 7
