Alan Winfield left this review about The Ram

The Ram is a large pub that was an Home Ales tied house when i did my first few visits to it.
Once inside the pub has had a massive makeover and now as a more contempary feel and look,there is a wide area to the front which has comfy bench seating and chairs,the bar is situated midway down the long room on the left side,the floor is bare boarded,as the room goes further to the rear it narrows,there is a separate room behind the bar to the right,there was a wedding reception taking place in this room.
There was good selection of real ales on the bar,we had a drink of Cathedral Heights Churchills Pride which went down well,the other beers noted were Leatherbrithers ?,Newark BLH4,Flipside Copper Penny,London Pride and GK IPA,there was also some real Ciders on the bar.
Background music was playing and the pub was very busy on our late Saturday afternoon visit.
Not really my type of pub but the beer was nice.

Pub first visited as an Home Ales tied house 20/3/1983
