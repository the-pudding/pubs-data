Will Larter left this review about Dig Brew Co

A basic brewery tap that opens towards the end of each week, so I was lucky to find it open additionally on a Wednesday for the World Cup semi-final between England and Croatia. There's a bar with six hand pumps and they also do key keg beers, by the evidence of their rubbish bin when I was passing earlier. My half of Dig Brew Moby IPA 6% rounded off a day of beers that were all just a little bit stronger than I would have preferred, and with this being the 12th pub of the day I cannot recall much about this one, though my notebook tells me it cost £2.35.

Date of visit: 11th July 2018
