Alan Winfield left this review about The Station

The Station is has the name implies opposite Ilkley train station.
Once inside there is a single oblong shaped room which runs to the rear,the room is carpeted and the seating is comfy bench type.
There are three TVs all were showing football.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Leeds Pale which was a nice drink,the other beer was John Smiths Cask.
I thought this was a decent enough pub to have a drink in before getting the train back to Leeds.

Pub visited 7/11/2015

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Station

Basic town pub opposite the railway terminus, focusing on TV sport and live music. Plain furniture and limited decor. Could have done without competing TV screens and jukebox, but friendly enough though. Two of four handpumps in operation, offering Tetley's Cask and Leeds Pale (£3.15). Selection of filled rolls for sale.
