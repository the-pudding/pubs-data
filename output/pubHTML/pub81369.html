Colin Walker left this review about Chisholm's Bar

This is the former D.J's pub. Very tidy interior, but felt a little cramped due to the single bar with 2 serving areas. A pool tournament was ongoing when I visited so the atmosphere was quite subdued until a game was over then the talking started. The pub does sandwiches during the day but not meals, still very pleasant enough. The barmaid was very helpful about information about the pub and the ongoing of the pool tournament.
