Reviews of Alphabet Brewing Co Taproom (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Rob Hunter left this review about Alphabet Brewing Co Taproom

The brewery tap for the Alphabet Brewing Co is open on Fridays from 5pm to 10pm and Saturdays from midday to 10pm.

Visited on Saturday night and the place was fairly busy considering its location and the weather conditions. No cask ale but there were at least 10 of their own keg beers available. Tasters were offered (and graciously accepted). I had the Flat White (Stout) which I had been wanting to try for a while and this was rather enjoyable. My drinking accomplice had the Juice Willis which was also decent. I could have stayed in here longer but we had other places we wanted to visit so we moved on.

Cans and bottles are also available, with a discount (20 or 25%) for taking away.

Although it is a bit of a grim walk when it's dark and raining, there are other breweries in the area so you can make a bit of a crawl of it.

This isn't the kind of place that would suit the traditionalists but I definitely enjoyed my trip and will be back.

On 30th September 2019 - rating: 8
