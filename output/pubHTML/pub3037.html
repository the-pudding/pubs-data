Richard Nicholson left this review about The Fox Inn

A good local pub, friendly staff and locals. It serves 2 real ales to sample, Oakham Ales JHB and Woodforde's Wherry, both were good pints. Pub exterior not exciting but nice and tidy inside, well worth another visit to try the food and enjoy the beer.
