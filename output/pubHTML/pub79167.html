Clive Thompson left this review about The English Restaurant

I used to have the occasional 'business meeting' here when I worked nearby, especially after the Gun across the road closed. Despite its name it's just as much a bar as a restaurant, with tables for eating on the right as you enter and more traditional pub seating on the left. There are a couple of handpumps with beers from local smaller breweries. A bit pricey, and plays to the tourists at Spitalfields Market opposite.
