Real Ale Ray left this review about The Adelphi

This is a large student pub situated in a prime spot, on the Adelphi Quarter. There were lots of advertisements on boards outside promoting shots and cheap drinks. Thursday night from 8pm is known as quids night were you can get a drink for a £1. A pint of Carlsberg or Strongbow was promoted at £1.25.
The pub consisted of one large room, with a long bar along the back wall. There were various different seating areas and the most noticeable was the six half moon shaped red leather booths towards the middle. Quite a few students taking advantage of the cheap meals on our mid afternoon visit. One ale on handpump Ringwood Young Scrumper.
