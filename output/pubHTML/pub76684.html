Reviews of John Millington (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Real Ale Ray left this review about John Millington

A very impressive looking Hyde's establishment that was once Millington Hall. This multi roomed pub had a lot of interesting features such as the original sandstone window openings along with the leaded glass windows. Original black oak ceiling beams and tastefully wallpapered areas also added the historical feeling when visiting this pub. Five handpumps with four in use, three of which were from Hyde's and the guest was Wainwright. Prices in here were reasonable and their Hyde's beers were in excellent condition.

On 22nd October 2018 - rating: 7
