Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Lochlands Bar

This side-street tavern doesn't stand out as you approach, but it turns out to be a decent, old-fashioned (in a good way) two-room establishment. The main corner bar has a very traditional dark-wood interior, with mostly sports-related decoration. Thankfully, it is also one of the few real ale outlets in town, with Wainwright and Inveralmond Ossian (£3.40) on the two handpumps when I visited.
