Graham Coombs left this review about The Gardeners Arms

Slightly contrived rebuilding of a country road pub, aiming squarely at the wedding and function market. Lots of beams, stone floor, dark wood etc. Not unpleasant, but lacking in atmosphere. At time of visit was offering Deuchars IPA (reasonable condition) plus another pump not in use. Has separate function suite, accommodation and also a large garden for children.
