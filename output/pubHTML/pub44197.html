Quinno _ left this review about Talbot Hotel

A pub with a beautiful stone wall interior - some real character. Had a number of real ales on when I went, but I didn't think much of them myself - the Double Dragon tasted tinny and the Champflower from stillage was foul; I assumed it was the landlords home brew (it wasn't), it came complete with strange mushroom slither in the glass. I want to like the place...but the beer needs to be much better than this. Potential though.

On 28th August 2006 - rating: 5
