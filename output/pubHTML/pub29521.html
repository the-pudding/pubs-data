ROB Camra left this review about The Hole In The Wall

After a number of years in the doldrums, with a landlady who couldn't be bothered, especially with cleaning, I'm pleased to report the rebirth of the Hole In The Wall. It's been taken over by a new landlord & landlady, both of whom were very pleasant & chatty. The place has been given a much needed deep clean and now sparkles like a new pin. There are now 6 handpumps on the bar, four of which were on selling beers from the extended Marston's range. The pint of Wychwood Wychmyst that I had was in excellent condition. They now have acoustic music sessions a couple of nights a week and they also do food at lunchtimes. It's still early days, but it looks very promising for the future now. A bit of good news on the Scarborough pub front for a change.Well done guys and the best of luck.

On 21st April 2012 - rating: 8
