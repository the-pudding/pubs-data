Alan Winfield left this review about The Farmer's Arms

The Farmers Arms is a decent looking stand alone pub that is away from the middle of northenden.
Once inside i went into a smartish room that had a cottagy feel to it with carpets and normal tables and chairs,i dont know if there are any other rooms as i did'nt look.
When i visited this pub it was a Greenalls tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Greenalls bitter and this was a fairly decent drink the other beer was Greenalls mild.
This was a nice comfortable pub that was popular with older drinkers.
