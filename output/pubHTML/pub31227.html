Alan Winfield left this review about The Spread Eagle

The Spread Eagle looks a bit different to most pubs i have been in.
Once inside the pub we went into a smartish room that looked quite nice and was also comfortable,the pub also did food.
There was a good choice of real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Caythorpe Dark Jem,this was a really nice dak mild that went down very well,the other beers were Caythorpe One Swallow,Taylor Landlord and Black Sheep bitter.
I really enjoyed my drink in this pub and would happily come back here again.
