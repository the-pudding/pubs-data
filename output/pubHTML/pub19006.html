Al Bundy left this review about Spinners Arms

Not too bad a pub but its still a bit bland. Basically its 5 areas to sit plus an outside bit to the side. It seems to be food led as all the tables were set up for diners. The bar is to your right on entering and has 3 handpulls which were dispensing Moorhouse White Witch, Stray Dog and JW Lees MPA. The interior is modern. The beer isn't too bad but whether its worth the walk out to it is up to you. If you decide to sit outside then beware as there is a field of cows behind the pub and the smell can be agricultural to say the least.

Chorley Phantom Drinker left this review about Spinners Arms

What can I say. Very poor excuse for a pub. Flat insipid beer and that includes shock horror the Fosters larger, place dirty and far to warm to be comfortble to sit inside unless wearing a mankini. Had a pint of Thwaites and wished I hadnt. Knew something was amiss when the barmaid was swilling Mrs Phantoms Larger and lime on the pump. A small squirt of gas at the end to give the head. No atmosphere no cleanliness no nothing. Didnt stick around to sample the food but left after one pint. Poor. We dont want pubs like this in Chorley.
