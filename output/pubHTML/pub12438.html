Komakino . left this review about Whittle Taps

A too-cool-for-school bar in a town full of them, named after the inventor of the jet engine with plenty of distressed decor and a variety of hodgepodge style trappings. Four ales available including Thwaites Bomber, St Austell Proper Job, Prescott Hill Climb and a winner in Titanic's majestic Plum Porter which was in hearty shape and reasonable (for Cheltenham) at £1.90 for a half. Three boxes of real cider also available, so a generous '6' for the effort.

On 27th May 2017 - rating: 6
