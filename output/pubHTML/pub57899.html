Will Larter left this review about The Yorkshire House

Just off Castle Street in the centre of town, it would be quite easy to miss this one, but it's worth stepping off the beaten track for. Set in an old building that doesn't look publike, the interior is slightly rough, with a strange smell apparent on entering: a mingled scent of unwashed dogs and bleach - I would imagine this would become less noticeable later in the day; my visit was soon after opening. On the bar were Salopian Shropshire Gold, Adnams Ghost Ship, Robinsons Trooper and Westons Old Rosie Cider. I quite liked it here despite the smell, one of my other senses being tickled by the presence of the Animals and the Sex Pistols on the sound system.

Date of visit: 13th February 2018
