Chris Sawle left this review about George and Dragon Inn

Cracking village boozer in the upper reaches of South Tynedale. Flagged floors, open fires. Lovely pint of Kingstone cider. Opens Monday and Tuesday lunchtimes, which must be applauded. Shuts all day Wednesday. Very lovable, be great in winter.
