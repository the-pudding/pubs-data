ROB Camra left this review about Knowsley

Greene King have indeed spent a lot of money on this pub and maybe some more since the last review as the whole place had a "new smell". It's an L shaped pub with lots of different areas, even though it's one room. There's a pool table to the left at the rear. Most of the furniture is dining tables and chairs although there are some high tables. There are also some booths towards the rear. Lots of handpumps with 9 on during my visit, sadly 7 of these were GK beers and the other two from large breweries. Having said that my pint of GK IPA Gold was in good nick and at £2.55 quite cheap for the area. It's not going to become a regular haunt unless the beer range improves, but it's worth a visit for one.

On 3rd February 2014 - rating: 6
