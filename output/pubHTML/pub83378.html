ROB Camra left this review about KRO

This is the sister bar to the two Manchester bars of the same name. It's a corner bar on two levels. There's a small outside terrace and a conservatory. There are some comfy seats to both the front and rear, the rest are all standard tables & chairs. Fairly simply decorated with prints around the walls. Very friendly welcome from the manager. I asked about real ale as there is a handpump. He told me that when they opened they tried to sell it, but it didn't work here. They've no plans at the moment to try again as they have two craft keg pumps that are selling well. I had a half of the craft keg at £2.25. It's a nice, friendly place and I'd happily call in again. Shame about the real ale.

On 15th December 2015 - rating: 7
