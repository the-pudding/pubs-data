Alan Winfield left this review about The Langtry

When i went in this pub it was called Lillie Langtry,the pub is situated to the south of the city centre and is on the apex of two roads.
Once inside there is one room that is on two levels,the area nearest the two roads is on a higher level.
The pub was quite smart and had a relaxed atmosphere,this pub is a Greene King tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of GK IPA and this was a decent enough drink the other beer was GK Abbot.
I thought this was quite a nice pleasant pub to have a drink in.
