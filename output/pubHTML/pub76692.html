Graham Coombs left this review about Toby Carvery Hopgrove

Large and slightly dowdy roadhouse, mainly given over to the carvery side of things but retaining a definite bar area for drinkers. Beers included a couple of local ales and certainly drinkable. Cask Marque listed.

Real Ale Ray left this review about Toby Carvery

Have to agree with Gill, this Toby is well worth a visit. It is slighty out of the centre of York and would be a bit of a walk, but unfortunately it's along a very busy stretch of road with no footpaths, so had to take a taxi which was a bit pricey. We started here before our York crawl, so had a good lunch and a few beers beforehand.

This Toby had a better ale selection than most we have been to, and the Leeds Pale Ale was superb. The carvery meat was first class too, so we will be back while staying locally.
