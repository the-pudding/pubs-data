Alan Winfield left this review about The Old Thatched Inn

The Old Thatched Inn is set sideways onto the main street and is named this because of its thatched roof.
The main entrance to this pub is at the rear and once inside the pub had been opened out into one large smart drinking area,this pub is a Marstons house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Pedigree and this was a decent drink the other beer was Hobgoblin.
This was'nt a bad pub but i think it looked better from the outside than on the inside.
