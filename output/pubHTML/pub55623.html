Karen Sabell left this review about Braefel Inn

Would rate this pub 10 out of 10. My daughter and I visited this pub several times whilst in Cornwall for 3 weeks property hunting. Food was excellent and good value for the money. All the staff were extremely friendly on all our visits. We will certainly be returning there for our meals on our return holiday in April and August 2015.

Philip Carter left this review about Braefel Inn

Large pub connected to Perran Quay caravan touring site. Pub is open to the public and has a large parking area. Sells real ale. There is a swimming pool in the garden, though I think this is just for the residents. Serves lunch & evening meals and has a kids menu. No dogs allowed, except guide dogs.
