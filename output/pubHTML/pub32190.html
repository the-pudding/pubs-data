Reviews of Wild Pig (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Oggwyn Great left this review about Brooklands Hotel

Large pub handy for Shrewsbury Towns new ground (£5 parking charge on matchdays), its a large two room pub , the bar is to the left and is pretty big , the lounge is smaller and more geared to dining(food looked good , i did not eat)children are welcome in the lounge , its all fairly clean and tidy in here , the service is efficient rather than friendly , the usual tap stuff plus two real ales , London Pride and Speckled Hen which were OK (Why no local beers ? there used to be) There is a large beer garden , very busy on the sunny day of my visit (Shrewsbury and Accrington fans mixing happily).
Its OK in here when visiting the New Meadow but not worth a special trip

On 25th April 2011 - rating: 5
