Alan Winfield left this review about The Irv Inn

The Irv Inn is a small pub which is almost opposite the Crossed Shuttle.
Once inside there is a smallish square shaped room,the bar is on the right side,the room is bare boarded,there are slight raised areas either side of the front door,these areas had a large barrel for a table and tall stools,the seating in the main area is normal tables and chairs.
There were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Saltaire Blonde which was quite a nice drink,the other beer was Doom Bar.
The pub was empty on my early Saturday dinner visit as it had just opened,there was some decent background music playing.
This is a decent pub to do if on a crawl round Pudsey.

Pub visited 4/4/2015
