Will Larter left this review about Artisan Ales (Old Tap Room)

Just a few steps away from Ludlow's busy and tourist-infested town centre is the Artisan Ales beer shop and micropub. The bar is upstairs, with a lounge containing a few sofas and half a dozen tables and stools. Beer is served direct from the barrel - the stillage is in an adjoining room. I was unlucky in that most of the beers had run off during the weekend, so that on my Tuesday afternoon visit there was only Ludlow Gold available - this was in very good condition. The owner was friendly and chatty, and I would be happy to return next time I'm in Ludlow.

Date of visit: 18th April 2017
