Will Larter left this review about The Goose

A large open-plan pub in a former bank, this is not really my sort of pub any more than it would have been Real Ale Ray's (see review below). Its saving grace is the ale selection, with Pheasantry PA, Springhead Roaring Meg and Batemans Yella Belly available at the time of our visit, priced below £3 a pint. There was also a real cider on hand pump. Not the sort of place I'd want to stop in for long, and if there hadn't been an interesting beer or two on, I'd have had no hesitation in turning round and walking out.

Date of visit: 1st July 2015
