moby duck left this review about The Mayford Arms

A sizable Greene King pub opened out inside but with four distinct areas,the main bar area,a restaurant area, and a couple of smaller rooms one with a dartboard and jukebox, the other housing a pool table,the floor is carpeted except the immediate bar area which is wooden boards, there are many old photos on the walls of local areas in days gone by.
On the bar along with the usual keg offerings are three pumps, all Greene King related beers,Abbott, IPA and Old Golden Hen. There was a sprinkling of after work customers who seemed friendly enough as was the service.Not a bad pub but given the beer choice I wouldn't go out of my way.

On 24th June 2015 - rating: 6
