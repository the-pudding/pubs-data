Oggwyn Great left this review about The Duke

This place has had a bit of a make-over , it is much lighter and feels a lot cleaner than before , still loads of TVs around and live music at weekends .
Better news as well the handpulls have been re-instated with Hobgoblin and a decent(when it warmed up) Woods Shropshire Lass .
A decent improvement.

2 room pub recently refurbished , no cask ale on but beer wetherspoons prices , large bar with 2 pool tables and darts , large lounge with small raised area used for bands/entertainment and area partioned off for diners/quite drinkers .Plenty of TV screens around showing sport . It can get very busy in here at weekends and a bit rowdy at times , i prefer it earlier on or in the week
