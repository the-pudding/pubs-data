Aqualung . left this review about The Three Cocks

This pub was taken over a few years ago by a couple from a famous Essex pub the Golden Lion in Rochford. It's on the Northern side of the main town centre but not too far out. It consists of a single long room with the bar in the centre. To the right is a "Saloon" area with a carpet and tables and chairs. The left "Public" area has a wooden floor with a raised area at the far end containing a really good darts throw and a Northants Skittles area. I don't know Northants well and this is the first time I've come across this pub game. There is a function room somewhere that is used to host four beer festivals a year. I noticed a TV screen that was turned off and there was an 80s soundtrack playing.
The bar has a set of four hand pumps at the front and a further three at the side. One of the front ones was unclipped and the rest had Mighty Oak Maldon Gold & Kings (presumably due to the Essex connection), Oakham Inferno, Grainstore 1050, Full Mash Nevermore Stout and Cottage Duchess. I went for the Nevermore (£3.10) and the 1050 (£3.20) both in excellent condition. I was the only one in at midday on a Friday but the young barmaid was very friendly. I thought this was a top pub and would gladly return.

On 10th April 2016 - rating: 9

A very friendly environment, made to feel welcome by all, has a cracking karaoke night on saturdays,an open mic night on thursdays and a popular pub quiz on mondays,a free function room with a private bar and the offer of a hot/cold buffet if required. Organised events for example the most recent was a slight of hand magic show along with a phsycic, most entertaining. landlord and landlady (ali and doug)fulfill the atmosphere of the pub. Are open to suggestions for themed nights and put the effort in to make the evenings enjoyable.
