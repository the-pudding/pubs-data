Mark Poxon left this review about Gupshill Manor

More of a restaurant than a traditional pub. Food is very good and regulars get a discount card. Beer is ok. Wide selection of wines.

Large Beer Garden with heaters.

Has a separate annexe for events and there is often live music - but this is in a separate building to the pub and restaurant.

Has historic links - situated on the site of the Battle of Tewkesbury (1471) - the penultimate battle of the War of the Roses.

On 5th November 2009 - rating: 8
