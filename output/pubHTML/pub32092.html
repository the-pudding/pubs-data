Alan Winfield left this review about The Kings Head

The Kings Head Inn is a decent looking white painted pub that is situated on the west side of Market Drayton.
Once inside the pub had two seperate rooms,i had a drink in the bar which was to the right,this was qute a smart and comfortable room with comfy bench seats and normal tables and chairs,the room was also carpeted,there was a dart board and TV to the rear left.
The lounge to the right was smaller and looked quite smart.
The pub is a Marstons tied house and there were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Marstons Burton bitter which was a decent drink,the other beer was Pedigree.
This was a decent enough pub to have a few drinks in but it was far too quiet for my liking and almost empty on my Saturday dinner visit.

Pub visited 15/6/2013
