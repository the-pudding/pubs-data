Oggwyn Great left this review about Britannia Inn

Just round the corner from the station and originaly called the Engine and Tender it has been known as the Britania since 1856 , its a split level pub with a pool table on the upper floor to the rear , there is an alcove on the lower floor with a dartboard , there is also a small outside drinking area .
Usual keg taps and two handpulls with Salopian Shropshire Gold and Hop Twister , i had the Hop Twister which was decent if a tad warm .
Very quiet on my visit but the locals were friendly as was the barman .
Its a friendly little pub this if nothing special

On 29th July 2012 - rating: 6
