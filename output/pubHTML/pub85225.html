Real Ale Ray left this review about The Dandy Cock

Only a short walk from the Barrel and Bean and an even shorter walk back to the railway station. This was formerly part of the antique shop next door and the micro has acquired some further seating space at the back of the shop next door. The interior of this micro is full of antique artefacts such as standing lamps, ornaments, picture frames and a chesterfield settee. The bar can be found in the far right hand corner and hosted four handpumps, a choice of six ciders and one hundred types of gin. If you bring your dog along there was beer for dogs sold in bottles on the bar at £3 each. One of the customers I was chatting to at the bar brought his dog back later and bought it a beer, which was served in a dog bowl obviously. and the dog lapped it up, it was a warm day though.
Beer wise we went for the Totally Brewed Meland Gold and Dancing Duck 22.

On 20th August 2017 - rating: 8
