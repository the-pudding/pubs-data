Reviews of The Pelham Arms (Average Rating: 5½ of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

E TA left this review about The Pelham Arms

A raucous backstreet local, built in Victorian times by the Pike Spicer Brewery. The downstairs is a single L-shaped bar with a pool table, 2 dartboards, TV and a wealth (or is it a poverty?) of gaming machines. When we visited it was overrun with unruly children being encouraged to misbehave by their unruly parents. There was also a dreadful live band playing who didn’t realise that excess volume does not mask lack of talent. The staff however were pleasant. There was only one ale on, Doombar, which was in average condition. Probably fine as a local but not somewhere to come for a quiet pint, and I doubt I’d include it on a crawl.

On 3rd December 2018 - rating: 5
