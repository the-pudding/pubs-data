Gill Smith left this review about Bell Inn

Called into this bar while on a coach trip round Gloucestershire pubs. There was a busy bar area, and 3 ales available, 2 from Young's brewery and Purity Bunny Hop. We chose the Young's Special which was fine.

Alan Winfield left this review about Bell Inn

The Bell Inn is a fairly large pub with lots of ivy growing up the walls and it is on the eastern side of Stow.
When i went in this pub it was a Whitbread tied house and the one real ale on here was Flowers IPA and i thought it was a decent pub.
When i took this recent photo the pub was'nt open so i car'nt comment as to what beers are now on here.
