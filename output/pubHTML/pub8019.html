Graham Coombs left this review about The Park

A large suburban pub, facelifted in the faddish shades of light grey, with an equally faddish selection of sofas and bucket chairs in bright colours. Extends round the central island bar with areas for darts, pool and live music. Large patio garden at rear. A couple of Dartmoor Ales on draught seemed in fair condition.
