Graham Coombs left this review about The Winking Man

Roadside pub on edge of the moors with something of a 1980's feel to it. Mainly attracts food trade but had several good ales on pump, including local examples. Entrance is through a lounge, with the bar area behind and a games area and more seating at rear. Function room behind bar and entertainment at weekends.
