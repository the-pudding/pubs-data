Colin Walker left this review about Tingle Shooter Bar & Cafe

Tingle is situated less than 300metres from Glasgow Central station. The place sits in the lower end of Mitchell Street. At this end the street is more like an alleyway. Its sits opposite the House of Fraser staff entrance and you will find at lunchtime some of the employees having lunch. This is not a place where you would find your Grandpa, as the place seems more like a nightclub. Although the opening hours of Mon - Sat 10:00 to 24:00 & Sun 12:00 to 24:00 show normal pub hours for this area. The place has cocktails all at £3.50p, Bombers such as Jager Bombs for those associated with these type of drinks at £2.50p & and a varied choice of bottled beers from £2.00p up £3.50p. A pint of Tennents is lees than £3.00p. There is food available but is more the café / burger style. There is an NCP car park 50metres up the street. But, if drinking in Glasgow is better to take the train.
