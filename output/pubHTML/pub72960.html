Alan Winfield left this review about The Hastings

The Hastings is a largish pub on the busy willerby road in west Hull.
There were two rooms in this pub i went in the room at the front which was quite a big room and this was carpeted and fairly comfortable and there were two large bay windows with seating in them,there was also a smart looking bar at the back of the pub.
There were no real ales on here with the only bitters being Stones and John Smiths smooth so i had a Guinness.

On 1st March 2011 - rating: 6
