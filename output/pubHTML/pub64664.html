Old Boots left this review about The Auld Hoose

An uneven U shaped bar with a niceish horseshoe counter. The outer signage suggested there might be a problem but as it's a GBG listed pub I persevered. Inside I found three pumps, two of disappointment and one of expectation - it was OK not special, but OK. Apart from the niceish counter, there's bench seating at wooden tables , some old pictures on the walls and some old and new posters, a bit like a dark, student canteen. It's nice enough and worth dropping in for one if in the area, I wouldn't make a special trip but it could be combined with the excellent Cask & Barrel and maybe the wonderful Dagda Bar too.

E TA left this review about The Auld Hoose

The Auld Hoose is a basic, slightly scruffy establishment serving food and real ale to a varied mix of students, real ale lovers and a few strange individuals. The staff are also strange, but that only adds to the character. Three handpumps on this time, one less than my last visit a year ago. The ale is excellent, kept at the right temperature and in perfect condition - a pint of Hobgoblin, which seems to be a regular ale here, was very much appreciated. Despite the high quality of the beers, though, I can never really relax and enjoy myself here, but I would recommend anyone passing to give it a try.

On 22nd May 2010 - rating: 6
