Al Bundy left this review about The Farmers Arms

A nice looking country pub from the outside but with a modern interior. Open plan with a front part and a rear part. To the left on entering is a pool table area. The bar is at the front and there are 2 handpumps on the bar. Both pumps were dispensing beers from Farm Yard Brewery; Pale Piglet and Haybob. The interior is modern in decor and style and its a nice pub.
