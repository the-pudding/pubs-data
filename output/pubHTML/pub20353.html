Alan Winfield left this review about Engineers Arms

The Engineers Arms is now called the Engine,this pub is situated Coalvilles main shopping street and it is opposite the Monkey Walk spoons.
Once inside there is a single room with the bar facing,there are no real ales on the here with only keg beers being available.
The pub describes itself as a pre club pub with lots of ice cold beers.
This pub is for the younger drinkers of Coalville.
