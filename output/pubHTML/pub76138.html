Real Ale Ray left this review about The Berkeley Tavern

A spacious pub with a function room. There is a long front bar with some settees and three large windows to watch the world go by. Has a mixture of locals and visitors on day trips. Three ales on offer Sharps Doom Bar and Deuchers. I went for Cullercoats Brewery Rocket IPA, which was excellent. Cullercoats is a new brewery, which is brewed at Cullercoats just a few miles along the coast.

On 10th August 2012 - rating: 7
