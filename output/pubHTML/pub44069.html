Steve C left this review about Kirkland Arms

The Felinfoel Brewery owned Kirkland, named after the 1905 Grand National winner, was empty apart from one other customer when I visited on a recent Thursday afternoon. The empty left hand bar houses a pool table, jukebox and two gaming machines that were switched off. A small plasma screen was also switched off and the pub dog, Rizla, was lounging on the banquette seating that surrounds the aforementioned pool table. The slightly smaller right-hand bar has a quiz machine and two plasma screens, the larger of which was airing European Championship athletics and diving. Sky and BT Sport were both advertised.
Available from the bar was a standard keg range and the two hand pumps were drawing Felinfoel’s Celtic Pride and Double Dragon. I didn’t see any food advertised in this local’s boozer.

On 11th September 2018 - rating: 5
