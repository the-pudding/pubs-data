Reviews of 75 Church Street (Average Rating: 1 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about 75 Church St

there are plenty of places up their own backside in Lancaster but nothing quite as awful as this. decor-wise they have tried to copy the Borough (gentlemans club chic) but there are no keg fonts or hand pumps, just cans and bottles of extortionate craft beer. I wondered if I was even in the right place because it doesn't even feel like a bar let alone a pub. some bloke was playing some preposterous piano jazz to a room of a few people (bar staff and friends of bar staff). £5 for a small can of some crafty slop. just truly terrible, no wonder it's always dead.
