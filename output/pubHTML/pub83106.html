Graham Coombs left this review about The Lake Hotel

Smart and friendly small hotel in lovely location overlooking Scotland's only lake. The tastefully-furnished Port Bar opens onto a large conservatory and waterside garden area with magnificent views. Rating sadly reduced as no real ales but it does have a good range of bottled beers.

On 10th July 2015 - rating: 6
