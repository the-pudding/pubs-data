Graham Coombs left this review about Tair Pluen

A former Litten Tree pretending to be a Spoons, handy for the bus station. A big open plan place with the bar at the back, mainly tabled, featuring a 'library' corner, various TV screens and a pool table. A couple of ales from Beacon (Gold and Red) but that sampled wasn't in the best of condition.

On 13th August 2019 - rating: 6
