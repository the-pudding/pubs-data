David Ross left this review about The Louden Tavern

Formerly called “The Stadium,” this is round the corner from Ibrox Stadium and across the road from Ibrox Subway station. Painted blue, white and red and proclaiming itself “The Ultimate Rangers Supporters Pub,” it has a "sister pub" elsewhere in the city. As it was closed on the Friday evening I tried to go in, I assume that it’s only now open on match days. Judging by its website, it seems to be more or less owned by the Supporters’ Club and is probably unlikely to appeal to anyone but Rangers fans.
