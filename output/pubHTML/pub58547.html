Old Boots left this review about Herriots Hotel

The bar part of the hotel is now known as Rhubarb Bar & Restaurant but remains unchanged from previous descriptions. A nice old room wasted a bit by modern fake additions, it has stripey carpet and leather chairs on a split level. Three pulls on the counter but only an indifferent Naylors beer on plus the usual range of kegs.

Real Ale Ray left this review about Herriots Hotel

Very trendy interior with lots of fashionable wallpapers, large orange lampshades and a paint finished bar. Consists of a lounge bar, raised area on the left near the front windows. Restaurant is situated on the right. Copper Dragon Golden Pippin and Theakston Best were well kept, but do exspect to pay above central town pub prices. Friday evening was moderately busy, so found seating no problem compared to the busy town centre. A relaxing atmosphere and good surroundings to finish our evening.

On 22nd April 2012 - rating: 8
