Soup Dragon left this review about Central Hotel

More of a comment than a review. This is a terraced stone built pub, with some mouldings around the windows and door. I entered to find an open plan room with a pool table, but having the dog, i asked if it was ok to bring her in - the friendly lady said it was, but as there were no real ales on, or a keg MILD i decided against it; it was the same choices as the last few places i had been. I left.
