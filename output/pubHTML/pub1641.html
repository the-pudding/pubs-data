Quinno _ left this review about The Bull

Change of licensee a while back, a refurb and a general upswing. Usually has three Wadworth beers on in good nick.

August 2014
Big and open-plan inside, usually stocks one or two of the Wadworth range in decent condition. The refurbished room off the left hand side of the bar is nice though. Sports bar upstairs with a myriad of screens, pool table and darts.
