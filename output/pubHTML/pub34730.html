Graham Coombs left this review about Waveney Inn

Part of the Waveney River Centre, an extensive collection of boatyards, camping sites, lodges etc tucked away in a remote corner of Broadland. It is a new building, with clean modern interiors and the occasional nautical or rustic decoration but would otherwise not feel out of place in a city centre. The bar is set towards the rear with an area of easy chairs to the side, faced by a couple of tiers with more of a cafeteria feel leading through to the tabled area outside in the front. Draught ales seemed to be from Adnams and Woodfordes. About as far as you could get from being a quaint country pub, but still a useful watering hole, especially for boaters and walkers.

On 7th July 2018 - rating: 6
