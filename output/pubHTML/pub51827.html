Sally McKean left this review about Queens Hotel

The Queen's Hotel is just 6 miles from the spectacular Mull of Galloway, Scotland's Land's End.

Stunning coastline and views out to sea provide inspiration for artists and an idyllic retreat for those seeking refuge from the pressures of modern life.

The Rhins peninsula also offers great sea angling opportunities.

Drummore is also only a few miles from the famous Logan Royal Botanic Gardens, Britain's most exotic gardens which benefit from the warm gulf stream air.

The Queen's Hotel, a Grade II Listed Building, is a traditional hotel with old world charm.

You'll always find a warm welcome at The Queen's Hotel, Drummore.
