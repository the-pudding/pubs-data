Will Larter left this review about Linlithgow Tap

This is a sort of all-things-to-all-people type of pub. If you like real ale, there's a choice of six hand pumps. (Orkney Dark Island, Kinneil Yill, Fyne Jarl and Inveralmond Ossian at the time of my visit, if my notes are to be believed.) If you like lager, they have lager. If you like pub architecture, the bar is octagonal. Foot rail and hooks - you got it. Radio - oh yes. As you may have gathered, this place didn't really float my boat, but don't let me put you off going along and making up your own mind.

Date of visit: 20th March 2017
