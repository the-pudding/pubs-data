Aqualung . left this review about The Glasshouse

This is a huge modern barn of a place that is food orientated, hosts entertainment events, has TVs everywhere and has a function room that caters for private events. I got here around 11:00 on a Saturday morning to find a few people in.
Although there are four hand pumps two were unused and one had a London Pride clip turned. This left just Marston's Wells Eagle IPA which was being sold for £1.50. Unfortunately it was sour and cloudy so it was replaced with John Smiths Smooth Crap.
For some reason this place is GBG 2018 listed. I couldn't wait to get out of it.
