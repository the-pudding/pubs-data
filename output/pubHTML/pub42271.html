Ian Mapp left this review about The Bell Inn

Very smart Gastro pub, with a Farmers bar in the Right hand Side on Entry.

Very smart furniture and a nice outside patio area.

Two Real Ales - Doom Bar and my chosen Thwaites Lancaster Bomber. Superb condition. £1 and shrapnel back from a £5.

Some photos at my walking blog - http://mappiman.blogspot.co.uk/2015/07/180715-bredon-hill-by-eck.html
