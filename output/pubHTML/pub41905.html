Quinno _ left this review about The Victoria

Rock pub, three quarters of the way up the hill to Swindon old town so stopped in here for a breather. Multi-area’d with yellow walls. Page 3-style porn in the gents, naff. Three ales on, Doom Bar, 6X and West Berks Good Old Boy. I had a half of the latter which was cloudy with bits floating round in it. A ludicrous mark-up on halves as well, two quid mine cost. Suspect it only survives because of the live music.

On 10th July 2015 - rating: 5

Alan Winfield left this review about The Victoria

The Victoria is just down the hill and slightly away from the old town area.
This pub is a rockers pub and it had the music to go with it which i dont mind.
Once inside here the pub has a funny layout there is a smallish room at the front then you go down steps to get to another room which is larger,there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of London Pride this very nice the other beers were Shepherd Neame Spitfire and Wadworth 6X.
I was quite happy having a drink in here and would have liked to stay for a couple more drinks.

On 14th November 2010 - rating: 7
