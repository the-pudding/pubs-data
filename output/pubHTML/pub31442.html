michael furn left this review about George & Dragon

The pub is by owned by Everards 01295 711500.
Its served by bus, bus stops half hourly daytime 500 Brackley- Banbury.
The pub is open 1200 till close 7 days a week.Real ales are from the Everards guest list. food served 1200-3pm on Mondays, 12pm till 830pm Tuesdays to Saturday, and 12 till 7pm Sundays includes sunday roasts.
Ideal for getting off the M40 at junction 11 at Banbury, then A361 Banbury to Daventry, turn right off A361 for Chacombe.
The pub food is a reasonable price between £5 and £15.
Although Cask Marque, staff not all that knowledgable as to real ale.Its beer quality as to real ale is poor.
It has a decent bar person female wise, who keeps the character of the pub alive.Always asked to be served by her.

The Pub has two cellar ghosts called Duncan and Lenny, thses can be seen also in the bar at all hours.

On 24th September 2012 - rating: 1
