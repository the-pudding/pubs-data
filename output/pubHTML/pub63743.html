Will Larter left this review about The Red Well Inn

A roadside gastro pub and hotel, though drinkers (of beer or coffee) are welcome too. There are three hand pumps, two of which were operative at the time of our visit, with a couple of unchallenging blonde beers from local micros. The décor is tasteful and the staff friendly. The countryside around here is pleasant, so I imagine this would be a nice place to stay, though probably a bit out of my price range!

Date of visit: 28th November 2015
