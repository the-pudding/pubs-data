Graham Coombs left this review about The Stanhope Arms

Solid and bustling pub opposite Gloucester Road tube station, popular with tourists for food. Reasonable range of beers for Greene King, with Landlord and Sambrook's Pale alongside Abbot, IPA and London's Glory. A quick pit stop rather than somewhere you would linger.

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Stanhope Arms

High ceilings and presumably a rather transient customer base (being very close to Gloucester Road station) give this place a somewhat cold atmosphere. However, there is a reasonable amount of interest in the façade and interior decor of this Taylor Walker outlet. The 'L'-shaped bar is also a reasonable size but it can feel pretty cramped unless you manage to get a seat in the main corner area. The beer is generally well kept, with the four handpumps offering Youngs London Gold, Bombardier, Pride and Hobgoblin (£3.95) on my latest visit.
