Graham Coombs left this review about Games Room

Student bar on two floors above coffee shop, featuring a whole lot of pool and snooker tables, table tennis, darts, table football and many board games. A mundane range of keg beers with one handpump serving Doom Bar - to be fair condition of the latter was good. Great if you are a student or playing games, but otherwise don't bother.
