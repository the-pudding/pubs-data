Will Larter left this review about Navigation Inn

A good looking roadside inn that has been painted white without and grey within. Although the interior is very smart, and has obviously had a lot of money spent on it, I found it cold and soulless. Everything is of good quality and, who knows, probably impeccable taste too, but it just didn't float my boat (sorry). It didn't help to have some rather annoying pop music playing on the sound system. There were four hand pumps, and the beer, which was served without a sparkler (hooray!) was in top condition.

Date of visit: 30th September 2016
