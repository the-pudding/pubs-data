Quinno _ left this review about Cannie Man

A rare beast indeed - a genuinely good estate pub. L-shaped bar with burgundy walls with gold/burgundy leaf wallpaper. Plenty of punter entertainment; dart boards, pool, bar billiards, live music, SKY Sports. Busy with a relaxed and friendly crowd and a good mix of people. Four pumps with three on; Bombardier, Pride and Cottage Comet (good) with West Berks GOB just gone off. Friendly landlord sought us out as he didn’t recognise us and asked how we were doing! Now that’s impressive. Was surprised to learn that only a few short years back there was no ale here at all, so quite a transformation. All power to their elbow, I’d make a point of doing this place again and urge others to do likewise. 7.5
