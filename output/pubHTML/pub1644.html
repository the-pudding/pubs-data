Quinno _ left this review about George Hotel

The beer seems to have dropped down again, which is shame.

March 2014
Pretty building, very IKEA-like refit of a hotel bar, lots of white walls, pine-coloured and chocolate-coloured fittings. Music TV and funny-coloured lights. Nice view of the town centre from the windows and outside. Big on sports and a younger clientèle, very 'awight geezer' mockneyesque. Three ales, Pride, Doom and a guest on offer, usually decent. The 'boarded' floor is actually some sort of furry lino! Not my bag but might appeal to others.

Malden man left this review about George Hotel

This is a large hotel building in the centre of town at a busy road junction. The public area at the front is known as The Village Bar. It is unusual in so far that although the building itself is fairly old, with beamed ceilings and so on, the bar area has been done out in a bland modern style. The room is fairly small and rectangular, the light wood bar is straight ahead on entering. The floor is sanded boards, walls are brown and beige and there are high tables with stools and unbacked tall pouffe type things. Two recessed fake log fires each end. The TV had the rugby on. Two boards outside indicate the business is up for sale/lease, so hoping it will continue naturally, but the decor and ambience were not to my taste. Two pumps, Pride and Doom Bar; the Pride was ok, if a little cold.
