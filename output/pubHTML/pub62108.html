Al Bundy left this review about The George and Dragon

A good pub but not the best in Conwy (that'll be The Albion). Has a thin bar room to the left which leads to a beer garden out back. You can also reach the beer garden via a passageway at the front. Real ale is available.

Clive Thompson left this review about The George and Dragon

Decent drinkers pub with a good community feel to it but I felt a bit like an outsider. Old Speckled Hen, Directors, Abbot and Bass, plus Addlestones and Old Rosie ciders. Beer garden at the rear.
