Komakino . left this review about The Fishmongers Arms

Slightly out from the centre of Southgate, this is an open plan, split-level pub ticking numerous boxes for its patrons, with pool table (pink and black balls), dartboard, live sports and jukebox. A staple-shaped bar serves a games area to the left, a deep bay window area in front of the bar whose space is under-utilised with the presence of five leather sofas, and a split-level area to the right. Three stumps, with only one ale on (Purity Mad Goose - £4.10 a pint and OK) with a real cider available in the form of Rosie's Pig. Fairly busy on a Sunday arvo visit, it's no world-beater, but it's cost enough inside, even if it does seem a little homogenised and the locals seemed friendly enough.
