Quinno _ left this review about The Bulls Head

A traditional two-roomer with classic cosy interior décor and clean as a whistle. A good selection of locals in, including an old gits corner. Two spots of note, a bugle and a miniature of the Burton Union system in a glass case. Two ales on, classic Potteries clichés of Pedigree and Bass, my half of the latter was in very good shape. Desmond was also very happy with the quality of his bubbles. I really enjoyed my brief stay in here, it is an exemplar of what a good village pub should be. 8.5
