Please Note: This review is over a year old.

Quinno _ left this review about The Ivy

Tidy and cosy place with fairly modern fittings e.g. light wood panelling, red / gold pub carpet and roaring wood burning stove. A couple of dining rooms off the main bar. Red cream and gold striped wallpaper like the Nags Head off Only Fools and Horses. Busy. Six pumps with five ales and Old Rosie. Beers were Bass, Black Sheep, Doom Bar, Bateman's Nosey Rosey and Pheasantry Beer Humbug (ok but probably toward end of barrel). Cloth eared barmaid tried to serve us pints. TV sport on the go. Nice enough overall but no outstanding features bar the extended beer range. 6.5

On 16th December 2017 - rating: 7
