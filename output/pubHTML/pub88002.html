Reviews of Archway 49 (Worcester Brewing Company) (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Quinno _ left this review about Archway 49 (Worcester Brewing Company)

Straightforward and basic ‘under the arches brew place’ which has fairly limited opening hours but appears to function as a pub on a weekly basis with live music and other events but don’t expect much in the way of creature comforts. Participant of the Worcester Pub Beer Festival, there were a few ales on stillage so they'd made an effort. To be honest their own beers weren’t much to write home about taste-wise though the condition was fine if a bit flat in that stillagey sort of way (NBSS 3,3). A better choice was the guest, Church End Brewers Truth (3.5). Worth a nose on a crawl if it’s open.

On 15th April 2019 - rating: 6
