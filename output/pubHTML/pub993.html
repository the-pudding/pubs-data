Reviews of The Tavistock (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Tavistock

Comprehensively refurbished pub / restaurant with plenty of seating in the main bar area, the carvery behind a partly opened-out wall and a further dining area at the back. Modern furniture and decor throughout, with music just above background level. Just the dull Eagle IPA (£3.40) from a pair of handpumps, but at least this was fresh and in good condition.
