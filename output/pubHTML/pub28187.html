john Longley left this review about Kings Head

We called in for a meal, as we had not booked our reception was not very welcoming, let alone friendly. The meal was marred by an over microwaved roll (rock hard) and a very watery inferior lasagna. I was sitting on a settee near a window. During the meal I put my hand on the seat and touched something cold. It turned out to be a black slug. The Landlady did not appear to be surprised when I told her but did deduct he cost of the puddings from my bill. However the next day I realised that I had been overcharged by about £10. I canot recommend this pub. and would advise others not to eat there
