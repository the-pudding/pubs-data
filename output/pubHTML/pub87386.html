Reviews of Cut & Craft (Average Rating: 6 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

ROB Camra left this review about Cut & Craft

An impressive conversion from an outdoor wear shop, this restaurant and bar specialises in steak and craft beer. One large room all set up for dining with a few barstools. When we called in just for a drink we were told we could sit at any of the tables for two if we wanted. I suspect this will not be the case on a Saturday night. Loads of pictures and prints around the walls and a light and airy feel to the place. Sadly all the craft kegs were fairly standard and uninteresting. I suspect that Molson Coors have something to do with the place judging by the beer selection. However we spent a pleasant half hour supping a pint and a red wine. It's nice enough and we may eat here sometime in the future, but a craft destination bar it is not.

On 15th February 2019 - rating: 6
