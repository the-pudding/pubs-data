Clive Thompson left this review about Rumours

Previously Rumours and now the Waterside Bar, although the banner announcing this looks far from permanent. Small front bar with a sports bar to the left with fruit/quiz machines, pool table, dartboard and a fairground boxing contraption. There is a Rangers FC theme throughout the pub, even down to the baize of the pool table. Pretty rough pub judging by the clientele, and indeed bar staff. Advertising 'dancers' on Sat night, not sure what type but I didn't see a stage. Think it may be associated with Venus nightclub. Daytime offers on keg, no cask.
