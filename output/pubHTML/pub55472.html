Graham Coombs left this review about Gibsons

Old building next to Micklegate Bar with its main selling point being barbecued food, cooked over a fire pit (recommended, incidentally). It does have a reasonably pubby feel however, and there is one handpull selling a house badged 4.1% ale brewed in Masham, not readily identifiable but very drinkable. There is a courtyard garden at rear, artistically furnished from old pallets!
