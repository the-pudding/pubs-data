Alan Winfield left this review about The Foresters Arms

The Foresters Arms is well hidden away in the newthorpe area of Eastwood.
This is a proper old pub and once inside there were a few different rooms,i have been in this pub a few times and always enjoyed having a drink here.
The pub used to be a Kimberley tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Kimberley bitter and this was a nice drink the other beer was Kimberley mild.
