Jon B left this review about Fifteens Standish

modern gastro-ish pub opened in the former Last Orders after a major renovation. it's probably the kind of place Standish needed to be honest, but I do wonder if the other pubs in the town are suffering from its massive popularity. quite typical Fifteens decor, dimly lit and quite cosy but in a slightly seedy way that makes it difficult for me to get comfortable. four real ales available.
