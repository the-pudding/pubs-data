Bucking Fastard left this review about The Duke Of Marlborough

Situated at one corner of the Blenheim estate and on the busy A44,this roadside pub has accomodation and offers meals but has retained a pub vibe.The building is local stone and the modest interior has a real fire at either end of the bar room,with the larger wood burner by the entrance infront of a couple of sofas.The far end looks a bit more dining orientated but just popping in for a beer is fine.However given it's location,most customers will arrive by car.
Three real ales with the choice being Hook Norton Lion and Hooky,with Moorhouses Broomstick Bitter on my visit .Not much to get the pulse racing for the real ale drinker but a neat and tidy pub with friendly service from behind the bar.

On 30th November 2015 - rating: 6
