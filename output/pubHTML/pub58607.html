Graham Coombs left this review about The Winning Post

I don't know whether someone read my earlier review, but it has happened! The splendid building has been reinvigorated, with closed-off rooms refurbished and reopened with a modern feel, yet retaining the original features. The main bar is now bright and welcoming, not least by the offering of a good range of ales (and Cask Marque listed too). the large function room still seemed dowdy, presumably waiting its turn, but otherwise this is now an excellent pub and should be a great asset to the local community. Easily wins the "most improved" award.

Graham Coombs left this review about The Winning Post

Massive-looking locals' pub in suburban area One main bar, with a smaller part off to the side and a function room at rear. Sadly something of a run-down feel, and crying out for a sympathetic restoration of the original features. John Smiths Draft on handpump, and that sampled not in the best of condition. Enormous potential, but the size of the car park probably dictates redevelopment. (original rating of 3 removed)
