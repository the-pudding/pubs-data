Aqualung . left this review about The Overdraught

As noted below this is a conversion of a shop and a bank before that. It's modern inside but I didn't really get the feeling of it being anything other than a pub. It's a large open plan room with the bar on the right and plenty of low tables and chairs. It was quiet on a Wednesday evening.
The bar has seven hand pumps which had one unused, five Totally Brewed beers and Abbeydale Stuntman. I went for the Totally Brewed Crazy Like A Fox (£3.40) and on a quick Friday visit Captain Hopbeard (£3.80) I only remembered the discount for the Friday visit.
I quite liked this one and would definitely return.

On 27th July 2018 - rating: 9
