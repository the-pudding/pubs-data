Aqualung . left this review about The Corryvreckan (JD Wetherspoon)

The Corryreckan is part of a small leisure complex adjoining the Ferry Terminal and the Railway Station. Inside it's a modern and a bit of a barn. Fixtures and fittings are reasonable and everything is on the single ground floor. The service was variable as it tended to be groups of people coming in ordering food and coffees. I did wonder what it would be like if a busy summer ferry arrived. There are two decent sized paved patios overlooking the harbour.
The bar has ten hand pumps which had two unused, one available soon, the Scottish JDW Trio Of Doom, Arran Dark, Sambrook's Battersea Rye, Everard's Arcadia Session IPA and Leex Dirty Dozen. I went for the Arran Dark (£2.29) which sadly wasn't that good. It's not the greatest Spoons but is in a nice location.

On 18th May 2017 - rating: 6
