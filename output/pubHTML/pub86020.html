Aqualung . left this review about The Bird & Barrel

This is a proper micropub with no distractions and owned by Bexley Brewery. It consists of a small square room with low seating. There's a small bar counter at the back left with three hand pumps and three keg options on a wall. They also do a few speciality spirits.
The three beers on my visit were Bexley Hills & Hoppy & Golden Acre and a joint venture Mild with Clarkshaw brewery. I went for the Mild which was OK but didn't retain any condition.
This is quite a small place and I would return but wonder if there's a question mark over the beer quality.

On 3rd September 2018 - rating: 7
