Oggwyn Great left this review about Flyford Arms

Old looking pub on the main Worcester - Evesham road , there is a small covered outside drinking area , inside any remains of old pub have disappeared , there is a central island bar , the bulk of the pub is given over to dining with only a small part on the entrance side of the bar given over to drinkers , its all very clean and modern , lots of light woods , vases with twigs and wine racks comprise the decor
Usual taps plus one real ale , London Pride , it was OK if nothing special as was the service .
Very much a food pub
