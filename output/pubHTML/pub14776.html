Richard Byford left this review about Queen Adelaide

A very comfortable pub with a good selection of real ales. Guest beers change frequently. Has an open fire which is very homely in the winter months.
The restaurant serves a nice selection of simple dishes, well priced.
The outside area is quite large and they have a play area for children.
