Clive Thompson left this review about The Litten Tree

Previously The Litten Tree, now The Green Man and owned by the Town & City Pub Co. Located near Leverstock Green Cricket Club on the mian road (bus 320 among others). A decent locals pub, quite large with dartboard and a pool table in it's own area in the corner of the pub. Three or four TV screens showing multi-channels. Outdoor paved areas with seating to the front and side. Cask Marque approved, four cask beers, Courage Best, Pride, Bombardier and local Tring Brewery Bring me Sunshine at a decent £2.95 a pint.

On 30th May 2011 - rating: 6
