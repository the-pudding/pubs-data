ROB Camra left this review about Bullock Smithy

This is a Flaming Grill pub, so you know pretty much what to expect, lots of family friendly areas and a kiddies grab machine. This one is a bit more pub like than many and there were no kids in at all during my Friday afternoon visit. 2 handpumps on the bar, but only Sharps Atlantic actually on, which was just about OK. There's a 10% CAMRA discount as well. I went to play on the quiz machine and won £4, but it didn't pay out. I told the manager about it and he said "Sorry mate, it's not my machine, I can't do anything about it". Total crap legally and I told him so. He still refused to give me the money though, so I'll retaliate on here. I would have scored this pub a little higher if it wasn't for the management bullocks. It's too much to hope for that his name was Smith I suppose. :-))
