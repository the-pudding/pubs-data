Gill Smith left this review about The Horse and Plough

What a find this Castle Rock pub is in Bingham. 10 ales and a good variety from micro breweries as well as their own ales. Hard choice to make as we were on a coach trip and I only had time for a quick one, but the Nomadic Uncivilised went down very well. The pub is a single room and th ceiling beams are adorned with pump clips. It was a friendly market town pub and well worth a visit.

Real Ale Ray left this review about The Horse and Plough

A comfortable traditional pub serving good food and beer. Six ales on handpump on our visit, London Pride, Theakston XB, Blue Monkey PG Sips, Falstaff 3 Faze, Caladonian Coffee Porter and Deuchers IPA. Enjoyed the Falstaff and Theakston XB, friendly staff.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Horse and Plough

The Horse and Plough is a newish pub in an old building that was opened about four years ago.
Inside it seems like this pub has been open for years there are stone floors and different drinking areas i think there were about 7 or 8 pumps on the bar with a few nationals London Pride, Adnams Bitter but there was also about four micro brewery beers on from Oldershaws and brewerys further afield the drink we tried recently was very drinkable and i would be very happy to stay here all night.
