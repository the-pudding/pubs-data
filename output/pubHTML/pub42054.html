Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Navigation Inn

Refurbished detached two-bar pub / restaurant near the Worcester & Birmingham Canal but not overlooking the waterway. Quite tastefully done, with a mix of modern and traditional elements. Bar food quite pricey for what it is, but at least when is says that this is served until 21:00 that indeed proves to be the case (even on a quiet Wednesday evening, and very welcome after two previous failures to obtain sustenance on my evening walk). Two real ales on handpump in each part of the central servery: a 'house' ale of unknown origin and Wye Valley Butty Bach (£3.95).
