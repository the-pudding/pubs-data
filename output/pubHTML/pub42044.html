Reviews of Hop Pole Inn (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Hop Pole Inn

Good-looking pub, with a partly opened-out and slightly split-level interior featuring a couple of small seating areas either side of the entrance and then the main bar area with a dartboard beyond. There is also a separate pool room to one side and a beer garden at the back. Three of four handpumps in operation, offering Wainwright, Butty Bach and OBJ from the Georgeous Beer Company at Ironbridge (fine value at just £2.65).

On 10th August 2019 - rating: 7

Andrew Stephens left this review about Hop Pole Inn

The pub looks quite small from the front view but extends back a long way,it was opened out many years ago having had the old layout of bar,snug,lounge etc. It was actually the first pub I ever had a drink in - many years ago. However to get back upto date,the pub sells real ales and Thursday/Friday/Saturday/Sunday evenings host's live music of differing styles. Well worth a visit.
