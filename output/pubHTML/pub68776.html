Clive Thompson left this review about Monico Bars

There are two distinct bars, firstly a lounge bar that can be accessed via the passageway near Whites Tavern where there is plenty of seating, some nice decorative mirrors and food available. The public bar is at the front and is more of a drinkers bar, as it should be.
