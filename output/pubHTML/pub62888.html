Alan Winfield left this review about The Water Bridge

The Water Bridge is a large modern pub that is situated close to the busy A50 on the northern edge of Uttoxeter.
Once inside there is a large single L shaped room with the bar facing on entry,the room is carpeted throughout,the seating normal tables and chairs,background music was playing.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Pedigree,this was a decent enough drink.
This is not really a pub to go out of your way to do,but is handy for a quick drink and meal if travelling in this area or to take children for a family meal.
