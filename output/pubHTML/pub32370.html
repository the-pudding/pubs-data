Alan Winfield left this review about Darcy's

Darcy's is a small corner pub which is now the only pub left open on victoria road.
Once inside there was a single room with the bar to the right,the area near the bar was bare boarded,the left side had bench seating,small stools and was carpeted,this area was packed with people watching the Fulham v Stoke game.
There were lots of hops hanging from a beam near the bar.
There were four real ales on the bar all from micro brewers,i had a drink of Lymestone Stone Cutter this was a very nice well kept beer,the other beers were Coastal Dana,Cheshire Brewers Engine Vice and Revolution EP,all beers were quite cheap with my drink costing £1.10 for a half.
I had a good chat with the youngish landlady who was proud of the beers she had on and price of them,she was also quite knowledgeable about pubs in Fenton and Longton.
This pub is well worth a visit if you like different real ales.
