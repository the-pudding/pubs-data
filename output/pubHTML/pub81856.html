Graham Coombs left this review about The Cardinal's Hatt

This was apparently once a small roadside inn, but it has been extended and modernised beyond recognition. The clean modern interior is laid out and furnished like an upmarket hotel, with a couple of rooms off the main bar and a restaurant to one side. Patio area and holiday accommodation at rear. Tribute and Jail Ale on offer - food good but not cheap. Ok if you are looking for somewhere a bit posher than average.
