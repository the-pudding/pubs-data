Aqualung . left this review about Tapps

This place had been open for just over a year on my visit. It's a fairly typical micropub with a square room that has the bar on the rear left and there is a small area at the rear. Although there are no TVs or other electronic stuff they did have music playing and they also do speciality spirits. I visited on the Wednesday and Thursday evenings so there were a few people in.
On both days there were five beers on and I tried Conwy Black Witch, Big Hand Palewulf and Alechemy Secret Citra. These were £3.50 for the first two and £3.80 for the stronger third one. All were in good nick but the Secret Citra was superb and I stuck to that the second night. The prices are expensive for North Wales but maybe not Llandudno.
I thought this was an excellent addition to Llandudno's pub choice.

On 16th November 2018 - rating: 8
