Reviews of Sefton Arms (Average Rating: 5 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Al Bundy left this review about The Sefton Arms

Very nice lookinglarge pub. This is a Greene King pub so the real ale selection is not particularly good. Pedestrian Greene King beers from the six handpulls, which consisted of IPA, Speckled Hen and a World Cup themed beer called Un-beer-lievable (which in keeping with Chris Kamara was rubbish) plus the usual fizzies are your choices. Open plan inside with some raised areas to sit.

Toby Jug left this review about The Sefton Arms

If you like being surrounded by children when drinking at the weekend, or "a very young crowd" during the week this is the place for you. I feel at the weekend it can't decide if its a pub or a restraunt. There are certainly for the discerning drinker better pubs within a 1 minute walk
