ROB Camra left this review about The Honeywell Arms

A two roomed locals local. Darts to the left and a lounge to the right with a pool area off the lounge. It's a Robinsons pub but doesn't sell any real ale. Rough as a robbers dog and full of the local D4's swearing at each other. It was very busy though on a Friday afternoon. I had a quick half of fizz and left them to it. I'll never visit this one again.

On 20th April 2015 - rating: 2
