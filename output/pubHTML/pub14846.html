Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about John Bunyan

Very pleasant McMullen's country pub set behind the triangular green of a small hamlet southeast of Wheathampstead. The two-room interior has been given an unexpectedly light makeover with 2/3rd-height wood panelling on the walls, above which there are all sorts of decorative crockery. Also has a front patio area and a grassed beer garden to one side. Four real ales on handpump: the 3.8% 'house' Pilgrims Poison, AK, Country and Rivertown Eight Hop (£2.00, half). NB - Check opening hours before visiting because it closes in the afternoons, but with a friendly welcome and the bonus of finding some local honey on sale, this one is well worth seeking out.
