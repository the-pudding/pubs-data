Graham Coombs left this review about Chequers

A modern bar in the shops by Stoneleigh station, dominated by a large screen and with ceiling disco lights accounting for lack of much furniture. Available to hire for functions but open generally on Thursday and Friday evenings and all day Sunday, plus Saturday evenings if not privately booked. Unsurprisingly only keg available, in the form of Fosters, Guinness and Peroni.
