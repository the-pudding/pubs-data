Old Boots left this review about The Roebuck

Largely devoted to dining there are some bar stools and a couple of fireside stools by a small round table for drinkers, as well as two leather armchairs and use of some more settles and stools when not in use by diners. The two room front section is a modern interpretation of a traditional pub with plum painted walls, odd wallpaper and a mix of laminate and flag floors. Decor comprises old photos, prints and a stuffed deer's head. The rear is an open plan dining area with lots of exposed wood and brick. There are 6 pulls of well kept Yorkshire beers on offer. There's a "walkers welcome" sticker in the window which is just as well as it's a long, steep walk up from Otley town centre and it's probably best to pick your moment to avoid clashing with lots of diners.
