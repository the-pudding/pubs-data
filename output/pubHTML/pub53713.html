Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Phoenix

The dark and rather unappealing façade on the unbranded M&B (Castle) pub hides an even darker but slighly more appealing interior. The main staple-shaped bar area has fairly plain wooded furniture and Ruby Jean's Diner menus, and there is also a basement bar which hosts regular comedy nights and live music events. Four handpumps, offering the ubiquitous Doom Bar and Tribute, plus Windsor & Eton Knight of the Garter (£3.90) and Cameron's 150 on this visit.

Steve C left this review about The Phoenix

This is an OK pub in a very popular location making it quite a mission to get served during busy periods. Pride, Black Sheep, Rev. James, Frulli and premium lagers were all available during my visit and I found the staff to be friendly even though they were very rushed.

I think that there are better pubs nearby, but this place is OK for a few swifties.
