Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Oak Tree

Thoroughly refurbished suburban pub on the Ipswich Road, and the only such place for some distance in any direction. Split about 50:50 between the bar and main dining areas (both partially sub-divided), with a conservatory added at one end. Attractive semi-modern furniture and light decor throughout. Several groups eating out and a few people just having a drink on a Thursday evening. Two real ales offered from the three handpumps: Doom Bar and Otter Bitter (£3.80).

On 5th November 2016 - rating: 6
