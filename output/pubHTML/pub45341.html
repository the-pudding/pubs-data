Tom Barlow left this review about Wheatsheaf Inn

A delightful pub serving seven wonderful real ales as well as four lagers and a local Welsh keg cider all kept to the highest standards.

Renound for its friendly welcome and open coal fire the Wheatsheaf really is at the heart of the Llantrisant community.

Wonderful home cooked food is sereved in our own resterant where steak dishes are a speciality.

The weekends are full of live music and a wonderful aptmospere by all comers.
