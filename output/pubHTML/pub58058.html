Komakino . left this review about The Talkhouse

A Fuller's eatery, all stone and wood internally. Yes, you can have a drink, but it'll be limited to a Chiswick brew (London Pride or Red Fox on my visit over three pumps). Piped jazz / bossa nova music and the advertisement of its live counterpart continue the pretentiousness. This place has been around for a hell of a long time and still retains a lot of its old character, and the small bar area located to the right on entry would work as a drink-only haven if it weren't for the overloud music.

On 28th November 2016 - rating: 3
