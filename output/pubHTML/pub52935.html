Maggie Watson left this review about The Kings Arms

We think this is a great pub, with a warm traditional welcome. The old fashioned interior gives it a lovely atmosphere. There are plenty of locals at the bar. The beer is good and they serve local Winkleigh cider. You can take your dog - you can take the whole family! We run self catering accommodation at Wheatland Farm, not far from Winkleigh, and recommend it to all our guests. I've never heard a bad review from any of them - and it's not the only one to choose from!
