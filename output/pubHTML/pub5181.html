Alan Winfield left this review about The Wig & Pen

A decent looking pub from the outside.
Once inside there is a single L shaped room with the bar on the mid left side,it is carpeted and has normal tables and chairs.
The pub is a St Austell tied house,there were three real ales on the bar,we had a drink of St Austell Proper Job,which went down very well,the other St Austell beers were Tribute and HSD.
We thought this was a pleasant and comfortable pub to have a drink in.

Pub visited 14/8/2017

I L left this review about The Wig & Pen

Nice corner street pub with a raised entrance from the road junction entering in to a corner of the now one roomed bar this is a St Austell brewery pub serving HSD and Tribute. there are now Luce bands performing regularly which suggests a change of ownership since our lad visit a few years ago. A friendly atmosphere still exists in here and is Aldo. Handy after walking down the hill from the station before entering the centre of town. Handy for the courts as the name of this place would suggest.
