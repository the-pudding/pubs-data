Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Farm

Busy pub / restaurant housed in some historic brick-built farm buildings now swallowed-up in suburbia. The one with the bar has been partly opened-out to leave various small seating areas around the 'U'-shaped counter, and across the way another is used as the restaurant. The interiors have been thoroughly modernised and unfortunately are less interesting than one might hope. On the real ale front, Doom Bar, and two beers from Purity - Mad Goose (£4.10) and Bunny Hop - were available from three of four handpumps.

On 30th September 2018 - rating: 6
