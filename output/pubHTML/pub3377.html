Soup Dragon left this review about The Mount Inn

A cream rendered terraced building in two parts and beer patio.

The interior is open plan, but having a couple of different areas. The decor is cream wallpaper and white walls, with green seats (where i was), wood floor and a nice fireplace. There is Sky TV and old pictures of Chester dotted about, along with other ephemera. The bar is wood. Service was fine and it had a mixed clientele.

Beer was all tap stuff, with Black Sheep on handpull. A disappointment really.

Nothing wrong with the place, just a bit of a non-entity really. I wouldn't seek it out, but i am sure at some stage i will drift back in.
