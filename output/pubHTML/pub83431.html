Aqualung . left this review about The Old Dancer

The Old Dancer is more of a bar than a pub being part of a small independent chain that includes the Treacle Tap in Macclesfield and the Young Pretender in Congleton. As mentioned it's a smallish single room with a wooden floor and small basic low tables and chairs. There is an upstairs bar as well that I didn't look into if it was even open on an early Wednesday afternoon when it was fairly quiet.
Downstairs there are six hand pumps that had one unused, a cider, Great Heck Amish Mash & Chopper, Hartington Best and Redwillow Faithless 53. I went for the Redwillow beer (£3.50) which was in excellent nick. I thought this place was good but possibly a bit trendy.
