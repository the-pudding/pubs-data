David Davies left this review about The Racehorse Inn

What a wonderful restaurant. A fantastic menu with very reasonable prices and plenty of drinks on offer to satisfy anyone. The owner and staff bent over backwards to accomodate us (something other diners said happens all the time to them and they've been going there for years) and the food selection was superb, our meals cooked to perfection. I've ate in practically every pub and restaurant within a 20 mile radius of The Racehorse and none of them come even close for food, ale and hospitality. This Racehorse is a definate winner.
