Old Boots left this review about Golden Lion Inn

Formerly two rooms, probably originally two cottages, and now basically open plan, to the left is the village shop, which is dismantled for darts matches, with a traditional flag floored public bar and also home to the pool table. To the right is a carpeted lounge with proper pubby furnishing of bench seating as well as tables and chairs. Decorated in traditional village pub fashion with the brasses and pictures expected, the lounge has a big screen as well and a set of Guinness flying toucans. Four handpumps with Theakstons and as a guest Adnams, at the weekend the beer count increases which shows the attention to detail that makes the quality excellent in the pub.
