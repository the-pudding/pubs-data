Reviews of The Swan (Average Rating: 8 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Ian Mapp left this review about The Swan

Collection of former cottages or farm houses. In through an inconspicuous front door, like entering someones front room. Bar to the left, dining to the right which leads onto to further dining in the Conservatory - heated with a huge log burner.

Good choice of 4 real ales - Tribute, Ubu and Purity and an excellent Prescott Hill Climb Pale Ale.

Sunday lunch was excellent - good value, portions and service.

Recommended.
