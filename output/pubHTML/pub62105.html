John Horton left this review about The Vaynol Arms

Climbers pub. Basic pub with average beer - Robinsons when last visited a few months ago. Usual pub games. Large carpark separates pub from small church and a climbing club cottage. Does food but I've never eaten here so I'm not going to comment.
