Alan Winfield left this review about Kings Arms Hotel

The Kings Arms is a largish pub that is at the bottom of the high street next to a mini roundabout.
Once inside this pub was quite plush with a few different rooms i had a drink in the room at the front which was carpeted and very comfortable.
When i visited this pub it was a free house and there was one real ale on the bar this was Hook Norton bitter and this was a decent drink.
This pub is now an Arkells tied house.
