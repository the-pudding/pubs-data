Reviews of The Halzephron Inn (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about The Halzephron Inn

Splendid old-school stone-built pub up on the cliffs looking out over the bay. A long row of rooms makes use of all available space, including an outbuilding and a snug tucked behind the bar. Plenty of old furniture on hand and the beamed bar area is topped by a swathe of old hops. Very popular for food and much of the pub is laid up accordingly, but drinkers are still able to find a space. There are also tables outside at the front with a superb view over the sea as well as a garden behind. Draught beers included Lushingtons, Porthleven and Doom Bar with a cider on handpump also.
