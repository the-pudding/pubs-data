Ian Mapp left this review about The Bell Inn

Very smart country pub that seems to play on the fact that its a little secret.

Interior is immaculate with a bar with high stools - everywhere looked setup for dining.

Wickwar Bob was in super condition and we were offered an 8 pint loyalty card for any location selling the beer.

Christine Beddis left this review about The Bell Inn

Found this little gem in the small village of Selsley just outside Stroud whilst visiting family in Stonehouse just a couple of minutes up the road. Nowadays with all the chain owned "plastic Food" selling pubs its a real pleasure when you come across a locals pub that is so friendly and welcoming. The menu was packed with great value homemade dishes along with specials boards all produced by the chef using the best local produce (he assured us). The landlord Tony Jones has only been here a few months and says the Bell has still many changes to come whilst keeping its historic charm, I look forward to returning soon to see how its going.

On 2nd October 2007 - rating: 10
