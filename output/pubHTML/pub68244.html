Oggwyn Great left this review about Cottage Springs Inn

Dead at 5 30 on a Saturday night , the pub is a single L shaped room , the larger part of which is cluttered up with a pool table , table football , a couple of large speakers and a kareoke machine , just about halving the size of the pub . Only 2 real ales on Bankss Bitter and Mild , i had a Mild which was OK , the service was OK but the only other people there were the Landlord and a Barman who both spent the whole time F`in at the fruit machine , i wont bother again
