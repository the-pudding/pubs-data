Alan Winfield left this review about The Wellington Tavern

The Wellington Tavern is standard looking town pub that is one of a few open pubs in the middle of Dewsbury.
Once inside there is a single oblong shaped room,the bar was midway down the room on the right side,there is a raised area to the front right which had tall tables and stools and some normal tables and chairs,there is comfy bench seating down the left side,the room is carpeted throughout,the rear area had a pool table.
There were no real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Worthington Cream crap which was cold and crap.
The pub was very busy on my late Saturday afternoon visit.
This is not really a pub to go out of your way to do.
