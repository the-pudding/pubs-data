E TA left this review about Miller & Carter

Previously a Harvester, this grand building with plenty of potential continues to be wasted, although it’s wasted more expensively now. There is a large private car park with its own one-way system, and there is a tidy outside seating area that just falls short of being a garden. The setting is nice, though there is too much traffic noise for it to be restful outside. Inside feels surreal, like Disney had decided to to design a palace for a low-budget American princess. Lots of scarlet soft furnishings. The staff were quite pleasant, the majority of the clients were well dressed and well-behaved, the air was heavily scented, the food looked artificially plump and there was no real ale – regardless of the the staff’s protestations Guinness, Estrella and Peroni just don’t fall into that category, and in any case we got the feeling that casual drinkers were not welcome. We decided not to mill around and carted ourselves to a proper pub, unlikely to return in the near future.
