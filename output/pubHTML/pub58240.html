Alan Winfield left this review about The Neville Arms

The Nevile Arms is a nice looking bare brick built pub that is opposite a very small village green.
Once inside this pub was very smart and comfortable with a few different rooms,the pub used to be a Kimberley tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Kimberley bitter and this wa a decent drink the other beer was Kimberley mild.
This pub is now more than likely a Gk house with their bland beers on the bar.
