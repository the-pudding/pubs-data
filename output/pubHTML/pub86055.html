Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Parc Britannia

Large Table Table pub / restaurant attached to a Premier Inn hotel. Usual anonymous furniture and decor in the various seating / dining areas. Thankfully, it does do real ale, with Brains SA and a Winter's Ale (brewery unknown) available from the brace of handpumps.
