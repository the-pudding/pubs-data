Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Anglesey Arms

Imposing JW Lees establishment, just off the north end of Telford's famous bridge. Partly opened-out to leave a variety of seating / dining areas, but the emphasis is definitely on the food. Having said that, there is still a reasonable amount of space for those just looking for a drink, and the whole place was doing very good business on a Sunday lunchtime. Upmarket furniture and decor. Four real ales from the five handpumps: Bitter, MPA, Dragon's Fire and Christmas Plum Pudding (£3.40).

On 20th January 2018 - rating: 7
