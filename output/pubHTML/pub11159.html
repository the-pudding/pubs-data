Steve C left this review about The Rajdutt

The Rajdutt is an Indian restaurant in the middle of nowhere, hence the large car park. There is a bar where locals can stop for a drink when picking up a take-away. The wait for food to take out is notoriously long so there is plenty of time for a beer. The bar supports draught Carlsberg and Kingfisher, the first of which was not the best. Thankfully bottles of Cobra were at hand so I didn’t have to endure the below par draught whilst I waited for the hourly bus that stops outside.
I found the staff to be friendly and I was made to feel more than welcome, but this is a restaurant so I wouldn’t return just for a beer. I've not left a rating.
