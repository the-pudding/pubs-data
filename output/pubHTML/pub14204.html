moby duck left this review about The Dog & Partridge Tavern

Once headquarters for the Monster Raving Loony Party a few years ago ,it has now had makeover a couple of years ago, and is now a little smarter and upmarket than it was before. Although modernised it is still reasonably pub like but with equal emphasis on food.a single opened out long room, wooden floored with standard tables and chairs more for eating purposes,a central bar area and at the other end a pool table dartboard and large TV screen for sport.On the bar are three pumps with one unclipped on a Sunday lunchtime,the two on were Caledonian Golden XPA and Caledonian Dog & Partridge , not very exciting but the XPA at £3.75 was in decent condition and not too bad,a mix of usual keg stuff was on offer plus a couple of slightly more premium lagers.The food here is reputed to be up to a very good standard but I've not tried it. The pubs ok but I wouldn't go out of my way.
