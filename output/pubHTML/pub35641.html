Graham Coombs left this review about The Crown

Large pub retaining the two original entrances, although they both lead into the same place, which is a large central bar surrounded by nice traditional furnishings and leading off into various areas behind. Large outdoor area at rear, as well as few tables to the front. A fair range of local and regional ales were on offer, alongside the usual Doom Bar etc. The sampled Rebellion Roasted Nuts (a beer not a snack) was in good form.

moby duck left this review about The Crown

A sympathetically modernised pub but still traditional at heart. I only counted six handpumps as opposed to the eight in John's review below, Its possible I could have missed a couple somewhere. Beers on were Rebellion IPA, St Austell Tribute and Proper Job,Doom Bar, Abbot and coming soon was Titanic Plum Porter. There was quite a good feel about this place, I quite liked it.
