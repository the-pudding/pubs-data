Real Ale Ray left this review about Dipton Mill Inn

Well worth a visit, as this is the home of the Hexamshire Brewery. Serves home cooked food, very friendly landlord with a good hearty laugh. I enjoyed the Devils Elbow, Devils Water, Shire Bitter, Whapweasel, Old Humbug and the Blackhall English Stout. Will defo call again.
