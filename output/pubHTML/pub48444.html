Tex Icecubes left this review about Eskdale Hotel

No real ale and an extremely limited number of pumps. It was hard to tell what the beers were they were all obscured by the price labels.
Mainly people having meals.
A rugby theme with caricatures of rugby players on the wall.
I noticed that the two alkies who were in the Douglas walked straight past this place to go to the Crown. Dont think its too popular with the locals.
But I suppose if you were just passing through it might be a good place to have a meal. Its very tidy. Cant fault it for tidiness.
