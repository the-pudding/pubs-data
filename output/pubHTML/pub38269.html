Soup Dragon left this review about The Crown

A light rendered detached elongated cottage-styled pub with black paint work and a patio area.

The interior had a couple of rooms, a small bar that i didnt really see to the left on entry and the main lounge, where we went, to the right. This room is an L-shaped room around the serving bar, with a pool table and a TV, which was showing the football. The decor seemed to be light grayish walls with a white and brown wood beam ceing and wood floor. The service was functional, there was no music and just a few locals in. A few old photos were on the walls.

Beer; all tap, no real ale. There was an M&B MILD that was so disgusting i took the drinks back, left them on the bar and in the absence of anything to substitute them for, i simply left. To be fair to the guy behind the bar, i didn't give him chance to offer to change them, but it takes skill to keep keg beer that badly.

Nothing for the real ale fan here. Definately one for the locals.
