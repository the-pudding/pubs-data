Tris C left this review about Folly Wine bar

The downstairs of this place seems to have been founded in 1997 but I used to go to the upstairs bar and restaurant which I've not visited since 1987. If unchanged, it's a nice place for the type and was certainly the place to be seen in Petersfield; the bread and soup was decent and cheap.
