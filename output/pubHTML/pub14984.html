N Smith left this review about The Bell

A lovely pub set off the busy A120.
Very much inviting you inside to take a break from those long journeys.

Surprisingly large inside given the general-looking exterior.
Corporate décor as expected with a nice area to the left with leatherette padded banquettes. The right hand area is more open with two pool tables and access to a well-frequented smoking patio leading to a larger garden.

Naff piped music and muted Sky Sports News. Very cosy atmosphere, perfect for the now cold winter nights; All the drinks seem very competitively priced. Cloud WiFi available though the reception cut out randomly.

Staff very friendly too.

All in all a very pleasant experience. I will certainly be taking my walking boots in there again :)

On 11th November 2016 - rating: 10
