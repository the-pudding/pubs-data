Graham Coombs left this review about Wendron New Inn

A cosy little stone-built pub retaining a two room layout. The left hand side is a fairly plush bar, with stone walls, while the right hand side is a smartly laid-up dining area. Garden at rear. Draught beers were Tribute and Lushingtons.

Gill Smith left this review about Wendron New Inn

Caught the 34 bus from Redruth yesterday to visit this 2011 Good Beer Guide pub, and found it to be a cosy friendly pub. 2 people were having a meal and there was one of the locals chatting to them with the landlord. There were 3 ales available, and we chose Skinners Betty Stogs and Dartmoor Friggin, both served in excellent condition. Food menu looked good, but time we had before the next bus to Helston limited our choice to soup, and it was a very tasty mushroom soup served up with thick bread and loads of butter. The bar area was clean and tidy, and the room behind was laid out as a restaurant. There is a beer garden for sunny days.

On 8th June 2011 - rating: 8
