Alan Winfield left this review about Ranelagh Arms

The Ranelagh Arms is a decent looking well kept back street pub.
Once inside the pub still has two seperate rooms,there is a small oblong shaped bar to the right which was empty on my early evening visit,the lounge to the left is larger and squareish in shape,this room was very busy with a good mix of drinkers.
There was comfy bench seating to the front and a raised area to the left,the room was carpeted and looked quite smart.
The pub is a Charles Wells tied house,the only one i found in a town which used to have load of their houses,there were two real ales on the bar,i had a drink of Charles Wells Eagle bitter which went down very well,the other beer was Courage Directors.
The pub had a good lively atmosphere,there was some very good music playing at quite a loud volume,just how a like it.
I really enjoyed my short time in this pub.

Pub visited 21/6/2014
