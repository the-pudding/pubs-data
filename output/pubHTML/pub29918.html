Will Larter left this review about The Shoulder Of Mutton

Home of the Weldon microbrewery, this large pub has the look of a village institute or club, with the large room at the front recently converted from smaller rooms providing an almost hall-like space. There were six hand pumps but just three beers on at the time of our visit, only one of these being their own: Dragline. The other two were from Oakham Brewery (Green Devil and Red Macaw). There were a few locals in, but it was fairly quiet for early Sunday afternoon. Not really my sort of place, with the tables and chairs too regularly spaced giving a school dining room feel, and a TV set in the corner showing music videos, which is something I can do without.

Date of visit: 24th August 2014
