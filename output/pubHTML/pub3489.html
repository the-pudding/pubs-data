Nick Willis left this review about Crown Inn

Food orientated pub with 6 real ales on, 4 from Marstons and 2 others. The Salopian Vortex was in excellent nick. Plenty of families eating here. The car park would be very crowded when filling up as they've tried to cram as many marked spaces as they can.
