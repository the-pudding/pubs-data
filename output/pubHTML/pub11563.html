john mcgraw left this review about The Anchor Inn

Great pub selling the full range of Shepherd Neames beers in a very nice location next to the river.A single front bar with a consevatory at the back which overlooks the river and where most of the food is served.There is an outside terrace at the side of the river where you can while away the long summer days.Very friendly staff.Didn't try the food but it looked very nice.Good bus service from Colchester.

On 7th November 2007 - rating: 10
