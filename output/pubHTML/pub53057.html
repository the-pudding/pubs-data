Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about West End Tavern

Popular village local at lunchtimes and in the early evening, with a reasonable 'pub food' menu. Traditional furniture and decor in the three distinct parts of the bar, offering a range of seating options (but mind your head at the low spot...). Compact patio beer garden to the rear. Friendly management. Usually has the regular Harveys plus a guest (which happened to be the splendid Harvey's Old, £3.95, on my latest visit) from the two pairs of handpumps.
