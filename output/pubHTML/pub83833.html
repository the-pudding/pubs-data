Komakino . left this review about Bricklayers Arms

A cavernous barn-like two-room 30s-build hidden in the depths of Downley on top of the valley which keeps a large room to the right for dining and a separate one to the right for drinking accessed via a closed door. Seems to offer everything under the sun in terms of entertainment, with ads for a Quiz Night, Live Music and Karaoke along with a pool table, a pinball machine and large screen, presumably for sporting events. Rebellion IPA and Roasted Nuts were the two ale options.

On 10th March 2017 - rating: 4
