ROB Camra left this review about The Cross Keys Hotel

This is a massive, one roomed oub, with an equally large bar. Lots of vinyl rcords on the wall and lots of differing areas. Some large TV screens, including one in the smokers covered area outside. Two pool tables, two quiz machines and two handpumps, but, sadly, not much in the way of character. The hotel prices are very cheap and seem to be aimed at contractors. Certainly I wouldn't want to be staying here. The two beers were Copper Dragon Golden Pippin and Bank Top Flat Cap. The Bank Top was in decent nick and was only £2 per pint. This one should really be a lot better than it is.
