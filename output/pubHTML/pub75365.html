Alan Hurdle left this review about Bryn Howel Hotel

Large Hotel in its own grounds signposted off the A539 between Trevor an Llangollen. It retains the elegance of its former status as a country residence. The wood panneled bar has an original log burning fireplace. As with other "Llangollen Hotels" in this (up for sale) group, no Real Ales are served.
