Alan Winfield left this review about Manvers Arms

The Manvers Arms used to be a Shipstones tied house and it was a fairly decent pub once inside.
There were two real ales on the bar with both Shipstones mild and bitter being very nice drinks here.
I am not sure if the pub has real ale on now as i did'nt go in when i took this photo.
