ROB Camra left this review about Firk Inn Pub

This is a small pub rather than a micropub. They sell all the standard lagers etc alongside cask ales and real ciders. The clientele were a bit rough and ready to say the least on our Tuesday afternoon visit, but they all seemed harmless enough. It's one small room with a fruit machine and a quiz machine. Three beers available and my pint was in good nick. I doubt that we'll bother with this one again though.
