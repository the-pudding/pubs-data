Reviews of The Snug (Average Rating: 2 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Komakino . left this review about The Snug

A chain cocktail bar diagonally opposite The Angel Vaults, this had a disinterested bouncer outside at 4pm on a Bank Holiday August Sunday. No real ale: there were four keg beers (Birra Moretti, Amstel, San Miguel and something called Maltsmiths IPA) and three bottled beers (Heineken, Sol and BrewDog Punk IPA - a warm bottle of which set me back £4.10). A great Motown soundtrack played in the background, but if you're over 30, expect a struggle to get served. I waited over 5 minutes for a girl to complete two cocktails, whilst two male members of staff dicked around in the background.

On 17th September 2018 - rating: 2
