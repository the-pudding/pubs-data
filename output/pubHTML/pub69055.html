Colin Walker left this review about Collage Corner Bar

The Collage Corner Bar situated less than 100 metres from Glasgow Central train station. The pub is part of the Radisson Blu Hotel building, which is just round the corner on Argyle Street. The place has floor to ceiling windows, which makes it quite difficult to see inside on a sunny day, although inside you can watch street life in Glasgow go by. The pub is a past winner of the Theme Bar Design Award for Scotland and is famed for their burgers and beers from around the world. It is very stylish inside and may not suit the traditionalist pub customer. The pub is open Tuesday to Thursday 4pm to 12am, Friday 3pm to 12am & Saturday 12pm to 1am. But, is closed Sunday & Monday.
