Graham Coombs left this review about Wheal Dream

A modern golf and football complex which has a small modern bar in a white-painted room. Behind there is a restaurant/carvery which also caters for functions. Betty Stogs and Porthleven were available on handpump and seemed in good order. Not a traditional pub but ok for a pint, especially if eating.

On 16th September 2018 - rating: 6

Gill Smith left this review about Wheal Dream

This is a bar and restaurant alongside a golf course just outside Wendron near Helston. We went there for a real ale beer festival held in grounds alongside the bar/restaurant. They had a couple of handpulls on in the bar. Large car park and outside drinking area.
