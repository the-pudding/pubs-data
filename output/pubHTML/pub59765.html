Please Note: This review is over a year old.

John Trim left this review about The Trooper Inn

This was my first visit to the Trooper Inn. It rates really as an eatery. The food is good but pricey, I paid £17.00 for a dish of lamb cutlets. The beer is well kept but the range is a little limited. Not really my type of establishment & I really objected to the 10% service charge levied on a party of 8 or more. We were 10 walkers in for lunch. I complained & the charge was dropped. The upshot was that I did not feel disposed to leave a tip. A roadside pub & not for locals or discerning customers who want a bit more character in their pub.I rate it a 6.

On 25th March 2012 - rating: 6
