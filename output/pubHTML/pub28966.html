Alan Winfield left this review about The Black Swan

The Black Swan is a decent pub and it was quite smart inside with carpets throughout and comfortable seating.The front area of the pub was for drinkers and the rear was set out for diners but nobody was eating on my visit.
This used to be a Mansfield tied house (now Marstons)and it still had the Mansfield signs outside,there were no real ales on here withe the only beers being Mansfield keg beers so i had a Guinness.
This was a decent pub that would be improved if a real ale was available.
