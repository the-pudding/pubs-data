E TA left this review about Hare & Hounds

An award winning roadside inn which claims not to be a gastro but looks like one and sells bistro-style food as well as good quality pub grub. It also has a GBG entry. From the car park, which is adjacent to a large garden, you enter through a small bar which has the air of a funeral parlour, while the larger main bar is cosier and more like a pub/restaurant. There are plenty of timbers and stone features on display, and there are other country pub cliches like over-stated exposed fireplaces and flag flooring dotted around. The staff are very friendly but do look a bit silly in their black uniform of polo shirt and baseball cap. They run regular quiz nights and meal deals, and there is a thriving local cadre. Four ales on, I had Bath Ales's Gem which was pretty good. Very happy to stop here for food and drink when passing and I would definitely include in a crawl.

On 20th November 2014 - rating: 7
