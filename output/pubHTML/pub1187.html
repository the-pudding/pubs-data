Komakino . left this review about The Clay Pipe

A newish estate pub next to a Tesco Express which had a sea of blokes watching a Premier League at full volume match on various large screens on my Sunday afternoon visit. They offer food and there seemed to be a quieter, more presentable room to the right perhaps designated for this, with the room I entered into bare-boarded with various high tables and chairs, a couple of pool tables and dartboard. Ales were from the unimaginative front being Sharp's Doom Bar and Fuller's London Pride. Based on my visit, I think I'd have had more fun in the Tesco Express next door.

On 13th March 2017 - rating: 3
