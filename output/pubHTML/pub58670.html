Reviews of The Bell Inn (Average Rating: 7 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Graham Coombs left this review about The Bell Inn

A large and smartly furnished old pub, strategically on a main road located by the river bridge, guaranteeing a heavy throughput of tourists. The interior seems to be a rather long and narrow series of rooms with a bar running the length of them, leading through to a restaurant area, all in an aged brewer's tudor feel. Outside there is an extensive lawned area leading down to its own moorings at the water's edge. Seemed to have mainly local ales on offer and in fair condition.
