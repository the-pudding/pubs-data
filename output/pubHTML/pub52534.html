Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Angel

Prominent village pub retaining separate public and lounge bars. Dining area and restaurant beyond. Traditional furniture and decor throughout. Pool table. Reasonably-priced menu. Two of three handpumps in operation, offering Pride and Wherry (not very good unfortunately). Overall: nice pub, shame about the beer. (NB - Review dates from August 2012.)
