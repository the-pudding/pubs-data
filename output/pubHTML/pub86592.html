Real Ale Ray left this review about Tap & T'ales

A new fledgling micropub which was converted from a former shop. The owner also has the Tap & Vent micropub in Longridge. He told us that he saw us looking through the window at his Longridge micro around 3pm and that he would have let us in for drink earlier than the 4pm opening time, especially as we were visitors to the area.
The décor in this micro was pretty similar to that of the Tap & Vent. Six handpumps with two ciders and four ales. We went for the Faversham Steam Brewery Whitstable Pale, which was excellent. The owner told us that he used to work for the Faversham Steam Brewery and still enjoys their beers.

On 18th July 2018 - rating: 7
