Alan Winfield left this review about Gardeners Rest

The Gardeners Rest is situated on a small street just off the main Biddulph to Tunstall road in the village of Brindley Ford.
Once inside the pub has a single square shaped room with the bar facing,the room is carpeted and has bench seating and normal tables and chairs,there is a darts board to the front right and tables skittles table to the rear right,a TV was showing Rugby.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Brew X1 this was a decent drink.
The pub was very busy on my Saturday afternoon visit,unlike the two pubs further up the road.
This is a nice livly pub.

Pub visited 23/8/2014
