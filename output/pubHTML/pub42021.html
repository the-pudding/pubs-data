John Bloomfield left this review about Crown & Trumpet Inn

Cotswold stone inn, complete with oak beams and log fires along with plenty of Flowers Brewery memorabilia. The menu offers pub grub featuring locally grown produce, making it popular with locals, tourists and walkers. It also offers an unusual range of pub games, and entertainment including live music Jazz and Blues nights.Timothy Taylors Landlord, Stanway beers that alternate throughout the seasons of the year and other guest beers are sourced from Gloucestershire micro breweries, many different Gins available

peter ashworth left this review about Crown & Trumpet Inn

The Crown And Trumpet is a quaint old fashioned pub that is just off the main road that runs through Broadway.On entry the small beamed bar is to your left with a seating area in front of this.There are more seats in the central area and to the right is an area set out for diners.Over the bar are a few framed beer awards plus a great deal of other miscellany including old glass bottles,horse brasses and a piano
There were 2 cask ciders and 6 cask ales on show on our Friday lunchtime visit with the Artist's Ale from the local Stanway Brewery being on top form
Definitely worth a visit if you are in the area.
