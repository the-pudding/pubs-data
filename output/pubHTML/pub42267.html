Real Ale Ray left this review about Wyche Inn

This is a popular stop off point for walkers, as they serve good food and well kept beer. Also the pub has a very good seated patio area with great views. The interior is comfortable with lots of seating areas, also there is a further lounge area with two pool tables on a lower level. Wyche is the local name for salt and you can see the original pathway at the rear of the pub, where the salt was drawn. There were four ales on handpump, we went for the Hobson's Bitter and Wood Street Snowbell.
