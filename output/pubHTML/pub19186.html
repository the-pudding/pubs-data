Colin Walker left this review about The Bug

The former Prince Arthur Hotel is now called The Bug. The place is for drinkers who like cheap drinks and there are always plenty of them on offer. The place is family friendly during the day but does get rowdy, but not violent during the weekend evening. The interior was upgraded with new seating a few years. No real ale.
