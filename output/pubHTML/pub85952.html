Komakino . left this review about Maypole Farm

A Farmhouse Inn that has recently sprung up on a new estate just off the Kempston junction of the A421 and vies for trade with the next door Brewers Fayre. Carvery is the shtick here, the business area of which is located to the left of the entrance-facing bar. Four pumps served up GK IPA x 2 and Abbot Ale x 2 - £1.75 for a half of a decent enough Abbot. Only really worth visiting if a) you're staying at the nearby Premier Inn, b) peckish on a long journey where this is the midpoint and carvery floats your boat, or c) a completist.
