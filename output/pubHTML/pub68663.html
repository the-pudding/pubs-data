Michelle Holmes left this review about Revolution

Smart trendies bar, phenomenally overpriced drinks. With 2 people waiting 2 bar staff on and the manager quite keen to just štand behind the bar without getting her handss 'dirty' so to speak, definately room for improvement. Loads of variety on the drinks menu, typical modern pub food on offer. Children allowed, easily accessible, however toilets upstairs. I would only recommend taking older children in.

Chester Tourist left this review about Revolution

Trendy Vodka Bar located in the City Centre, frequented mostly by the 20 something crowd. Spacious modern inside with a dance floor at the back. Toilets are upstairs. Lively at weekends, you have to pay (£2?) to get in after 9pm on Saturdays. Italian food menu available. Vodka cocktails and mixers available. Seating is on both sides and there is an open area next to the bar. Drinks are quite expensive.
