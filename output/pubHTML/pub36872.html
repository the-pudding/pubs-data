Nick Davies left this review about Cottage Of Content

Nice old two roomed pub in the middle of the village. Main room is a traditional low beamed stone floor affair with a dining room to the right. There's outdoor seating to the front and another area out the back. Service is friendly if a but haphazard, it took a while to extract when ended up as a very nice pint of Ubu. There was also Doombar available, which is a pity. Food is the usual pub fare, and you would probably be wise to avoid Sunday lunchtimes unless it's a nice day and you can sit outside due to The Curse Of The Sunday Roast. Regular live music and they run a fully serviced campsite in season.

Well worth a visit and superior to the choices in nearby Bidford.

On 8th May 2016 - rating: 7
