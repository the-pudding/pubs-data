S W left this review about The Green Dragon

Recently taken over and a great refurbishment completed (albeit bits in the courtyard). Serving good fresh food sourced locally as much as possible, real ales, traditional cider, great wine list, craft beers/ciders/spirits with no sign of the usual suspects on the bar which was refreshing. Not as refreshing as the Gin menu...hmmmm.

This pub is growing steadily and they seem to be getting it right.

Well worth a visit
