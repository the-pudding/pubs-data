Komakino . left this review about The Griffin

A Hungry Horse-style pub in the Rugby outskirts, which is a two-roomer - to the left a bar area with pool table centre-stage and quieter, raised area far left. All dining-based on yer right. Three pumps, but just one clipped with our old friend Sharp's Doom Bar. Not worth the bother

On 10th May 2017 - rating: 2
