Will Larter left this review about Sango Sands Oasis

A smartly presented bar adjacent to and apparently in the same ownership as the caravan and camping site on the cliffs above Sango Bay. There's a fairly recent brick-built extension with wide windows for the view, the main building has wood panelling and stained glass windows in the partitions. On the bar, it's keg only, as is typical in many parts of Scotland, but they do have Orkney ales in bottles - these are kept in the chiller display cabinet behind the bar, but if you ask they will bring you one from store which will be at the right temperature for drinking.

There's a horrendously loud juke box, but fortunately there are no loudspeakers installed in the extension, which makes the noise level just about OK for a normal conversation.
