Graham Coombs left this review about Three Horseshoes

Light and bright triangular pub on road junction on way out of town. Very clean and tidy, nicely laid out with eating tables towards the rear, a good drinking area around the central bar, and a games area in the pointy bit. Nice welcome and very helpful staff. Draught beers seemed to be Brakspears Bitter and monthly special; former very well kept. Food not sampled but sounded good.

Steve of N21 left this review about The Three Horseshoes

This is a generic local a little way out of Henley on the Reading Road.
I presume it was once a two bar pub but now knocked through into one large roomed place. It still keeps separate areas with there being a public type section supporting a pool table to the left of the bar as you walk in, and then a larger carpeted lounge area to the right which supports a wood floored dining area right at the end. There is a flat screen tele in the lounge section for the sport but it's tucked away close to the bar area and not obtusive.
It's a fairly standard back street boozer with no great redeeming features, but the lunchtime food we had could not be faulted and although we didn't appreciate it at the time, the Brakespears Beers we had here were probably the best kept of the weekend.

On 8th October 2009 - rating: 6
