Al Bundy left this review about Caradoc Hotel

Open. This pub has now reopened. Its a large looking Victorian pub. Situated directly opposite the docks this is a traditional looking pub. Inside is two roomed. The first room you enter is an empty room with no tables and only chairs pushed up against the walls. A dart board is here too. To your left through some doors is the lounge. A fairly mundane area with a dance floor bit to the side. A pool table is also in the room but could very well get rolled into the vacant room whenever the pub is busy. No real ale so just Fosters, Carling, Smiths, Strongbow and Guinness on draught.

On 19th September 2017 - rating: 5
