ROB Camra left this review about The Milestone

We have called in twice and eaten both times since my last review. The food is excellent and the beer is still good too.

13/11/2012
This is mainly a restaurant, but it's open from 12 - 11 and they only serve food from 12 - 3 and 5 - 9 so they must expect drinkers. It's very nicely done out inside with several dining areas with the main room doubling up as a drinking area. There are also stools around the bar. There are 2 handpumps on the bar serving changing guests. I had a pint of Sheffield Seven Hills which was in good nick. The staff were very friendly as well. I think we may well eat here on our next visit as the menu looked great and the food being served did too.

On 3rd December 2013 - rating: 8

Nissed out on this one during my visit to Sheffield last week, but have been in a few times over the last couple of years as they usually have a couple of ales on the bar, often from Kelham Island and Wentworth breweries. There was always an interesting menu although we never ate there, and it was a clean and tidy bar.
