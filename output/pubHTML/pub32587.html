Alan Winfield left this review about The Talbot Inn

The Talbot Inn is a decent looking mock tudor pub that is just off the town centre.
Once inside the pub has two seperate rooms,we had a drink in the room to the left which is the lounge,this room is square shaped with the bar facing,the room is carpeted and has bench seating and small stools,it seemed a bit down at heel to me and in need of a slight refurb.
The bar on the right side is oblong shaped and quite basic with bench seating a pool table and darts board.
There was one real ale on the bar which was Pedigree,this was a decent enough drink.
This is a pretty basic pub.
