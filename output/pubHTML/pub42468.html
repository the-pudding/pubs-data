Ian Mapp left this review about Manor Arms Hotel

Had a period of being closed down but now reopened/refurbished as a country dining inn. All tables setup for dining and the inside has been knocked through around a central bar.

Four real ales on - Ludlow Gold, a couple from Wye Valley and one I fail to remember. HPA in good condition.

Sunday lunch was superb. Excellent service.
