Oggwyn Great left this review about Urbano 32

Well i would not class this as a pub , but it has been added so here is a review .
This is a Pizza Restaurant , enter past a couple of tables on the street down some steps , it has a long narrow interior , a small bar on the left with seating to the right then past the bar seating to both sides , there is an open kitchen towards the rear which you pass on the way to the toilets with their strange saloon style doors .
Only draught beer is Moretti , there is a selection of canned craft beers and lagers .
Staff are young and friendly , its OK if you want a Pizza but i would not recomend it as a pub .
