Alan Winfield left this review about Royal Oak Inn

The Royal Oak is a largish stand alone pub that is set back and above the road.
There were a few tables at the front of the pub and once inside there was a small bar facing this area had a stone floor,there was another small room to the left which had carpets and normal tables and chairs,to the right was a larger raised seating area this had bench seating and small tables and stools and to the rear of this room there was a pool room which had bare boards and looked quite nice in my opinion.
There were six real ales on the bar i had a drink of Coniston Bluebird and this was a very nice drink, the other beers were Taylor Landlord, Jennings Cocker Hoop / Cumberland,Pedigree and Tetley bitter.
This was a very pleasant pub to have a drink in and i thought it was a very nice pub.

Pub visited 21/8/2011
