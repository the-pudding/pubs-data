Aqualung . left this review about The Red Lion(JD Wetherspoon)

This one looks like a conversion of an original pub. The entrance has low tables and chairs with the bar area at the rear in what looks like a modern extension. There's a decent size patio to the right as well as a car park. There's a fire station across the road so you don't need to worry about the place burning down.
The bar has ten hand pumps which had three available soon, Abbot, Doom Bore, Bullmastiff Welsh Red, Phoenix Double Gold, Shepherd Neame Bishops Finger, Yeovil Star Gazer and Borough Iron Runner. I went for the Double Gold (£2.09) which was in excellent nick.
I thought this was a decent Spoons and it's GBG 2017 listed.

On 20th March 2017 - rating: 7
