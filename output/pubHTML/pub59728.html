Richard Arwkrite left this review about The East End Arms

Located on a isolated roadside position parking can be a bit of a problem.The pub has a small old fashioned bar with friendly locals. Ringwood bitter and 49er were on tap and ok. The pub has a seperate room for eating and a small garden area.Popular with walkers and solo cyclists on our visit.
