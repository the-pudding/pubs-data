Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Taproom TN23

First Hop Stuff taproom outside of London, housed in a corner building with several previous uses as one sort of bar or another. Stripped right out and redecorated in a semi-industrial style, this place has plenty of space and is currently sparsely furnished. Additionally, some canteen tables can also be found out on the pavement on one side. Four of their brews, five guests from the likes of Wiper & True, Deya, Angels & Demons and Wild Beer and a cider available from the ten craft keg taps on the bar-back, but I went for the Old Dairy Copper Top (£4.50) which was the solitary real ale available from the pair of handpumps.

On 19th May 2019 - rating: 6
