Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Sports Bar & Grill

Large new bar / restaurant in Crossrail Place. Anonymous modern furniture and decor in a variety of seating / dining areas. As you would expect from the name, plenty of sports TVs (although most on different channels so hard to follow) but it's not really what most people would expect from the name. Just EPA, Brakspear Bitter and Pedigree (£4.60) available from a total of nine handpumps on the main counter and another trio from the smaller island bar at the back.
