Quinno _ left this review about The Goose in Bracknell

Formerly the Market Inn, the pub is located directly outside the main entrance to Bracknell BR in a Victorian building. It’s a Goose chain pub, so expect the usual sub-JDW airport lounge fittings and cheap fried food. My visit yesterday at least found a number of decent ales (Pride, Bombardier, Doom Bar, Marstons EPA and, surprisingly, Hammerpot) competitively-priced (less than £3.50 for a pint of the aforementioned and a cola). The three I tried were all in fairly decent shape, if a bit on the cold side. Staff were generally pleasant unlike some the punters, two of whom were sub-TOWIE. Sticky tables, muted sports TV and chart muzak complete the ‘ambience’. I’ll be generous due the cheap and fairly decent ales.
