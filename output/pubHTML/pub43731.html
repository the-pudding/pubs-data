Steve C left this review about Prince of Wales

The Prince of Wales is down a side street just off the main drag so can be easily missed by those not in the know. I popped in on a recent Monday evening to find a handful of boisterous locals in the small left hand bar area. The right hand bar was empty, as was the large dining/function room at the rear. I noted advertisements for a beer garden, but I did not investigate on this occasion.
During my visit some sporadic music emanated from the jukebox and I was able to enjoy some darts practice as nobody was using the adjacent pool table. I counted three hand pumps on the bar, but two of these were unused which left Nut Brown Ale as the lone cask option amongst the standard keg.
This pub has odd opening times: Mon noon – 23:45; Tue - Thu 16:00 - 23:45; Fri & Sat noon – 00:45, Sun noon – 23:45.
