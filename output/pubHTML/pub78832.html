Colin Walker left this review about Corbie Inn

The Corbie Inn opened with its present name in 2011 Six ales can normally be found on tap, one of which comes from the independent Kinneil Brew House at the back of the premises, others mostly from other Scottish Breweries although the a few from south of the border do appear. The main building on the right is the main pub section with the restaurant to the left. There is an encloded beer garden at the rear. There is a car parking area to the right of the building. As per other posts below the pub has won many awards.
