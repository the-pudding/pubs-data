ROB Camra left this review about Copperfields (Grand Hotel)

Smart and friendly bar which is part of the Grand Hotel. We called in on a Monday lunchtime at about 12:30 and there were already a few people in. It's one fairly compact room with a raised area at the rear. 4 real ales available of which one is always from the nearby Cullercoats brewery. When in Rome etc, so I had a pint of the Cullercoats Jack The Devil which was in good nick. The prices are reasonable for the surroundings and there's a 20p per pint CAMRA discount. It's a bit out of the way as all the other pubs in Tynemouth are on or around Front Street, but it's well worth the pleasant walk along the seafront. We both liked it here and will definitely call in again next time we visit Tynemouth.

On 2nd March 2015 - rating: 8
