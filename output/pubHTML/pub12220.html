andrette baker left this review about The Castle

well im shocked to have just read the review about the ruined wedding at the castle and find this hard to believe as i had my 40th birthday party at this venue and my friend had her wedding reception there too,and i can say hand on heart that the management and staff were FIRST CLASS and totally professional.Nothing was too much trouble and was also offered a late licence if i required one which i took at NO EXTRA CHARGE.All i can say that there must have been some major sort of problem for them to want to close early what happened? did all your guests leave early? I know for a fact that this pub does stay open late so im sorry but i totally reccommend this venue to anyone wanting a lovely cosy and welcoming occasion. Well done!
