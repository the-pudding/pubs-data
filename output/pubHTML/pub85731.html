Al Bundy left this review about The Buyers Club

By where The Flying Picket was this is a craft beer/cocktail bar hidden down an alleyway and to the right in a courtyard at the side of the Old Blind School building. Its a thin wide bar and sort of trendy. Its a single room and the long bar has no handpumps or beer taps on the counter. There are, however, 8 taps, in two banks of four, on the wall behind dispensing crafty beers. Its not my kinda place but its not bad.
