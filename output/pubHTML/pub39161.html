Soup Dragon left this review about The Dartmouth Arms

A nice detached extended cottage-styled pub in white render. It has both a patio area and a large beer garden to the side. There is a separate builing which houses 'Little Italy', an Italian restaurant.

The interior has a couple of rooms connected at the back by a corridor that also takes you to the restaurant. The room to the right on entry is a lounge, with table and is in cream and green, with cream ceiling and wood floor. It has a fireplace and some plates and old pictures provide interest. The room to the left is a ber room, with a pool table. It is in red and cream, with a red tile floor. It has a large fireplace, with small stove fire. Again, lots of old pictures adorn the walls and there are some pewter jugs. There was no TV that i saw and the music was general and at a decent level. There service was very friedly and there were just a few people in, but of mixed ages.

Beer; usual tap stuff with Peroni and Birra Moretti (not really a surprise with the Italian resturant) with Enville, Enville Porter, Holdens Golden Glow, Theakston's Bitter and Old Swan (Ma Pardoes) Original MILD. The Original, Glow, Porter and Enville were all class - God, what a great choice.

What can i say? This place was excellent, the kind of place you could spend all day in, indeed, and the following day......
