Komakino . left this review about Brewers Fayre Elmbury Lodge

A Brewers Fayre with bolt-on Premier Inn next door, serving an industrial estate next to M5 Junction 9. Three real ale stumps - two with clips turned around (for Sharp's Doom Bar and Brain's Rev James) and one unclipped. There's a niceish garden out front, but the overloud muzak was a little unedifying and as it's out of the way, it's a tricky tick.

On 28th March 2019 - rating: 2
