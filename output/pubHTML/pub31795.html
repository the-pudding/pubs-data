Quinno _ left this review about Royal Standard

A Greene King pub by the (in)famous shark-roofed house. Made-over but has a couple of homely touches inside - rugs, real fire etc. Friendly staff member on a break said hello to us as we entered. Mute Sky News on TV. A sign by the fruit machine said that drinks would be delivered direct to you if you were having a session on it - unusual. Four ales; GK’s Golden Hen, Abbot and IPA with St Austell Proper Job as a guest, which was in excellent condition. Not too bad.

On 20th May 2014 - rating: 7
