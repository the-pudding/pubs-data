Jonathan Wilde left this review about The Lamb

This pub essentially remains the same since my last visit save for the addition of a Cooperative convenience store and flats built in the garden, as you do. A large open room pub with small back room to the right with a pool table in it and a quieter seating area mirrored on the left.

There are 4 hand pumps serving a mixture of ale, cider and even some mild available.

The front has undergone a facelift as part of the development of the site with some of the previous parking area replaced with a smaller area, looking more for use by tenants and staff, but now with seating area to replace what has been lost in the way of gardens. The function room was lost in the development of the site but at least it remains a community asset. Just shows what joined up thinking can achieve.

Paul Clifford left this review about The Lamb

The Lamb in Cadbury Heath has been shut since May 2008 and was closed due to trouble over two weekends. It is due to re-open in mid November 2008 with new Managers after a full refurb. However there will be no live music, jukebox or pool table and, due to licencing requirements door staff will be employed on weekends.
