Dan Mckinley left this review about The Oddfellows

This really great pub is in a fantastic position, only 1 minutes walk from the beach in Lowestoft. The decor is excellent after a recent refurb(wood floors etc), The beer is fantastic (Adnams pub so obviously good beer) with an vast selection + ciders. Also the food is worth a visit. There is Outside space for those who want to drink in the sun. One of the best pubs in town and easily the best for character!!!
