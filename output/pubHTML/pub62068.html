Andrew Miller left this review about Woodlands Tavern

The Woodlands is a great little pub in a small village. Bit hard to find but that just gives it more charm! The food when I and my wife eat there is always excellent, nothing like the normal pub-grub you find in some places. A great selection of beers is always available and my tells me that the wine list is pretty strong as well!

I would highly recommend anyone living in the area to find the place and visit, and if your around the area make a trip to vist - it is well worth while.

Andrew

On 17th September 2010 - rating: 10
