Reviews of Hoggs 'n' Heifers (Average Rating: 9 of 10) Add Review see review guidelines

Jon B left this review about Hoggs 'n' Heifers

I think this is the one pub in Higher Bentham that you need to visit, for what its worth. A free house in a converted... something or other, I don’t know. Bare brick walls all around and a clean comfortable room to drink in. Possibly/probably quite a lively atmosphere on a night. Real ales from Cross Bay, Kirkby Lonsdale and Dark Horse and my pint of Zenith from the former was spot on.

On 4th August 2019 - rating: 9
