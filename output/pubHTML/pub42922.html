Alan Winfield left this review about The Slip Inn

The Slip Inn is in the middle of Morley.
I thought this looked like a decent Tetley house so i thought i would get a decent drink here i was wrong there was no real ale on here only John Smiths smooth which i dont drink so i had a Guinness.
The pub was quite smart inside and also very busy but it would have been a lot better with a decent beer on.
