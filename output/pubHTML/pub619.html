john mcgraw left this review about Royal Oak

Large single bar pub with seperate dining area. Although the emphasis is on food(which looked very good and substantial)they sell 3 real ales,two of which were from the Great Western Brewery Bristol. Both were in excellent condition especially "Classic Gold"
