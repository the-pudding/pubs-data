Komakino . left this review about The Bell

One of three pubs surrounding Toddington's Market Square, this has a snorkel-shape with a central bar up a step, an open pool anteroom hidden behind the bar and a largish, oblong bar area which leads to a rear beer garden. Just one handpump dispensing GK IPA - but not, as it "wasn't on" despite the clip facing outwards. A handful of good-spirited locals were having a laugh around the bar, but I decided against hanging around and joining them.

On 26th September 2017 - rating: 4
