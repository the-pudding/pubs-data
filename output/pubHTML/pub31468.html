Alan Winfield left this review about The Star Inn

The Star Inn is a nice looking stone built pub.
Once inside there was a room to the left which had the bar in it,this room was divided into two different areas,the room had a stone floor and normal tables and chairs there was also a tropical fish tank which looked quite nice.
The pub was very busy with most people having meals but none of the tables were laid out for diners so as a drinker i could sit anywhere in the pub.
This pub is a Hook Norton tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Hookey bitter and this was a really nice drink the other beer was Old Hookey.
This was a decent pub with very nice beer.

Pub visited 19/5/2012

michael furn left this review about The Star Inn

The pub is situated across road from Sulgrave Manor.
Its has a good Hook Norton beer range, plus good food.
As a daytime bus service 508 Brackley to Banbury.
The Pub as also featured for a number of years, for its beer quality, and is highly recommended.
Pub opening hours are 1200 -1500 and 1800 2300 Tuesday to Saturday , and Sunday 1200-1600.
Meals are basic , and not so much Gastro , Sunday lunch £9.50p with a full plate.
The pub has 4 en suite rooms available for close by Silverstone Circuit.
and Bee who helps out at the bar, ring 01295 760389.
