Haydn Lamb left this review about The Drum and Monkey

The Drum and Monkey near Upton Upon Severn is a delightful 17th Century Inn with log fires in winter. Traditional and reasonably priced bar food is available along with good real ales and fine wines and there is a good children’s menu. Outside there is a lovely courtyard garden. I have eaten here several times with my wife and family lately and found it excellent.

On 8th September 2010 - rating: 9
