Bucking Fastard left this review about The Bishop's Cave

A shop conversion,you enter past ranks of thin high tables and chairs,with a tiny bar midbuilding on the right,then a modest cheese counter at the rear with a few fridges for wine and bottled beer.
There are just 3 kegged beers,comprising a continental lager and two mainstream craft beers,which didn't excite.
There is occasional live music nights where booking is advised,and quiz nights but the feel here is more smart wine bar than boozer.There is a more impressive sister operation in St Albans.
