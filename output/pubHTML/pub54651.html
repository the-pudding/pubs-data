Clive Thompson left this review about Daly's Public House

This pub used to be called Daly’s but is now the Dome Bar, which I think may have been a previous name. It’s a reasonable pub that is good for watching sports as they have three plasma screens that can show different channels. It’s also popular for live music and they often have tribute bands on. Popular darts pub and has it’s own team. No real ale and only standard lagers but serves a decent pint of Guinness.
