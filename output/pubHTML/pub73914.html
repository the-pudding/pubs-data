Quinno _ left this review about The Swan Inn

Two-roomed locals place. Left is a dining area with no pumps - these are in the right bar. Three in total with two on, Wickwar BOB (fine) and Sharps Atlantic. Modern interior with tiled floor, light pine effect bar and furnishings. Oddly, two framed pics of 18th century military personnel with dogs heads, eurgh. Real fire unlit. Pool, darts and two screens with Sky Sports News. Decent locals place if not exactly stand-out in any way.
