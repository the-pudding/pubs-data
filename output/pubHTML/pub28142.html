Philip Carter left this review about Eels Foot Inn

Large pub, from the outside but the bar area is small, lots of staff so waiting time is short. Gets busy when the sun is out, as it has a large garden next to one of the broads. has rowing boats for hire. Sells food all day. Sells real ale, Adnams & GK IPA on sale at the time.
