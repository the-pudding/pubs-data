Al Bundy left this review about Last Orders at the Dog and Partridge

This now seems to be called The Dog And Partridge. Whether its part of the Last Orders chain I don't know but it used to be/is a Last Orders. A long pub with a variety of edukashunaly challenged alckies in today including a Rocky Marciano bloke who seemed to be challenging the world for whatever title his mind was racing through. 2 handpumps on the bar with only Theakstons Bitter on. A small area consists of about 4 or 5 fruit machines. To the rear was what seemed to amount to a boxing ring with a pool table in. Its the kind of place you should go if you're feeling down cos all you need to to is take a look around at some of the people in here and you will feel a whole lot better about yourself. And this was of an afternoon.
