I L left this review about The Red Lion

This Victorian pub consists of three separate drinking areas all with access to the bar. Upon entering via the street, the room to the right is accessed only via this door and has a small serving hatch to the bar. The main two rooms are accessed to the left but also from the side entrance where there is also a covered smoking area. The front room leads through to the rear drinking area and both these have the main bar area shared between them. The few times we have been in here it has been busy with a wide range of age groups. Bar staff are friendly making this a great atmosphere and well worth visiting let alone the 8 real ales on during our recent visit. All the ones we tried were well kept including a very nice pint of Theakstons Mild.

On 23rd October 2010 - rating: 9
