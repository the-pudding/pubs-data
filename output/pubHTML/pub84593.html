moby duck left this review about Koffee Pot

Only just realised this is on the site as a bar, Its certainly a cafe by day, the breakfasts were alright, I had a couple here. Apparently its different by night serving craft beers, cocktails and spirits. As a Cafe Its a solid 7/10, but I can't rate it as a bar as I didn't experience that.

ROB Camra left this review about The Koffee Pot

As Al says this is a café that happens to sell draught ale. It still feels like a café though with cheap tables and chairs and a pervading smell of chips and coffee. I think Al's been very generous with his score of 4.

Al Bundy left this review about The Koffee Pot

Though it sounds like its just a cafe it is also a bar. It has 2 banks of T-bars serving a variety of lager-a-likes. A single room with wooden benches in the middle. A rear area was separated by a curtain on this early evening visit. No real ale though I believe I saw some bottled stuff advertised
