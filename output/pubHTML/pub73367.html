Colin Walker left this review about Broadwood Farm

This pub is part of Crown Carveries. The drink on offer available is two bottles for £5, choose from Becks, Corona, Budweiser, WKD, or Smirnoff Ice , Stella 4% and cask ale for just £2.25 a pint, John Smiths for just £2.40 a pint & Cocktails for just £3.75 each or £6 for two with pitchers at only £8.49. There is a pub quiz every Thursday. The pub is open 11am - 11pm. Breakfast is available Mon-Sun: 9am - 11.30am and you can each as much as you want as it is unlimited. The Carvery is available Mon-Sun: 11.30am - 9.30pm. Parking is available in front of the building.
