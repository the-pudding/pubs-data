Quinno _ left this review about World Turned Upside Down

This place has got pretty battered in the interim since my last visit and now desperately needs TLC as the newly-opened Trooper Potts 5 minutes away will surely clean out 50% of this place’s casual punters. Three pumps all clipped with Doom Bar which in fairness was one of the best examples of this liquid I’ve had for a good while and drinking it actually verged on the enjoyable rather than the tolerable. Service behind the bar was the friendly side of Whitleyesque.

March 2011
Carvery outlet that serves a couple of cheap ales (usually Wadworth 6X and London Pride). Bar to right as you enter, with a fairly decent window seating area. To the rear is a large raised area where the hot meat action takes place. Nothing to write home about but the last place left in Whitley to get a decent drink so that's worth something.
