Al Bundy left this review about Tanskeys

Cafe bar/bistro on the seafront with a corking view of the marine lake, the River Dee and beyond to Wales. Sunsets are spectacular. Has had different names in the past. As a pub kinda thing its more cafe than pub (serves Guinness and Peroni on draught but sells bottles of Bombardier and Speckled Hen) but you do have the view so its worth something.
