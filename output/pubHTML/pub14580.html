Bucking Fastard left this review about White Lion

Much larger inside than it would appear from the outside,the L shaped interior is completely open plan with a pine floor and a number of flatscreens showing the Friday night rugby.There are a number of high tables close to the bar,while the dog leg has more conventional tables and chairs,with french windows onto the beer garden.
It's a well used community boozer but the pubco ownership is obvious given the ale choice of Doom Bar,London Pride (ok nick) and GK Rockin' Rudolph with a fourth pump unused.
Well know for it's Friday fish and chip suppers,the pub serves a purpose although it doesn't have a huge amount of charm.

On 22nd December 2018 - rating: 6
