Graham Coombs left this review about George Hotel Of Stamford

Classic and upmarket small town hotel, famous for its pub sign which is one of the few remaining crossing the road. Large rambling collection of restaurants, lounges and function rooms behind, and a large courtyard outdoor area, but for drinkers the action is in the room fronting the main road, which is a very-civilised and traditional oak panelled hotel bar with leather furniture. Three good ales on handpump and in fine order. Prices at hotel standards, but then so is the service.
