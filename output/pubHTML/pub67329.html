ROB Camra left this review about Pinchitos Wine Bar

Formerly a tapas bar this is now a sports bar. It's one small room with several large TV's showing horse racing on some and football on the others. There's also a quiz machine that I managed to win £4 on. There's no real ale of course, so I had a half of fizz at £1.50. Not my sort of place really, but a profit of £2.50 and a free half eased the burden.
