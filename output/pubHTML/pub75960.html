Al Bundy left this review about Y Delyn

Only opens evening time. It is a 2 roomed place with the front part housing the bar. This is a thin L-shaped area which extends to a rear room. Lots of Spanish/Cuban/blah blah stylings about the decor and music. Very dark and mysterious lighting gives it a cellar bar feeling. One handpump for a reasonable beer. Plus a John Cooper Clarke look-a-like bloke behind the bar.
