Alan Winfield left this review about The Marcia Inn

This is a decent village pub that has been done up there are a few picnic tables at the front and the flowers at the front make this look welcoming.
There were three real ales on the bar and i tried the Black Sheep bitter and this was not a bad drink the other beers were Bombadier and John Smiths cask.
