Graham Coombs left this review about The Jolly Farmers

Splendid little traditional village inn of several rooms. Strong food trade, which sounded excellent although not sampled. Ales from Woodfordes, Adnams and guests. No car park worth the name but easy to find a (sensible) space on nearby roads.
