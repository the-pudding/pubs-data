Komakino . left this review about Applecart

A new Table to Table establishment in a semi-service area on the Didcot/Milton interchange junction off the A34, which offered London Pride and Adnams Ghost Ship as cask concessions and Doom Bar and OSH as chilled fridge offerings. Obviously aimed at the dry trade, a bottle of BrewDog This.Is.Lager cost £4.05.
