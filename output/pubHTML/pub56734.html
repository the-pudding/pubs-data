Philip Gaskin left this review about The Green Man

I had not visited this pub for several years and it has not changed. It has a well worn pub atmosphere to the point of becoming scruffy in some places. The bar area is quite small with a long restaurant area leading off it. Four real ale pumps, but only Adnams Bitter and Youngs Bitter were available. However, it was the Monday afternoon of the second of two four day bank holidays. We will visit again.

On 3rd May 2011 - rating: 8
