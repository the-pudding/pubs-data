Aqualung . left this review about The Stannary Court (JD Wetherspoon)

Although this is a shop conversion the building is listed so at least it has a pleasing exterior. Inside it's a more typical rectangular room with a low ceiling. The furnishings are slightly better than average.
There are ten hand pumps which had the IPA version of the JDW Trio Of Doom with Doom Bore and Abbot doubled, the ubiquitous Dartmoor Jail doubled, Hanlons Stormstay, Summerskill Indiana Bones and South Hams Wild Blonde. I went for the Stormstay (£2.29) which was in good nick. This isn't a great Spoons but for a small town one I thought it was fine.

On 13th November 2017 - rating: 7
