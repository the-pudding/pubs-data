Alan Winfield left this review about The Royal Oak Inn

The Royal Oak is a decent pub on the eastern side of Charmouth.
This is a smallish Palmers tied house that had a couple of rooms i went in the smallish lounge which was quite a nice room to have a drink in.
There were three palmers real ales on the bar i had a drink of Palmers best bitter which was a very nice drink the other beers were Bridport bitter and Palmers 200.
I thought this was the best pub in Charmouth and i would happily visit again.

Pub visited 23 August 2000.
