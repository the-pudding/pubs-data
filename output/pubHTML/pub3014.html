Alan Winfield left this review about The Crossed Keys

The Cross Keys is a largish stand alone pub that is on the very busy oundle road.
I only went in this pub because i got off a bus and noticed it was just up the road,once inside i went into a smartish room that must have been the lounge this room was quite comfortable and fairly busy on a Saturday teatime.
There were no real ales on here and the only bitter was John Smiths smooth crap so i had a Guinness which was ok.
This wa a decent enough pub that was let down by having no real ales on.

Pub visited 31/3/2007
