David Jones left this review about Anchor Inn

Great little pub, the most westerly in Shropshire and the second highest (after the Kremlin in Clee Hill). Very very remote. The main problem is finding it open - the landlord really does run this "on the side". 7pm onwards is the best bet. Worth it though, just to experience somewhere that is fast disappearing... and no doubt this will at some point too.

On 15th December 2011 - rating: 6
