Alan Winfield left this review about The Rose & Crown

The Rose and Crown is a pub in a rural area once inside it has one long narrow room that has different levels probably because the pub is on a slight slope there were three real ales on the bar though nothing special with Greene King IPA,Old Speckled Hen and O'Hanlons Stout the Greene King IPA was bland as you would except.
