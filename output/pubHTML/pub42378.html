Richard Arwkrite left this review about The Talbot

Just off the A44 beside the River Teme in beautiful countryside this large pub restaurant is well known to fishermen,ramblers and people who know a good hideaway when they see it.Disabled entry is via the hotel reception and the bar/restaurant is on one level. Extensive pricey menu , here Brakes is a dirty word.Lovely onsight brewed ales this is a place for that special treat of a weekend away perhaps. Me? I'm lucky, I live not far away.
Apart from the Farmers market on the carpark, a short walk over the footbridge brings you to a butchers that cures its own bacon,makes black pudding and pork pies. Knightwick is the place to Pig Out in every sense.
