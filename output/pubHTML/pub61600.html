Quinno _ left this review about The Sportsman

Pleasantish Greene King pub-eatery. The ale used to be quite good under the stewardship of a bloke who looked like a nightclub bouncer; it isn't anywhere near as nice now. Usually Morlands Original plus a GK list beer. Staff can be dopey.
