Alex Conway left this review about The Wellington Inn

Tucked down Alma Terrace is The Wellington, a Sam smiths pub most people would not know about. It occupies two of the centre terraced houses and is much larger than it looks oon the outside with several different rooms, some with fires.
The beer is the usual Sam Smiths range at their usual competitive prices, Old brewery Bitter still just £1.80 a pint.
Although relatively unknown this is my Pick of the Sams pubs I York, it is very nicely decorated and the fires are a great touch in winter. In summer there is also a great outside seating arer near the river to soak up the sun, definitely worth a visit.
