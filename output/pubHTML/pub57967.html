Komakino . left this review about Indian Queen

A former pub (The New Inn) in the middle of nowhere now converted into an Indian restaurant. Only one ale available, which was 'Indian Queen' brewed by Wychwood (possibly Oxford Gold) with an interesting choice (Revisionist India Spiced Pale Ale) end of barrel. Dead on an early Sunday afternoon, the bar is available to drinkers, although quite where they should sit is anyone's guess with all the tables laid out for dining. Fortunately the weather was clement so I was able to sit outside, but not one I'd seek to check out again having paid a visit.

On 5th July 2016 - rating: 3
