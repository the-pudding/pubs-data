Quinno _ left this review about Benson Blakes Bar & Grill

Went in with a group of friends en route to one of BSE's less salubrious nightspots. Wasn't expecting much from the outside but was fairly surprised. One ale was on sale (unexpectedly) which was Nethergate Umbel Magna (hooray!) which in the GK stranglehold of BSE was fantastic. The quality of the beer was good (though turnover was rapid which helps). Staff were impressively quick and good-natured.The interior is obviously very 'modern bar', all pine and chocolate colours. It was a Friday night so it was quite rammed and fairly uncomfortable to stand without knocking into someone. Clientèle were pre-clubbers - girls flashing flesh with hair rigidly straightened and lads with too much gel wearing the latest Primark number, talking quite loudly over the less-than-background music.

Overall, not bad. If you're on a night out here, you could do worse.

On 8th June 2009 - rating: 6
