Steve C left this review about The Globe

The Globe is an airside bar with some seating roped off from the main concourse. Tennents, Stella, Peroni, San Miguel, Punk IPA, Guinness and Aspall’s Cider are the keg options, no real ale. Food is available, the standard burger is £11.45.
