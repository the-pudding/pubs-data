Quinno _ left this review about Y Llew Coch

A very strange little pub in a very strange little town. The pub split into two sections, one section houses the pool table and strange music in a dark slate-hewn room. The bigger lounge area is painted burgundy and houses an arced big screen. Hancocks HB was the token ale on when I visited, though I didn't sample as I managed to miss it as it was only advertised in one bar! Had a pint of nitrokeg mild instead which was OK.

To be honest this place seemed pretty depressing. Was pleased to get out.

On 28th August 2006 - rating: 2
