Al Bundy left this review about Great Ale at the Market

As noted this is smack bang in the middle of an indoor market. Similar to a bar in Warrington market and the Arndale Centre this is basically a small stall. 3 handpumps on the bar but only the 2 for cask, the other for cider, but The Fell Robust Porter was nice so good on em. The adjacent bus station has been relocated so not sure what the future will be for this part of Bolton.

ROB Camra left this review about Great Ale

Small ale bar in the middle of Bolton Market. Lots of wooden tables in the middle of the market to sit at, shared with the other food stalls. 2 handpumps on the bar, a couple of craft kegs and lots of bottled beers. This really isn't my scene, sitting having a beer in the middle of a market. The beer was well kept, but the ale stall ws surrounded by a load of noisy scallies. The bar is only open Tuesday, Thursday, Friday & Saturday 9 - 18:30.

peter ashworth left this review about Great Ale Year Round

This micro bar is positioned in the food square of Bolton's newly furbished Market Hall and very handy for the bus station.There is a constant rotation of locally sourced real ales and a large selection of around 70 bottles of ale and cider to go at,although did not try any bottles as I was on a roll on the Chocolate Stout.
I think the bar is open every day with some days till 6 30pm with 25p off a pint on Tuesdays.
