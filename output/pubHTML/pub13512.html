Steve C left this review about Drydock @ Stanwell House Hotel

The Dry Dock is the Stanwell House Hotel bar. Located directly to the left of the hotel entrance it consists of a very small front bar area and a small separate seating area. Fifteen people would make the atmosphere extremely cosy. Cocktails and spirits are the order of the day. During my visit on a recent Tuesday evening the lone draught lager, 1936 Biere, was off. There was one other customer and no distractions such as background music so the ambience was sedate to say the least. This place is not on my revisit list and if I was staying in the hotel I would venture out to find somewhere a little more interesting.

On 4th November 2016 - rating: 3
