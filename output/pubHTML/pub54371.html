Ken Lancaster left this review about Toby Carvery Thorpe Bay

After waiting for 15 minutes for service in the bar for service I called out 'Hello'. An angry and belligerent member of staff appeared 5 minutes later and said: " I was serving another customer. IS THAT ALRIGHT WITH YOU SIR?" I asked him not to be rude. He insisted, despite his heavy sarcasm that he was not rude and then he went on to berate me about calling out 'Hello'. A throughly unpleasant experience. There are plenty of good eateries along the seafront where the staff does not believe that being rude to custmers is not a virtue. so I would recommend that you don't visit this pub.

On 19th October 2016 - rating: 1
