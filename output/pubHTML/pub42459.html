Old Boots left this review about Lamb & Flag

Traditional looking pub from the outside although partly signed as an Italian restaurant. The main drinking room is at the back with 4 pulls 3 from two crafty plus Wye Valley HPA, all the regulars were on draught Guinness however. Long yard at the back, a sitting room at the front and the Italian restaurant upstairs.

Alan Winfield left this review about Lamb & Flag

The Lamb and Flag is a narrow fronted pub that is on the main road heading north out of Worcester but it is quite close to the middle of Worcester.
Once inside there are two rooms and i had a drink in the lounge which was comfortable and quite cosy there was also a more basic bar.
This pub is a Marstons tied house and there were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Mansfield Riding bitter and this was a very nice drink that had travelled well,the other beers were Marstons Pedigree and Marstons bitter.
I quite liked this pub.
