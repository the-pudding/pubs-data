Graham Coombs left this review about The Lord Gascoigne

A large family dining pub in the Hungry Horse chain, with typical garish modern interior, liberally sprinkled with adverts and special offers, but fine for taking the kids to. Draught ales at time of visit were GK IPA and Speckled Hen; sadly despite Greene King quality assurance and Cask Marque listing, the sampled IPA had definitely seen better days. Large car park and outdoor area at rear, and handy for the station.

On 16th September 2017 - rating: 6
