Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about Crown Hotel

Refurbished Robinson's pub between the town hall / municipal buildings and the Earle Street bridge. Opened out inside to leave a 'J'-shaped bar with semi-traditional furniture but light decor. A couple of notices caught my attention; one saying that the pool queue deposit is £5, but the other proclaiming that a starter or desert could be obtained (presumably with a main meal) for just 5p! On the real ale front, Unicorn and Wizard (£2.30) are available from the four handpumps.

On 12th March 2016 - rating: 6
