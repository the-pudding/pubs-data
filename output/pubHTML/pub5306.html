Richard Whiteley left this review about The Old England

A lovely Village pub serving good food everyday, real ales on offer and a large selection of drinks available. Regular quiz nights on a Tuesday and Thursday from 8pm, only £1 entry per team and you get a supper too. The bar offers pool and darts, Sky Sports shows all the live football on the large screen and tv's with makes for a great sporting atmosphere. Poker night to be coming soon. Catch the early bird offer of a main meal and desert for £5.50, 12pm till 2pm and 5pm till 7pm monday to friday and saturday 12pm till 2pm. Sunday roasts are only £6.95 with childrens and OAP portions available, all fresh veg and hand carved meats of turkey pork and beef with chefs own recipe yorkie pud's.

On 20th September 2010 - rating: 10
