Just a quick pint, then I'm off left this review about The Cask Bar

Grim 1960s shopping parade meets 21st-century pub, so turning out rather more interesting than initial appearances would suggest. Nothing very notable about the square bar area, but people are doubtless here for the beer so the focus is on the five handpumps anyway... At the end of a busy day around the town, I went for Refresher from Slater's (£3.40) rather than the Oakham Citra, Kelham Island Pale Rider, Fat Cat Milk Stout or Sarah Hughes Dark Ruby Mild. Worth a stop if in the area.

ROB Camra left this review about The Cask Bar

A micropub housed in what looks like a 1960's estate arcade of shops. Nothing much of note or architectural interest inside although there is a small beer garden/yard at the rear, which is where we sat. 5 handpumps on the bar, all on. I fancied something light and hoppy, so I went for Oakham Citra, which was a very well kept pint. Not my favourite micro, but I was happy enough calling in here for one.
