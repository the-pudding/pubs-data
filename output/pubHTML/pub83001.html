Al Bundy left this review about Biggies Diner

This has now reopened and is called Biggies. Its themed as an American Diner place. The bar has the usual lagers and stuff and there is one handpump dispensing Salopian Gold on this visit. The rear of the place is the main diner area with the front housing the servery, behind which you will find 6 tellies along the wall. If you like to be-bop-a-looba to Buddy Holly and Eddie Cochran this is for you daddio.

19/6/15
A hotel bar next to The Wheatsheaf. Small bar area at the front extending back to a dining area. Two real ales on from local breweries.
