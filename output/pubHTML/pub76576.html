John Yeadon left this review about Toby's Tea Room

Toby's Tea Room is a cafe in a listed barn down a canal towpath at Bottom Farm by the side of the Leeds and Liverpool Canal out in the countryside.
But it is not your regular bar.
It caters for passing traffic on foot, cycle and horse, but it is not practical to access it by car.
You can get a pot of tea and something sticky there, and they are licenced to provide harder stuff such as wine, beer and cider.
But don't expect it to be open at just any time.
If it is a warm sunny summer's day on the weekend then I would say it would be open. On other occasions I would recommend you follow their website instructions and ring them.
Finally, Toby is a horse apparently.
