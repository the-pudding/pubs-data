Helen Iwanczuk left this review about The Kilkeney Inn

The Kilkeney Inn has had a major refurbishment and is open for business once more. Set in a very picturesque part of Gloucestershire, The Kilkeney is the epitome of a classy country pub. They have Bombadier and Youngs Gold on tap, to name but a few. I couuld happily have spent a few hours in this very pleasent pub.

Keith Johnson left this review about The Kilkeney Inn

The food here is not cheap but it is Very good. The St Austells Tribute on hand pump was also well kept. They also had Youngs Bitter on handpump. It has been recently renovated so I hope it suceeds. All they have to do is get rid of the awful cover versions on the sound systems.
