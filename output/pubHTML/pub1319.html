Will Larter left this review about The Crown & Cushion

We called in here on our way back from the Chequers, and found ourselves in the middle of an uproarious quiz - I'm not sure if this was a regular occurrence or a one-off for the bank holiday. Not being on the same wavelength as the yahoos who made up the majority of the crowd, we drank up quickly and left without making a note of the available beers. All I can say is that Batemans XB was one of them.

Date of visit: 3rd May 2015
