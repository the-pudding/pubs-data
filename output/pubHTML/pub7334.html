custodian 42 left this review about The Gate Inn

Well set back with car park at the front. Main room to left with snug to right which is where everybody was. No real ale at all and unfortunately we were served Strongbow in the snug. which was a mistake as when we sat in the main room we spotted Thatchers. Large TVs in the main room and some form of open area, presumably for bands? Not one to come back to.
