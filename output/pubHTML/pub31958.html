Old Boots left this review about The Red Lion Hotel

A traditional town pub, it's wide and thin with an alcove reaching back at one side of the bar counter. It's equipped with all the usual kit, darts board, pool table, bandit, juke box and a biggish sound and lighting setup. It's a Greene King pub but a pleasant surprise was the presence of Lees Bitter and a beer from Titanic next to the IPA, the usual collection of national kegs brands is also available. The Titanic was in particular good condition. Furnishing is mainly leather chairs and round wooden tables, there is constant music from the juke box. It's friendly enough and less middle class than a lot of Witney pubs. Live music, Karaoke and disco are all advertised and there's a big screen.

On 28th March 2012 - no rating submitted
