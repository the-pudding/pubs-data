Aqualung . left this review about The Red Lion (JD Wetherspoon)

The Red Lion is an original pub and is grade II listed, hence no dark blue and gold plastic signage. It's been given a typical modern JDW makeover with eating areas either side at the front and a smallish square bar area behind with a mix of seating. As it's just under five years old the fixtures and fitting are better than average.
Being a relatively small JDW in a smallish market town means that half of the ten hand pumps were taken up with the JDW Trio Of Doom. At least the rest were all in use with Grainstore 1050, Adnam's Mosaic, Great Newsome Frothingham Best, Gadd's No 5 and Elland Beyond the Pale. I went for the 1050 (£2.05) which was in excellent nick and also had a go at the Steak Club (£6.09).
I thought this was a pretty good Spoons, it's GBG 2017 listed.

Joe Derr left this review about Red Lion Inn

In the Thetford and Brandon times a few weeks ago.

"Red Lion options under review -- SOME readers will doubtless be interested in what has happened to the Red Lion, Market Place, Thetford, over recent days. The building is owned by Thetford Town Council and was most recently managed by a tenant of the council's under the terms of a 15 year lease, which commenced in 2001. The tenant agreed recently to surrender the lease and the council is now reviewing its options. The boarding of windows is looked upon as a necessary safety measure. ED CHAMBERS Clerk to Thetford Town Council."
