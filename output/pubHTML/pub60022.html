Al Bundy left this review about The Metropolitan

This is a huge pub. Multi-roomed with the entrance room being split into two. To the rear is a large high ceilinged hall-like room and to the left of this is a large, bright, airy dining room. A total of 8 handpumps are on the bar with 4 clipped for Timothy Taylor Landlord, 2 for Deuchars and the rest for guest ales and ciders. Its a very grand place and I liked it.

Alan Winfield left this review about The Metropolitan

The Metropolitan is a very large oldish pub.
Once inside i went in a large room that was fairly smart and comfortable, there were other rooms but i did'nt look to see what was in these.
There were three real ales on the bar i had a drink of Beartown Kodiac Gold and this was quite a nice drink the other beers were Taylor Landlord and Deuchars IPA.
This was not really my sort of pub but at least the beer was very nice.
