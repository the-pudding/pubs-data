Alan Winfield left this review about The Plough Inn

The Plough is a nice looking white painted pub that is situated on Statherns main street.
There are a few picnic tables outside to watch people working at the garage opposite,once inside there were two rooms with a smart cosy lounge and a fairly nice bar,this pub used to be an Home Ales tied house and there were two real ales on the bar i had a drink of Home bitter and this was a very nice drink the other beer was Home mild.
I dont know what beers this pub now has on.
