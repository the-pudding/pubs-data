stuart gregory left this review about Blue Bell Inn

improved alot in last few years and lots of pub sports going on most of the year. the pub has two teams in the asfordby and district darts league and two skittles teams playing out of the pubs skittle alley, there are also two petanque pits in the grounds of the pub with a number of teams playing out of them in the leicestershire 1990 league. the grass is well maintained and monday meal deals attracts lots of customers. the pub also holds a xmas darts competition on boxing day and all money raised gose to local charities. the local horticultural socierty holds its open skittles competition there along with the village fate every two years and open gardens every two years, the next hoby open gardens will be in summers of 2012-2014-2016-2018 and fate july 9th 2011 and then summer of 2013-2015 and 2017.
