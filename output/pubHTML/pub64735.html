Colin Walker left this review about The Kinleith Arms

The Kinleith Arms sits about 5 miles from Edinburgh city centre and is a very traditional style pub. The pub achieved the “warm welcome” commendation award in 2011 and even when visiting in Oct 2014 I found no reason that the pub should not receive the award again as the staff gave me a very friendly welcome when I entered. The lounge is where meals can be held in more comfortable surroundings away from the main bar. The sun trapped, secluded beer garden is also available for those who like to sit outside. There is also seating at the front of the building. Bar meals are available until 20:00, plus early bird specials 2 for £14.00, Mon to Fri 15:00 to 17:00 and Sun 12:30 to 15:00. Parking is available at the left of the pub.
