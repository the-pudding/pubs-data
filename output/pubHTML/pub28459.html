Real Ale Ray left this review about The Eagle

Expect to see more diners than drinkers. The pub was busy on our Tuesday afternoon visit, service was good, staff friendly and helpful. The pub consists of a large central lounge and bar, with a spiral staircase leading upstairs to seat about 35 diners. There was a large room on the right, which had a long dining table down the middle of it. There was another room at the rear leading into the garden. Three ales on handpump, London Pride, Doombar and their house beer called Eagles Nest, which is brewed by bass.
