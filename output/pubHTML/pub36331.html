Rob Hunter left this review about Three Horse Shoes

Visited November 2017 early evening. Several real ales from the Working Hand Brewery plus one called Atom which was an IPA. I had The Mogul (by the Working Hand) as I fancied a dark ale, and it was good.

Very friendly, the locals (mainly older chaps) welcomed me in and we had a bit of banter at the bar (but not the cringey type).

Peter Carr left this review about Three Horse Shoes

5 real ales and one real cider on tap. The beers were in excellent condition on each visit.
My friends and I had good meals that were reasonable priced.They have a good menue but not very flexible. The staff were friendly.
