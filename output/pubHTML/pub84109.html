Graham Coombs left this review about Gylly Beach Cafe

A swish modern beach café with a (deservedly) good reputation for food. The clean and bright main room is faced by a long veranda with excellent views over the bay. In the evening it becomes a restaurant, but the bar is open at all times. Sadly no real ale but Tribute is available on keg along with a few other beers and ciders.
