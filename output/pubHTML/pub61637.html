Quinno _ left this review about Shepherds House

After a brief period of closure there are new licensees.

November 2011
Large roadside pub on the A4. Usually three ales on, a couple from Loddon and Doom Bar though Hogs Back and West Berks have been spotted - quality can be variable. Pool and large screen TV for the football. Food is offered. Clientèle on the younger and gruffer side.
