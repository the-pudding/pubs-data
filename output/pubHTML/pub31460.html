Ian Mapp left this review about Red Lion Inn

Now open. Found when walking the Millennium Way - there is an advert for it along the Oxford Canal.

Lovely building - in a thatched terrace along a narrow lane, opposite the church.

Formal area for dining to the right, with a bar to the left - I noticed three real ales - Doombar and Purity Ubu (chosen) and one other.

Beer in great condition and a decent lunch.

Visit blogged at http://bit.ly/2g6yTTv
