Colin Walker left this review about The Compass

The Compass sits less than 200metres from Dalgety Bay train station. The Compass being a Belhaven pub offers plenty of Belhaven beers. The bar does Guest beers on tap and beers from the Inveralmond brewery plus Innis & Gunn draught Lager or Punk IPA. There are plenty of various bottled beers from around the world. There are meals available up until 9pm everyday. There are offers such as 2 main meals for £10.99, and a burger and drink from £8.29 and two steaks and a bottle of wine for £19.99 or a sharing platter with a bottle of wine* for £14.99 also available with 4 pint pitcher of beer. The Belhaven steak pie is well recommended. The television viewing shows live football on Sky Sports and BT Sports in HD. If you sign up on the Compass website you will receive a discount at Belhaven pubs, simply fill in your details and in return you’ll get a £5 voucher to spend and everyone who enters their details is automatically entered into a competition to win an iPad mini. The places is open Monday - Thursday 11:30am – midnight, food is served 12pm - 9pm, Friday 11.30am - 1am, food is served 12pm - 9pm, Saturday 10.00am - 1am, food is served 10pm - 9pm & Sunday 10am – midnight, food is served 10am - 9pm. There is plenty of parking available around the building.
