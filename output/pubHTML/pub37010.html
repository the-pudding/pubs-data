Nick Davies left this review about Kings Head

Things have improved on the beer front, Warwickshire Yer'Bard currently available, and very nice it is too.

2012 notes
----------

Pleasant if unremarkable Victorian village local consisting of two houses knocked together providing a variety of drinking areas: Sky TV and darts to one side, eating to the other. Very popular with the locals, walkers and Leamington diners-out. In the current GBG with a couple of interesting guest ales. You'd think they'd make a feature of the Warwickshire Brewery 50 yards down the road but no such luck.
