john mcgraw left this review about The Fisherman's Cot

Large rambling thatched Marston's pub in a most delightful setting on the river Exe.On a nice summers day you can sit out by the river and enjoy your beer and listen to the peace and quiet. Although the emphasis is on food the Marstons Pedigree was in excellent condition. The "Hawkridge" ham salad was superb and a very ample portion at that.
