Real Ale Ray left this review about The Blue Bell Inn

A shardlow Brewery tap with a choice of three Shardlow ales, two guest ales and two ciders, on our visit. This is a traditional two roomed pub, with a lounge and bar side, served by a central bar area. The locals including the barmaid were friendly and chatty and made us most welcome. We went for the Shardlow Golden Hop and Peakstone ales Oblivion, both of which were top class. The bus stop is also across the road from the pub, which was very handy for us heading back to Burton on Trent.

James Tolley left this review about Blue Bell Inn

Has a bar and lounge as well as pool room inside, with big screen TV which has Prem Plus. Dining room supplying fresh and pleasant pub food. Recently decked patio area outside with brollies and heat lamps. Also has outdoor BBQ in the summer (cajun chicken sandwich is my favourite). I'm looking forward to spending a few summer days down there.

The pub also has 2 guest rooms with en-suite.
