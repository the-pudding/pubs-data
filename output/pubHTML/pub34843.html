Real Ale Ray left this review about Six Bells Inn

This country pub, featuring an inglenook fireplace is now more of a restaurant than a pub. The only ales on offer are bottled and opening times are limited to evenings or weekends. Also has a lot of purpose built accommodation around the garden area.
