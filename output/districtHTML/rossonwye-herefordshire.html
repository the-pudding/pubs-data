Ye Olde Ferrie Inn

Symonds Yat

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 01 May 2019

This pub has an idyllic setting, right by the river. The only downside to that is the road down to it. I am sure I have seen it featured "worlds most dangerous roads"! Not much wider than the car it heads steeply down to the pub with no passing place... [more>]

Inn on the Wye

Walford

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 01 May 2019

Large pub and hotel near (as the name suggests!) the River Wye. 3 beers on - Ringwood 49er, Wye Valley Butty Bach and Lost Fossil. I went for the fossil and it was fine. We ate here and the food was very good. Happy to call again.
