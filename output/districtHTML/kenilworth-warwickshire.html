New Kenilworth pub reviews

Holiday Inn

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 15 July 2019

Bar within the hotel which is open to the public. You know what to expect! There are much better options nearby but if you want to tick it off feel free to do so. We popped in for a quick bottle for that reason but I'd gladly miss it out next time!
