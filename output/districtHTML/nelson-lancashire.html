Will's

Reviewed by peter ashworth on 10 June 2019

Will's is a new bar in Barrowford and also classes itself as a wine and pizza bar. There was no cask just a few beer taps. This place is closed Monday's and Tuesdays.

The Lounge

Reviewed by peter ashworth on 10 June 2019

The Lounge is a quite smart cafe bar with an outdoor area at the front.There was no cask beer on but 5 beer and lager taps.
