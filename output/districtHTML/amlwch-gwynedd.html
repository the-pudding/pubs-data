The Adelphi

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 14 August 2019

By far the best pub for ale in Amlwch with 3 on offer. Butty Bach, Hobgoblin gold and Purple Moose Elderflower ale. The Butty Bach was in good nick. Well worth a visit.
