The Foresters Arms

Kirdford

Reviewed by moby duck on 26 June 2019

A 17th century Hall & Woodhouse country pub ,traditional inside as you would expect with horse brass laden wooden beams and stone floors. Three pumps with Badger Best Bitter,Tanglefoot and Fursty Ferret. Nice enough.
