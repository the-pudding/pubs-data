New Beaumaris pub reviews

The Bull

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 12 August 2019

Imposing pub / hotel which is rightly listed and features in Camra's inventory of historic pub interiors. A good choice of beers including Bass, Hancocks HB, Jaipur and Conwy Beachcomber Blonde. The 2 I tried were in good nick. Well worth a visit.

George & Dragon

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 12 August 2019

Another one of the many Robinson pubs on the Isle of Anglesey. 5 beers on including Trooper, Unicorn, Dizzy Blonde and Cumbria Way. I tried the latter and it was fine. Worth a visit.
