Duke of Cambridge

Tilford

Reviewed by Komakino . on 31 August 2019

Foodie pub away from Tilford green, heaving on our Saturday arvo visit with lunchtime dinners. Three locals beers from Tilford Brewery which is based in a building next to the pub premises - Red Mist, Gold and Wit - all on fine form.
