Bar Story

Reviewed by john gray on 16 March 2019

In a railway arch a few doors away from Brick.Shabby chic style but busy enough.Lots of outside seating.Dull beer range .Hobgoblin-gold at £2.60 a half on keg was the default choice.No cask.Another summer months venue.
