New Marlborough pub reviews

The Bear at Marlborough

Reviewed by Gill Smith on 12 May 2019

Popped in here while in Marlborough for a brief time. Arkells house with 3B and Wiltshire Gold on. I chose the Wiltshire Gold but was not impressed with it at all.

The Green Dragon

Reviewed by Gill Smith on 12 May 2019

Wadworth house and we enjoyed their 6x and Horizon beer. IPA also available. Lovely multi roomed pub with a good atmosphere on the main street.
