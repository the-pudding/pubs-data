The Fifth Wheel

Reviewed by peter ashworth on 18 May 2019

The Fifth Wheel is situated at Lymm Services just off the M6 and has been open for a couple of years.Thought this would be a small bar but in fact is a quite large place with a fully stocked bar with 5 beer pumps, but sadly no cask.
