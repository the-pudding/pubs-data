The Valley

Reviewed by ROB Camra on 25 June 2019

This pub is now located in what used to be the upstairs rooms. They still have a pool table, but the bar is now up here as well. Up a few steps from the street there are now 2 fairly comfortable rooms. It is a bit like drinking in someone's house tho... [more>]

Crown Tavern

Reviewed by custodian 42 on 25 June 2019

Marstons pub with a large room. Bar to ear as you enter. Surprising selection of ales - Snecklifter, Old Empire, Youngs Special and of course, Pedigree. Worth the walk out from town.
