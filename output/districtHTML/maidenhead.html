Kings Arms

Cookham

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 28 January 2019

Immaculate C17th coaching house. Multiple rooms, roaring fires, friendly bar staff and manager. It was the Rebellion Brewery signage outside this freehouse that tempted me in. The IPA was in superb condition. £4.40.
