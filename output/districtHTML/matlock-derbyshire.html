The Devonshire Arms

Beeley

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 15 April 2019

Open at 11am for a drink, which was good news. But bring your money - £4.85 for Timothy Taylor Landlord. Nice country pub but nothing to justify the costs. Catering (and ripping off) the tourists at Chatsworth, I would imagine.
