Lorelei Hotel

Reviewed by Delboy 20 on 15 October 2010

Great little hotel bar. If you are a real ale fan you might spend longer in here than any other Porthcawl pubs. It can get a bit packed as it is not that big. There was a good selection of beers available on my visit and excellent service.
