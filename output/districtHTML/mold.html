New Mold pub reviews

The Hand

Gwernaffield

Reviewed by Alan Hurdle on 17 March 2019

Re-opened in March 2019 after refurbishment. A village local which retains its traditional 2-roomed interior with games room. 2 real ales from Big Hand. Opening times 4.30-11 (Mon-Thurs), 3-11 (Fri), 12-12 (Sat), 12-11 (Sun).
