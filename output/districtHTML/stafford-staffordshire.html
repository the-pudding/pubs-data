New Stafford pub reviews

Sun Inn

Reviewed by Alan Hurdle on 25 September 2019

A very good pub as most Titanic houses seem to be. Also like some of their other pubs, 6 guest beers compliment 6 of their own beers. The pub was all White on the Night! with the White Star and the White Rat both being excellent.
