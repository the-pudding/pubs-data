The Bamburgh Castle Inn

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 09 May 2019

Instantly forgettable pub experience... tartan carpets, wooden paneling and all tables setup for big menu dining. Dogs allowed in one area. Farne Island in good condition but this and a pepsi was neatly £8. Seemed expensive.
