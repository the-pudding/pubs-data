New Hexham pub reviews

The Tannery

Reviewed by Danny O'Revey on 23 April 2019

High ceiling, two rooms & a small Christmas section. Trad dark wood tables & chairs its a nice bar. Piped punk music on my visit. The beer is ok quality

Heart Of Northumberland

Reviewed by Danny O'Revey on 23 April 2019

Gastro pub, well its more restaurant than somewhere you would just go for a drink due to the layout. Expensive and rather average. However, the beer choice is not formula and the quality is good, so a couple of extra marks for this.
