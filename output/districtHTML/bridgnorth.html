New Bridgnorth pub reviews

Bulls Head

Chelmarsh

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 24 February 2019

Decent, unpretentious country inn seemingly doing well on the food trade... popular with Sunday Lunch diners. Central bar, stone floors, three real ales on - noticed HPA and Butty Bach from Wye Valley.
