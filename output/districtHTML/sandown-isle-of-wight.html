New Sandown pub reviews

The Coast

Reviewed by Graham Coombs on 29 August 2019

One of three terraced bar-restaurants overlooking the beach. This one has a slightly plasticky interior, with a terrace and deck outside leading down to the esplanade. The bar does boast a handpump but sadly it appears to be out of use.
