The High Cross

Reviewed by hondo . on 06 October 2019

Former public toilets now a bar. Located on the route from seven sisters tube to whl. Craft keg served. Narrow interior that retains original tiling.

The Bricklayer

Reviewed by hondo . on 06 October 2019

Just up from whl the pub retains it’s tiled exterior. Interior festooned in fitba scarfs,flags and memorabilia. Standard draught selection.
