The Bull

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 18 August 2019

Very chainey feel to the place - catering for standard pub dining. Large bar - plenty of staff on. Nice patio out the front. McMullens Country was in very, very good condition - so can't complain about the cellarmanship.
