Cinque Port Arms

Reviewed by Just a quick pint, then I'm off on 19 May 2019

Very traditional Old Town pub with dark wood-panelled walls in the compact bar area. Three of the four handpumps were in use when I visited, offering the ubiquitous Harvey's (£3.90) plus London Pride and ESB from Fuller's.
