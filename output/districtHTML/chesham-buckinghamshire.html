New Chesham pub reviews

The Blue Ball

Reviewed by Ian Mapp on 26 May 2019

Pleasant county pub - seemingly in the middle of nowhere. I was first in at lunchtime and a bit sceptical as to how the first London Pride of the day would be. It was perfect. Nice outdoor area. Traditional pub interior setup for dining.
